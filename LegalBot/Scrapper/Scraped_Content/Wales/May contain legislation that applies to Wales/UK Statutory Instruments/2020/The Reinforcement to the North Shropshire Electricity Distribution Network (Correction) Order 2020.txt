The Reinforcement to the North Shropshire Electricity Distribution Network (Correction) Order 2020
Made
28th September 2020
Coming into force
29th September 2020
The Reinforcement to the North Shropshire Electricity Distribution Network Order 2020 (“the Order”)(1), which granted development consent within the meaning of the Planning Act 2008 (“the Act”)(2), contains correctable errors within the meaning of paragraph 1 of Schedule 4 to the Act.
In accordance with paragraph 1(5)(a) of Schedule 4 to the Act, before the end of the relevant period, the Secretary of State received a written request from the applicant(3), for the correction of errors and omissions in the Order.
In accordance with paragraph 1(7) of Schedule 4 to the Act, the Secretary of State has informed Shropshire County Council, as the relevant local planning authority that the request has been received.
The Secretary of State, in exercise of the powers conferred by section 119 of, and paragraphs 1(4) and (8) of Schedule 4 to, the Act, makes the following Order:

S.I. 2020/325.
(2)
2008 c. 29. Relevant amendments to Schedule 4 have been made by paragraph 70 of Schedule 13 and part 20 of Schedule 25 to the Localism Act 2011 (c. 20).
(3)
The terms ‘applicant’, ‘relevant period’ and ‘relevant local planning authority’ are defined in paragraph 4 of Schedule 4 to the Act.Citation and commencement
