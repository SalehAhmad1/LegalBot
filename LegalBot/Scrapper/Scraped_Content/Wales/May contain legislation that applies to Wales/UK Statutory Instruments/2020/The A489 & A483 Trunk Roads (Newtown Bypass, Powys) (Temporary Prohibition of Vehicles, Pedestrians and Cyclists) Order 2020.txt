2020 No. 949 (W. 212) 
ROAD TRAFFIC, WALES 
The A489 & A483 Trunk Roads 
(Newtown Bypass, Powys) 
(Temporary Prohibition of Vehicles, 
Pedestrians and Cyclists) Order 
2020 
Made 
4 September 2020 
Coming into force 
14 September 2020 
The Welsh Ministers, as traffic authority for the 
relevant lengths of the A489 & A483 Trunk Roads, are 
satisfied that traffic on a specified length of the trunk 
roads should be prohibited because of works proposed 
on or near the roads. 
The Welsh Ministers, therefore, in exercise of the 
powers conferred upon them by section 14(1) and (4) 
of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(1), make this 
Order. 
Title, Commencement and Interpretation 
1. The title of this Order is the A489 & A483 Trunk 
Roads 
(Newtown 
Bypass, 
Powys) 
(Temporary 
Prohibition of Vehicles, Pedestrians and Cyclists) 
Order 2020 and it comes into force on 14 September 
2020. 
2. In this Order: 
“exempted vehicle” (“cerbyd esempt”) means any 
vehicle being used by the emergency services,  in 
connection with the works giving rise to this Order 
and to access appropriate properties; 
                                                                               
(1) 
1984 c.27; Section 14 was substituted by the Road Traffic 
(Temporary Restrictions) Act 1991 (c.26), section 1(1) and 
Schedule 1. By virtue of S.I. 1999/672, and section 162 of, 
and paragraph 30 of Schedule 11 to, the Government of 
Wales Act 2006 (c.32), these powers are now exercisable by 
the Welsh Ministers in relation to Wales. 
 
 
2
“the trunk roads” (“y cefnffyrdd”) means the A489 
Newtown to Aberystwyth Trunk Road and A483 
Swansea to Manchester Trunk Road known as 
Newtown Bypass that extends from the Llanidloes 
Road Roundabout to Pool Road Roundabout, 
Newtown, Powys;  
“works period” (“cyfnod y gwaith”) means that 
period commencing at 08:00 hours on 14 
September 2020 and ending when the temporary 
traffic signs are permanently removed. 
Prohibitions 
3. No person may, during the works period, drive or 
cause or permit any vehicle, other than an exempted 
vehicle, to proceed on the length of the trunk roads. 
4. No person may, during the works period, ride any 
pedal cycle on the length of the trunk roads. 
5. No pedestrian may, during the works period, 
proceed on the length of the trunk roads. 
Application 
6. The prohibitions in articles 3, 4 and 5 apply only 
during such times and to such extent as indicated by 
traffic signs. 
Duration of this Order 
7. The maximum duration of this Order is 18 
months.
