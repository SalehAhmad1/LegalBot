The Secretary of State previously decided that it was in the public interest to restrict flying in the 
area specified in the Schedule to the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Duxford) Regulations 
2020(a) (â€œthe principal Regulations) by reason of a flying display scheduled to take place there. 
The Secretary of State makes these Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by article 239 
of the Air Navigation Order 2016(b). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Duxford) 
(Revocation) Regulations 2020 and come into force with immediate effect. 
Revocation of Regulation 
2. The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Duxford) Regulations 2020 are revoked.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations revoke the statutory instrument which imposed airspace restrictions on flying 
in the area specified in the Schedule to that instrument, the event referred to being cancelled.
