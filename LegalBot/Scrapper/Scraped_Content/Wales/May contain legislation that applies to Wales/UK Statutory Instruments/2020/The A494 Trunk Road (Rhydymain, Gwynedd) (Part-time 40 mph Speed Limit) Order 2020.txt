ROAD TRAFFIC, WALES 
The A494 Trunk Road (Rhydymain, 
Gwynedd) (Part-time 40 mph Speed 
Limit) Order 2020 
Made 
24 November 2020 
Coming into force 
1 December 2020 
The Welsh Ministers, as traffic authority for the 
relevant length of the A494 trunk road, in exercise of 
the powers conferred upon them by sections 84(1)(c), 
84(1A) and 84(2) of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 
1984(1), after giving public notice of their intention to 
do so and after consultation with the Chief Officer of 
North Wales Police, make this Order. 
Title, Commencement and Interpretation  
1. The title of this Order is the A494 Trunk Road 
(Rhydymain, Gwynedd) (Part-time 40 mph Speed 
Limit) Order 2020 and it comes into force on 1 
December 2020. 
2. In this Order—

(a) any vehicle being used for the purposes 
described in section 87 of the Road Traffic 
Regulation Act 1984(2);

air force purposes and being driven by a 
person for the time being subject to the orders

Restriction 
3. No person may drive any motor vehicle, other 
than an exempted vehicle, at a speed exceeding 40 
miles per hour on the length of the trunk road at 
Rhydymain, Gwynedd that extends from a point 270 
metres west of the centre-point of its junction with the 
unclassified road at the western end of Rhydymain to a 
point 215 metres east of the centre-point of its junction 
with the unclassified road at the eastern end of 
Rhydymain. 
Application 
4. The restriction in article 3 applies only during 
such times and to such extent as indicated by traffic 
signs.
