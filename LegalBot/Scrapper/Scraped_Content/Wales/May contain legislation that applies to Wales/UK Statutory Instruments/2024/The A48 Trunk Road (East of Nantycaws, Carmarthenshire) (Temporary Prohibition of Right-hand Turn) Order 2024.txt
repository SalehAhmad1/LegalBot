The A48 Trunk Road (East of

(Temporary Prohibition of Right-

Made 
26 February 2024 
Coming into force 
2 March 2024 
The Welsh Ministers, as traffic authority for the 
relevant length of the A48 trunk road, are satisfied that 
traffic on a specified length of the trunk road should be 
prohibited because of works proposed on or near the 
road. 
The Welsh Ministers, therefore, in exercise of the 
powers conferred upon them by section 14(1) and (4) 
of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(1), make this 
Order. 
Title and commencement  
1. The title of this Order is the A48 Trunk Road 
(East of Nantycaws, Carmarthenshire) (Temporary 
Prohibition of Right-hand Turn) Order 2024 and this 
Order comes into force on 2 March 2024. 
Interpretation 
2. In this Order— 
“the trunk road” (“y gefnffordd”) means the A48 
London to Fishguard trunk road; 
“works period” (“cyfnod y gwaith”) means the 
period commencing at 20:00 hours on 2 March

Prohibition  
3. No person may, during the works period, permit 
any vehicle to proceed in the A48 eastbound dedicated 
right-turn lane into the recycling centre and landfill 
site east of Nantycaws, Carmarthenshire. 
Application 
4. The prohibition in article 3 applies only during 
such times and to such extent as indicated by traffic 
signs. 
Duration of this Order 
5. The maximum duration for this Order is 12 
months.
