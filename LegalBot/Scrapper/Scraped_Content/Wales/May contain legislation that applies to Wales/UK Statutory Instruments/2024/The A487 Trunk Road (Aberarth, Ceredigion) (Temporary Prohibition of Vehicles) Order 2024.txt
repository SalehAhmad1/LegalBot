2024 No. 703 (W. 98) 
ROAD TRAFFIC, WALES 
The A487 Trunk Road (Aberarth, 
Ceredigion) (Temporary Prohibition 
of Vehicles) Order 2024 
Made 
 24 May 2024 
Coming into force 
3 June 2024 
The Welsh Ministers, as traffic authority for the A487 
trunk road, are satisfied that traffic on a specified 
length of the trunk road should be prohibited because 
of works proposed on or near the road. 
The Welsh Ministers, therefore, in exercise of the 
powers conferred upon them by section 14(1) and (4) 
of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(1), make this 
Order. 
Title and commencement  
1. The title of this Order is the A487 Trunk Road 
(Aberarth, Ceredigion) (Temporary Prohibition of 
Vehicles) Order 2024 and this Order comes into force 
on 3 June 2024. 
Interpretation 
2. In this Order— 
“exempted vehicle” (“cerbyd esempt”) means any 
vehicle being used by the emergency services or in 
connection with the works giving rise to this 
Order; 
“the trunk road” (“y gefnffordd”) means the A487 
Fishguard to Bangor trunk road; 
                                                                               
(1) 
1984 c. 27; section 14 was substituted by the Road Traffic 
(Temporary Restrictions) Act 1991 (c. 26), section 1(1) and 
Schedule 1. By virtue of S.I. 1999/672, and section 162 of, 
and paragraph 30 of Schedule 11 to, the Government of 
Wales Act 2006 (c. 32), these functions are now exercisable 
by the Welsh Ministers in relation to Wales. 
 
2
“works period” (“cyfnod y gwaith”) means 
intermittent periods commencing at 19:00 hours on 
3 June 2024 and ending when the temporary traffic 
signs for each period of works are permanently 
removed. 
Prohibition 
3. No person may, during the works period, drive 
any vehicle, other than an exempted vehicle, on the 
length of the trunk road that extends from its junction 
with the U1553 to Aberarth Church to a point 400 
metres north-east of the centre-point of its junction 
with the B4577 at Aberarth, Ceredigion. 
Application 
4. The prohibition in article 3 applies only during 
such times and to such extent as indicated by traffic 
signs. 
Duration of this Order 
5. The maximum duration of this Order is 18 
months.
