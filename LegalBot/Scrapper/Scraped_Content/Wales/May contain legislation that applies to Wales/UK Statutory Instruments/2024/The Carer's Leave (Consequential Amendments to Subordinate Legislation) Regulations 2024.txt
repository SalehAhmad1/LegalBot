28th February 2024EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Regulations)
These Regulations make amendments to secondary legislation in consequence of the Carer's Leave Act 2023, which makes provision for entitlement to carer’s leave.
These Regulations are part of a package of legislation to bring the statutory right to carer’s leave into effect. A full impact assessment of the affect that this package will have on the costs to business is available from the Department of Business and Trade, Labour Market Directorate, Old Admiralty Building, Trafalgar, London, SW1A 2DY.
