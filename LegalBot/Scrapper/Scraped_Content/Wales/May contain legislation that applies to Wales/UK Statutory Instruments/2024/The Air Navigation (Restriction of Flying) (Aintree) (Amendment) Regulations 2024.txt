Coming into force with immediate effect 
The Secretary of State has decided that it is necessary in the public interest to amend the radius of 
the restricted area in the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Aintree) Regulations 2024(a). 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(b). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Aintree) 
(Amendment) Regulations 2024 and they come into force with immediate effect. 
Amendment of the Regulations 
2. The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Aintree) Regulations 2024 are amended as 
follows. 
3. In regulation 3(1) for “radius of 1 nautical mile” substitute “radius of 1.5 nautical miles”.
