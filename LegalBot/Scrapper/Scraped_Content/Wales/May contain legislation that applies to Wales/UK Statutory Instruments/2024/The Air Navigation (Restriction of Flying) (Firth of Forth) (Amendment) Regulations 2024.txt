The Secretary of State has decided that it is necessary in the public interest to amend the 
coordinates for the flying restrictions in Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Firth of Forth) 
Regulations 2024(a). 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(b). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Firth of Forth) 
(Amendment) Regulations 2024 and they come into force on 21st March 2024. 
Amendment of the Regulations 
2.—(1) The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Firth of Forth) Regulations 2024 are 
amended as follows. 
(2) In regulation 3(1), after sub-paragraph (a) insert— 
“(aa) 560106N 0032431W,”.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations amend the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Firth of Forth) Regulations 
2024 (S.I. 2024/392) by inserting an additional coordinate to amend the restricted area. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Notice to Aviation (NOTAM).
