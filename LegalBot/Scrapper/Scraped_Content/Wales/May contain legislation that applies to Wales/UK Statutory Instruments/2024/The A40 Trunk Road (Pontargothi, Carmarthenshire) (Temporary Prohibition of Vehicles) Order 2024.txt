ROAD TRAFFIC, WALES 
The A40 Trunk Road (Pontargothi, 
Carmarthenshire) (Temporary 
Prohibition of Vehicles) Order 2024 
Made 
 19 January 2024 
Coming into force                   26 January 2024 
The Welsh Ministers, as traffic authority for the A40   
trunk road, are satisfied that traffic on a specified 
length of the trunk road should be prohibited because 
of works proposed on or near the road. 
The Welsh Ministers, therefore, in exercise of the 
powers conferred upon them by section 14(1) and (4) 
of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(1), make this 
Order. 
Title and commencement  
1. The title of this Order is the A40 Trunk Road 
(Pontargothi, 
Carmarthenshire) 
(Temporary 
Prohibition of Vehicles) Order 2024 and this Order 
comes into force on 26 January 2024. 
Interpretation 
2. In this Order—  
“exempted vehicle” (“cerbyd esempt”) means any 
vehicle being used by the emergency services or in 
connection with the works giving rise to this 
Order; 
“the trunk road” (“y gefnffordd”) means the length  
of the A40 Llandeilo to Carmarthen trunk road; 
                                                                               
(1) 
1984 c. 27; section 14 was substituted by the Road Traffic   
(Temporary Restrictions) Act 1991 (c. 26), section 1(1) and 
Schedule 1.  By virtue of S.I. 1999/672, and section 162 of, 
and paragraph 30 of Schedule 11 to, the Government of 
Wales Act 2006 (c. 32), these powers are now exercisable by 
the Welsh Ministers in relation to Wales. 
 
2
“works period” (“cyfnod y gwaith”) means the  
period commencing at 10:00 hours on the 26 
January 2024 and ending when the temporary 
traffic signs are permanently removed. 
Prohibition  
3. No person may, during the works period, drive 
any vehicle, other than an exempted vehicle, on the 
length of the trunk road that extends from a point 45 
metres west of the junction with Cwrt y Cresselly to a 
point 60 metres east of the junction with Ty Newydd 
House at Pontargothi. 
Application 
4. The prohibition in article 3 applies only during 
such times and to such extent as indicated by traffic 
signs. 
Duration of this Order 
5. The maximum duration of this Order is 18 
months.
