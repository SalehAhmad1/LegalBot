of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a).

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations impose restrictions on flying in the vicinity of Wellesbourne Mountford 
Airfield, Warwickshire, by the reason of the intended holding there of a Royal Aero Club Air 
Race scheduled to take place on 21st and 22nd September 2024. 
A large number of aircraft will be involved in high energy manoeuvres during the event and 
therefore the Civil Aviation Authority and the Department for Transport agree that flying should 
be restricted in the vicinity of the display for reasons of public safety. 
The times mentioned in regulation 3(2) are Co-ordinated Universal Time (UTC) which is one hour 
behind British Summer Time (BST). 
In relation to the permission mentioned in regulation 3(3)(a)(i), the Wellesbourne Mountford 
Airfield, Air Race air-ground service may be contacted as notified. 
In relation to the permission mentioned in regulation 3(3)(a)(ii), the Royal Aero Club Records, 
Racing and Rally Association Race Officials may be contacted as notified. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Aeronautical Information Circular 
(AIC) and Notice to Aviation (NOTAM).
