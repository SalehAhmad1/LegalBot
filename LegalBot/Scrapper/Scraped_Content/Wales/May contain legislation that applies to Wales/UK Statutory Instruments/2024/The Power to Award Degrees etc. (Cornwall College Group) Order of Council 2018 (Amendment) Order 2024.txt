STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS
2024 No. 621
EDUCATION, ENGLAND
The Power to Award Degrees etc. (Cornwall College Group) Order of Council 2018 (Amendment) Order 2024
Made
3rd May 2024
Coming into force
16th May 2024
The Office for Students makes this Order in exercise of the powers conferred by section 45(1) of the Higher Education and Research Act 2017(1).
The Office for Students has requested advice from the relevant body regarding the quality of, and standards applied to, higher education provided by the provider and has had regard to that advice, in accordance with section 46(1)(c) and (7) of that Act.
The Office for Students has complied with the procedure on giving notifications in accordance with section 48 of that Act.
The Office for Students has complied with the requirements for the granting of a foundation degree only authorisation set out in section 42(4) of that Act.
(1)
2017 c. 29.Citation and commencement
1.  This Order may be cited as the Power to Award Degrees etc. (Cornwall College Group) Order of Council 2018 (Amendment) Order 2024 and shall come into force on 16th May 2024.Variation of Power to Award Degrees etc. (Cornwall College Group) Order of Council 2018
2.—(1) The Power to Award Degrees etc. (Cornwall College Group) Order of Council 2018 is varied in accordance with paragraph (2).
(2) For article 2, substitute—
“2.  Cornwall College Group shall be competent to grant awards of the kind mentioned in paragraph 2A of section 76 of the said Act for a fixed term beginning on 16th May 2018 and expiring on 15th May 2026.”.Jean Arnold
Deputy Director of Quality
Office for Students
3rd May 2024EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order varies Cornwall College Group’s existing Degree Awarding Powers authorisation so that it remains valid until 15th May 2026.
An impact assessment has not been produced for this instrument as it has no impact on businesses and civil society organisations. The instrument has no impact on the public sector.
