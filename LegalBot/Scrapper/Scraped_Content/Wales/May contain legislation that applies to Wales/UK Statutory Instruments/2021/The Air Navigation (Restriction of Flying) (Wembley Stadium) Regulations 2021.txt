The Secretary of State has decided that it is in the public interest to restrict flying in the vicinity of 
Wembley Football Stadium, London, by reason of the holding of football matches associated with 
the UEFA Euro 2020 football championship. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Wembley 
Stadium) Regulations 2021 and come into force on 13th June 2021. 
Interpretation 
2. In regulation 3(1) the times referred to are Co-ordinated Universal Time(b). 
Restricted airspace 
3.—(1) Subject to paragraph (2), no aircraft is to fly below 2,500 feet above mean sea level 
within the area bounded by a circle having a radius of 1.5 nautical mile whose centre is at 
513322N 001646W between— 
(a) 0900 hours and 2259 hours on 13th June 2021,  
(b) 1400 hours and 2259 hours on 18th June 2021, 
(c) 1400 hours and 2259 hours on 22nd June 2021, 
(d) 1400 hours and 2259 hours on 26th June 2021, 
(e) 0900 hours and 2259 hours on 29th June 2021, 
(f) 1400 hours and 2259 hours on 6th July 2021, 
(g) 1400 hours and 2259 hours on 7th July 2021, and 
(h) 1400 hours and 2259 hours on 11th July 2021.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations impose restrictions on flying in the vicinity of Wembley Stadium, London 
during football matches associated with the UEFA Euro 2020 football championship. A large 
number of spectators will attend the events and therefore the Metropolitan Police considers that 
flying should be restricted in the vicinity of the Stadium for reasons of public safety and security. 
This has been agreed by the Civil Aviation Authority and the Department for Transport.  
The times mentioned in regulation 3(1) are Co-ordinated Universal Time (UTC) which is one hour 
behind British Summer Time (BST). 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Aeronautical Information Circular 
(AIC) and Notice to Airmen (NOTAM).
