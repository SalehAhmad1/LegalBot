(1)
2006 c. 40.Citation and commencement
1.  This Order may be cited as the Inspectors of Education, Children’s Services and Skills (No. 4) Order 2021 and comes into force on 11th November 2021.Appointment of Her Majesty’s Inspectors of Education, Children’s Services and Skills
2.  The persons named in the Schedule to this Order are appointed as Her Majesty’s Inspectors of Education, Children’s Services and Skills.Ceri King
