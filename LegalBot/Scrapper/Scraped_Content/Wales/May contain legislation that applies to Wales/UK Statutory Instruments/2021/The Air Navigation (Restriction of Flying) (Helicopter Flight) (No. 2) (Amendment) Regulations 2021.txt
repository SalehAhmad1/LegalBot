The Secretary of State has previously decided that it is in the public interest to restrict flying in the 
vicinity of a route between Edinburgh and Stirling by reason of an intended Helicopter Flight. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Helicopter 
Flight) (No. 2) (Amendment) Regulations 2021 and come into force on 29th June 2021. 
Amendment to the Regulations 
2.—(1) The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Helicopter Flight) (No. 2) Regulations 
2021(b) are amended as follows. 
(2) In regulation 3 paragraph (1)(b) for “5 nautical miles” substitute “4.3 nautical miles”. 
(3) In regulation 3 paragraph (1)(d) for “11.5 nautical miles” substitute “10 nautical miles”.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations amend the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Helicopter Flight) (No. 2) 
Regulations 2021 (S.I. 2021/757). They amend the specific radius around two separate co-
ordinates for the Helicopter Flight. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Notice to Airmen (NOTAM).
