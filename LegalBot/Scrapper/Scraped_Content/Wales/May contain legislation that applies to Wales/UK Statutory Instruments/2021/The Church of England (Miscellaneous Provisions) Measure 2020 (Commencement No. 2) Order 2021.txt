The Church of England (Miscellaneous Provisions) Measure 2020 (Commencement No. 2) Order 2021
Made
29th April 2021
The Archbishops of Canterbury and York, in exercise of the powers conferred by section 16 of the Church of England (Miscellaneous Provisions) Measure 2020(1), make the following Order:
(1)
2020 No. 1.Citation
1.  This Order may be cited as the Church of England (Miscellaneous Provisions) Measure 2020 (Commencement No. 2) Order 2021.Commencement on 14th July 2021
2.  Sections 1(4) and (5) of the Church of England (Miscellaneous Provisions) Measure 2020 come into force on 14th July 2021.+Justin Cantuar:
Archbishop of Canterbury
+Stephen Ebor:
Archbishop of York
29th April 2021EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This is the second Commencement Order bringing into force provisions of the Church of England (Miscellaneous Provisions) Measure 2020.
The Order brings section 1(4) and (5) (which provide a definition of “religious community” by reference to recently enacted Canonical provision) into force on 14th July 2021.NOTE AS TO EARLIER COMMENCEMENT ORDERS
