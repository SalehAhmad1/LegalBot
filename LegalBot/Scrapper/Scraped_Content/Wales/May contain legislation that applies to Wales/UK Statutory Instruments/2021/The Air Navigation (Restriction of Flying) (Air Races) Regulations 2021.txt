The Secretary of State has decided that it is necessary in the public interest to restrict flying in the 
vicinity of the aerodromes at Beccles, Haverfordwest, Sandown, Llanbedr, Leeds East and 
Leicester by reason of the intended holding of the Aero Club Air Races. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Air Races) 
Regulations 2021 and come into force on 22nd May 2021. 
Interpretation 
2. In regulation 3 the times referred to are Co-ordinated Universal Time(b).

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations impose restrictions on flying in the vicinity of the aerodromes at Beccles, 
Haverfordwest, Sandown, Llanbedr, Leeds East and Leicester by reason of the intended holding of 
the Aero Club Air Races.  
A large number of aircraft will be involved in high energy manoeuvres during the races and 
therefore the Civil Aviation Authority and the Department for Transport agree that flying should 
be restricted in the vicinity of the races for reasons of public safety. 
The times mentioned in regulation 3 are Co-ordinated Universal Time (UTC), which for the 
purpose of these Regulations is one hour behind British Summer Time (BST). 
In relation to the permissions mentioned in Regulations 3(7) to 3(12), the relevant air ground 
service can be contacted as notified.  
In relation to the permission mentioned in Regulations 3(13)(a), the Royal Aero Club Records 
Racing and Rally Association Clerk of the Course may be contacted on telephone number 
07725629947.  
Details of the Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Aeronautical Information Circular 
(AIC) and Notice to Airman (NOTAM). 
The point of contact in the Civil Aviation Authority is Mr S.J. Maxted, Airspace Regulator, Safety 
and Airspace Regulation Group, on telephone number 03301383216.
