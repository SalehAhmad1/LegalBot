The Secretary of State has previously decided that it is in the public interest to restrict flying in the 
vicinity of Old Warden Aerodrome, Bedfordshire by reason of the intended holding of flying 
displays that are scheduled to take place there. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Old Warden) 
(Amendment) (No. 2) Regulations 2021 and they come into force on 14th August 2021. 
Interpretation 
2. In regulation 3(1) the times referred to are Co-ordinated Universal Time(b). 
Amendment 
3. The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Old Warden) Regulations 2021(c) are amended as 
follows— 
(a) in regulation 3 paragraph 3(e) for “1715 hours and 1945 hours” substitute “1700 hours and 
2000 hours”.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations amend the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Old Warden) Regulations 
2021 (S.I. 2021/359, as amended by S.I. 2021/475). The amendment alters the specific timings on 
14th August 2021. 
The times mentioned in regulation 3 are Co-ordinated Universal Time (UTC) which is one hour 
behind British Summer Time (BST). 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Notice to Aviation (NOTAM).
