22nd December 2021 
Coming into force with immediate effect 
The Secretary of State has previously decided that it is in the public interest to restrict flying in the 
vicinity of Edinburgh by reason of the intended holding of events associated with the New Year 
celebrations. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Edinburgh) 
(Revocation) Regulations 2021 and they come into force with immediate effect. 
Revocation of Regulations 
2. The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Edinburgh) Regulations(b) are revoked.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations revoke the statutory instrument which imposed restrictions on flying in the 
vicinity of Edinburgh (S.I. 2021/1311) as the intended New Year celebrations scheduled to be held 
there have now been cancelled.
