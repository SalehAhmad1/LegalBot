27 September 2021EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Regulations)
These Regulations bring into force section 1 of the Fire Safety Act 2021 (“the Act”) in relation to premises in Wales.
Section 1 of the Act makes amendments to article 6 (application to premises) of the Regulatory Reform (Fire Safety) Order 2005 (S.I. 2005/1541). Section 4 of the Act contains the commencement provisions.
