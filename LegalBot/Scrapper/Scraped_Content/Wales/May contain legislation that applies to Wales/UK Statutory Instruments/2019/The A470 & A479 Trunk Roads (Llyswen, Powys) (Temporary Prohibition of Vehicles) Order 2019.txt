ROAD TRAFFIC, WALES 
The A470 & A479 Trunk Roads 
(Llyswen, Powys) (Temporary 
Prohibition of Vehicles) Order 2019 
Made 
22 October 2019 
Coming into force 
28 October 2019 
The Welsh Ministers, as traffic authority for the A470 
and A479 Trunk Roads, are satisfied that traffic on 
specified lengths of the trunk roads should be 
prohibited because of works proposed on or near the 
roads. 
The Welsh Ministers, therefore, in exercise of the 
powers conferred upon them by section 14(1) and (4) 
of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(1), make this 
Order. 
Title, Commencement and Interpretation 
1. The title of this Order is the A470 & A479 Trunk 
Roads (Llyswen, Powys) (Temporary Prohibition of 
Vehicles) Order 2019 and it comes into force on 28 
October 2019. 
2. In this Order: 
“the trunk road(s)” (“y gefnffordd/cefnffyrdd”) 
means the A470 Cardiff to Llangurig Trunk Road 
and the A479 Glanusk Park to Llyswen Trunk 
Road; 
“works period” (“cyfnod y gwaith”) means the 
period commencing at 09:00 hours on 28 October

Prohibition 
3. No person may, during the works period, drive 
any vehicle on the lengths of the trunk roads specified 
in the Schedule to this Order. 
Application 
4. The prohibition in article 3 applies only during 
such times and to such extent as indicated by traffic 
signs. 
Duration of this Order 
5. The maximum duration of this Order is 18 
months.

SCHEDULE  
The length of the A470 trunk road at Llyswen, Powys 
that extends from a point 175 metres south-west of the 
centre-point of its junction with the A479 to a point 
600 metres north-west of the centre-point of its 
junction with the B4350. 
The length of the A479 trunk road that extends from a 
point 35 metres north-west of the centre-point of its 
junction with Llys Meillion estate road to the centre-
point of its junction with the A470.
