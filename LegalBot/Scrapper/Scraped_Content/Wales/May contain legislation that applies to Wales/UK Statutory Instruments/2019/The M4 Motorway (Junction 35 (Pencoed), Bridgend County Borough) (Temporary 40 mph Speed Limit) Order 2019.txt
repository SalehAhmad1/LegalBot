ROAD TRAFFIC, WALES 
The M4 Motorway (Junction 35 
(Pencoed), Bridgend County 
Borough) (Temporary 40 mph 
Speed Limit) Order 2019 
Made 
16 October 2019 
Coming into force 
21 October 2019 
The Welsh Ministers, as traffic authority for the 
relevant length of the M4 Motorway, are satisfied that 
traffic on specified lengths of the motorway should be 
restricted because of works proposed on or near the 
motorway. 
The Welsh Ministers, therefore, in exercise of the 
powers conferred upon them by section 14(1), (4) and 
(7) of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(1), make 
this Order. 
Title, Commencement and Interpretation 
1. The title of this Order is the M4 Motorway 
(Junction 35 (Pencoed), Bridgend County Borough) 
(Temporary 40 mph Speed Limit) Order 2019 and it 
comes into force on 21 October 2019.

described in section 87 of the Road Traffic

air force purposes and being driven by a

Restriction 
3. No person may drive any motor vehicle, other 
than an exempted vehicle, at a speed exceeding 40 
miles per hour on the lengths of the eastbound and 
westbound exit slip roads of the motorway at Junction 
35 (Pencoed), Bridgend. 
Application 
4. The restriction in article 3 applies only during 
such times and to such extent as indicated by traffic 
signs. 
Suspension 
5. Any statutory provisions restricting the speed of 
vehicles on the lengths of the motorway are suspended 
during such times as the restriction specified in article 
3 is in operation. 
Duration of this Order 
6. The maximum duration of this Order is 18 
months.
