CIVIL AVIATION 
The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Lodge Shinness, 
Lairg, Highlands) (Emergency) (Revocation) Regulations 2022 
Made 
- 
- 
- 
- at 14:40 p.m. on 26th August 2022 
Coming into force with immediate effect 
The Secretary of State previously decided that it was necessary in the public interest to restrict 
flying in the vicinity of Lairg by reason of an emergency having arisen in that area. These 
regulations are no longer considered necessary. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Lodge 
Shinness, Lairg, Highlands) (Emergency) (Revocation) Regulations 2022 and they come into force 
with immediate effect. 
Revocation of Regulations 
2. The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Lodge Shinness, Lairg, Highlands) (Emergency) 
Regulations 2022 (b) are revoked.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations revoke the statutory instrument which imposed emergency restrictions on 
flying in the vicinity of Lairg (S.I. 2022/921) as the emergency in question having passed.
