Coming into force with immediate effect 
The Secretary of State had previously decided that it was necessary in the public interest to restrict 
flying at London Luton Airport by aircraft of a specified type. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and Commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (London Luton 
Airport) (No. 2) (Revocation) Regulations 2022 and they come into force with immediate effect. 
Revocation of Regulations 
2. The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (London Luton Airport) (No. 2) Regulations 
2022(b) are revoked.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations revoke the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (London Luton Airport) (No. 
2) Regulations 2022 (S.I. 2022/419) which imposed restrictions on the departure of a specified 
aircraft type from London Luton Airport. Those Regulations are no longer considered necessary.
