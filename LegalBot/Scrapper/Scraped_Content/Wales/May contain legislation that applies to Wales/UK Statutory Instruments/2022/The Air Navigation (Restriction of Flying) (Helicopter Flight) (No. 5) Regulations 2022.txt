EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations impose restrictions on flying in the vicinity of Gloucestershire, South Wales, 
Wiltshire, Oxfordshire, Berkshire and Greater London by reason of two intended helicopter flights 
that are scheduled to take place there. The Civil Aviation Authority and the Department for 
Transport agree that it is in the public interest to restrict flying in the vicinity of the relevant areas 
of the two helicopter flights to avoid the risk that transient aircraft may disrupt them. These 
Regulations do not apply insofar as flying is already prohibited by the Air Navigation (Restriction 
of Flying) (Nuclear Installations) Regulations 2016 (S.I. 2016/1003) or the Air Navigation 
(Restriction of Flying) (Highgrove House) (Restricted Area EG R105) Regulations 2018 (S.I. 
2018/907). 
The times mentioned in regulations 3(1) and 3(2) are Co-ordinated Universal Time (UTC) which 
is one hour behind British Summer Time (BST). 
In relation to the permissions mentioned in regulation 3(4), Royal Air Force Brize Norton, Bristol 
Airport, Cardiff Airport and Farnborough Radar may be contacted as notified. 
Full details of these Regulations will be promulgated by Notice to Aviation (NOTAM).
