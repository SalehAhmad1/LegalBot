ROAD TRAFFIC, WALES 
The A40 Trunk Road (Pensarn 
Roundabout to Pont Lesneven 
Roundabout, Carmarthen, 
Carmarthenshire) (Temporary 
Prohibition of Vehicles) Order 2022 
Made 
22 March 2022 
Coming into force 
26 March 2022 
The Welsh Ministers, as traffic authority for the 
relevant length of the A40 trunk road, are satisfied that 
traffic on a specified length of the trunk road should be 
prohibited because of works proposed on or near the 
road. 
The Welsh Ministers, therefore, in exercise of the 
powers conferred upon them by section 14(1) and (4) 
of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(1), make this 
Order. 
Title and commencement  
1. The title of this Order is the A40 Trunk Road 
(Pensarn Roundabout to Pont Lesneven Roundabout, 
Carmarthen, Carmarthenshire) (Temporary Prohibition 
of Vehicles) Order 2022 and this Order comes into 
force on 26 March 2022.

“exempted vehicle” (“cerbyd esempt”) means any

Prohibition  
3. No person may, during the works period, drive 
any vehicle, other than an exempted vehicle, on the 
length of the trunk road that extends from its junction 
with the western side of Pensarn Roundabout to its 
junction with the eastern side of Pont Lesneven 
Roundabout at Carmarthen, Carmarthenshire. 
Application 
4. The prohibition in article 3 applies only during 
such times and to such extent as indicated by traffic 
signs. 
Duration of this Order 
5. The maximum duration for this Order is 18 
months.
