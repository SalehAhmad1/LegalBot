29th June 2022EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Regulations)
These Regulations bring into force, on the 1st September 2022, the substantive provisions of the Education (Careers Guidance in Schools) Act 2022. A full impact assessment has not been produced for this instrument as no, or no significant, impact on the private, voluntary or public sector is foreseen.
