10th May 2022EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This instrument corrects a number of errors introduced by the Social Security (Disability Assistance for Working Age People) (Consequential Amendments) Order 2022 (S.I. 2022/177) to certain social security legislation applying in England and Wales and Northern Ireland. This instrument is being issued free of charge to all known recipients of that instrument.
A full impact assessment has not been produced for this instrument as no, or no significant, impact on the private, voluntary or public sectors is foreseen.
