The Secretary of State has previously decided that it is necessary in the public interest to restrict 
flying in the London and Windsor areas by reason of the funeral of Her late Majesty Queen 
Elizabeth II and now considers it necessary in the public interest to amend the specific 
exemptions. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (London and 
Windsor) (Amendment) Regulations 2022 and they come into force on 19th September 2022. 
Amendment 
2.—(1) The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (London and Windsor) Regulations 2022(b) 
are amended as follows. 
(2) In regulation 3— 
(a) in subparagraph (3)(a)(iii) omit “or”, and 
(b) omit subparagraph (3)(a)(iv).
