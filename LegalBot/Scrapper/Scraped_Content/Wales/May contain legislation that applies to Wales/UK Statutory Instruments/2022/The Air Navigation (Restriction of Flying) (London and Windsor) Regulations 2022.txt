The Secretary of State has decided that it is necessary in the public interest to restrict flying in the 
areas specified in regulation 3(1) by reason of the funeral of Her late Majesty Queen Elizabeth II. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (London and 
Windsor) Regulations 2022 and they come into force on 19th September 2022. 
Interpretation 
2. In regulation 3(1) the times referred to are Co-ordinated Universal Time(b).

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations impose restrictions on flying in the vicinity of Central London and Windsor by 
reason of the funeral of Her late Majesty Queen Elizabeth II. These restrictions form part of the 
overall security arrangements for this event and have been agreed with the Civil Aviation 
Authority and the Department for Transport. These Regulations do not apply insofar as flying is 
already prohibited by the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Isle of Dogs) Regulations 2004 
(S.I. 2004/2091), the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (City of London) Regulations 2004 
(S.I. 2004/2092), the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Specified Area) Regulations 2005 
(S.I. 2005/964), the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Hyde Park) Regulations 2017 (S.I. 
2017/1300), and the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Windsor) (Restricted Area EG R156) 
Regulations 2021 (S.I. 2021/1137). 
The times mentioned in regulations 3(1) are Co-ordinated Universal Time (UTC) which is one 
hour behind British Summer Time (BST).
