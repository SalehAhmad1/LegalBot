The Secretary of State has previously decided that it is in the public interest to restrict flying by 
reason of the intended holding of flying displays by a jet formation display team. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Jet Formation 
Display Teams) (Amendment) Regulations 2022 and they come into force on 2nd June 2022. 
Amendment of the Regulations 
2. In the Schedule of the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Jet Formation Display Teams) 
Regulations 2022(b) for “Midlands Air Festival 521146N 0015358W 8,300 feet 2 and 3 June 
2022” substitute “Midlands Air Festival 521146N 0015358W 8,300 feet 2, 3 and 4 June 2022”.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations amend the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Jet Formation Display 
Teams) Regulations 2022 (S.I. 2022/356) which restrict flying in the vicinity of where jet 
formation display teams intend to give flying displays. They amend the dates scheduled for the 
displays at the Midlands Air Show with the addition of a display scheduled to take place there on 
4th June 2022.
