The Secretary of State has decided that it is in the public interest to restrict the flying of unmanned 
aircraft in the area specified in regulation by 3 by reason of the intended holding of the Diamond 
League International Athletics Event. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement

Regulations 2022 and they come into force on 20th May 2022.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations impose restrictions on the flying of unmanned aircraft in the vicinity of 
Birmingham during the holding of the Diamond League International Athletics event that is 
scheduled to take place there on 21st May 2022. In view of the high visibility of this event and at 
the request of the West Midland Police Service, the Civil Aviation Authority and Department for 
Transport agree that the flying of unmanned aircraft should be restricted for reasons of safety and 
security. 
The times mentioned in regulation 3(1) are Co-ordinated Universal Time (UTC) which is one hour 
behind British Summer Time (BST). 
In relation to the permission mentioned in regulation 3(2), West Midlands Police Service may be 
contacted on telephone number 07387 097458. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Notice to Aviation (NOTAM).
