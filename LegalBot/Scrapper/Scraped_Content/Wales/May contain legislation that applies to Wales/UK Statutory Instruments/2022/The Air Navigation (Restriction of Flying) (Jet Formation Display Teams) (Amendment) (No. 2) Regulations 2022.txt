The Secretary of State has decided that it is in the public interest to amend the restriction of flying 
for the Jet Formation Display Teams by adding an additional two display dates. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Jet Formation 
Display Teams) (Amendment) (No. 2) Regulations 2022 and they come into force on 7th July 
2022. 
Amendment of the Regulations 
2. In the Schedule to the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Jet Formation Display Teams) 
Regulations 2022(b) for “Southport 533900N 0030114W 8,100 feet 9 and 10 July 2022” 
substitute “Southport 533900N 0030114W 8,100 feet 7, 8, 9 and 10 July 2022”.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations amend the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Jet Formation Display 
Teams) Regulations 2022 (S.I. 2022/356, as amended by S.I. 2022/537) by adding additional 
displays at Southport on 7th and 8th July 2022.
