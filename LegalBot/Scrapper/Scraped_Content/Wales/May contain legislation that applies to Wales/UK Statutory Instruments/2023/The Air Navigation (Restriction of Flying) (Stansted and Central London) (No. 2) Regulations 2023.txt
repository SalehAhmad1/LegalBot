The Secretary of State has decided that it is necessary in the public interest to restrict flying in the 
area specified in regulation 3 by reason of the intended visit there by a guest of the United 
Kingdom Government. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Stansted and 
Central London) (No. 2) Regulations 2023 and they come into force on 31st October 2023. 
Interpretation

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations restrict flying in the vicinity of London Stansted Airport, within a corridor 
between London Stansted Airport and London, and in Central London on the occasion of a visit by 
a guest of the United Kingdom Government which is due to take place on 31st October 2023 to 
2nd November 2023 (inclusive). 
As part of the overall security arrangements for the visit, the Civil Aviation Authority and the 
Department for Transport agree that it is in the public interest that flying should be restricted in the 
relevant areas for reasons of public safety and security. 
These Regulations apply except insofar as flying is already prohibited by the Air Navigation 
(Restriction of Flying) (Isle of Dogs) Regulations 2004 (S.I. 2004/2091), the Air Navigation 
(Restriction of Flying) (City of London) Regulations 2004 (S.I. 2004/2092), the Air Navigation 
(Restriction of Flying) (Specified Area) Regulations 2005 (S.I. 2005/964), and the Air Navigation 
(Restriction of Flying) (Hyde Park) Regulations 2017 (S.I. 2017/1300). 
The times mentioned in regulation 3 are Co-ordinated Universal Time (UTC) which is the same 
time as Greenwich Mean Time (GMT). 
In relation to the permission mentioned in regulation 3(3)(c) and 3(5)(d)(ii), the Metropolitan 
Police Service may be contacted as notified. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Briefing Sheet and Notice to 
Aviation (NOTAM).
