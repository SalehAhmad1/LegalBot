The Secretary of State has decided that it is necessary in the public interest to revoke the 
restrictions on flying specified in the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Glasgow) 
(Emergency) Regulations 2023(a) (“the principal Regulations”) by reason of an emergency having 
passed. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(b). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Glasgow) 
(Emergency) (Revocation) Regulations 2023 and come into force with immediate effect. 
Revocation of Regulations 
2. The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Glasgow) (Emergency) Regulations 2023 are 
revoked.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations revoke the statutory instrument which imposed emergency restrictions on 
flying in the area specified in the Schedule to that instrument, the emergency in question having 
passed.
