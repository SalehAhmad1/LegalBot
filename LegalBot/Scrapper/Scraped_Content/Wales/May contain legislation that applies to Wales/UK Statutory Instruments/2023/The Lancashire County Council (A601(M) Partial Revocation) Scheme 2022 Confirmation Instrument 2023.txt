THE SCHEDULE
THE LANCASHIRE COUNTY COUNCIL (A601(M) PARTIAL REVOCATION) SCHEME 2022
Lancashire County Council, being the highway authority for the special road, makes this Scheme in exercise of their powers under section 16 of the Highways Act 1980 and of all other powers enabling them in that behalf.
1.  The Council are authorised to revoke the special status road along the route described in the Schedule to this Scheme.
2.  The centre line of the road route is indicated by a heavy black line on the deposited plan.
3.  In this Scheme:-
“the Council” means the Lancashire County Council;
“the deposited plan” means the plan marked “The Lancashire County Council (A601(M) partial revocation) Scheme 2022” sealed with the Common Seal of the County Council and deposited at the offices of the County Council at County Hall, Preston, PR1 8XJ. A certified copy is also deposited at the offices of the Department for Transport.
“the route” is the route described in the Schedule to this Scheme.
4.  This Scheme hereby revokes the Scheme specified in the first column of the Schedule to this Scheme to the extent specified in the second column of that Schedule.
5.  This Scheme may be cited as the Lancashire County Council (A601(M) partial revocation) Scheme 2022.Paragraph 3
THE SCHEDULE TO THE SCHEME

The Lancashire County Council (Carnforth Link) Special Road Scheme 1985 (as confirmed by the Lancashire County Council (Carnforth Link) Special Road Scheme 1985 Confirmation Instrument 1985)(1). A route of 1.38 kilometres in length starting at Junction 35 of the M6 Motorway and proceeding in a generally northerly direction up to and including the Junction 35A roundabout at the A6 north of Carnforth.

S.I. 1985/1536. The Scheme confirmed by that S.I. was partially revoked by S.I. 2020/165.EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Instrument)
This Instrument confirms a Scheme (The Lancashire County Council (A601(M) Partial Revocation) Scheme 2022) to revoke part of the Lancashire County Council (Carnforth Link) Special Road Scheme 1985 (as confirmed by the Lancashire County Council (Carnforth Link) Special Road Scheme 1985 Confirmation Instrument 1985 (S.I. 1985/1536)) so that a length of road (as described in the second column of the Schedule to the Scheme confirmed by this Instrument) will cease to be a special road. That length of road will then be classified as an all-purpose ‘A’ road.
The Secretary of State has modified the Lancashire County Council (A601(M) Partial Revocation) Scheme 2022 as follows:
1.  For “The Lancashire County Council make”, substitute “Lancashire County Council, being the highway authority for the special road, makes”.
2.  In the paragraph numbered “1”, for “Schedule 1 of”, substitute “the Schedule to”.
3.  In the paragraph numbered “3”:
(a)
before “the Department for Transport”, insert “the offices of”; and
(b)
for “Schedule 1 of”, substitute “the Schedule to”.
4.  In the paragraph numbered “4”, for “Second Schedule hereto to the extent specified in the second column of the Second Schedule”, substitute “Schedule to this Scheme to the extent specified in the second column of that Schedule”.
5.  “THE SCHEDULE 1” is deleted and “THE SCHEDULE 2” becomes “THE SCHEDULE TO THE SCHEME” and in that Schedule:
(a)
the word “PARTIALLY” is added to the title of the first column; and
(b)
the following wording is added to the first column: “(as confirmed by the Lancashire County Council (Carnforth Link) Special Road Scheme 1985 Confirmation Instrument 1985)”, with a footnote; and
(c)
under “EXTENT OF REVOCATION”, for “approximately 2.1”, substitute “1.38”.
