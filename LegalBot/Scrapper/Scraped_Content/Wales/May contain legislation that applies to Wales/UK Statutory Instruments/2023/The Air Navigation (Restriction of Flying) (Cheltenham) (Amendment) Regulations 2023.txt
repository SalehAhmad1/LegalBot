The Secretary of State has decided that it is necessary in the public interest to amend the 
restriction on flying in the vicinity of the Cheltenham Festival. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Cheltenham) 
(Amendment) Regulations 2023 and they come into force on 14th March 2023. 
Amendment of the Regulations 
2. The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Cheltenham) Regulations 2023(b) are amended as 
follows. 
3. In regulation 3(2)(c)(i) for “National Police Service” substitute “National Police Air Service”.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations amend the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Cheltenham) Regulations 
2023 (S.I. 2023/31) by amending the exemption related to the National Police Air Service. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Notice to Aviation (NOTAM).
