The Secretary of State has decided that it in the public interest to restrict the flying of unmanned 
aircraft in the area specified in Regulation 3(1) by reason of the intended gathering there of a large 
number of persons at a football match. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Wembley 
Stadium) Regulations 2023 and they come into force on 26th March 2023.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations impose restrictions on the flying of unmanned aircraft in the vicinity of 
Wembley Football Stadium, London during a UEFA EURO 2024 qualifying football match that is 
scheduled to take place there on 26th March 2023. 
As a large number of spectators are due to attend the match the Metropolitan Police Service 
considers that unmanned aircraft flying should be restricted in the vicinity of the Stadium for 
reasons of public safety and security and this has been agreed with by the Civil Aviation Authority 
and the Department for Transport. 
The times mentioned in regulation 3(1) are Co-ordinated Universal Time (UTC) which is one hour 
behind British Summer Time (BST). 
In relation to the permission mentioned in regulation 3(2), the Metropolitan Police Service may be 
contacted on telephone number 07342 718340. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Briefing Sheet and Notice to 
Aviation (NOTAM).
