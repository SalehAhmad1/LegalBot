The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a).

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations impose restrictions on the flying of unmanned aircraft in the vicinity of 
Greenwich between 2nd May 2023 and 7th May 2023 (inclusive) by reason of the intended 
mooring there of a ship. 
At the request of the Metropolitan Police Service, the Civil Aviation Authority and the 
Department for Transport agree that the flying of unmanned aircraft should be restricted in the 
vicinity of the mooring for reasons of public safety and security. 
The times mentioned in regulation 3(1) are Co-ordinated Universal Time (UTC) which is one hour 
behind British Summer Time (BST). 
In relation to the permission mentioned in regulation 3(2), the Metropolitan Police Service may be 
contacted as notified. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by a Briefing Sheet and Notice to 
Aviation (NOTAM).
