ECCLESIASTICAL LAW, ENGLAND
The Church of England (Miscellaneous Provisions) Measure 2020 (Commencement No. 3) Order 2023
Made
15th November 2023
The Archbishops of Canterbury and York, in exercise of the powers conferred by section 16 of the Church of England (Miscellaneous Provisions) Measure 2020(1), make the following Order:
(1)
2020 No. 1.Citation
1.  This Order may be cited as the Church of England (Miscellaneous Provisions) Measure 2020 (Commencement No. 3) Order 2023.Commencement
2.  Section 8 of the Church of England (Miscellaneous Provisions) Measure 2020 (parochial registers) comes into force on the day after the day on which this Order is made.+Justin Cantuar:
Archbishop of Canterbury
+Stephen Ebor:
Archbishop of York
15th November 2023EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order is the third and final Commencement Order bringing into force provisions of the Church of England (Miscellaneous Provisions) Measure 2020.
This Order brings section 8 of that Measure (parochial registers) into force on the day after the day on which this Order is made.NOTE AS TO EARLIER COMMENCEMENT ORDERS
