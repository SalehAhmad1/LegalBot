Coming into force with immediate effect 
The Secretary of State for a reason affecting the public interest deems it necessary to amend the 
Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Liverpool) (Emergency) Regulations 2023(a) (“the 
principal Regulations”). 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(b). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Liverpool) 
(Emergency) (Amendment) Regulations 2023 and they come into force with immediate effect. 
Restricted airspace 
2.—(1) The principal Regulations are amended as follows. 
(2) In regulation 2, for “1,100” substitute “3,500”. 
(3) In the Schedule, column 1, for “Liverpool” substitute “Bebington”. 
(4) In the Schedule, column 2, for “1 nautical mile” substitute “2 nautical miles”.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations amend the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Liverpool) (Emergency) 
Regulations 2023 (S.I. 2023/1058) by amending the altitude and size of the restricted area and the 
name of the specified area.
