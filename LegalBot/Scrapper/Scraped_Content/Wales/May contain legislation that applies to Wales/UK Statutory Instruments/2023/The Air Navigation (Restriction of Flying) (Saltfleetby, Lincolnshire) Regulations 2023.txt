article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations impose restrictions on the flying of unmanned aircraft in the vicinity of 
Saltfleetby, Lincolnshire by reason of an intended VIP visit there. 
In view of the high visibility of the visit and at the request of Lincolnshire Police, the Civil 
Aviation Authority and the Department for Transport agree that the flying of unmanned aircraft 
should be restricted in the vicinity of the event for reasons of public safety and security. 
The times mentioned in regulation 3(1) are Co-ordinated Universal Time (UTC) which is one hour 
behind British Summer Time (BST). 
In relation to the permission mentioned in regulation 3(2), Lincolnshire Police may be contacted 
as notified. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by a Briefing Sheet and Notice to 
Aviation (NOTAM).
