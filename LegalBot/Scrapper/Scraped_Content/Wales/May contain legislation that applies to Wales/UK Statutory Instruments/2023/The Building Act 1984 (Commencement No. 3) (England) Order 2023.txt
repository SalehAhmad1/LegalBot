11th September 2023EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order brings into force section 33 of the Building Act 1984 (c. 55) so far as relating to England. (By virtue of section 134(1A) only Welsh Ministers have the power to commence section 33 in relation to Wales.) Section 33 makes provision in relation to testing and sampling.NOTE AS TO EARLIER COMMENCEMENT ORDERS
(This note is not part of the Order)
