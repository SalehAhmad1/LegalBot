Made

Coming into force

The Welsh Ministers, as traffic authority for the A470 
Trunk Road, in exercise of the powers conferred upon 
them by sections 14(1) and 124 of, and paragraph 27 
of Schedule 9 to, the Road Traffic Regulation Act 
1984(1), make this Order. 
Title and Commencement  
1. The title of this Order is the A470 Trunk Road 
(Upper Boat to Bridge Street Interchange, Pontypridd, 
Rhondda Cynon Taf) (Temporary 50 mph Speed 
Limit) Order 2018 (Revocation) Order 2019 and it 
comes into force on 17 June 2019. 
Revocation 
2. The A470 Trunk Road (Upper Boat to Bridge 
Street Interchange, Pontypridd, Rhondda Cynon Taf) 
(Temporary 50 mph Speed Limit) Order 2018(2) is 
revoked.
