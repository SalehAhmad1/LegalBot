ROAD TRAFFIC, WALES 
The A40 Trunk Road (Westbound 
Slip Roads at Johnstown and St 
Clears, Carmarthenshire) 
(Temporary Prohibition of Vehicles) 
Order 2023 
Made 
3 March 2023 
      Coming into force                      13 March 2023 
The Welsh Ministers, as traffic authority for the A40 
trunk road, are satisfied that traffic on specified lengths 
of the trunk road should be prohibited because of 
works proposed on or near the road. 
The Welsh Ministers, therefore, in exercise of the 
powers conferred upon them by section 14(1) and (4) 
of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(1), make this 
Order. 
Title and commencement  
1. The title of this Order is the A40 Trunk Road 
(Westbound Slip Roads at Johnstown and St Clears, 
Carmarthenshire) (Temporary Prohibition of Vehicles) 
Order 2023 and this Order comes into force on 13 
March 2023.

2. In this Order—  
“exempted vehicle” (“cerbyd esempt”) means any 
vehicle being used by the emergency services or in

“works period” (“cyfnod y gwaith”) means

the 13 March 2023 and ending when the temporary

Prohibition  
3. No person may, during the works period, drive   
any vehicle, other than an exempted vehicle, on the 
lengths of the trunk road specified in the Schedule to 
this Order. 
Application 
4. The prohibition in article 3 applies only during 
such times and to such extent as indicated by traffic 
signs. 
Duration of this Order 
5. The maximum duration of this Order is 18 
months.
