The Additional Learning Needs Code (Appointed Day) (Wales) Order 2021
Made
26 March 2021
The Welsh Ministers, in exercise of the power conferred on them by section 5(4)(b) of the Additional Learning Needs and Education Tribunal (Wales) Act 2018(1), make the following Order:
(1)
2018 anaw 2.Title
1.  The title of this Order is the Additional Learning Needs Code (Appointed Day) (Wales) Order 2021.Appointed Day
2.  The day appointed for the coming into force of the Additional Learning Needs Code (a copy of which was laid in draft before Senedd Cymru on 18 March 2021 and which was approved by a resolution of Senedd Cymru on 23 March 2021) is 1 September 2021.Kirsty Williams
Minister for Education, one of the Welsh Ministers
26 March 2021EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order appoints 1 September 2021 as the day on which the Additional Learning Needs Code (“the Code”) issued under section 4 of the Additional Learning Needs and Education Tribunal (Wales) Act 2018 (“the Act”) by the Welsh Ministers comes into force.
The Code imposes statutory requirements and includes statutory guidance in relation to identifying and meeting the additional learning needs of children and young people under Part 2 of the Act.
