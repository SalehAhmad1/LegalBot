ROAD TRAFFIC, WALES 
The M4 Motorway (Junction 25A 
(Grove Park) to Junction 28 
(Tredegar Park), Newport) 
(Temporary Prohibition of Vehicles 
with a weight in excess of 44 
Tonnes) Order 2021 
Made 
27 May 2021 
Coming into force 
29 May 2021 
The Welsh Ministers, as traffic authority for the 
relevant length of the M4 motorway, are satisfied that 
traffic on specified lengths of the motorway should be 
prohibited because of the likelihood of danger to the 
public and the workforce. 
The Welsh Ministers, therefore, in exercise of the 
powers conferred upon them by section 14(1) and (4) 
of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(1), make this 
Order. 
Title and commencement  
1. The title of this Order is the M4 Motorway 
(Junction 25A (Grove Park) to Junction 28 (Tredegar 
Park), Newport) (Temporary Prohibition of Vehicles 
with a weight in excess of 44 Tonnes) Order 2021 and 
this Order comes into force on 29 May 2021. 
Interpretation 
2. In this Order:  
                                                                               
(1) 
1984 c. 27; section 14 was substituted by the Road Traffic   
(Temporary Restrictions) Act 1991 (c. 26), section 1(1) and 
Schedule 1.  By virtue of S.I. 1999/672, and section 162 of, 
and paragraph 30 of Schedule 11 to, the Government of 
Wales Act 2006(c. 32), these powers are now exercisable by 
the Welsh Ministers in relation to Wales. 
 
2
“exempted vehicle” (“cerbyd esempt”) means any 
vehicle in connection with the works giving rise to 
this Order; 
“the motorway” (“y draffordd”) means the M4 
London to South Wales Motorway; 
“prohibition period” (“cyfnod y gwaharddiad”) 
means the period commencing at 00:01 on the 29 
May 2021 and ending when the temporary traffic 
signs are permanently removed.       
Prohibition 
3. No person may, during the prohibition period, 
drive any vehicle with a weight in excess of 44 tonnes, 
on the lengths of the motorway specified in the 
Schedule to this Order.  
Application 
4. The prohibition in article 3 applies only during 
such times and to such extent as indicated by traffic 
signs. 
Duration of this Order 
5. The maximum duration of this Order is 18  
months.
