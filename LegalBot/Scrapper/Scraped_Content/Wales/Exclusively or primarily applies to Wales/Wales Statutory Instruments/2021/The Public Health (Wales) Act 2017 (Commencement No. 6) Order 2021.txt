25 February 2021EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order made by the Welsh Ministers brings into force specified provisions of the Public Health (Wales) Act 2017 (“the Act”).
Article 2 brings into force Chapter 1 of Part 3 (sections 4 to 29) of the Act on 1 March 2021, to the extent that those provisions have not already been commenced. Article 2 also brings into force Schedule 1 (fixed penalties) and Schedule 2 (smoking: consequential amendments) to the Act. Schedule 1 is introduced by sections 27 and 49 of the Act, and Schedule 2 is introduced by section 29 of the Act.NOTE AS TO EARLIER COMMENCEMENT ORDERS
