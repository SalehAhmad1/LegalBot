28 October 2020EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order brings into force provisions of the Additional Learning Needs and Education Tribunal (Wales) Act 2018 (“the Act”).
The provisions listed in article 2 come into force fully on 2 November 2020. These provisions comprise powers to make subordinate legislation, interpretation provisions and provisions otherwise related to those powers to make subordinate legislation.
The provisions listed in article 3 also come into force on that date, but only for the purposes of making regulations, or issuing or revising the code under section 4 of the Act.
Article 4 brings into force on 4 January 2021 provisions of the Act related to the designation of additional learning needs co-ordinating officers. It also brings into force, for all remaining purposes, provisions related to the list of independent special post-16 institutions in Wales and England, at which a local authority may secure education or training for a child or young person.
