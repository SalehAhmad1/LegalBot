Road (A487) (Chimneys Link Trunking and De-
Trunking, Pembrokeshire) Order 2022 and it comes 
into force on 22 July 2022.

Highways to become trunk roads 
3. The highways described in Schedule 1 to this 
Order and which are shown on the deposited plan shall 
become trunk roads as from the date when this Order 
comes into force. 
4. The centre lines of the new trunk roads are 
indicated by a heavy black line on the deposited plan. 
Lengths of trunk road ceasing to be trunk roads 
5. The length of the trunk road described in Schedule 
2 to this Order,  and shown by broad striped hatching 
on the deposited plan, shall cease to be trunk road and 
shall become a classified road as from the date on 
which the Welsh Ministers provide notification of this 
to Pembrokeshire County Council, who will become 
the highway authority for that length of road.
