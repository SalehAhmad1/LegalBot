Made 
            7 March 2018 
Coming into Force 
12 March 2018 
The Welsh Ministers, as traffic authority for the A470 
Maentwrog to East of Conwy Trunk Road, are 
satisfied that traffic on a specified length of the trunk 
road should be restricted and/or prohibited because of 
works proposed on or near the road. 
The Welsh Ministers, therefore, in exercise of the 
powers conferred upon them by section 14(1), (4) and 
(7) of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(1), make 
this Order. 
Title, Commencement and Interpretation  
1. The title of this Order is the A470 Trunk Road (Pont 
yr Afanc, Betws-y-Coed, Conwy County Borough) 
(Temporary Speed Restrictions and No Overtaking) 
Order 2018 and it comes into force on 12 March 2018. 
2.  In this Order:  
 
“exempted vehicle” (“cerbyd esempt”) means: 
 
(a) any vehicle being used by the emergency

(1) 1984 c.27; Section 14 was substituted by the Road Traffic

relation to Wales.

Restrictions and Prohibition 
3.  No person may, during the works period, drive or 
cause or permit any vehicle to proceed at a speed 
exceeding 40 miles per hour in the length of the trunk 
road. 
      4. No person may, during the works period, drive or 
cause or permit any vehicle to proceed at a speed 
exceeding 10 miles per hour in the length of the trunk 
road.  
  5. No person may, during the works period, use or 
permit the use of a vehicle, other than an exempted 
vehicle, in such a way that it overtakes any other vehicle 
in the length of the trunk road during such times as the 
restriction referred to in article 4 is in operation. 
 
3
Suspension 
6. Any statutory provisions restricting the speed of 
vehicles on the relevant length of the trunk road are 
suspended during such times as the restrictions specified 
in articles 3 and 4 are in operation. 
Application 
7.   (1) The restrictions and prohibition in articles 3, 4 
and 5 apply only during such times and to such extent as 
indicated by traffic signs. 
       (2) No speed limit imposed by this Order applies 
to an exempted vehicle. 
Duration of this Order 
8.  The maximum duration for this Order is 18 months.
