ROAD TRAFFIC, WALES 
The A44 Trunk Road (Lovesgrove, 
Aberystwyth, Ceredigion) 
(Temporary 40 mph Speed Limit & 
No Overtaking) Order 2018 
Made 
1 June 2018 
Coming into force 
7 June 2018 
The Welsh Ministers, as traffic authority for the A44 
Llangurig to Aberystwyth Trunk Road, are satisfied 
that traffic on specified lengths of the trunk road 
should be restricted and/or prohibited because of the 
likelihood of a danger to the public on or near the road. 
The Welsh Ministers, therefore, in exercise of the 
powers conferred upon them by section 14(1), (4) and 
(7) of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(1), make 
this Order. 
Title, Commencement and Interpretation 
1. The title of this Order is the A44 Trunk Road 
(Lovesgrove, Aberystwyth, Ceredigion) (Temporary 
40 mph Speed Limit & No Overtaking) Order 2018 
and it comes into force on 7 June 2018.

Restriction and Prohibition 
3. No person may, during the restricted period, drive 
or cause or permit any vehicle to proceed at a speed 
exceeding 40 miles per hour on the lengths of the trunk 
road described in the Schedule. 
4. No person may, during the restricted period, use 
or permit the use of a vehicle, other than an exempted 
vehicle, in such a way that it overtakes any other 
vehicle on the lengths of the trunk road described in 
the Schedule. 
Suspension 
5. Any statutory provisions restricting the speed of 
vehicles on the lengths of the trunk road specified in 
the Schedule are suspended during such times as the 
restriction specified in article 3 is in operation. 
Application 
6.—(1) The restriction and prohibition in articles 3 
and 4 apply only during such times and to such extent 
as indicated by traffic signs. 
(2) No speed limit imposed by this Order applies to       
an exempted vehicle.

SCHEDULE 
 
The length of the trunk road at Lovesgrove, 
Aberystwyth, Ceredigion that extends from a point 124 
metres west of the centre-point of its junction with the 
A4159 to a point 510 metres west of the centre-point 
of that junction. 
 
The length of the trunk road at Lovesgrove, 
Aberystwyth, Ceredigion that extends from a point 124 
metres east of the centre-point of its junction with the 
A4159 to a point 846 metres east of the centre-point of 
that junction.
