2018 No. 189 (W. 40) 
ROAD TRAFFIC, WALES 
The A40 Trunk Road (Park Road 
and Hereford Road, Abergavenny, 
Monmouthshire) (Temporary 
Prohibition of Vehicles & 
Pedestrians) Order 2018 
Made 
5 February 2018 
Coming into force 
12 February 2018 
The Welsh Ministers, being the traffic authority for the 
A40 Raglan to Llandovery Trunk Road, are satisfied 
that traffic on a specified length of the trunk road 
should be prohibited because of works proposed on or 
near the road. 
The Welsh Ministers, therefore, in exercise of the 
powers conferred upon them by section 14(1) and (4) 
of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(1), make this 
Order. 
Title, Commencement and Interpretation 
1. The title of this Order is The A40 Trunk Road 
(Park Road and Hereford Road, Abergavenny, 
Monmouthshire) (Temporary Prohibition of Vehicles 
& Pedestrians) Order 2018 and it comes into force on 
12 February 2018. 
2. In this Order: 
 
“exempted vehicle” (“cerbyd esempt”) means any 
vehicle being used by the emergency services and in 
connection with the works giving rise to this Order; 
 
 
(1)    1984 c.27; section 14 was substituted by the Road Traffic 
(Temporary  Restrictions) Act 1991 (c.26), section 1(1) and 
Schedule 1.  By virtue of S.I. 1999/672, and section 162 of, and 
paragraph 30 of Schedule 11 to, the Government of Wales Act 
2006(c.32), these powers are now exercisable by the Welsh 
Ministers in relation to Wales. 
 
2
“the trunk road” (“y gefnffordd”) means the length 
of the A40 Raglan to Llandovery Trunk Road and 
footways as follows: 
i.  The length of the trunk road that extends from a  
point 15 metres east of the centre point of the  
junction of Park Avenue and Park Road to a 
point 145 metres south of the centre point of the 
junction of Hereford Road and Park Road; 
ii.    The length of the footway on the northern side 
of the trunk road known as Park Road that 
extends from a point 5 metres east of the centre 
point of Bailey Park entrance to its junction 
with Hereford Road in an easterly direction; and 
iii.   The length of the footway on the western side of 
the trunk road known as Hereford Road that 
extends from its junction with Park Road to its 
junction with Lion Street in a southerly 
direction. 
 
“works period” (“cyfnod y gwaith”) means 
intermittent periods commencing at 20:00 hours on 12 
February 2018 and ending when the temporary traffic 
signs for each period of works are removed. 
Prohibitions 
3. No person may, during the works period, drive or 
cause or permit any vehicle, to proceed on the length 
of the trunk road. 
4. No person may, during the works period, proceed 
along the lengths of the footway. 
Application 
5. The prohibitions of article 3 and 4 applies only 
during such times and to such extent as indicated by 
traffic signs. 
Duration of this Order 
6. The maximum duration for this Order is 18 
months. 
 
Signed under authority of the Cabinet Secretary for
