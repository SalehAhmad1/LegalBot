2017 anaw 2.Title and interpretation

Part 5 (intimate piercing); and

section 94 (interpretation) for the purpose of Part 5.Vaughan Gething

3 January 2018EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order made by the Welsh Ministers brings into force specified provisions of the Public Health (Wales) Act 2017 (“the Act”).
Article 2 brings provisions of the Act relating to the intimate piercing of children into force on 1 February 2018.NOTE AS TO EARLIER COMMENCEMENT ORDERS
