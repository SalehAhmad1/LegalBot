ROAD TRAFFIC, WALES 
The A55 Trunk Road (Junction 22 
(Old Colwyn) to Junction 23 
(Llanddulas), Conwy County 
Borough) (Temporary Prohibition 
of Vehicles, Cyclists and 
Pedestrians) Order 2018 
Made 
6 July 2018 
Coming into force 
15 July 2018 
The Welsh Ministers, as traffic authority for the 
relevant lengths of the A55 Trunk Road, are satisfied 
that traffic on specified lengths of the trunk road 
should be prohibited because of works proposed on or 
near the road. 
The Welsh Ministers, therefore, in exercise of the 
powers conferred upon them by section 14(1) and (4) 
of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(1), make this 
Order. 
Title, Commencement and Interpretation 
1. The title of this Order is the A55 Trunk Road 
(Junction 
22 
(Old 
Colwyn) 
to 
Junction 
23 
(Llanddulas), Conwy County Borough) (Temporary 
Prohibition of Vehicles, Cyclists and Pedestrians) 
Order 2018 and it comes into force on 15 July 2018. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
              (1)     1984 c.27; section 14 was substituted by the Road Traffic   
(Temporary  Restrictions) Act 1991 (c.26), section 1(1) and 
Schedule 1.  By virtue of S.I. 1999/672, and section 162 of, and

2006(c.32), these powers are now exercisable by the Welsh 
Ministers in relation to Wales. 
 
2
2.—(1) In this Order:  
“exempted vehicle” (“cerbyd esempt”) means any 
vehicle being used for the purposes described in 
section 87 of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 
1984 Act(2) or in connection with the works giving 
rise to this Order; 
 
“the trunk road” (“y gefnffordd”) means the A55 
Chester to Bangor trunk road; 
 
“works period” (“cyfnod y gwaith”) means the  
period commencing at 20:00 on the 15 July 2018 
and ending when the temporary traffic signs are 
permanently removed. 
       (2)  Any reference in this Order to a numbered 
article is a reference to the article bearing that 
number in this Order. 
Prohibition 
3. No person may, during the works period, drive or 
cause or permit any vehicle, other than an exempted 
vehicle, to proceed on the lengths of the trunk road 
specified in the Schedule. 
4.  No person may, during the works period, ride or 
cause to be ridden, any pedal cycle on the lengths of 
the trunk road specified in the Schedule. 
5.  No pedestrian may, during the works period, 
proceed on the lengths of the trunk road specified in 
the Schedule. 
Application 
6. The prohibition in articles 3, 4 and 5 apply only 
during such times and to such extent as indicated by 
traffic signs. 
Duration of this Order 
7. The maximum duration of this Order is 18 
months.

SCHEDULE 
 
Temporary Prohibition of Vehicles  
1. The entire length of the A55 westbound carriageway 
that extends from the start of the kerb nosing of the 
splitter island at Junction 23 (Llanddulas) to the 
centre-point of Junction 22 (Old Colwyn). 
2. The entire length of the westbound entry slip road at 
Junction 23 (Llanddulas). 
3. The entire length of the westbound exit slip road at 
Junction 22 (Old Colwyn). 
4. The entire length of the A55 eastbound carriageway 
that extends from the nosing of the eastbound exit slip 
road at Junction 22 (Old Colwyn) to the end of the 
kerb nosing of the splitter island prior to the eastbound 
entry slip road at Junction 23 (Llanddulas). 
5. The entire length of the eastbound entry slip road at 
Junction 22 (Old Colwyn).  
6. The entire length of the eastbound exit slip road at 
Junction 23 (Llanddulas).
