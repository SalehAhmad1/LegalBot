Made

The Welsh Ministers, in exercise of the powers conferred by sections 18(1), 203 and 204(1) of, and paragraph 5 of Schedule 3 to, the National Health Service (Wales) Act 2006(1), after completion of the consultation prescribed under section 18(3) of that Act, make the following Order:
(1)
2006 c. 42Title, commencement and interpretation
1.—(1) The title of this Order is the Velindre National Health Service Trust (Establishment) (Amendment) Order 2018 and it comes into force on 2 August 2018.
(2) In this Order, “the principal Order” (“y prif Orchymyn”) means the Velindre National Health Service Trust (Establishment) Order 1993(1).
(1)
As amended by S.I. 1999/826; S.I. 2002/442 (W. 57); S.I. 2002/2199 (W. 219); S.I. 2009/2059 (W. 178); S.I. 2012/1262 (W. 157) and S.I. 2017/912 (W. 223).Amendment of article 1 of the principal Order

3.  For article 2 of the principal Order, substitute—
“2.    Establishment of the trust
There is hereby established an NHS trust which shall be called Velindre University National Health Service Trust or Ymddiriedolaeth Gwasanaeth Iechyd Gwladol Prifysgol Felindre.”Significant teaching commitment
4.  After article 4 of the principal Order, insert—
“4A.    Significant teaching commitment
(1) The trust is to be regarded as having a significant teaching commitment by virtue of paragraph 5(3)(b) of Schedule 3 to the National Health Service (Wales) Act 2006(1).
(2) One of the non-executive directors must be appointed from Cardiff University.”

19 July 2018EXPLANATORY NOTE
This Order amends the Velindre National Health Service Trust (Establishment) Order 1993 (“the principal Order”) in order to change the name of Velindre National Health Service Trust to Velindre University National Health Service Trust.
As Velindre National Health Service Trust will now have a significant teaching commitment, the Order specifies this and further amends the principal Order to prescribe that one of the non-executive directors must be appointed from Cardiff University.
