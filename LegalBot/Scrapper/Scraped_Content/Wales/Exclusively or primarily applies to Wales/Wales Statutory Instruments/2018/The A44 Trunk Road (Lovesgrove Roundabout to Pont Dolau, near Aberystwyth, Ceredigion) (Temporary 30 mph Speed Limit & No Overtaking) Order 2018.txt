ROAD TRAFFIC, WALES 
The A44 Trunk Road (Lovesgrove 
Roundabout to Pont Dolau, near 
Aberystwyth, Ceredigion) 
(Temporary 30 mph Speed Limit & 
No Overtaking) Order 2018 
Made 
30 January 2018 
Coming into force 
5 February 2018 
The Welsh Ministers, being the traffic authority for the 
A44 Newtown to Aberystwyth Trunk Road, are 
satisfied that traffic on a specified length of the trunk 
road should be restricted and/or prohibited because of 
works proposed on or near the road. 
The Welsh Ministers, therefore, in exercise of the 
powers conferred upon them by section 14(1), (4) and 
(7) of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(1), make 
this Order. 
Title, Commencement and Interpretation  
1. The title of this Order is The A44 Trunk Road 
(Lovesgrove Roundabout to Pont Dolau, near 
Aberystwyth, Ceredigion) (Temporary 30 mph Speed 
Limit & No Overtaking) Order 2018 and it comes into 
force on 5 February 2018.

services; and

(2) Any reference in this Order to a numbered article

Order. 
Restriction and Prohibition 
3. No person may, during the works period, drive or 
cause or permit any vehicle to proceed at a speed 
exceeding 30 miles per hour on the length of the trunk 
road. 
4. No person may, during the works period, use or 
permit the use of a vehicle, other than an exempted 
vehicle, in such a way that it overtakes any other 
vehicle on the length of the trunk road. 
Suspension 
5. Any statutory provisions restricting the speed of 
vehicles on the length of the trunk road specified in 
article 2 are suspended during such times as the 
restriction specified in article 3 is in operation. 
Application 
6.—(1) The restriction and prohibition in articles 3 
and 4 apply only during such times and to such extent 
as indicated by traffic signs.

(2) No speed limit imposed by this Order applies to

Duration of this Order 
7. The maximum duration of this Order is 12 
months.
