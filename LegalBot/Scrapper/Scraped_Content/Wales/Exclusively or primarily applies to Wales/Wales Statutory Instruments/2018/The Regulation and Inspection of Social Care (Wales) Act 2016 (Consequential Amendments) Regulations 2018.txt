SOCIAL CARE, ENGLAND AND WALES
The Regulation and Inspection of Social Care (Wales) Act 2016 (Consequential Amendments) Regulations 2018
Made
15 February 2018
Coming into force in accordance with article 2
The Welsh Ministers make the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by section 186 of the Regulation and Inspection of Social Care (Wales) Act 2016(1) and section 198 of the Social Services and Well-being (Wales) Act 2014(2).
A draft of these Regulations was laid before the National Assembly for Wales under section 186(4) of the Regulation and Inspection of Social Care (Wales) Act 2016 and section 196(6) of the Social Services and Well-being (Wales) Act 2014 and has been approved by a resolution of the National Assembly for Wales.
