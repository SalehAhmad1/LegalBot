(Amendment) Order (Northern Ireland) 2023 and shall come into operation on 8th January 2024.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Order) 
This Order amends The Parking and Waiting Restrictions (Londonderry) Order (Northern Ireland) 
2023 (“the Order”) by the addition of a no waiting at any time restriction (loading and unloading 
permitted) on the lengths of road specified in Article 2. Vehicles are excepted from the restriction 
in certain circumstances. 
A penalty charge (£90) is payable with respect to a vehicle involved in a contravention of this 
Order in accordance with the Traffic Management (Northern Ireland) Order 2005 and the Penalty 
Charges (Prescribed Amounts) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2006. 
Traffic signs indicating the effect of the Order will in due course be placed on the roads.
