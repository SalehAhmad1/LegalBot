The Department for Infrastructure(a) makes the following Order in exercise of the powers 
conferred by Articles 37(3), 37(4) and 38(1) of the Road Traffic Regulation (Northern Ireland) 
Order 1997(b) and now vested in it(c). 
The Department has consulted such persons as it considered appropriate in compliance with 
paragraphs 1 and 2 of Schedule 5 to that Order. 
The Department has published a notice in compliance with paragraphs 1 and 3 of that Schedule. 
No objection or other representation has been received. 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as the Roads (Speed Limit) Order (Northern Ireland) 2023 and shall 
come into operation on 30th May 2023. 
Speed restriction on certain roads 
2. The Department directs that the length of road specified in Schedule 1 shall be a restricted 
road. 
Application of speed limits 
3. The driving of motor vehicles at a speed exceeding 20 miles per hour on the lengths of road 
specified in Schedule 2 is prohibited. 
4. The driving of motor vehicles at a speed exceeding 40 miles per hour on the lengths of road 
specified in Schedule 3 is prohibited. 
5. The driving of motor vehicles at a speed exceeding 50 miles per hour on the length of road 
specified in Schedule 4 is prohibited.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Order) 
This Order— 
(a) makes the length of road specified in Schedule 1 a restricted road; 
(b) applies a speed limit of 20 miles per hour to the lengths of road specified in Schedule 2; 
(c) applies a speed limit of 40 miles per hour to the lengths of road specified in Schedule 3; 
(d) applies a speed limit of 50 miles per hour to the length of road specified in Schedule 4; 
and 
(e) revokes the provisions in Schedule 5. 
In relation to the revoked provision in Schedule 5— 
1. The speed limit on the length of Gortmore Park, Omagh (paragraph 1 of Schedule 5) is now 
set at 20 miles per hour. 
2. The speed limit on the length of Shandon Park, Omagh (paragraph 2 of Schedule 5) is now set 
at 20 miles per hour. 
3. The speed limit on the lengths of Randalstown Road, Antrim (paragraph 3 of Schedule 5) are 
now partly 30 miles per hour restricted by street lighting, partly 30 miles per hour (restricted road) 
and partly set at 40 miles per hour. 
4. The speed limit on the length of Magheraknock Road, Cargacreevy (paragraph 4 of Schedule 
5) is now set at 40 miles per hour.
