EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Order) 
This Order amends The Waiting Restrictions (Saintfield) Order (Northern Ireland) 2017 (“the 
Order”) by amending the definitions of postal packet and universal service provider and by the 
addition of a no waiting at any time restriction (loading and unloading permitted) on the length of 
road specified in Article 2. Vehicles are excepted from the restriction in certain circumstances. 
A penalty charge (£90) is payable with respect to a vehicle involved in a contravention of this 
Order in accordance with the Traffic Management (Northern Ireland) Order 2005 and the Penalty 
Charges (Prescribed Amounts) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2006. 
Traffic signs indicating the effect of the Order will in due course be placed on the road.
