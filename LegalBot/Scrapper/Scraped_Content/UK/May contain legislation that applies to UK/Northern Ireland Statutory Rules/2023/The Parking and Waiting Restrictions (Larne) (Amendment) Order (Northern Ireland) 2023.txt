EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Order) 
This Order amends the Parking and Waiting Restrictions (Larne) Order (Northern Ireland) 2017 
(“the Order”) by the addition of a no waiting at any time prohibition (loading and unloading not 
permitted) on the lengths of road specified in Article 2. 
Vehicles are excepted from the prohibition in certain circumstances. 
A penalty charge (£90) is payable with respect to a vehicle involved in a contravention of the 
Order in accordance with the Traffic Management (Northern Ireland) Order 2005 and the Penalty 
Charges (Prescribed Amounts) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2006. 
Traffic signs indicating the effect of this Order will in due course be placed on the road.
