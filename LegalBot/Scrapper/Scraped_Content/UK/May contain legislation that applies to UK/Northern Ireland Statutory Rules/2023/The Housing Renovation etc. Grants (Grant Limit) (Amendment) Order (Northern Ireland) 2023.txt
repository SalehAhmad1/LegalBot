(2)
S.I. 2003/412 (N.I. 2)Citation and commencement

(This note is not part of the Order)
The Housing Renovation etc. Grants (Grant Limit) Order (Northern Ireland) 2003 makes provision in relation to the amount of housing grants that the Housing Executive may pay.
These Regulations amend Articles 2 and 3 of the Housing Renovation etc. Grants (Grant Limit) Order (Northern Ireland) 2003 enabling the Housing Executive to pay a higher maximum amount of mandatory disabled facilities grant.
