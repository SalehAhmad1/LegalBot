The Department for Infrastructure(a) makes the following Order in exercise of the powers 
conferred by Articles 37(3), 37(4) and 38(1) of the Road Traffic Regulation (Northern Ireland) 
Order 1997(b) and now vested in it(c). 
The Department has consulted such persons as it considered appropriate in compliance with 
paragraphs 1 and 2 of Schedule 5 to that Order. 
The Department has published a notice in compliance with paragraphs 1 and 3 of that Schedule. 
No objections were received. 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as the Roads (Speed Limit) Order (Northern Ireland) 2024 and shall 
come into operation on 5th February 2024. 
Application of speed limits 
2. The driving of motor vehicles at a speed exceeding 20 miles per hour on the length of road 
specified in Schedule 1 is prohibited. 
3. The driving of motor vehicles at a speed exceeding 40 miles per hour on the lengths of road 
specified in Schedule 2 is prohibited. 
Revocations 
4. The provisions described in Schedule 3 are revoked.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Order) 
This Order— 
(a) applies a speed limit of 20 miles per hour to the length of road specified in Schedule 1; 
(b) applies a speed limit of 40 miles per hour to the length of road specified in Schedule 2; 
(c) revokes the provisions in Schedule 3. 
In relation to the revoked provisions in Schedule 3— 
1. The speed limit on the length of Branch Road, Carnlough (paragraph 1 of Schedule 3) is now 
partly 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting) and partly set at 40 miles per hour. 
2. The speed limit on Moneydig Park, Garvagh (item 187 of paragraph 2 of Schedule 3) is now 
30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting). 
3. The speed limit on the length of Moneydig Road, Garvagh (item 224 of paragraph 2 of 
Schedule 3) is now set at 40 miles per hour. 
4. The speed limit on the length of Coast Road, Drains Bay, Larne (paragraph 3 of Schedule 3) 
is now partly 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting) and partly set at 40 miles per hour. 
5. The speed limit on the length of Tullywiggan Road, Cookstown (paragraphs 4 and 5 of 
Schedule 3) is now set at 40 miles per hour.
