EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Order) 
This Order amends the Parking and Waiting Restrictions (Londonderry) Order (Northern Ireland) 
2023 by the addition of a no waiting at any time restriction (loading and unloading permitted) on a 
length of road at Spruce Meadows, Londonderry. Vehicles are excepted from the restrictions in 
certain circumstances. 
A penalty charge (£90) is payable with respect to a vehicle involved in a contravention of this 
Order in accordance with the Traffic Management (Northern Ireland) Order 2005 and Penalty 
Charges (Prescribed Amoun) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2006. 
Traffic signs indication the effect of the Order will in due course be placed on the road.
