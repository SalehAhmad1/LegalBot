The Department for Infrastructure(a) makes the following Order in exercise of the powers 
conferred by Articles 37(3), 37(4) and 38(1) of the Road Traffic Regulation (Northern Ireland) 
Order 1997(b) and now vested in it(c). 
The Department has consulted such persons as it considered appropriate in compliance with 
paragraphs 1 and 2 of Schedule 5 to that Order. 
The Department has published a notice in compliance with paragraphs 1 and 3 of that Schedule. 
No objection has been received. 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as the Roads (Speed Limit) (No. 3) Order (Northern Ireland) 2024 
and shall come into operation on 19th February 2024. 
Application of speed limits 
2. The driving of motor vehicles at a speed exceeding 20 miles per hour on the roads specified in 
Schedule 1 is prohibited. 
3. The driving of motor vehicles at a speed exceeding 50 miles per hour on the lengths of road 
specified in Schedule 2 is prohibited. 
De-restriction of speed on certain roads 
4. The Department directs that the lengths of road specified in Schedule 3 shall not be restricted 
roads notwithstanding that a system of street lighting furnished by lamps placed not more than 185 
metres apart is provided on those lengths of road. 
Revocations 
5. The provisions described in Schedule 4 are revoked.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Order) 
This Order— 
(a) applies a speed limit of 20 miles per hour to the lengths of road specified in Schedule 1; 
(b) applies a speed limit of 50 miles per hour to the lengths of road specified in Schedule 2; 
(c) makes the lengths of road specified in Schedule 3 de-restricted roads; and 
(d) revokes the provisions in Schedule 4. 
In relation to the revoked provisions in Schedule 4— 
1. The speed limit on the length of Old Ballygawley Roundabout (paragraph 1 and item 5 of 
paragraph 2 of Schedule 4) is now set at 50 miles per hour. 
2. The speed limit on the length of Annaghilla Road, Ballygawley (item 4 of paragraph 2 of 
Schedule 4) is now set at the national speed limit (60 miles per hour). 
3. The speed limit on the length of Eglish Road, Dungannon (paragraph 2 of Schedule 4) is now 
30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting).
