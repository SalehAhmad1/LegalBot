The Department for Infrastructure(a) makes the following Order in exercise of the powers 
conferred by Article 68(1), (3) and (5) of the Roads (Northern Ireland) Order 1993(b) and now 
vested in it(c). 
The Department in accordance with Article 68(4) of that Order is of the opinion that the lengths of 
road described in the Schedule are not necessary for road traffic. 
Notice has been published, served and displayed in compliance with paragraphs 1, 2 and 3 of 
Schedule 8 to that Order. 
One hundred objections have been received and duly considered. Eight objections have been 
subsequently withdrawn.   
Citation, commencement and interpretation 
1.—(1) This Order may be cited as the Kings Lane, Warrenpoint (Stopping-Up) Order (Northern 
Ireland) 2024 and shall come into operation on 30th September 2024. 
(2) In this Order “the contractor” means Killowen Contracts Limited whose registered office is 
situated at 35 Bridge Road, Burren, Warrenpoint, Newry, BT34 3QT. 
Application 
2.—(1) The lengths of road described in the Schedule shall be stopped-up to vehicular traffic at 
the lines specified therein. 
(2) The contractor shall carry out such works as the Department considers necessary to prevent 
or restrict to such an extent as the Department thinks fit, access to the road described in the 
Schedule by vehicular traffic.  
3.—(1) All existing cables, wires, mains, pipes or other apparatus placed along, across, over or 
under the road to be stopped-up shall be retained.
