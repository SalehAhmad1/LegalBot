The Department for Infrastructure(a) makes the following Order in exercise of the powers 
conferred by Articles 37(3), 37(4) and 38(1) of the Road Traffic Regulation (Northern Ireland) 
Order 1997(b) and now vested in it(c). 
The Department has consulted such persons as it considered appropriate in compliance with 
paragraphs 1 and 2 of Schedule 5 to that Order. 
The Department has published a notice in compliance with paragraphs 1 and 3 of that Schedule. 
No objections were received. 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as the Roads (Speed Limit) (No. 2) Order (Northern Ireland) 2024 
and shall come into operation on 19th February 2024. 
Application of speed limits 
2. The driving of motor vehicles at a speed exceeding 40 miles per hour on the lengths of road 
specified in Schedule 1 is prohibited. 
De-restriction of speed on certain road 
3. The Department directs that the length of Botera Upper Road, Route C668, Omagh, from its 
junction with Clanabogan Road, Unclassified No. 1614, to a point 81 metres south-west of that 
junction shall not be a restricted road notwithstanding that a system of street lighting furnished by 
lamps placed not more than 185 metres apart is provided on that length of road. 
Revocations 
4. The provisions described in Schedule 2 are revoked.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Order) 
This Order— 
(a) applies a speed limit of 40 miles per hour to the lengths of road specified in Schedule 1; 
(b) makes the length of Botera Upper Road specified in Article 3 a de-restricted road; and 
(c) revokes the provisions in Schedule 2. 
In relation to the revoked provisions in Schedule 2— 
1. The speed limit on the length of Newtownsaville Road, Omagh (item 318 of paragraph 1 of 
Schedule 2) is now set at 40 miles per hour. 
2. The speed limit on the length of Springhill Road, Omagh (item 319 of paragraph 1 of 
Schedule 2) is now set at 40 miles per hour. 
3. The speed limit on the length of Botera Upper Road, Omagh (item 328 of paragraph 1 of 
Schedule 2) is now set at 60 miles per hour (national speed limit). 
4. The speed limit on the length of Clanbogan Road, Omagh (item 348 of paragraph 1 of 
Schedule 2) is now set at 40 miles per hour. 
5. The speed limit on the length of Glenpark Road, Omagh (item 16 of paragraph 2 of Schedule 
2) is now a longer length of road set at 40 miles per hour. 
6. The speed limit on the length of Gortin Road, Omagh (item 17 of paragraph 2 of Schedule 2) 
is now a longer length of road set at 40 miles per hour.
