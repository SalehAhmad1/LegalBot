The Department for Infrastructure(a) makes the following Order in exercise of the powers 
conferred by Articles 37(3), 37(4) and 38(1)(a) of the Road Traffic Regulation (Northern Ireland) 
Order 1997(b) and now vested in it(c). 
The Department has consulted such persons as it considered appropriate in compliance with 
paragraphs 1 and 2 of Schedule 5 to that Order. 
The Department has published a notice in compliance with paragraphs 1 and 3 of that Schedule. 
No written objections or other representations have been received. 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as The Roads (Speed Limit) (No. 2) Order (Northern Ireland) 2019 
and shall come into operation on 29th July 2019. 
Speed restriction on certain roads 
2. The Department directs that each of the lengths of road specified in Schedule 1 shall be a 
restricted road. 
Application of speed limits 
3. The driving of motor vehicles at a speed exceeding 20 miles per hour on the road specified in 
Schedule 2 is prohibited. 
4. The driving of motor vehicles at a speed exceeding 40 miles per hour on the road and lengths 
of road specified in Schedule 3 is prohibited. 
5. The driving of motor vehicles at a speed exceeding 50 miles per hour on the lengths of road 
specified in Schedule 4 is prohibited.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Order) 
This Order— 
(a) makes the lengths of road specified in Schedule 1 a restricted road; 
(b) applies a speed limit of 20 miles per hour to the road specified in Schedule 2; 
(c) applies a speed limit of 40 miles per hour to the road and lengths of road specified in 
Schedule 3; 
(d) applies a speed limit of 50 miles per hour to the lengths of road specified in Schedule 4; 
and 
(e) revokes the provisions in Schedule 5. 
In relation to the revoked provisions in Schedule 5— 
1. The speed limit on the length of Cathedral Road, Armagh (paragraph 1 of Schedule 5) is now 
included in a longer length of road at 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting). 
2. The speed limit on the length of Fernisky Road, Kells (paragraph 2 of Schedule 5) is now 
partly 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting) and partly set at 40 miles per hour. 
3. The speed limit on the length of Ballymaguigan Road and Gracefield Road, Magherafelt 
(paragraph 3 of Schedule 5) is now 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting). 
4. The speed limit on the length of Gilford Road, Portadown (paragraph 4 of Schedule 5) is now 
included in a longer length of road set at 40 miles per hour. 
5. The speed limit on the length of Ardmore Road, Derryadd, Craigavon (item 158, paragraph 5 
of Schedule 5) is now included in a longer length of road set at 40 miles per hour. 
6. The speed limit on the length of Summerisland Road, Portadown (item 42, paragraph 5 of 
Schedule 5) is now included in a longer length of road set at 40 miles per hour. 
7. The speed limit on the length of Hillsborough Road, Moneyreagh (paragraph 6 of Schedule 5) 
is now partly set at 30 miles per hour (restricted road) and partly set at 40 miles per hour. 
8. The speed limit on the length of Clooney Road, Greysteel (paragraph 7 of Schedule 5) is now 
included in a longer length of road set at 40 miles per hour. 
9. The speed limit on the lengths of Roguery Road, Gorthill and Roguery Road, Moneyglass 
(paragraph 8 of Schedule 5) is now included in a longer length of road set at 40 miles per hour.
