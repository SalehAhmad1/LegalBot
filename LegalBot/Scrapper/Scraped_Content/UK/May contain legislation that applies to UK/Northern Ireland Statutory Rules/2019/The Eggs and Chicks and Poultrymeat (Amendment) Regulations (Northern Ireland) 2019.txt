Made

The Department of Agriculture, Environment and Rural Affairs(1), being designated(2) for the purpose of section 2(2) of the European Communities Act 1972(3) in relation to the common agricultural policy of the European Union in exercise of the powers conferred under that section 2(2) of that Act, makes the following Regulations.
There has been open and transparent public consultation during the preparation of these Regulations as required by Article 9 of Regulation (EC) No. 178/2002 of the European Parliament and of the Council(4) laying down the general principles and requirements of food law, establishing the European Food Safety Authority and laying down procedures in matters of food safety.

(This note is not part of the Order)
These regulations update European Union legislative references.
Part 2 amends the Eggs and Chicks Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2010 and the Poultrymeat Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2011 to provide that any reference to a specified EU Regulation is to be construed so as to include a reference to another specified EU Regulation
