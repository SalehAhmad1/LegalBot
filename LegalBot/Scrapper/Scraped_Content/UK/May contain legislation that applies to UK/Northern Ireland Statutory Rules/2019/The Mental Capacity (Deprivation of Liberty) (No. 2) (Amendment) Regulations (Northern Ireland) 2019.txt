Form Title
Form 6 Medical reportEXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
These Regulations replace the Form 6 (medical report) in the Mental Capacity (Deprivation of Liberty) (No. 2) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2019 with a new Form 6.
The new Form 6 (medical report) inserts a new section (section 7) which is a division of the original section 6 to ensure information on the risks, and the proportionality of the risks, is adequately captured on the Form.
