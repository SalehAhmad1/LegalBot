The Department for Infrastructure(a) makes the following Order in exercise of the powers 
conferred by Articles 37(3), 37(4) and 38(1)(a) of the Road Traffic Regulation (Northern Ireland) 
Order 1997(b) and now vested in it(c). 
The Department has consulted such persons as it considered appropriate in compliance with 
paragraphs 1 and 2 of Schedule 5 to that Order. 
The Department has published a notice in compliance with paragraphs 1 and 3 of that Schedule. 
No written objection or other representations have been received. 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as The Roads (Speed Limit) Order (Northern Ireland) 2019 and shall 
come into operation on 11th April 2019. 
Application of speed limits 
2. The driving of motor vehicles at a speed exceeding 40 miles per hour on the lengths of road 
specified in Schedule 1 are prohibited. 
Revocations 
3. The provisions described in Schedule 2 are revoked.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Order) 
This Order— 
(a) applies a speed limit of 40 miles per hour to the lengths of road specified in Schedule 1; 
and 
(b) revokes the provisions in Schedule 2. 
In relation to the revoked provisions in Schedule 2— 
1. The speed limit on the entire length of Hospital Road, Omagh (paragraphs 1 and 2 of 
Schedule 2) is now 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting). 
2. The speed limit on the lengths of Deverney Road, Omagh (paragraphs 3 and 4 of Schedule 2) 
is now partly 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting) and partly set at 40 miles per hour. 
3. The speed limit on the length of Annaghmore Road, Coalisland (item 5 of paragraph 5 of 
Schedule 2) is now partly 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting) and partly set at 40 miles 
per hour. 
4. The speed limit on the length of Moor Road, Coalisland (item 6 of paragraph 5 of Schedule 2) 
is now partly 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting) and partly set at 40 miles per hour. 
5. The speed limit on the length of Drumnakilly Road, Omagh, (paragraph 6 of Schedule 2) is 
now partly 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting) and partly set at 40 miles per hour. 
6. The speed limit on the length of Washing Bay Road, Coalisland, (paragraph 7 of Schedule 2) 
is now partly 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting) and partly set at 40 miles per hour. 
7. The speed limit on the length of Donaghanie Road, Omagh, (paragraph 8 of Schedule 2) is 
now partly 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting) and partly set at 40 miles per hour.
