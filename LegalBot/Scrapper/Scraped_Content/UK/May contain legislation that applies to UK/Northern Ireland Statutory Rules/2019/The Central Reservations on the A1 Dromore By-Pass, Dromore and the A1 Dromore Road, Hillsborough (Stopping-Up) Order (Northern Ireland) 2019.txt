The Department for Infrastructure(a) makes the following Order in exercise of its powers 
conferred by Article 68(1), (3) and (5) of the Roads (Northern Ireland) Order 1993(b) and now 
vested in it(c). 
The Department in accordance with Article 68(4) of that Order is of the opinion that the lengths of 
the roads described in the Schedule are not necessary for road traffic. 
Notice has been published, served and displayed in compliance with paragraphs 1, 2 and 3 of 
Schedule 8 to that Order. 
One objection has been received and duly considered. 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as The Central Reservations on the A1 Dromore By-Pass, Dromore 
and the A1 Dromore Road, Hillsborough (Stopping-Up) Order (Northern Ireland) 2019 and shall 
come into operation on 7th October 2019. 
Application 
2.—(1) The lengths of road described in the Schedule shall be stopped-up to road traffic at the 
lines specified therein. 
(2) The Department shall carry out such works as it considers necessary to prevent or restrict to 
such extent as the Department thinks fit, access to the lengths of road described in the Schedule by 
road traffic. 
3.—(1) All existing cables, wires, mains, pipes or other apparatus placed along, across, over or 
under the roads shall be retained. 
(2) All existing rights as to the use or maintenance of such cables, wires, mains, pipes or other 
apparatus shall be preserved.

SCHEDULE 
Article 2 
ROADS TO BE STOPPED UP TO ROAD TRAFFIC 
 
1. The north-west side of the central reservation of the A1 Dromore By-Pass, Dromore at a line 
from a point 389 metres north-east of the centre of the Banbridge Road over bridge, for a distance 
of 7 metres in a north-easterly direction, more particularly delineated and marked A-B on map 
number IN1/18/761715. 
2. The south-east side of the central reservation of the A1 Dromore By-Pass, Dromore at a line 
from a point 396 metres north-east of the centre of the Banbridge Road over bridge for a distance 
of 7 metres in a south-westerly direction, more particularly delineated and marked C-D on Map 
number IN1/18/761715. 
3. The north-west side of the central reservation of the A1 Dromore Road, Hillsborough at a line 
from a point 719 metres south-west of the centre of the Dromore Road over bridge for a distance 
of 6 metres in a north-easterly direction, more particularly delineated and marked E-F on Map 
number IN1/18/761724. 
4. The south-east side of the central reservation of the A1 Dromore Road, Hillsborough at a line 
from a point 717 metres south-west of the centre of the Dromore Road over bridge for a distance 
of 6 metres in a south-westerly direction more particularly delineated and marked G-H on Map 
number IN1/18/761724.
