The Department for Infrastructure(a) makes the following Order in exercise of the powers 
conferred by Article 4(1), (2) and (3) of, and paragraph 5 of Schedule 1 to, the Road Traffic 
Regulation (Northern Ireland) Order 1997(b) and now vested in it(c). 
The Department has consulted such persons as it considered appropriate in compliance with 
paragraphs 1 and 2 of Schedule 1 to that Order. 
The Department has published a notice in compliance with paragraphs 1 and 3 of that Schedule. 
One written objection has been received and subsequently withdrawn and no other representation 
has been received. 
The Department has modified the draft Order. 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as The Waiting Restrictions (Lisburn) (Amendment) Order (Northern 
Ireland) 2019 and shall come into operation on 6th August 2019.

27.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Order) 
This Order amends The Waiting Restrictions (Lisburn) Order (Northern Ireland) 2015 (“the 
Order”) by the addition of a no waiting at any time restriction on the lengths of the road specified 
in Article 2. Vehicles are excepted from the restriction in certain circumstances. 
A penalty charge (£90) is payable with respect to a vehicle involved in a contravention of the 
Order in accordance with the Traffic Management (Northern Ireland) Order 2005 and The Penalty 
Charges (Prescribed Amounts) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2006. 
Traffic signs indicating the effect of this Order will in due course be placed on the road.
