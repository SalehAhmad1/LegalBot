STATUTORY RULES OF NORTHERN IRELAND
2022 No. 147 (C. 17)
AGRICULTURE
The Agriculture (2020 Act)(Commencement No. 1) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2022
Made
23rd March 2022
The Department of Agriculture, Environment and Rural Affairs, in exercise of the powers conferred by section 57(4)(a) of the Agriculture Act 2020(1), makes the following Regulations:
(1)
2020 c. 21Citation and commencement
1.  These Regulations may be cited as the Agriculture (2020 Act)(Commencement No. 1) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2022.Provisions of the Act coming into operation on 28th March 2022
2.  The day appointed for the coming into operation of paragraph 8 of Part 2 of Schedule 6 (exceptional market conditions: powers available to DAERA) to the Agriculture Act 2020 is 28th March 2022.Sealed with the Official Seal of the Department of Agriculture, Environment and Rural Affairs on 23rd March 2022.
Norman Fulton
A senior officer of the Department of Agriculture, Environment and Rural AffairsEXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Regulations)
These Regulations bring into operation paragraph 8 of Part 2 of Schedule 6 (Exceptional market conditions: powers available to DAERA) to the Agriculture Act 2020.
