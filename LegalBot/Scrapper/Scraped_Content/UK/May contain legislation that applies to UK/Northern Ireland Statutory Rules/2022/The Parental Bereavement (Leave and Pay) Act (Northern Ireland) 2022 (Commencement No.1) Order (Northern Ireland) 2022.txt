STATUTORY RULES OF NORTHERN IRELAND
2022 No. 136 (C. 16)
EMPLOYMENT
PARENTAL BEREAVEMENT
The Parental Bereavement (Leave and Pay) Act (Northern Ireland) 2022 (Commencement No.1) Order (Northern Ireland) 2022
Made
22nd March 2022
The Department for the Economy, in exercise of powers conferred by section 5(5) of the Parental Bereavement (Leave and Pay) Act (Northern Ireland) 2022(1), makes the following order.
(1)
2022 c. 5 (N.I.)Citation and Interpretation
1.  This Order may be cited as the Parental Bereavement (Leave and Pay) Act (Northern Ireland) 2022 (Commencement No.1) Order (Northern Ireland) 2022.Citation and Interpretation
2.  In this Order, “the Act” means the Parental Bereavement (Leave and Pay) Act (Northern Ireland) 2022.Appointed day
3.  The day appointed for the coming into operation of Part 1 of the Schedule to the Act (and section 4 of the Act in so far as it relates to that Part) is the 23rd March 2022.Sealed with the Official Seal of the Department for the Economy on 22nd March 2022.
Colin Jack
A senior officer of the DepartmentEXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order brings into operation Part 1 of the Schedule (and Part 4 so far as it relates to that Part) to the Parental Bereavement (Leave and Pay) Act (Northern Ireland) 2022 (c. 5). Part 1 of the Schedule makes consequential amendments to other legislation, including references to Parental Bereavement Leave and Pay where relevant.
