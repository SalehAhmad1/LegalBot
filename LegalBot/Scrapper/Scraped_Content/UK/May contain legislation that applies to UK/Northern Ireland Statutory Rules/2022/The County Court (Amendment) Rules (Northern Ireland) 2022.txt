The County Court (Amendment) Rules (Northern Ireland) 2022
Made
20th September 2022
Coming into operation -
3rd October 2022
The County Court Rules Committee makes and the Department of Justice, after consultation with the Lady Chief Justice, allows the following Rules in exercise of the powers conferred by Article 47 of the County Courts (Northern Ireland) Order 1980(1).
(1)
S.I. 1980/397 (N.I. 3); Article 47 was amended by the Constitutional Reform Act 2005 (c. 4) and paragraph 33 of Schedule 17 to, and paragraph 118 of Schedule 18 to, the Northern Ireland Act 1998 (Devolution of Policing and Justice Functions) Order 2010 (S.I. 2010/976).Citation and Commencement
1.  These Rules may be cited as the County Court (Amendment) Rules (Northern Ireland) 2022 and shall come into operation on 3rd October 2022.
Commencement Information
I1
Rule 1 in operation at 3.10.2022, see rule 1Amendments to the County Court Rules (Northern Ireland) 1981
2.  The County Court Rules (Northern Ireland) 1981(1) are amended as follows—
(1) In Order 26, rule 3(1) for “£3,000” substitute “£5,000”.
(2) In Order 55, rule 19—
(a)
in paragraph (1)(b) for “£3,000” substitute “£5,000”;
(b)
in paragraph (2) for “£3,000” substitute “£5,000”.
(3) In Appendix 1, in Form 10A for “£3,000” substitute “£5,000”.

Minister of JusticeEXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Rules)
These Rules amend the County Court Rules (Northern Ireland) 1981 (S.R. 1981 No. 225) to take account of the increase in the small claims court jurisdiction from £3,000 to £5,000.
