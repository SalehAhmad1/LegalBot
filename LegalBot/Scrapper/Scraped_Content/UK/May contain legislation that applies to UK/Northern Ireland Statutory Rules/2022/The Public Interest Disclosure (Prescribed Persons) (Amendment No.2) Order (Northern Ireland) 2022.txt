STATUTORY RULES OF NORTHERN IRELAND
2022 No. 290
EMPLOYMENT
The Public Interest Disclosure (Prescribed Persons) (Amendment No.2) Order (Northern Ireland) 2022
Made
27th October 2022
Coming into operation
18th November 2022
The Department for the Economy makes the following Order in exercise of the powers conferred by Article 67F of the Employment Rights (Northern Ireland) Order 1996(1) and now vested in it(2).
(1)
S.I. 1996/1919 (N.I. 16); Article 67F was inserted by Article 3 of the Public Interest Disclosure (Northern Ireland) Order 1998 (S.I. 1998/1763 (N.I. 17)) and amended by section 14(1)(c) of the Employment Act (Northern Ireland) 2016 (c.15 (N.I)).
(2)
The functions of the Department of Economic Development under the Employment Rights (Northern Ireland) Order 1996 were transferred to the Department of Higher and Further Education, Training and Employment by Article 4(b) of, and Schedule 2, Part 2 to, the Departments (Transfer and Assignment of Functions) Order (Northern Ireland) 1999 (S.R. 1999 No. 481). The Department of Higher and Further Education, Training and Employment was renamed the Department for Employment and Learning by section 1(1) of the Department for Employment and Learning Act (Northern Ireland) 2001 (c.15 (N.I.)), and following the dissolution of that department by section 1(10) of the Departments Act (Northern Ireland) 2016 (c.5 (N.I.)), its functions under the Employment Rights (Northern Ireland) Order 1996 were transferred to the Department for the Economy by Article 6(1)(c) of the Departments (Transfer of Functions) Order (Northern Ireland) 2016 (S.R. 2016 No. 76).Citation and commencement
1.  This Order may be cited as the Public Interest Disclosure (Prescribed Persons) (Amendment No.2) Order (Northern Ireland) 2022 and shall come into operation on 18th November 2022.

(3) At the appropriate place insert—
“Office for Environmental Protection (28) Matters relating to the functions of the Office for Environmental Protection, as set out in the Environment Act 2021(29).”

Department for the EconomyEXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order amends the Schedule to the Public Interest Disclosure (Prescribed Persons) Order (Northern Ireland) 1999 (S.R. 1999 No. 401).
It adds the Office for Environmental Protection, and the description of matters for which it is prescribed, to the list of prescribed persons.
It also amends the description of matters for which the Financial Conduct Authority is prescribed.
