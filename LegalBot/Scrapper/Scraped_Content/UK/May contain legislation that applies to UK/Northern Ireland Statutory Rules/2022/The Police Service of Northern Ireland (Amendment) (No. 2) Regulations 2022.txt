STATUTORY RULES OF NORTHERN IRELAND
2022 No. 217
POLICE
The Police Service of Northern Ireland (Amendment) (No. 2) Regulations 2022
Made
16th September 2022
Coming into operation
17th September 2022
The Department of Justice, in exercise of the powers conferred by sections 25(1) and 26(1) of the Police (Northern Ireland) Act 1998(1), makes the following Regulations. Those powers are now vested in the Department(2).
In accordance with sections 25(8) and 26(6) of that Act the Department has consulted the Northern Ireland Policing Board and the Police Association for Northern Ireland before making these Regulations.
(1)
1998 c. 32. Relevant amendments are made by section 78(1) and paragraph 23 of Schedule 6 to the Police (Northern Ireland) Act 2000.
(2)
Article 5 of and paragraphs 9 and 10 of Schedule 3 to the Northern Ireland Act 1998 (Devolution of Policing and Justice Functions) Order (S.I. 2010/976) transferred the regulation making powers of the Secretary of State to the Department of Justice.Citation and commencement
1.  These Regulations may be cited as the Police Service of Northern Ireland (Amendment) (No. 2) Regulations 2022 and shall come into operation on 17th September 2022.

(This note is not part of the Regulations)
These Regulations amend the Police Service of Northern Ireland Regulations 2005 and the Police Service of Northern Ireland and Police Service of Northern Ireland Reserve (Injury Benefit) Regulations 2006 to allow for the public holiday on 19th September 2022, the date of Her Majesty Queen Elizabeth II’s State Funeral.
