3.—(1) The Department for Infrastructure may make information held by it for the purposes of Part 2 of the 1981 Order available to persons employed by the authorities of any country or territory outside the United Kingdom with responsibility under the law of that country or territory for the regulation of drivers or vehicles, provided that country or territory has entered into an agreement with the United Kingdom for the sharing of such information or has been designated for the purpose of licence exchange under Article 19D(2) of the 1981 Order

(This note is not part of the Regulations)
Article 19I of the Road Traffic (Northern Ireland) Order 1981 makes provision for access to driver licensing information. These Regulations will further allow for the sharing of driver licensing information with each country or territory that the United Kingdom has entered into an agreement for the sharing of such information and also those countries or territories which have been designated for the purpose of exchanging driver licences. The Regulations also determine that the purpose of providing access to driver licensing information is restricted to the discharge of responsibilities for the regulation of drivers or vehicles.
