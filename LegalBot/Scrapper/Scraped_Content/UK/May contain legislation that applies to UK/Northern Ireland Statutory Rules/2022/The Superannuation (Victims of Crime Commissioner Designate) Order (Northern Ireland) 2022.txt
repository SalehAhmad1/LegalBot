STATUTORY RULES OF NORTHERN IRELAND
2022 No. 105
PENSIONS
The Superannuation (Victims of Crime Commissioner Designate) Order (Northern Ireland) 2022
Made
11th March 2022
Coming into operation
1st April 2022
The Department of Finance makes the following Order, in exercise of the powers conferred by Article 3(4) and (7) of the Superannuation (Northern Ireland) Order(1) and now vested in it(2).
(1)
S.I. 1972/1073 (N.I. 10).
(2)
By S.R. 1976 No. 281 Article 3 and Sch.1 and S.I. 1982/338 (N.I. 6) Article 6; the Department of Finance and Personnel was renamed the Department of Finance by section 1(4) of, and Schedule 1 to, the Departments (Northern Ireland) Act 2016 (c.5 (N.I. 11)).Citation and commencement
1.  This Order may be cited as the Superannuation (Victims of Crime Commissioner Designate) Order (Northern Ireland) 2022, shall come into operation on 1 April 2022 and have effect from 1 April 2022.Addition to list of offices
2.  There shall be added to the list of offices listed in Schedule 1 to the Superannuation (Northern Ireland) Order 1972, the office of Victims of Crime Commissioner Designate(1).
(1)
The Office of Victims of Crime Commissioner Designate was established by the Department of Justice.Sealed with the Official Seal of the Department of Finance on 11th March 2022.
(L.S.)
Patrick Neeson
A senior officer of the
Department of FinanceEXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order adds the office of Victims of Crime Commissioner Designate to the offices listed in Schedule 1 to the Superannuation (Northern Ireland) Order 1972, for which the Department of Finance may make pension schemes. It has effect from 1st April 2022.
