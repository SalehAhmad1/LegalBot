The Department for Infrastructure(a) makes the following Order in exercise of the powers 
conferred by Article 4(1), (2) and (3) of the Road Traffic Regulation (Northern Ireland) Order 
1997(b) and now vested in it(c). 
The Department has consulted such persons as it considered appropriate in compliance with 
paragraphs 1 and 2 of Schedule 1 to that Order. 
The Department has published a notice in compliance with paragraphs 1 and 3 of that Schedule. 
No written objection or other representation has been received. 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as the Parking and Waiting Restrictions (Belfast) (Amendment No. 
2) Order (Northern Ireland) 2022 and shall come into operation on 18th November 2022.

“Lengths of road on which vehicles must not wait 
12. Save as provided an Article 13, a person shall not cause or permit a vehicle to wait— 
(a) at any time on a length of road specified in Part 1, Part 2 or Part 13 of Schedule 2; 
(b) during the hours between 7.00 a.m. and 7.00 p.m. on the days Monday to Saturday 
inclusive on a length of road specified in Part 3, Part 4 or Part 14 of Schedule 2; 
(c) during the hours between 8.00 a.m. and 6.00 p.m. on the days Monday to Saturday 
inclusive on a length of road specified in Part 5 or Part 15 of Schedule 2; 
(d) during the hours between 8.00 a.m. and 6.00 p.m. on the days Monday to Friday 
inclusive on a length of road specified in Part 6 of Schedule 2; 
(e) during the hours between 9.30 a.m. and 4.30 p.m. on the days Monday to Friday 
inclusive on a length of road specified in Part 7 of Schedule 2;

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Order) 
This Order amends The Parking and Waiting Restrictions (Belfast) Order (Northern Ireland) 2018 
(“the Order”) by— 
(a) amending the definitions of postal packet and universal service provider; 
(b) Substituting new Articles 12 and 13(2) to include the new waiting restrictions in Parts 21 
and 22 of Schedule 2 (Article 2(2) and (3)); 
(c) the addition to Part 1 of Schedule 2 of the items specified in Article 2(4); 
(d) the addition to Part 2 of Schedule 2 of the items specified in Article 2(5); 
(e) the addition to Part 6 of Schedule 2 of the items specified in Article 2(6); and  
(f) the addition to Part 10 Schedule 2 of the items specified in Article 2(7). 
A penalty charge (£90) is payable with respect to a vehicle involved in a contravention of the 
Order in accordance with the Traffic Management (Northern Ireland) Order 2005 and The Penalty 
Charges (Prescribed Amounts) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2006. 
Traffic signs indicating the effect of this Order will in due course be placed on the roads.
