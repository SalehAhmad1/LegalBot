STATUTORY RULES OF NORTHERN IRELAND
2022 No. 34
INSOLVENCY PRACTITIONERS
The Insolvency Practitioners (Recognised Professional Bodies) (Revocation of Recognition) Order (Northern Ireland) 2022
Made
9th February 2022
Coming into operation
16th February 2022
The Department for the Economy(1) in exercise of the powers conferred on it by Article 350N(1) of the Insolvency (Northern Ireland) Order 1989(2) makes the following Order.
In accordance with Article 350N(1) of the Insolvency (Northern Ireland) Order 1989 the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants has requested that an order be made to revoke the Insolvency Practitioners (Recognised Professional Bodies) Order (Northern Ireland) 1991(3) insofar as it declares that the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants is a recognised professional body under Article 350 of the Insolvency (Northern Ireland) Order 1989. The Department is satisfied that it is appropriate in all the circumstances of the case to make that revocation.
The Association of Chartered Certified Accountants is known by that name as a result of having changed its name on 15th October 1996(4).
In accordance with Article 350N(3) of the Insolvency (Northern Ireland) Order 1989 the Department published a notice specifying that the Order is to take effect from 16th February 2022 and the reasons for making the Order.

(This note is not part of the Order)
The Insolvency Practitioners (Recognised Professional Bodies) Order (Northern Ireland) 1991 declares the bodies specified in the Schedule to that Order to be recognised professional bodies for the purposes of Article 350 of the Insolvency (Northern Ireland) Order 1989. The Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (formerly the Chartered Association of Certified Accountants) has asked to have their recognition as recognised professional body revoked.
Regulation 2 of this Order amends the Insolvency Practitioners (Recognised Professional Bodies) Order (Northern Ireland) 1991, by omitting the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants from the Schedule to that Order with effect from 00 February 2022. From this date the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants will cease to be a recognised professional body for the purposes of Article 350 of the Insolvency (Northern Ireland) Order 1989.
A notice specifying the date from which this Order takes effect and the Department’s reasons for making it, as required by Article 350N(3) of the Insolvency (Northern Ireland) Order 1989, was published on 14 February 2022. The notice is available online at https://www.economy-ni.gov.uk/publications/insolvency-service-notice-revocation-recognition-two-bodies-recognised-professional-bodies
