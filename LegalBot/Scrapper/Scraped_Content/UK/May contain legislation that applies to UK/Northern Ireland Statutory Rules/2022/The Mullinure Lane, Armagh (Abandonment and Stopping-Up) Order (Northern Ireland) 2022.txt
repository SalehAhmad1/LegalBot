The Department for Infrastructure(a) makes the following Order in exercise of the powers 
conferred by Article 68(1), (3) and (5) of the Roads (Northern Ireland) Order 1993(b) and now 
vested in it(c). 
The Department in accordance with Article 68(4) of that Order proposes on the completion of 
works providing alternative facilities for road traffic to abandon the area of Mullinure Lane 
described in Part 1 of the Schedule and to stop-up Mullinure Lane to vehicular traffic at the line 
described in Part 2 of the Schedule. 
Notice has been published, served and displayed in compliance with paragraphs 1, 2 and 3 of 
Schedule 8 to that Order. 
One objection has been received and subsequently withdrawn. 
Citation, commencement and interpretation 
1.—(1) This Order may be cited as the Mullinure Lane, Armagh (Abandonment and Stopping-
Up) Order (Northern Ireland) 2022 and shall come into operation on 16th March 2022. 
(2) In this Order “the contractor” means Charles Brand Limited whose registered office is 
situated at Rosemont House, 21-23 Sydenham Road, Belfast, BT3 9HA. 
Application 
2.—(1) The contractor shall carry out such works as the Department considers necessary for the 
provision of alternative facilities for road traffic and on the completion of works the area of 
Mullinure Lane described in Part 1 of the Schedule shall be abandoned and Mullinure Lane shall 
be stopped-up to vehicular traffic at the line described in Part 2 of the Schedule. 
(2) The contractor shall carry out such works as the Department considers necessary to prevent 
or restrict to such extent as the Department think fit, access to the road described in Part 2 of the 
Schedule by vehicular traffic.

3.—(1) All existing cables, wires, mains, pipes or other apparatus placed along, across, over or

SCHEDULE 
Article 2 
AREA OF ROAD TO BE ABANDONED AND ROAD TO BE 
STOPPED-UP 
PART 1 
AREA OF ROAD TO BE ABANDONED 
An area of 1849 square metres of road at Mullinure Lane, Armagh commencing at a point 439 
metres north of its junction with the Portadown Road for a distance of 287 metres in a north-
westerly direction, more particularly delineated and shown hatched and coloured red on map 
number IN1/18/723535. 
PART 2 
ROAD TO BE STOPPED-UP TO VEHICULAR TRAFFIC 
Mullinure Lane, Armagh at a line 4.3 metres north of its junction with Portadown Road more 
particularly delineated and marked A-B on map number IN1/18/723535. 
 
A copy of the map has been deposited at the Department’s Headquarters, Room 301, Clarence 
Court, 10-18 Adelaide Street, Belfast and DfI Roads and Rivers Southern Division, Marlborough 
House, Central Way, Craigavon. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Order) 
This Order abandons the area of road and stops-up the road described in the Schedule on 
completion of works to provide alternative facilities.
