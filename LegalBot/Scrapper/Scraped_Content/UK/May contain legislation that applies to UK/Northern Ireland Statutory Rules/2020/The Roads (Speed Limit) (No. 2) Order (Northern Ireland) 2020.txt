The Department for Infrastructure(a) makes the following Order in exercise of the powers 
conferred by Articles 37(3), 37(4) and 38(1)(a) of the Road Traffic Regulation (Northern Ireland) 
Order 1997(b) and now vested in it(c). 
The Department has consulted such persons as it considered appropriate in compliance with 
paragraphs 1 and 2 of Schedule 5 to that Order. 
The Department has published a notice in compliance with paragraphs 1 and 3 of that Schedule. 
No written objection or other representation has been received. 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as The Roads (Speed Limit) (No. 2) Order (Northern Ireland) 2020 
and shall come into operation on 8th December 2020. 
Application of speed limits 
2. The driving of motor vehicles at a speed exceeding 40 miles per hour on the road and lengths 
of road specified in Schedule 1 is prohibited. 
De-restriction of speed on a certain road 
3. The Department directs that Blaris Road, Unclassified No. 400, Lisburn, from a point 747 
metres north-west of its junction with Hillsborough Road, Route A1, to a point 1056 metres north-
west of that junction, shall not be a restricted road notwithstanding that a system of street lighting 
furnished by means of lamps placed not more than 185 metres apart is provided on that length of 
road. 
Revocations 
4. The provisions described in Schedule 2 are revoked.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Order) 
This Order— 
(a) applies a speed limit of 40 miles per hour to the road and lengths of road specified in 
Schedule 1; 
(b) makes the length of road specified in Article 3 a de-restricted road; and 
(c) revokes the provisions in Schedule 2. 
In relation to the revoked provisions in Schedule 2— 
1. The speed limit on the lengths of Donaghadee Road, Newtownards (paragraphs 1 and 6 of 
Schedule 2) is now included in a longer length of road set at 40 miles per hour. 
2. The speed limit on the length of Maydown Road, Londonderry (paragraph 2 of Schedule 2) 
no longer exists following a change in the road layout. 
3. The speed limit on the length of Novally Road, Ballycastle (paragraph 3 of Schedule 2) is 
now 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting). 
4. The speed limit on the length of Blaris Road, Lisburn (paragraph 4 of Schedule 2) is now 
partly set at 40 miles per hour and partly 60 miles per hour (national speed limit). 
5. The speed limit on the length of Tobermore Road, Draperstown (paragraph 5 of Schedule 2) 
is now included in a longer length of road set at 40 miles per hour. 
6. The speed limit on the length of Moor Road, Clonoe, Coalisland (paragraph 7 of Schedule 2) 
is now partly 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting) and partly set at 40 miles per hour.
