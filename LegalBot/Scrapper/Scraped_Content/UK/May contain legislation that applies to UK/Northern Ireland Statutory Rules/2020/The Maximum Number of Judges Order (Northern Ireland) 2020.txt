STATUTORY RULES OF NORTHERN IRELAND
2020 No. 248
COURT OF JUDICATURE, NORTHERN IRELAND
The Maximum Number of Judges Order (Northern Ireland) 2020
Made
11th November 2020
Coming into operation in accordance with Article 1
Affirmed by resolution of the Assembly on
14th December 2020
The Department of Justice makes the following Order in exercise of the power conferred by section 2(3) of the Judicature (Northern Ireland) Act 1978(1).
The Northern Ireland Judicial Appointments Commission has agreed to the making of this Order.
(1)
1978 c. 23, as amended by the Northern Ireland Act 2009 (c. 3) and the Department of Justice Act (Northern Ireland) 2010 (c. 3 (N.I.)).Citation and commencement
1.  This Order may be cited as the Maximum Number of Judges Order (Northern Ireland) 2020 and comes into operation on the day after the day on which it is affirmed by resolution of the Northern Ireland Assembly.Amendment of the Judicature (Northern Ireland) Act 1978
2.  In section 2(1)(1) of the Judicature (Northern Ireland) Act 1978 (the High Court: maximum number of judges) for “ten” substitute “fifteen”.
(1)
Relevant amendments are S.I. 2004/1985, S.I. 2001/958 and S.I. 1993/606.Sealed with the Official Seal of the Department of Justice on 11th November 2020
Naomi R. Long
Minister of JusticeEXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order amends section 2(1) of the Judicature (Northern Ireland) Act 1978 (1978 c. 23) to increase from ten to fifteen the maximum number of puisne judges in Her Majesty’s High Court of Justice in Northern Ireland.
