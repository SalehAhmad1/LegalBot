The Department for Infrastructure(a) makes the following Order in exercise of the powers 
conferred by Article 68(1) and (5) of the Roads (Northern Ireland) Order 1993(b) and now vested 
in it(c). 
The Department in accordance with Article 68(4) of that Order proposes on the completion of 
works providing alternative facilities for road traffic to abandon the areas of road described in the 
Schedule. 
Notice has been published, served and displayed in compliance with paragraphs 1, 2 and 3 of 
Schedule 8 to that Order. 
No objection has been received. 
Citation, commencement and interpretation 
1.—(1) This Order may be cited as The Seagoe Industrial Estate, Craigavon (Abandonment) 
Order (Northern Ireland) 2020 and shall come into operation on 27th January 2021. 
(2) In this Order “the Developer” means the Almac Group Limited, whose registered office is 
situated at Almac House, 20 Seagoe Industrial Estate, Craigavon, BT63 5QD. 
Application 
2. The Developer shall carry out such works as the Department considers necessary for the 
provision of alternative facilities for road traffic and on completion of the works the areas of road 
described in the Schedule shall be abandoned. 
3.—(1) All existing cables, wires, mains, pipes or other apparatus owned by the Department for 
the provision of street lighting, placed along, across, over or under the abandoned areas of road 
shall be relocated.

(2) All existing rights as to the use or maintenance of such cables, wires, mains, pipes or other

4.—(1) All other existing cables, wires mains, pipes or other apparatus placed along, across,

(2) All existing rights as to the use or maintenance of such cables, wire, mains, pipes or other

SCHEDULE 
Article 2 
AREAS OF ROAD TO BE ABANDONED 
3914.02 square metres of road at Seagoe Industrial Estate, Craigavon comprising five areas of 
carriageway, footpath and verge consisting of:— 
(a) 1353 square metres of carriageway and verge commencing at a point 113 metres east-
north-east of its junction with the B2 Seagoe Road, Craigavon and extending for 121 
metres in an east-north-easterly direction (marked A-B on map); 
(b) 2064 square metres of carriageway and verge commencing at a point 113 metres east-
north-east and then 68 metres south-south-east of its junction with the B2 Seagoe Road, 
Craigavon and extending for 117 metres in an east-north-easterly direction (marked C-D 
on map); 
(c) 249.35 square metres of footpath commencing at a point 121 metres east-north-east and 
then 99 metres south-south-east of its junction with the B2 Seagoe Road, Craigavon and 
extending for 111 metres in a north-north-westerly direction (marked E-F on map); 
(d) 186.10 square metres of footpath commencing at a point 182 metres east-north-east and 
then 95 metres south-south-east of the B2 Seagoe Road, Craigavon and extending for 66 
metres in a north-north-westerly direction (marked G-H on map); and 
(e) 61.57 square metres of footpath commencing at a point 114 metres east-north-east and 
then 16 metres north-north-west of its junction with the B2 Seagoe Road, Craigavon and 
extending for 24.56 metres in an east-north-easterly direction (marked I-J on map).
