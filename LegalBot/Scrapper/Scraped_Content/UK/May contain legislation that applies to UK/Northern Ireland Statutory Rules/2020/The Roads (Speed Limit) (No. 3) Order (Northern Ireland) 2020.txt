The Department for Infrastructure(a) makes the following Order in exercise of the powers 
conferred by Articles 37(3), 37(4) and 38(1)(a) of the Road Traffic Regulation (Northern Ireland) 
Order 1997(b) and now vested in it(c). 
The Department has consulted such persons as it considered appropriate in compliance with 
paragraphs 1 and 2 of Schedule 5 to that Order. 
The Department has published a notice in compliance with paragraphs 1 and 3 of that Schedule. 
No written objection or other representation has been received. 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as The Roads (Speed Limit) (No. 3) Order (Northern Ireland) 2020 
and shall come into operation on 8th December 2020. 
Application of speed limits 
2. The driving of motor vehicles at a speed exceeding 20 miles per hour on the roads specified in 
Schedule 1 is prohibited. 
3. The driving of motor vehicles at a speed exceeding 40 miles per hour on the lengths of road 
specified in Schedule 2 is prohibited. 
4. The driving of motor vehicles at a speed exceeding 50 miles per hour on the length of road 
specified in Schedule 3 is prohibited. 
Revocations 
5. The provisions described in Schedule 4 are revoked.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Order) 
This Order— 
(a) applies a speed limit of 20 miles per hour to the roads specified in Schedule 1; 
(b) applies a speed limit of 40 miles per hour to the lengths of road specified in Schedule 2; 
(c) applies a speed limit of 50 miles per hour to the length of road specified in Schedule 3; 
and  
(d) revokes the provisions in Schedule 4. 
In relation to the revoked provisions in Schedule 4— 
1. The speed limit on the length of the Knockloughrim – Gulladuff Road, now known as Quarry 
Road, Knockloughrim, (paragraph 1 of Schedule 4) is no longer needed as the speed limit has 
been superseded. 
2. The speed limits on the lengths of Centenary Park and Strule Park, Omagh (paragraph 2 of 
Schedule 4) are now set at 20 miles per hour. 
3. The speed limit on the length of Monea Road, Enniskillen, now known as Derrygonnelly 
Road, Enniskillen, (paragraph 3 of Schedule 4) is now included in a longer length of road set at 50 
miles per hour. 
4. The speed limit on Old Manse Road, Banbridge (paragraph 4 of Schedule 4) is now set at 40 
miles per hour. 
5. The speed limit on the length of Mossvale Road, Dromore (paragraph 5 of Schedule 4) is now 
included in a longer length of 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting).
