STATUTORY RULES OF NORTHERN IRELAND
2020 No. 169 (C. 4)
PUBLIC HEALTH
The Health Protection (Coronavirus, Wearing of Face Coverings) (Amendment) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2020 (Commencement) Order (Northern Ireland) 2020
Made
7th August 2020
The Department of Health(1), makes the following Order in exercise of the power conferred by regulation 1 of the Health Protection (Coronavirus, Wearing of Face Coverings) (Amendment) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2020(2).
(1)
Formerly the Department of Health, Social Services and Public Safety; see 2016 c. 5 (N.I.), s. 1(5)
(2)
S.R. 2020 No. 1641.  This order may be cited as the Health Protection (Coronavirus, Wearing of Face Coverings) (Amendment) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2020 (Commencement) Order (Northern Ireland) 2020.2.  10th August 2020 is the appointed day for the coming into operation of the Health Protection (Coronavirus, Wearing of Face Coverings) (Amendment) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2020.Sealed with the Official Seal of the Department of Health on 7th August 2020
(L.S.)
