STATUTORY RULES OF NORTHERN IRELAND
2021 No. 297
FAMILY LAW
The Female Genital Mutilation Protection Order (Relevant Third Party) Order (Northern Ireland) 2021
Made
5th November 2021
Coming into operation
1st December 2021
The Department of Finance(1), in exercise of the powers conferred by section 5A of, and paragraph 19(7) of Schedule 2 to the Female Genital Mutilation Act 2003(2), makes the following Order:
(1)
See section 1(4) of the Departments Act (Northern Ireland) 2016 (c. 5 (N.I.)).
(2)
2003 c. 31. Section 5A of, and schedule 2 to, the Female Genital Mutilation Act were inserted by Section 73(2) of the Serious Crime Act 2015 (c.9).Citation and commencement
1.—(1) This Order may be cited as the Female Genital Mutilation Protection Order (Relevant Third Party) Order (Northern Ireland) 2021.
(2) This Order comes into operation on 1st December 2021.Interpretation
2.  In this Order “HSC trust” means a Health and Social Care trust established under Article 10 of the Health and Personal Social Services (Northern Ireland) Order 1991(1).
(1)
S.I. 1991/194 (N.I. 1) as amended by section 32 of, and paragraph 1(1)(c) of Schedule 6 to the Health and Social Care (Reform) Act (Northern Ireland) 2009 (c. 1).Specification of HSC trust as a relevant third party
3.  A HSC trust is specified as a relevant third party for the purposes of paragraph 19(2)(b) of Schedule 2 to the Female Genital Mutilation Act 2003.Sealed with the Official Seal of the Department of Finance on 5th November 2021
Barry Armstrong

Department of FinanceEXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order specifies a Health and Social Care trust as a relevant third party under paragraph 19(2)(b) of Schedule 2 to the Female Genital Mutilation Act 2003 (c.31).
A relevant third party can apply to the court for a Female Genital Mutilation protection order without the leave of the court.
