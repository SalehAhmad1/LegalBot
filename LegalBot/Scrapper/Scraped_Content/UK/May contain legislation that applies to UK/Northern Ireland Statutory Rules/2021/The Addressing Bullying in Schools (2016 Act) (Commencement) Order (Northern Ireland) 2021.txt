STATUTORY RULES OF NORTHERN IRELAND
2021 No. 107 (C. 1)
EDUCATION
The Addressing Bullying in Schools (2016 Act) (Commencement) Order (Northern Ireland) 2021
Made
21st April 2021
The Department of Education, in exercise of the powers conferred by sections 5(3) of the Addressing Bullying in Schools Act (Northern Ireland) 2016(1), makes the following Order:
(1)
2016 c. 25 (N.I.)Citation
1.  This Order may be cited as the Addressing Bullying in Schools (2016 Act) (Commencement) Order (Northern Ireland) 2021Appointed Day
2.  The provisions of the Addressing Bullying in School Act (Northern Ireland) 2016 shall come into operation on 1st September 2021.Sealed with the Official Seal of the Department of Education on 21st April 2021
Richard Irwin
A senior officer of the
Department of Education
21st April 2021EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order brings into operation the sections of the Addressing Bullying in Schools Act (Northern Ireland) 2016.
