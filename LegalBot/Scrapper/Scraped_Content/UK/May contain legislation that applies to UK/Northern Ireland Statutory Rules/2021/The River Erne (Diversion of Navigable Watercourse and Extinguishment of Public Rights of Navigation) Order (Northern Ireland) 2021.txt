The Department for Infrastructure (a) proposes to provide for— 
(a) the diversion of part of the navigable watercourse specified in the Schedule; and 
(b) the extinguishment of any public rights of navigation over the navigable waters specified in 
Article 3. 
The Department considers it necessary or desirable in connection with the construction of a bridge 
over the navigable waters specified in the Schedule— 
(a) 
to divert part of the navigable watercourse specified in the Schedule; and 
(b) 
to extinguish any public rights of navigation over the navigable waters specified in 
Article 3. 
The Department has published and served a notice in compliance with paragraphs 1 and 2 of 
Schedule 8 to the Roads (Northern Ireland) Order 1993(b). 
In the absence of any objections being set before the Department during the statutory consultation 
period, the Department decided to proceed without subjecting the scheme to an examination 
through a public inquiry. 
The Department makes the following order in exercise of the powers conferred by Article 5 (1) 
and 6 of the Roads (Northern Ireland) Order 1993 and now exercisable by it(c). 
Citation, commencement and interpretation 
1. This Order may be cited as the River Erne (Diversion of Navigable Watercourse and 
Extinguishment of Public Rights of Navigation) Order (Northern Ireland) 2021 and comes into 
operation on 12 August 2021. 
2. In this Order—

SCHEDULE 
Articles 2 
LENGTH OF NAVIGABLE WATERCOURSE TO BE DIVERTED 
Navigation on part of the River Erne, is diverted by the bridge piers shown coloured red on 
Drawing 2, into the Navigation Watercourse as follows: 
Navigation Watercourse 
Single waterway, minimum 30 metres wide between bridge pier bases marked C and D on the 
main span as shown on Drawing 2. 
Navigation is also provided under the adjacent areas of the main and back spans as shown on 
Drawing 2 as approved by Waterways Ireland and marked E and F, G and H. 
Headway 
Headway clearances are indicated on Drawing 2 measured as above ordnance datum (AOD), 1 in 
100 year flood level. A headway minimum of 4.26 metres is provided over a width of 30m marked 
C and D on Drawing 2.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Order) 
This order provides for diversion of part of the navigable watercourse and the extinguishment of 
public rights of navigation over part of the navigable waters of the River Erne in association with 
the A4 Enniskillen Southern Bypass scheme.

0002, a copy of which has been deposited at the Department’s offices, Clarence Court, 10-18
