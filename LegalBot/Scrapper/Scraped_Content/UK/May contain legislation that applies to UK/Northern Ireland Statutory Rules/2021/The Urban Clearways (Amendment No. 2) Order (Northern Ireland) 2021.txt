EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Order) 
This Order amends The Urban Clearways Order (Northern Ireland) 2016 (“the Order”) by— 
(a) deleting the item relating to Shore Road, Newtownabbey; and 
(b) creating three lengths of urban clearway from Monday to Friday between the hours 8.00 
a.m. to 9.30 a.m. and 3.00 p.m. to 6.30 p.m. in both directions on lengths of Shore Road, 
Greenisland/Newtownabbey. 
A penalty charge (£90) is payable with respect to a vehicle involved in a contravention of the 
Order in accordance with the Traffic Management (Northern Ireland) Order 2005 and The Penalty 
Charges (Prescribed Amounts) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2006. 
Traffic signs indicating the effect of the Order will in due course be placed on the roads.
