EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Order) 
This Order amends The Urban Clearways Order (Northern Ireland) 2016 (“the 2016 Order”) by 
creating a length of urban clearway on the west side of Donegall Square East, Belfast on the days 
Monday to Friday between 8.00 a.m. to 9.30 a.m. and between 4.30 p.m. to 6.00 p.m. specified in 
Article 2(3). 
The Order also corrects a drafting error in the 2016 Order (Article 2(2)). 
A penalty charge (£90) is payable with respect to a vehicle involved in a contravention of the 2016 
Order in accordance with the Traffic Management (Northern Ireland) Order 2005 and The Penalty 
Charges (Prescribed Amounts) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2006. 
Traffic signs indicating the effect of this Order will in due course be placed on the road.
