Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as the Justice Act (Northern Ireland) 2016 (Relevant Benefits) Order 
(Northern Ireland) 2021 and shall come into operation 21 days after this Order is made.

“(za) universal credit;”.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Order) 
This Order amends section 14 of the Justice Act (Northern Ireland) 2016 (2016 c.21 (N.I.)) to add 
universal credit to the list of benefits from which deductions may be made under a collection order 
for the payment of a fine or other penalty.
