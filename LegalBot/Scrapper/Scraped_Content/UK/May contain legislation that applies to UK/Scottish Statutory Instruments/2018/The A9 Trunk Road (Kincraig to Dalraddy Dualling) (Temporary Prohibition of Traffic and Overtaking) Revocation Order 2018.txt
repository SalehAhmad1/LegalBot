Coming into force -

The Scottish Ministers make the following Order in exercise of the powers conferred by sections 
2(1) and (2), 4(1), 14(1)(a) and (4), 15(2) and 124(1)(d) of, and paragraph 27 of schedule 9 of, the 
Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(a) and all other powers enabling them to do so. 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as the A9 Trunk Road (Kincraig to Dalraddy Dualling) (Temporary 
Prohibition of Traffic and Overtaking) Revocation Order 2018 and comes into force on 16th 
January 2018. 
Revocation 
2. The A9 Trunk Road (Kincraig to Dalraddy Dualling) (Temporary Prohibition of Traffic and 
Overtaking) Order 2015(b) is revoked.
