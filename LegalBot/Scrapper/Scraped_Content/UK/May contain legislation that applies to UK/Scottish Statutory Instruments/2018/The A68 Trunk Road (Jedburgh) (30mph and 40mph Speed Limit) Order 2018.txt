The Scottish Ministers make the following Order in exercise of the powers conferred by sections 
82(2)(a) and (b), 83(1), 84(1)(a), and 124(1)(d) of, and paragraph 27 of schedule 9 of, the Road 
Traffic Regulation Act 1984(a) and all other powers enabling them to do so. 
In accordance with sections 83(1) and 84(2)(b) of that Act, they have given public notice of their 
intention to make the following Order, and have complied with Parts II and III of the Secretary of 
State’s Traffic Orders (Procedure) (Scotland) Regulations 1987(c). 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as the A68 Trunk Road (Jedburgh) (30mph and 40mph Speed Limit) 
Order 2018 and comes into force on 12th February 2018. 
Restricted roads 
2. The length of road specified in Part 1 of the schedule of this Order shall cease to be a 
restricted road. 
Speed restriction 
3. No person may drive any motor vehicle:— 
(a) at a speed exceeding 30 miles per hour on the length of road specified in Part 2 of the 
schedule of this Order; 
(b) at a speed exceeding 40 miles per hour on the length of road specified in Part 3 of the 
schedule of this Order.

Amendment 
4. Omit paragraph 2 of the schedule of the Trunk Roads (Restricted Roads) (Roxburghshire) 
Order 1975(a). 
Revocations 
5. The following orders are revoked:— 
(a) The Trunk Roads (Restricted Roads) (Jedburgh) Order 1977(b); and 
(b) The Trunk Roads (Restricted Roads) (Roxburghshire) (Variation) Order 1996(c). 
Exemption 
6. No speed restriction imposed by this Order applies to vehicles falling within regulation 3(4) 
of the Road Traffic Exemptions (Special Forces) (Variation and Amendment) Regulations 2011(d) 
when used in accordance with regulation 3(5) of those Regulations.

SCHEDULE 
Articles 2 and 3 
SPECIFIED LENGTHS OF ROAD 
PART 1 
That length of the A68 Edinburgh – Carter Bar Trunk Road in Jedburgh from a point 160 metres 
or thereby north of the point where it meets the extended centreline of Bankend North to a point 
81 metres or thereby south of the point where it meets the extended centreline of Oxnam Road 
(C15), a distance of 1.881 kilometres or thereby. 
PART 2 
That length of the A68 Edinburgh – Carter Bar Trunk Road in Jedburgh from a point 162 metres 
or thereby north of the point where it meets the extended centreline of Bankend North, to a point 
56 metres or thereby south of the point where it meets the extended southern kerbline of Oxnam 
Road (C15), a distance of 1.863 kilometres or thereby. 
PART 3 
That length of the A68 Edinburgh – Carter Bar Trunk Road in Jedburgh from a point 56 metres or 
thereby south of the point where it meets the extended southern kerbline of Oxnam Road (C15) to 
a point 110 metres or thereby south west of the point where it meets the extended southern 
kerbline of C30 (Inchbonny – Dolphinston), a distance of 703 metres or thereby.
