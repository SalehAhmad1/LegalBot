The Scottish Ministers make the following Order in exercise of the powers conferred by sections 
2(1) and (2), 4(1) and 14(1)(b) and (4) of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(a) and all other 
powers enabling them to do so. 
In accordance with section 14(1)(b) of that Act, they are satisfied that the traffic on the lengths of 
road specified in the schedule should be restricted because of the likelihood of danger to the 
public, or of serious damage to the road, which is not attributable to works. 
In accordance with section 14(3) of that Act, they have had regard to the existence of alternative 
routes suitable for the traffic which will be affected by this Order. 
Citation, commencement and cessation 
1. This Order may be cited as the A90 Trunk Road (Aberdeen Western Peripheral Route) 
(Stonehaven to Charleston) (Temporary 40 mph Speed Restriction) Order 2018, comes into force 
on 1st August 2018 and ceases to have effect on 30th November 2018. 
Speed restrictions 
2.—(1) The schedule of this Order has effect. 
(2) No person may use, or permit the use of, any vehicle on a length of road, or a part or parts of 
a length of road, specified in the schedule of this Order at a speed exceeding 40 miles per hour 
when that restriction is identified as applying to that length of road, or a part or parts of that length 
of road, by means of a traffic sign of the type described in item 1 (diagram 670 – maximum speed 
limit in mph) of the sign table in Part 2 of schedule 10 of the Traffic Signs Regulations and 
General Directions 2016(b).

Exemptions 
3. No speed restriction imposed by this Order applies to— 
(a) vehicles falling within regulation 3(4) of the Road Traffic Exemptions (Special Forces) 
(Variation and Amendment) Regulations 2011(a) when used in accordance with 
regulation 3(5) of those Regulations; or 
(b) vehicles being used in an emergency for coastguard purposes.

SCHEDULE 
Article 2 
In this schedule:— 
“A90 Trunk Road” means that part of the A90 Trunk Road trunked by the A90 (Aberdeen 
Western Peripheral Route) Trunk Road Order 2010(a) at Stonehaven and Charleston; and 
“A956 Trunk Road” means that part of the road constructed under the A956 (Aberdeen 
Western Peripheral Route) Trunk Road Order 2010(b) at Charleston. 
 
SPECIFIED LENGTHS OF ROAD 
1. That length of the A90 Trunk Road being the northbound on slip from its intersection with the 
Stonehaven north junction roundabout, in a generally south-easterly direction for a distance of 173 
metres or thereby. 
2. That length of the A90 Trunk Road being the southbound off slip from a point 246 metres or 
thereby northeast of its junction with the B979 to that junction, a distance of 246 metres or 
thereby. 
3. That length of the A90 Trunk Road being the northbound off slip from its intersection with 
the A956 Trunk Road, in a generally southerly direction for a distance of 403 metres or thereby. 
4. That length of the A90 Trunk Road being the southbound on slip from its intersection with the 
A956 Trunk Road, in a generally southerly direction for a distance of 331 metres or thereby.
