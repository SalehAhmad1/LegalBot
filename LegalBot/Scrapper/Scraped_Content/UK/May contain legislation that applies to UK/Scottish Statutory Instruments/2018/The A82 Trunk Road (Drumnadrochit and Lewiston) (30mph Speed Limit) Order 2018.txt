The Scottish Ministers make the following Order in exercise of the powers conferred by sections 
84(1)(a) and 124(1)(d) of, and paragraph 27 of schedule 9 of, the Road Traffic Regulation Act 
1984(a) and all other powers enabling them to do so. 
In accordance with section 84(2)(b) of that Act, they have given public notice of their intention to 
make the following Order, and have complied with Parts II and III of the Secretary of State’s 
Traffic Orders (Procedure) (Scotland) Regulations 1987(c). 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as the A82 Trunk Road (Drumnadrochit and Lewiston) (30mph 
Speed Limit) Order 2018 and comes into force on 1st October 2018. 
Speed restriction 
2. No person may drive any motor vehicle at a speed exceeding 30 miles per hour on the length 
of road specified in the schedule. 
Revocation 
3. The A82 (Drumnadrochit and Lewiston) (30mph and 40mph speed limit) Order 2006(d) is 
revoked. 
Exemption 
4. No speed restriction imposed by this Order applies to—

SCHEDULE 
Article 2 
SPECIFIED LENGTH OF ROAD 
That length of the A82 Dalnottar – Inverness Trunk Road from a point 180 metres or thereby east 
of the extended east gable of the Visitors Centre, Drumnadrochit Hotel, westwards, then 
southwards, then southeastwards, to a point 215 metres or thereby east of the extended front 
elevation of Borlum Bridge Cottage, Lewiston, a distance of 1.865 kilometres or thereby.
