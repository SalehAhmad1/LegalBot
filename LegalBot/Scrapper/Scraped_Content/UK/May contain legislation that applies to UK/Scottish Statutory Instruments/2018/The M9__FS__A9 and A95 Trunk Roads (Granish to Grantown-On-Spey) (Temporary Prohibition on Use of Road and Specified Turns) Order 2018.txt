The Scottish Ministers make the following Order in exercise of the powers conferred by sections 
2(1) and (2), 4(1) and 16A of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(a) and all other powers 
enabling them to do so. 
In accordance with section 16A(2) of that Act, they are satisfied that the traffic on those lengths of 
road specified in the schedule should be restricted or prohibited for the purpose of facilitating the 
holding of a relevant event and enabling members of the public to watch that relevant event. 
In accordance with section 16A(3) of that Act, they are satisfied that it is not reasonably 
practicable for the event to be held otherwise than on a road. 
In accordance with section 16A(8) of that Act, they have had regard to the safety and convenience 
of alternative routes suitable for the traffic which will be affected by this Order. 
Citation, commencement and cessation 
1. This Order may be cited as the M9/A9 and A95 Trunk Roads (Granish to Grantown-On-Spey) 
(Temporary Prohibition on Use of Road and Specified Turns) Order 2018, comes into force on 
25th August 2018 and ceases to have effect on 26th August 2018.

Prohibition of specified turns 
3. No person may use, or permit the use of, any vehicle on those lengths of road specified in Part 
1 of the schedule of this Order so as to make any turn specified in Part 2 of the schedule when a 
prohibition is identified as applying to those lengths of road by means of an appropriate traffic 
sign of the type described in item 7 (diagram 612 – no right turn for vehicular traffic) or item 8 
(diagram 613 – no left turn for vehicular traffic) of the sign table in Part 2 of schedule 3 of the 
Traffic Signs Regulations and General Directions 2016(b). 
Exemptions 
4. No prohibition imposed by this Order applies to— 
(a) a vehicle while being used in an emergency for fire brigade, ambulance, police or 
coastguard purposes; or 
(b) A vehicle being used for facilitating the holding of the relevant event.

SCHEDULE 
Articles 2 and 3 
PART 1 
SPECIFIED LENGTHS OF ROAD 
1. That length of the M9/A9 Edinburgh – Stirling – Thurso Trunk Road from a point 125 metres 
or thereby north of the junction with A95 Granish – Keith Trunk Road southwards to a point 200 
metres or thereby south of the junction with A95 Granish – Keith Trunk Road, a distance of 325 
metres or thereby. 
2. That length of the A95 Granish – Keith Trunk Road from its junction with the M9/A9 
Edinburgh – Stirling – Thurso Trunk Road in a generally eastwards, northeastwards and then 
southwards direction to its junction with the B970 Nethy Bridge Road eastwards of Grantown-on-
Spey, a distance of 21 kilometres or thereby. 
PART 2 
SPECIFIED TURNS 
1. All left turns from the southbound M9/A9 Edinburgh – Stirling – Thurso Trunk Road to the 
A95 Granish – Keith Trunk Road. 
2. All right turns from the northbound M9/A9 Edinburgh – Stirling – Thurso Trunk Road to the 
A95 Granish – Keith Trunk Road. 
3. All right turns from the southbound A95 Granish – Keith Trunk Road to the B9153 
Carrbridge Road. 
4. All right turns from the A939 leading from Grantown-on-Spey to the southbound A95 
Granish – Keith Trunk Road.
