The Scottish Ministers make the following Order in exercise of the powers conferred by sections 
2(1) and (2), 4(1) and 14(1)(a) and (4) of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(a) and all other 
powers enabling them to do so. 
In accordance with section 14(1)(a) of that Act, they are satisfied that the traffic on the length of 
road specified in the schedule should be restricted or prohibited because works are being or are 
proposed to be executed on or near that length of road. 
In accordance with section 14(3) of that Act, they have had regard to the existence of alternative 
routes suitable for the traffic which will be affected by this Order. 
Citation, commencement and cessation 
1. This Order may be cited as the M9/A9 Trunk Road (Inveralmond) (Temporary Prohibition of 
Specified Turns) Order 2018, comes into force on 10th June 2018 and ceases to have effect on 
23rd June 2018. 
Prohibition of specified turns 
2. No person may use, or permit the use of, any vehicle on the length of road specified in Part 1 
of the schedule of this Order so as to make any turn specified in Part 2 of the schedule when a 
prohibition is identified as applying to that length of road by means of an appropriate traffic sign 
of the type described in item 7 (diagram 612 – no right turn for vehicular traffic) or item 8 
(diagram 613 – no left turn for vehicular traffic) of the sign table in Part 2 of schedule 3 of the 
Traffic Signs Regulations and General Directions 2016(b).

2
Exemptions 
3. No prohibition imposed by this Order applies to— 
(a) a vehicle while being used in an emergency for fire brigade, ambulance, police or 
coastguard purposes; or 
(b) a vehicle being used for carrying out the works.

SCHEDULE 
Article 2 
PART 1 
SPECIFIED LENGTH OF ROAD 
That length of the M9/A9 Edinburgh – Stirling – Thurso Trunk Road being the northbound and 
southbound carriageways from a point about 500 metres or thereby north of the junction with 
Inveralmond Roundabout, Perth in a generally northwards direction to a point about 2 kilometres 
or thereby north of Inveralmond Roundabout, a distance of 1.5 kilometres or thereby. 
PART 2 
SPECIFIED TURNS 
1. All right turns from the southbound M9/A9 Edinburgh – Stirling – Thurso Trunk Road to the 
unclassified road leading to Berthapark Cottages and Ruthvenfield Road, Perth. 
2. All right turns from the unclassified road leading from Berthapark Cottages and Ruthvenfield 
Road, Perth to the southbound M9/A9 Edinburgh – Stirling – Thurso Trunk Road. 
3. All right turns from the northbound M9/A9 Edinburgh – Stirling – Thurso Trunk Road to the 
unclassified field entrance access road about 900 metres or thereby north of Inveralmond 
Roundabout, Perth. 
4. All right turns from the unclassified field entrance access road about 900 metres or thereby 
north of Inveralmond Roundabout, Perth to the northbound M9/A9 Edinburgh – Stirling – Thurso 
Trunk Road. 
5. All right turns from the southbound M9/A9 Edinburgh – Stirling – Thurso Trunk Road to the 
access road leading to Broxy Kennels, Perth. 
6. All right turns from the access road leading from Broxy Kennels, Perth to the southbound 
M9/A9 Edinburgh – Stirling – Thurso Trunk Road. 
7. All right turns from the northbound M9/A9 Edinburgh – Stirling – Thurso Trunk Road to the 
unclassified road leading to Denmarkfield, Luncarty. 
8. All right turns from the unclassified road leading from Denmarkfield, Luncarty to the 
northbound M9/A9 Edinburgh – Stirling – Thurso Trunk Road. 
9. All right turns from the southbound M9/A9 Edinburgh – Stirling – Thurso Trunk Road to the 
northbound M9/A9 Edinburgh – Stirling – Thurso Trunk Road. 
10. All right turns from the northbound M9/A9 Edinburgh – Stirling – Thurso Trunk Road to the 
southbound M9/A9 Edinburgh – Stirling – Thurso Trunk Road.
