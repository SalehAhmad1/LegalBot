The Scottish Ministers make the following Order in exercise of the powers conferred by sections 
82(2)(a), 83(1), 84(1)(a) and (c), and 124(1)(d) of, and paragraph 27 of, schedule 9 of the Road 
Traffic Regulation Act 1984(a) and all other powers enabling them to do so. 
In accordance with sections 83(1) and 84(2)(b) of that Act, they have given public notice of their 
intention to make the following Order, and have complied with Parts II and III of the Secretary of 
State’s Traffic Orders (Procedure) (Scotland) Regulations 1987(c). 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as the A85 Trunk Road (Crianlarich) (30mph and Part Time 20mph 
Speed Limit) Order 2018 and comes into force on 7th September 2018. 
Speed restrictions 
2. No person may drive any motor vehicle at a speed exceeding:— 
(a) 30 miles per hour on the length of road specified in Part 1 of the schedule of this Order; 
or 
(b) 20 miles per hour on the length of road specified in Part 2 of the schedule of this Order 
when that particular speed limit is identified as applying to that length of road by means 
of an appropriate traffic sign of the type described in item 1 (diagram 670 - maximum 
speed limit in mph) of the sign table in Part 2 of schedule 10 of the Traffic Signs 
Regulations and General Directions 2016(d).

Revocation 
3. The A82/A85 Trunk Road Crianlarich (30mph Speed Limit) and Crianlarich Primary School 
(Part-time 20mph Speed Limit) Order 2010(a) is revoked. 
Exemption 
4. No speed restriction imposed by this Order applies to— 
(a) vehicles falling within regulation 3(4) of the Road Traffic Exemptions (Special Forces) 
(Variation and Amendment) Regulations 2011(b) when used in accordance with 
regulation 3(5) of those Regulations; or 
(b) vehicles being used in an emergency for coastguard purposes.

Article 2 
SPECIFIED LENGTHS OF ROAD

That length of the A85 Stirling – Crianlarich Trunk Road at Crianlarich, from a point 214 metres

PART 2 
That length of the A85 Stirling – Crianlarich Trunk Road at Crianlarich, from a point 59 metres or
