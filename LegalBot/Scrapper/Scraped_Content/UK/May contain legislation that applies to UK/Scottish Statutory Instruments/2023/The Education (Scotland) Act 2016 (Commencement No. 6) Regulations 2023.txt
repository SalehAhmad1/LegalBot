14th March 2023EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Regulations)
These Regulations bring section 21 of the Education (Scotland) Act 2016 fully into force on 29 March 2023. Section 21, which inserts section 2ZA into the Education (Scotland) Act 1980, was previously commenced for the limited purposes of consulting on any regulations to be made under that section.NOTE AS TO EARLIER COMMENCEMENT REGULATIONS
