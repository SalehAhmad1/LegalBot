The Scottish Ministers make the following Order in exercise of the powers conferred by sections 
2(1) and (2), 4(1) and 14(1)(a) and (4) of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(a) and all other 
powers enabling them to do so. 
In accordance with section 14(1)(a) of that Act, they are satisfied that the traffic on the length of 
road specified in the schedule should be restricted or prohibited because works are being or are 
proposed to be executed on or near that length of road. 
In accordance with section 14(3) of that Act, they have had regard to the existence of alternative 
routes suitable for the traffic which will be affected by this Order. 
Citation, commencement and cessation 
1. This Order may be cited as the A96 Trunk Road (Tyrebagger Junction Area) (Temporary 
Prohibition of Specified Turns) Order 2023, comes into force on 10 April 2023 and ceases to have 
effect on 12 June 2023. 
Prohibition of specified turns 
2. No person may use, or permit the use of, any vehicle on the length of road specified in Part 1 
of the schedule so as to make any turn specified in Part 2 of the schedule when a prohibition is 
identified as applying to that length of road by means of an appropriate traffic sign of the type 
described in item 7 (diagram 612 – no right turn for vehicular traffic) or item 8 (diagram 613 – no 
left turn for vehicular traffic) of the sign table in Part 2 of schedule 3 of the Traffic Signs 
Regulations and General Directions 2016(b). 
Exemptions 
3. No prohibition imposed by this Order applies to—

SCHEDULE 
Article 2 
PART 1 
SPECIFIED LENGTH OF ROAD 
That length of the A96 Craibstone – Inverness Trunk Road from a point of 822 metres or thereby 
west of its junction with the centre line of the road known as the B979 to Tyrebagger, 
Aberdeenshire, in a generally easterly direction to a point of 504 metres or thereby west of the 
point where it meets the circulation lanes of the Craibstone Roundabout, a distance of 2.2 
kilometres or thereby. 
PART 2 
SPECIFIED TURNS 
1. All right turns from the eastbound carriageway of the A96 Craibstone – Inverness Trunk Road 
to the road known as the B979 to Tyrebagger, Aberdeenshire. 
2. All right turns from the road known as the B979 to Tyrebagger, Aberdeenshire, to the eastbound 
carriageway of the A96 Craibstone – Inverness Trunk Road.   
3. All U-turns.
