The Scottish Ministers make the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by section 102(2) of the Heat Networks (Scotland) Act 2021(1).
(1)
2021 asp 9.Citation and commencement
1.—(1) These Regulations may be cited as the Heat Networks (Scotland) Act 2021 (Commencement No. 3) Regulations 2023 and come into force on 30 May 2023.
(2) In these Regulations “the Act” means the Heat Networks (Scotland) Act 2021.Appointed day
2.—(1) The day appointed for the coming into force of the provisions of the Act specified in paragraph (2), insofar as not already in force, is 30 May 2023.
(2) The provisions are—
(a)
section 46 (power to designate heat network zone),
(b)

(c)
section 48 (designation of heat network zone by local authority),
(d)

(e)

(g)

(h)

16th May 2023EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Regulations)
These Regulations bring sections 46, 47, 48, 49, 50, 51, 52, 53, 54, 63, 65, 66, 67 and 93 of the Heat Networks (Scotland) Act 2021 (“the Act”) fully into force on 30 May 2023.
The Bill for the Act received Royal Assent on 30 March 2021. Sections 97 to 103 came into force on the following day.NOTE AS TO EARLIER COMMENCEMENT REGULATIONS
