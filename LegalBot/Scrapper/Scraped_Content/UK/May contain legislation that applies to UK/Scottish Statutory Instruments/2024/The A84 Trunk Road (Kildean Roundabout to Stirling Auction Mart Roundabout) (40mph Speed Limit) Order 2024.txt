The Scottish Ministers make the following Order in exercise of the powers conferred by 
section 84(1)(a) of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(a) and all other powers enabling them to 
do so. 
In accordance with section 84(2)(b) of that Act, they have given public notice of their intention to 
make the following Order, and have complied with Parts II and III of the Secretary of State’s 
Traffic Orders (Procedure) (Scotland) Regulations 1987(c). 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as the A84 Trunk Road (Kildean Roundabout to Stirling Auction 
Mart Roundabout) (40mph Speed Limit) Order 2024 and comes into force on 6 June 2024. 
Speed limit 
2. No person may drive any motor vehicle at a speed exceeding 40 miles per hour on the length 
of road specified in the schedule. 
Exemption 
3. No speed limit imposed by this Order applies to— 
(a) vehicles falling within regulation 3(4) of the Road Traffic Exemptions (Special Forces) 
(Variation and Amendment) Regulations 2011(d) when used in accordance with 
regulation 3(5) of those Regulations; or

SCHEDULE 
Article 2 
SPECIFIED LENGTH OF ROAD 
That length of the A84 Stirling - Lochearnhead Trunk Road from the point where it meets the A84 
Millennium Way, including the Kildean roundabout circulation lanes, westwards, including the 
Craigforth roundabout circulation lanes, westwards, including the Stirling Auction Mart 
roundabout circulation lanes, westwards to a point 85 metres or thereby west of the Stirling 
Auction Mart roundabout, a distance of 1.52 kilometres or thereby.
