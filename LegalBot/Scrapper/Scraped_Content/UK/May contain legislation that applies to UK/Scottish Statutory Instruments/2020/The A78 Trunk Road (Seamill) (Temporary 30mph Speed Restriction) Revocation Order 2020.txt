The Scottish Ministers make the following Order in exercise of the powers conferred by sections 
2(1), 4(1), 14(1)(a) and (4), 15(2) and 124(1)(d) of, and paragraph 27 of schedule 9 of, the Road 
Traffic Regulation Act 1984(a) and all other powers enabling them to do so. 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as the A78 Trunk Road (Seamill) (Temporary 30mph Speed 
Restriction) Revocation Order 2020 and comes into force on 18 June 2020. 
Revocation 
2. The A78 Trunk Road (Seamill) (Temporary 30mph Speed Restriction) Order 2017(b) is 
revoked.
