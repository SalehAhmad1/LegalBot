14th May 2020EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Regulations)
These Regulations amend the Adults with Incapacity (Ethics Committee) (Scotland) Regulations 2002 (“the 2002 Regulations”).
Regulation 2(2) amends regulation 3(4) and (5) to extend the consecutive period a person may be appointed to the Ethics Committee (“the Committee”) from 10 to 12 years.
Regulation 2(3) amends the 2002 Regulations to allow Ministers to appoint up to two Alternate Vice Chairs in addition to a Chair and Vice Chair.
Regulation 2(4) amends the 2002 Regulations to reduce the number of Committee members required to approve any research from 7 to 3 and adjust the description of persons to be included in that number. The amendment provides that one of the 3 persons must be the Chair or in the absence of the Chair the Vice-Chair or an Alternate Vice Chair.
