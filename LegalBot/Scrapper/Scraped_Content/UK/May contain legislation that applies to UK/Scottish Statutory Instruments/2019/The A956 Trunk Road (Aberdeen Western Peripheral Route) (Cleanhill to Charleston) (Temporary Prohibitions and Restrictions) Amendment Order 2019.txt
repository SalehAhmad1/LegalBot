The Scottish Ministers make the following Order in exercise of the powers conferred by sections 
2(1) and (2), and 14(1)(b) and (4) of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(a) and all other powers 
enabling them to do so. 
Citation, commencement and cessation 
1. This Order may be cited as the A956 Trunk Road (Aberdeen Western Peripheral Route) 
(Cleanhill to Charleston) (Temporary Prohibitions and Restrictions) Amendment Order 2019, 
comes into force on 17 January 2019 and ceases to have effect on 30 June 2019. 
Amendment of the A956 Trunk Road (Aberdeen Western Peripheral Route) (Cleanhill to 
Charleston) (Temporary Prohibitions and Restrictions) Order 2018 
2.—(1) The A956 Trunk Road (Aberdeen Western Peripheral Route) (Cleanhill to Charleston) 
(Temporary Prohibitions and Restrictions) Order 2018(b) is amended in accordance with 
paragraph (2). 
(2) In article 11(1), for sub-paragraph (a) substitute— 
“(a) a vehicle being used by any person in the exercise of their duty as a constable or as 
a member of a fire and rescue service, of an ambulance service, or the 
coastguard,”.
