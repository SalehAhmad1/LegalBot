The Scottish Ministers make the following Order in exercise of the powers conferred by section 14(1) of the Homelessness etc. (Scotland) Act 2003(1).
(1)
2003 asp 10.Citation and commencement
1.  This Order may be cited as the Homelessness etc. (Scotland) Act 2003 (Commencement No. 4) Order 2019 and comes into force on 7 November 2019.Appointed day
2.  The day appointed for the coming into force of sections 4(1) (inquiries as to intentional homelessness) and 8 (power to modify section 33 of the 1987 Act) of the Homelessness etc. (Scotland) Act 2003 is 7 November 2019.
