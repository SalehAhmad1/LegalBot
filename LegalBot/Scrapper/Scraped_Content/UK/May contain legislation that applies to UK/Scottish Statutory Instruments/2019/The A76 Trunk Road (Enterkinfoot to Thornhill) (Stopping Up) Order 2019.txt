The Scottish Ministers make the following Order, in exercise of the powers conferred by section 
68(1) of the Roads (Scotland) Act 1984(a), and of all other powers enabling them to do so. 
In accordance with section 68(1) of that Act they consider that the road described in the schedule 
of this Order will become unnecessary. 
In accordance with section 68(3) of that Act they are satisfied that a suitable alternative road will 
exist before the road is stopped up. 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as the A76 Trunk Road (Enterkinfoot to Thornhill) (Stopping Up) 
Order 2019 and comes into force on 12 April 2019. 
Length of Road to be stopped up 
2. The Scottish Ministers, as roads authority, order that the length of road described in Part 1 of 
the schedule shall be stopped up when the alternative road specified in Part 2 of the schedule is 
completed and open to traffic.

SCHEDULE  
Articles 2 and 3 
INTERPRETATION 
In this schedule — 
“the trunk road” means the existing A76 Dumfries - to Kilmarnock Trunk Road between 
Enterkinfoot and Thornhill, Dumfries and Galloway; 
“the plan SU1” means the plan marked “SU1” contained within the plan folio entitled “A76 
Trunk Road (Enterkinfoot to Thornhill) (Stopping Up) Order 2019” signed with reference to 
this Order and deposited at the offices of Transport Scotland, Trunk Roads and Bus 
Operations, Buchanan House, 58 Port, Dundas Road, Glasgow, G4 0HF; and 
“Point A” means the northern extent of the layby beside the northbound lane of the A76 
Dumfries – Kilmarnock Trunk Road 2.5 kilometres or thereby north of the Drumlanrig Castle 
entry road as shown on the plan SU1. 
PART 1 
LENGTH OF ROAD TO BE STOPPED UP  
From a point on the trunk road 152 metres or thereby north of Point A, in a generally south, south 
west direction for a distance of 638 metres or thereby, to a point 486 metres or thereby south, 
south west of Point A, as shown by the zebra hatching between points marked “1” and “2” on the 
plan SU1. 
PART 2 
ALTERNATIVE ROAD  
From a point on the trunk road, 182 metres or thereby north of point A, in a southerly direction for 
a distance of 753 metres or thereby, to a point on the trunk road 571 metres or thereby south of 
point A as shown by the black outline between points marked “3” and “4” on the plan SU1.
