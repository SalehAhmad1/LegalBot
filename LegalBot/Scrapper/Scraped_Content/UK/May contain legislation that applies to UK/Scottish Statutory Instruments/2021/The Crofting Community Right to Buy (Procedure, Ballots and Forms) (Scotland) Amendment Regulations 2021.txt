The Scottish Ministers make the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by sections 73(5) and (11), 75(2), (4), (6), and (7), 82(1) and (2), 90(6) and 98(3) of the Land Reform (Scotland) Act 2003(1) and all other powers enabling them to do so.

19th January 2021EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Regulations)
These Regulations are made in exercise of the powers in sections 73(5) and (11), 75(2), (4), (6), and (7), 82(1) and (2), 90(6) and 98(3) of the Land Reform (Scotland) Act 2003.
Regulation 2 amends dates contained in saving and transitional provisions in the Crofting Community Right to Buy (Procedure, Ballots and Forms) (Scotland) Regulations 2020, to align those dates with the coming into force date for those Regulations.
