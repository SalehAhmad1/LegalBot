22nd September 2021EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Regulations)
These Regulations commence section 7(2)(g) of the Fuel Poverty (Targets, Definition and Strategy) (Scotland) Act 2019. Section 7(2)(g) places a requirement upon Scottish Ministers to consult the Scottish Fuel Poverty Advisory Panel in preparing the Fuel Poverty Strategy.NOTE AS TO EARLIER COMMENCEMENT REGULATIONS
