The Scottish Ministers make the following Order in exercise of the powers conferred by sections 
2(1) and (2), 4(1), 14(1)(a) and (4), 15(2) and 124(1)(d) of, and paragraph 27(1) of schedule 9 of, 
the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(a) and all other powers enabling them to do so. 
In accordance with section 14(1)(a) of that Act, they are satisfied that the traffic on the lengths of 
road specified in the schedule should be restricted or prohibited because works are being or are 
proposed to be executed on or near those lengths of road. 
In accordance with section 14(3) of that Act, they have had regard to the existence of alternative 
routes suitable for the traffic which will be affected by this Order. 
In accordance with section 15(2) of that Act, they are satisfied that the execution of the works will 
take longer than eighteen months and they will revoke this Order as soon as the works are 
completed. 
Citation, commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as the M8/A8/A8(M) Trunk Road Junction 15 (Townhead) to 
Junction 18 (Charing Cross) (Temporary Prohibition of Traffic and Temporary Speed Restrictions) 
Order 2022, comes into force on 16 November 2022.

Speed restrictions 
3. No person may use, or permit the use of, any vehicle on the length of road, or a part or parts of 
a length of road, specified in Part 1 of the schedule of this Order at a speed exceeding 40 miles per 
hour when a restriction is identified as applying to that length of road, or a part or parts of that 
length of road, by means of a traffic sign of the type described in item 1 (diagram 670 – maximum 
speed limit in mph) of the sign table in Part 2 of schedule 10 of the Traffic Signs Regulations and 
General Directions 2016. 
4. No person may use, or permit the use of, any vehicle on the length of road, or a part or parts of 
a length of road, specified in Part 1 of the schedule of this Order at a speed exceeding 30 miles per 
hour when a restriction is identified as applying to that length of road, or a part or parts of that 
length of road, by means of a traffic sign of the type described in item 1 (diagram 670 – maximum 
speed limit in mph) of the sign table in Part 2 of schedule 10 of the Traffic Signs Regulations and 
General Directions 2016. 
Revocation 
5. The M8/A8/A89 Trunk Road Junction 15 (Townhead) to Junction 18 (Charing Cross) 
(Temporary Prohibition of Traffic and Temporary Speed Restrictions) Order 2021(b) is revoked. 
Exemptions 
6. No prohibition imposed by this Order applies to— 
(a) vehicles being used for carrying out the works, or 
(b) vehicles being used in an emergency for fire brigade, ambulance, police or coastguard 
purposes. 
(2) Paragraph 1(b) does not apply to any prohibition which has effect on the lengths of road 
specified in Part 2 of the schedule of this Order. 
7. No speed restriction imposed by this Order applies to— 
(a) vehicles falling within regulation 3(4) of the Road Traffic Exemptions (Special Forces) 
(Variation and Amendment) Regulations 2011(c) when used in accordance with 
regulation 3(5) of those Regulations, or 
(b) vehicles being used in an emergency for coastguard purposes.
