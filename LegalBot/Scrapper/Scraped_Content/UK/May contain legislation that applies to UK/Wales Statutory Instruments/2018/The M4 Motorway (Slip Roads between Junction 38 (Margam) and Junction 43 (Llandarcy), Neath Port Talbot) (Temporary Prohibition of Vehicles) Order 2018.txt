2018 No. 144 (W. 36) 
ROAD TRAFFIC, WALES 
The M4 Motorway (Slip Roads 
between Junction 38 (Margam) and 
Junction 43 (Llandarcy), Neath Port 
Talbot) (Temporary Prohibition of 
Vehicles) Order 2018 
Made 
 6 February 2018 
Coming into force 
 8 February 2018 
The Welsh Ministers, being the traffic authority for the 
relevant length of the M4 London to South Wales 
Motorway, are satisfied that traffic on specified 
lengths of the motorway should be prohibited because 
of works proposed on or near the motorway. 
The Welsh Ministers, therefore, in exercise of the 
powers conferred upon them by section 14(1) and (4) 
of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(1), make this 
Order. 
Title, Commencement and Interpretation  
1. The title of this Order is The M4 Motorway (Slip 
Roads between Junction 38 (Margam) and Junction 43 
(Llandarcy), 
Neath 
Port 
Talbot) 
(Temporary 
Prohibition of Vehicles) Order 2018 and it comes into 
force on 8 February 2018. 
2.—(1) In this Order:  
“exempted vehicle” (“cerbyd esempt”) means any 
vehicle being used for the emergency services or 
in connection with the works giving rise to this 
Order; 
                                                                               
(1) 1984 c.27; section 14 was substituted by the Road Traffic 
(Temporary Restrictions) Act 1991 (c.26), section 1(1) and 
Schedule 1. By virtue of S.I. 1999/672, and section 162 of, and 
paragraph 30 of Schedule 11 to, the Government of Wales Act 2006 
(c.32), these powers are now exercisable by the Welsh Ministers in 
relation to Wales. 
 
 
2
“the motorway” (“y draffordd”) means the M4 
London to South Wales Motorway;  
“works period” (“cyfnod y gwaith”) means 
intermittent periods commencing at 20:00 hours on 
8 February 2018 and ending when the temporary 
traffic signs for each period of works are removed. 
(2) Any reference in this Order to a numbered article 
is a reference to the article bearing that number in this 
Order. 
Prohibition 
3. No person may, during the works period, drive or 
cause or permit any vehicle, other than an exempted 
vehicle, to proceed on the entire lengths of the 
eastbound and westbound exit and entry slip roads of 
the motorway between Junction 38 (Margam) and 
Junction 43 (Llandarcy), Neath Port Talbot. 
Application 
4. The prohibition in article 3 applies only during 
such times and to such extent as indicated by traffic 
signs. 
Duration of this Order 
5. The maximum duration of this Order is 12 
months.
