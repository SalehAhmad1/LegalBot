The Welsh Ministers, as traffic authority for the 
relevant length of the A40 Trunk Road, in exercise of 
the powers conferred upon them by sections 82(2), 
83(1), 84(1)(a), (2) and 124(1)(d) of, and paragraph 
27(1) of Schedule 9 to, the Road Traffic Regulation 
Act 1984(1), and after consultation with the Chief 
Officer of Police for Dyfed-Powys Police, make this 
Order.

1. The title of this Order is the A40 Trunk Road 
(Haverfordwest, Pembrokeshire) (40 mph & 50 mph 
Speed Limits and Derestriction) Order 2018 and it 
comes into force on 24 August 2018.

Restrictions 
3. No person may drive any motor vehicle, other 
than an exempted vehicle, at a speed exceeding 40 
miles per hour on the length of the trunk road specified 
in Schedule 1 to this Order. 
4. No person may drive any motor vehicle, other 
than an exempted vehicle, at a speed exceeding 50 
miles per hour on the length of the trunk road specified 
in Schedule 2 to this Order. 
Derestriction 
5. The length of the trunk road specified in Schedule 
3 to this Order shall cease to be a restricted road for 
the purposes of Section 81 of the Road Traffic 
Regulation Act 1984. 
Revocations 
6. The A40 Trunk Road (Haverfordwest Bypass, 
Dyfed) (De-restriction) Order 1994(3) and the A40

SCHEDULES 
Lengths of the A40 trunk road at Haverfordwest, 
Pembrokeshire 
 
SCHEDULE 1 
40 mph Speed Limit 
 
The length of the trunk road that extends from a point 
103 metres north of its junction with the northern side 
of Withybush Roundabout to a point 46 metres south-
east of its junction with the south-eastern side of 
Cardigan Road Roundabout, including the circulatory 
carriageways of Withybush Roundabout and Cardigan 
Road Roundabout.  
 
SCHEDULE 2 
50 mph Speed Limit 
 
The length of the trunk road that extends from a point 
103 metres north of its junction with the northern side 
of Withybush Roundabout to a point 646 metres north 
of that junction. 
 
SCHEDULE 3 
Derestriction 
 
The length of the trunk road that extends from a point 
125 metres north of the centre-point of Scotchwell 
Roundabout to a point 220 metres north of the centre-
point of that roundabout.
