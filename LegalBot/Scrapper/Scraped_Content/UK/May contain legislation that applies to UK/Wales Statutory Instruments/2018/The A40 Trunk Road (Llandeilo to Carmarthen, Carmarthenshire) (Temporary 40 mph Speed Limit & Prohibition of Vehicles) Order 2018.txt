ROAD TRAFFIC, WALES 
The A40 Trunk Road (Llandeilo to 
Carmarthen, Carmarthenshire) 
(Temporary 40 mph Speed Limit & 
Prohibition of Vehicles) Order 2018 
Made 
20 July 2018 
Coming into force 
30 July 2018 
The Welsh Ministers, as traffic authority for the A40 
Trunk Road, are satisfied that traffic on specified 
lengths of the trunk road should be prohibited and/or 
restricted because of the likelihood of a danger to the 
public and because of works proposed on or near the 
road. 
The Welsh Ministers, therefore, in exercise of the 
powers conferred upon them by section 14(1), (4) and 
(7) of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(1), make 
this Order.

Restriction and Prohibition  
3. No person may drive any motor vehicle at a speed 
exceeding 40 miles per hour on the length of the trunk 
road specified in Schedule 1 to this Order. 
4. No person may, during the works period, drive or 
cause or permit any vehicle, other than an exempted 
vehicle, to proceed on the length of the trunk road 
specified in Schedule 2 to this Order. 
Application 
5.—(1) The restriction and prohibition in articles 3 
and 4 apply only during such times and to such extent 
as indicated by traffic signs. 
(2) No speed limit imposed by this Order applies to       
an exempted vehicle. 
Suspensions 
6. Any statutory provisions restricting the speed of 
motor vehicles on the length of the trunk road 
specified in Schedule 1 are suspended during such 
times as the restriction specified in article 3 applies. 
Duration of this Order 
7. The maximum duration of this Order is 18 
months.

SCHEDULES 
Lengths of the trunk road between Llandeilo and 
Carmarthen, Carmarthenshire 
 
SCHEDULE 1 
Temporary 40 mph Speed Limit 
 
The length of the trunk road that extends from a point 
240 metres west of the centre-point of its junction with 
the B4310 to Nantgaredig to a point 1.36 kilometres 
east of its junction with the C2050 Class III road to 
Whitemill. 
 
SCHEDULE 2 
Temporary Prohibition of Vehicles 
 
The length of the trunk road that extends from the 
western side of Llandeilo Roundabout, Llandeilo to the 
eastern side of Glangwili Roundabout, Carmarthen.
