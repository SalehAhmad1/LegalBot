2017 anaw 2.Title and interpretation
1.—(1) The title of this Order is the Public Health (Wales) Act 2017 (Commencement No. 3) Order 2018.
(2) In this Order, “the Act” (“y Ddeddf”) means the Public Health (Wales) Act 2017.Provisions coming into force on 31 May 2018

(a)
Part 8 (provision of toilets); and
(b)
Schedule 4 (provision of toilets: consequential amendments).Vaughan Gething

17 May 2018EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order made by the Welsh Ministers brings into force specified provisions of the Public Health (Wales) Act 2017 (“the Act”).
Article 2 brings into force certain provisions of the Act relating to the provision of toilets on the 31 May 2018.NOTE AS TO EARLIER COMMENCEMENT ORDERS
