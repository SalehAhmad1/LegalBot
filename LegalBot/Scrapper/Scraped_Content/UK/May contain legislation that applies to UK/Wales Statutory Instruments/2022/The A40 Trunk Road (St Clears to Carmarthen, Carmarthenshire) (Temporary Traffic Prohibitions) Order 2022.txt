ROAD TRAFFIC, WALES 
The A40 Trunk Road (St Clears to 
Carmarthen, Carmarthenshire) 
(Temporary Traffic Prohibitions) 
Order 2022 
Made 
20 December 2022 
Coming into force 
3 January 2023 
The Welsh Ministers, as traffic authority for the A40   
trunk road, are satisfied that traffic on specified lengths 
of the trunk road should be prohibited because of 
works proposed on or near the road. 
The Welsh Ministers, therefore, in exercise of the 
powers conferred upon them by section 14(1) and (4) 
of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(1), make this 
Order. 
Title and commencement  
1. The title of this Order is the A40 Trunk Road (St 
Clears to Carmarthen, Carmarthenshire) (Temporary 
Traffic Prohibitions) Order 2022 and this Order comes 
into force on 3 January 2023. 
Interpretation 
2. In this Order—  
“exempted vehicle” (“cerbyd esempt”) means any 
vehicle being used by the emergency services or in 
connection with the works giving rise to this 
Order; 
“the trunk road” (“y gefnffordd”) means the A40 
London to Fishguard trunk road; 
                                                                               
(1) 
1984 c. 27; section 14 was substituted by the Road Traffic   
(Temporary Restrictions) Act 1991 (c. 26), section 1(1) and 
Schedule 1.  By virtue of S.I. 1999/672, and section 162 of, 
and paragraph 30 of Schedule 11 to, the Government of 
Wales Act 2006(c. 32), these powers are now exercisable by 
the Welsh Ministers in relation to Wales. 
 
2
“works period” (“cyfnod y gwaith”) means 
intermittent periods commencing at 19:00 hours on 
3 January 2023 and ending when the temporary 
traffic signs are permanently removed. 
Prohibitions 
3. No person may, during the works period, drive   
any vehicle, other than an exempted vehicle, on the 
length of the trunk road specified in Schedule 1 to this 
Order. 
4. No person may, during the works period, permit 
any vehicle, other than an exempted vehicle, to make a 
right hand turn through the central reservation gaps in 
the length of the trunk road specified in Schedule 2 to 
this Order. 
Application 
5. The prohibitions in articles 3 and 4 apply only 
during such times and to such extent as indicated by 
traffic signs. 
Duration of this Order 
6. The maximum duration of this Order is 18 
months.

SCHEDULE 1 
 
Temporary Prohibition of Vehicles  
The length of the A40 eastbound entry slip road at St 
Clears that extends from its junction with Pentre Road 
to the nosing of the main eastbound carriageway.  
 
SCHEDULE 2 
 
Temporary Prohibition of  Right Hand Turns  
 
3
The length of the trunk road that extends from St 
Clears Roundabout to Carmarthen Roundabout in 
Carmarthenshire.
