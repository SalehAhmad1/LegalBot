2021 No. 87 (W. 24) 
ROAD TRAFFIC, WALES 
The A487 Trunk Road (Layby at 
Henfynyw, Ceredigion) (Temporary 
Prohibition of Vehicles) Order 2021 
Made 
22 January 2021 
Coming into force 
1 February 2021 
The Welsh Ministers, as traffic authority for the A487 
trunk road, are satisfied that traffic on a specified 
length of the trunk road should be prohibited because 
of works proposed on or near the road. 
The Welsh Ministers, therefore, in exercise of the 
powers conferred upon them by sections 14(1) and (4) 
of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(1), make this 
Order. 
Title, Commencement and Interpretation 
1. The title of this Order is the A487 Trunk Road 
(Layby 
at 
Henfynyw, 
Ceredigion) 
(Temporary 
Prohibition of Vehicles) Order 2021 and it comes into 
force on 1 February 2021. 
2.In this Order:  
“exempted vehicle” (“cerbyd esempt”) means any       
vehicle being used in connection with the works 
giving rise to this Order; 
“the trunk road” (“y gefnffordd”) means the layby 
on the A487 Fishguard to Bangor trunk road that 
extends from a point 50 metres south of the centre-
point of its junction with Cwrt Y Brenin to a point 
90 meters north-west of the centre-point of that 
junction at Henfynyw, Ceredigion; 
                                                                               
(1) 1984 c.27; section 14 was substituted by the Road Traffic   
(Temporary Restrictions) Act 1991 (c.26), section 1(1) and 
Schedule 1.  By virtue of S.I. 1999/672, and section 162 of, and 
paragraph 30 of Schedule 11 to, the Government of Wales Act 
2006(c.32), these powers are now exercisable by the Welsh 
Ministers in relation to Wales. 
 
 
2
“works period” (“cyfnod y gwaith”) means the  
period commencing at 08:00 on the 1 February 
2021  and ending when the temporary traffic signs 
are permanently removed.       
Prohibition 
3. No person may, during the works period, drive   
any vehicle, other than an exempted vehicle, on the 
length of the trunk road.  
Application 
4. The prohibition in article 3 applies only during 
such times and to such extent as indicated by traffic 
signs. 
Duration of this Order 
5. The maximum duration of this Order is 18  
months. 
 
Signed under authority of the Minister for Economy,
