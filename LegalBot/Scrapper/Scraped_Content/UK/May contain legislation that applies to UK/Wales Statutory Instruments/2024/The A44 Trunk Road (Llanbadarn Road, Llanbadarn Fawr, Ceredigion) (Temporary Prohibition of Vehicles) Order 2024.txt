ROAD TRAFFIC, WALES 
The A44 Trunk Road (Llanbadarn 
Road, Llanbadarn Fawr, 
Ceredigion) (Temporary Prohibition 
of Vehicles) Order 2024 
Made 
15 February 2024 
Coming into force                26 February 2024 
The Welsh Ministers, as traffic authority for the 
relevant length of the A44 trunk road, are satisfied that 
traffic on a specified length of the trunk road should be 
prohibited because of works proposed on or near the 
road. 
 The Welsh Ministers, therefore, in exercise of the 
powers conferred upon them by section 14(1) and (4) 
of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(1), make this 
Order. 
Title and commencement 
1. The title of this Order is the A44 Trunk Road 
(Llanbadarn Road, Llanbadarn Fawr, Ceredigion) 
(Temporary Prohibition of Vehicles) Order 2024 and 
this Order comes into force on 26 February 2024. 
Interpretation 
2. In this Order— 
“the trunk road” (“y gefnffordd”) means the length 
of the A44  Newtown to Aberystwyth trunk road; 
“works period” (“cyfnod y gwaith”) means the 
period commencing at 21:00 hours on 26 February

Prohibition 
3. No person may, during the works period, drive  
any vehicle on the length of the trunk road known as 
Llanbadarn Road that extends from its junction with 
Plascrug Avenue to a point 100 metres north-west of 
that junction at Llanbadarn Fawr.  
Application 
4. The prohibition in article 3 applies only during 
such times and to such extent as indicated by traffic 
signs.  
Duration of this Order 
5. The maximum duration for this Order shall be 18 
months.
