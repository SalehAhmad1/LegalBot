ROAD TRAFFIC, WALES 
The A40 Trunk Road (Nantgaredig, 
Carmarthenshire) (Temporary 
Prohibition of Vehicles) Order 2024 
Made 
28 June 2024 
Coming into force                        8 July 2024 
The Welsh Ministers, as traffic authority for the A40 
trunk road, are satisfied that traffic on a specified 
length of the trunk road should be prohibited because 
of works proposed on or near the road. 
The Welsh Ministers, therefore, in exercise of the 
powers conferred upon them by section 14(1) and (4) 
of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(1), make this 
Order. 
Title and commencement  
1. The title of this Order is the A40 Trunk Road 
(Nantgaredig, 
Carmarthenshire) 
(Temporary 
Prohibition of Vehicles) Order 2024 and this Order 
comes into force on 8 July 2024. 
Interpretation 
2. In this Order—  
“the trunk road” (“y gefnffordd”) means the 
length of the A40 Llandeilo to Carmarthen trunk 
road; 
“works period” (“cyfnod y gwaith”) means the 
period commencing at 20:00 hours on the 8 July 
2024 and ending when the temporary traffic signs 
are permanently removed. 
                                                                               
(1) 
1984 c. 27; section 14 was substituted by the Road Traffic   
(Temporary Restrictions) Act 1991 (c. 26), section 1(1) and 
Schedule 1.  By virtue of S.I. 1999/672, and section 162 of, 
and paragraph 30 of Schedule 11 to, the Government of 
Wales Act 2006 (c. 32), these powers are now exercisable by 
the Welsh Ministers in relation to Wales. 
 
2
Prohibition  
3. No person may, during the works period, drive. 
any vehicle on the length of the trunk road that extends 
from a point 150 metres west of the centre point of its 
junction with the B4310 road to a point 80 metres east 
of that said junction at Nantgaredig. 
Application 
4. The prohibition in article 3 applies only during 
such times and to such extent as indicated by traffic 
signs. 
Duration of this Order 
5. The maximum duration of this Order is 18 
months.
