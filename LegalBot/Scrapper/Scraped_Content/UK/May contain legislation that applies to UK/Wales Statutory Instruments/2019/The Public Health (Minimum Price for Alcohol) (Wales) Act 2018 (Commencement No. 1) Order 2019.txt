WELSH STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS
2019 No. 1336 (W. 233) (C. 45)
FOOD, WALES
PUBLIC HEALTH, WALES
The Public Health (Minimum Price for Alcohol) (Wales) Act 2018 (Commencement No. 1) Order 2019
Made
7 October 2019
The Welsh Ministers make the following Order in exercise of the power conferred on them by section 28(2) of the Public Health (Minimum Price for Alcohol) (Wales) Act 2018(1).
(1)
2018 anaw 5.Title
1.  The title of this Order is the Public Health (Minimum Price for Alcohol) (Wales) Act 2018 (Commencement No. 1) Order 2019.Provisions coming into force on 14 October 2019
2.  The appointed day for the coming into force of section 1 (minimum price for alcohol) of the Public Health (Minimum Price for Alcohol) (Wales) Act 2018, to the extent necessary for the making of regulations under that section, is 14 October 2019.Vaughan Gething
Minister for Health and Social Services, one of the Welsh Ministers
7 October 2019EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order, made by the Welsh Ministers, brings into force on 14 October 2019 section 1 (minimum price for alcohol) of the Public Health (Minimum Price for Alcohol) (Wales) Act 2018 to the extent necessary for the making of regulations under that section.
