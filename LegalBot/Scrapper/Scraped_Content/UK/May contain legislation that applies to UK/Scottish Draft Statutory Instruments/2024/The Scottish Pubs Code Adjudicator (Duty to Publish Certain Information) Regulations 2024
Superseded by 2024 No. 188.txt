(This note is not part of the Regulations)
These Regulations make provision consequential on, and ancillary to, the Tied Pubs (Scotland) Act 2021, to add the Scottish Pubs Code Adjudicator to the public bodies listed in schedule 8 of the Public Services Reform (Scotland) Act 2010. This makes the Scottish Pubs Code Adjudicator subject to duties under Part 3 of that Act to publish information about certain types of expenditure, the exercise of functions and the use of special advisers.
A Business and Regulatory Impact Assessment, Equality Impact Assessment, Data Protection Impact Assessment, and Fairer Scotland Duty Assessment has been prepared and placed in the Scottish Parliament Information Centre. Copies can be obtained from the Scottish Government, B1 Spur, Saughton House, Broomhouse Drive, Edinburgh EH11 3XD.
