The Secretary of State has previously decided that it is in the public interest to restrict flying by 
reason of the intended holding of flying displays by a jet formation display team. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Jet Formation 
Display Teams) (Amendment) (No. 2) Regulations 2021 and they come into force on 14th August 
2021.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations amend the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Jet Formation Display 
Teams) Regulations 2021 (S.I. 2021/536, as amended by S.I. 2021/655) which restrict flying in the 
vicinity of places where jet formation display teams intend to give flying displays. 
The Regulations add displays to be held at Odiham (14th August 2021), the Isle of Wight (16th 
September 2021), and at Duxford (18th and 19th September 2021). 
Full details of the amendment will be promulgated by Notice to Aviation (NOTAM).
