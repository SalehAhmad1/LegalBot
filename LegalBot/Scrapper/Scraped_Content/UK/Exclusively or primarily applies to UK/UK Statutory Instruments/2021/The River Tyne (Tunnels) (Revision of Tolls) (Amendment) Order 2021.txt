STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS
2021 No. 1211
HIGHWAYS, ENGLAND
The River Tyne (Tunnels) (Revision of Tolls) (Amendment) Order 2021
Made
18th October 2021
Coming into force
15th November 2021
The Secretary of State for Transport, in exercise of the powers conferred by section 13(1) of the Tyne and Wear Act 1976(1) makes the following Order:
(1)
1976 c. xxxvi amended by S.I. 2005/2222.1.  This Order may be cited as the River Tyne (Tunnels) (Revision of Tolls) (Amendment) Order 2021 and shall come into force on 15th November 2021.2.  In the Schedule to the River Tyne (Tunnels) (Revision of Tolls) Order 2021, for the words “Mechanically propelled vehicle other than a vehicle in Class 1; Having a height of 2 metres or less, or Having a height greater than 2 metres and less than 3 metres and maximum of 2 axles, or Being an articulated vehicle with tractor unit height of 2 metres or less and trailer height of less than 3 metres” substitute “Mechanically propelled vehicle other than a vehicle in Class 1, having a maximum weight not exceeding 3.5 tonnes”.Signed by authority of the Secretary of State
Simon Connick
A Senior Civil Servant
in the Department for Transport
18th October 2021EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order makes an amendment to the River Tyne (Tunnels) (Revision of Tolls) Order 2021 to reflect the removal of toll-booths in the implementation of free-flow traffic through the tunnels. The Order replaces one aspect of the definitions of the classes of vehicle.
