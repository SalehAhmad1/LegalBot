SCHEDULES 
 
SCHEDULE 1 
 
ROUTE OF THE NEW TRUNK ROAD 
The route of the new trunk road is between Llanddewi 
Velfrey and Narberth Rural in the County of 
Pembrokeshire as follows: 
A length of the trunk road commencing at a point 
approximately 278 metres east of Bethel Chapel, in the 
Community of Llanddewi Velfrey (marked A on the 
deposited plan) and extending in a generally westerly 
direction for a distance of approximately 4.46 
kilometres to a point on the trunk road approximately 
190 metres west of the entrance to Penblewin Farm, in 
the Community of Narberth Rural (marked B on the 
deposited plan).
