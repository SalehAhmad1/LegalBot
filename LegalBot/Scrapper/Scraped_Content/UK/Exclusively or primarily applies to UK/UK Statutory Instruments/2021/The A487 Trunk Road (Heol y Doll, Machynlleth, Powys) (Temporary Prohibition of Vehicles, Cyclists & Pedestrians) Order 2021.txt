W E L S H  S T A T U T O R Y  
I N S T R U M E N T S  
2021 No. 1002 (W. 236) 
ROAD TRAFFIC, WALES 
The A487 Trunk Road (Heol y Doll, 
Machynlleth, Powys) (Temporary 
Prohibition of Vehicles, Cyclists & 
Pedestrians) Order 2021 
Made 
7 September 2021 
Coming into force 
14 September 2021 
The Welsh Ministers, as traffic authority for the A487 
trunk road, are satisfied that traffic on a specified 
length of the trunk road should be prohibited because 
of works proposed on or near the road.  
The Welsh Ministers, therefore, in exercise of the 
powers conferred upon them by section 14(1) and (4) 
of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(1), make this 
Order. 
Title and commencement  
1. The title of this Order is the A487 Trunk Road 
(Heol y Doll, Machynlleth, Powys) (Temporary 
Prohibition of Vehicles, Cyclists & Pedestrians) Order 
2021 and this Order comes into force on 14 September 
2021. 
Interpretation 
2. In this Order— 
“exempted vehicle” (“cerbyd esempt”) means any 
vehicle being used by the emergency services or in 
connection with the works giving rise to this 
Order; 
                                                                               
(1) 
1984 c.27; Section 14 was substituted by the Road Traffic 
(Temporary Restrictions) Act 1991 (c.26), section 1(1) and 
Schedule 1. By virtue of S.I. 1999/672, and section 162 of, 
and paragraph 30 of Schedule 11 to, the Government of 
Wales Act 2006 (c.32), these functions are now exercisable 
by the Welsh Ministers in relation to Wales. 
 
2
“the trunk road” (“y gefnffordd”) means the length 
of the A487 Fishguard to Bangor trunk road that 
extends from a point 20 metres south to a point 20 
metres north of the railway bridge at Heol y Doll, 
Machynlleth, Powys;  
“works period” (“cyfnod y gwaith”) means the 
period commencing at 23:00 hours on 14 
September 2021 and ending when the temporary 
traffic signs are permanently removed. 
Prohibitions 
3. No person may, during the works period, drive 
any vehicle, other than an exempted vehicle, on the 
length of the trunk road. 
4. No person may, during the works period, ride any 
pedal cycle on the length of the trunk road. 
5. No pedestrian may, during the works period, 
proceed on the length of the trunk road. 
Application 
6. The prohibitions in articles 3, 4 and 5 apply only 
during such times and to such extent as indicated by 
traffic signs.  
Duration of this Order 
7. The maximum duration of this Order is 18 
months.
