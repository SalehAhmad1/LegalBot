STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS
2021 No. 885
EDUCATION, WALES
The Education (Chief Inspector of Education and Training in Wales) (No. 2) Order 2021
Made
21st July 2021
Coming into force
1st January 2022
At the Court at Windsor Castle, the 21st day of July 2021
Present,
The Queen’s Most Excellent Majesty in Council
Her Majesty, in exercise of the powers conferred upon Her by section 19(1) of the Education Act 2005(1) is pleased, by and with the advice of Her Privy Council, to order as follows:
(1)
2005 c. 18.Citation and commencement
1.  This Order may be cited as the Education (Chief Inspector of Education and Training in Wales) (No. 2) Order 2021 and comes into force on 1st January 2022.Appointment of Her Majesty’s Chief Inspector of Education and Training in Wales or Prif Arolygydd Ei Mawrhydi dros Addysg a Hyfforddiant yng Nghymru
2.  Owen Evans is appointed as Her Majesty’s Chief Inspector of Education and Training in Wales or Prif Arolygydd Ei Mawrhydi dros Addysg a Hyfforddiant yng Nghymru for a period of 5 years commencing on 1st January 2022.Revocation
3.  The Education (Chief Inspector of Education and Training in Wales) Order 2021 is revoked(1).
(1)
S.I. 2021/803.Ceri King
Deputy Clerk of the Privy CouncilEXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order appoints Owen Evans as Her Majesty’s Inspector of Education and Training in Wales, or Prif Arolygydd Ei Mawrhydi dros Addysg a Hyfforddiant yng Nghymru for a period of 5 years commencing on 1st January 2022. The Education (Chief Inspector of Education and Training in Wales) Order 2021 is revoked.
