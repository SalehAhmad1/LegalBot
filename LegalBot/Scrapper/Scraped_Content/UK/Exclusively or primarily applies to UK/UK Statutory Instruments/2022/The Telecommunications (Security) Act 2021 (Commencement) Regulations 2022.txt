STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS
2022 No. 931 (C. 70)
TELECOMMUNICATIONS
The Telecommunications (Security) Act 2021 (Commencement) Regulations 2022
Made
1st September 2022
The Secretary of State, in exercise of the powers conferred by section 28(2) of the Telecommunications Security Act 2021(1), makes the following Regulations.
(1)
2021 c. 31.Citation and interpretation
1.—(1) These Regulations may be cited as the Telecommunications (Security) Act 2021 (Commencement) Regulations 2022.
(2) In these Regulations “the Act” means the Telecommunications (Security) Act 2021.Provisions coming into force on 1 October 2022
2.  The following provisions of the Act, so far as not already in force by virtue of section 28(1) of the Act, come into force on 1 October 2022–
(a)
Sections 1 to 3 (so far as not already in force by virtue of section 28(1) of the Act);
(b)
Sections 4 to 13;
(c)
Section 24 (so far as not already in force by virtue of section 28(1) of the Act); and
(d)
Section 25(2).Matt Warman
Minister of State
Department of Digital, Culture, Media and Sport
1st September 2022EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Regulations)
These Regulations bring into force those provisions of the Telecommunications (Security) Act 2021 (c. 31) which are not already in force. Certain provisions were brought into force on Royal Assent by virtue of section 28(1) of the Telecommunications (Security) Act 2021.
