The Secretary of State has previously decided that it was necessary in the public interest to restrict 
the flying at Farnborough Airport and Biggin Hill Airport by two types of aircraft. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Revocation) 
Regulations 2022 and they come into force with immediate effect.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations revoke the statutory instruments which imposed restrictions on the departure of 
specified aircraft types from Farnborough Airport (S.I. 2022/258) and Biggin Hill Airport (S.I. 
2022/340). These two regulations are no longer considered necessary and are revoked.
