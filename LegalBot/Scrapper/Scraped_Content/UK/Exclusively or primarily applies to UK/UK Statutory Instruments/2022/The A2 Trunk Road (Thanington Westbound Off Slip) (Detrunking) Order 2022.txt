HIGHWAYS, ENGLAND
The A2 Trunk Road (Thanington Westbound Off Slip) (Detrunking) Order 2022
Made
31st May 2022
Coming into force
23rd June 2022
The Secretary of State for Transport makes this Order in exercise of powers conferred by sections 10(2) and 12(3) of the Highways Act 1980(1) and now vested in the Secretary of State(2):-
In accordance with section 10(2) of that Act, the Secretary of State has taken into consideration the requirements of local and national planning, including the requirements of agriculture, as is satisfied as to expediency.
This Order is made in compliance with the provisions of Parts I and III of Schedule 1 of that Act.

31st May 2022Regulation 3
SCHEDULE
LENGTH OF THE TRUNK ROAD CEASING TO BE A TRUNK ROAD
The length of the trunk road ceasing to be a trunk road is shown by parallel hatching on the plan and is a length of the A2 Trunk Road Westbound Off Slip from a point 2 metres south of the channel line of the junction with the A28 carriageway, for a distance of 213 metres in a generally south easterly direction.EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order removes the trunk road status from the length of the A2 Trunk Road Westbound Off Slip described in the Schedule to the Order, it will become the A2 principal road with responsibility for it transferred to the local highway authority, Kent County Council.
The detrunked section of highway will provide access to the Thanington Park development, with a replacement Westbound Off Slip provided from the A2 Trunk Road.
