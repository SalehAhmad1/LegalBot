The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations, in exercise of the powers conferred by section 36(3) of the Skills and Post-16 Education Act 2022(1).
(1)
2022 c. 21.Citation and extent
1.—(1) These Regulations may be cited as the Skills and Post-16 Education Act 2022 (Commencement No. 2) (England) Regulations 2022.
(2) These Regulations extend to England and Wales.Provision coming into force on 1st January 2023
2.  Section 14 of the Skills and Post-16 Education Act 2022 (information about technical education and training: access to English schools) comes into force on 1st January 2023.Robert Halfon
Minister of State
Department for Education
14th December 2022EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Regulations)
These Regulations bring into force section 14 of the Skills and Post-16 Education Act 2022 (c. 21) (“the Act”), which increases the requirements on schools in England to provide access to providers of technical education, on 1st January 2023. They are the second commencement regulations to be made under the Act.NOTE AS TO EARLIER COMMENCEMENT REGULATIONS
(This note is not part of the Regulations)
