The Secretary of State has previously decided that it is in the public interest to restrict flying in the 
area specified in regulation 3 by reason of the intended holding of rehearsals of a large formation 
flypast of aircraft to commemorate the Platinum Jubilee of Her Majesty the Queen that are 
scheduled to take place there, and now considers it necessary in the public interest to amend those 
restrictions. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Platinum 
Jubilee Flypast Rehearsals) (Amendment) Regulations 2022 and they come into force on 24th 
May 2022. 
Interpretation 
2. In regulation 3(1) the times referred to are Co-ordinated Universal Time(b).

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations amend the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Platinum Jubilee Flypast 
Rehearsals) Regulations 2022 (S.I. 2022/386). They amend airspace levels and timings and 
withdraw areas previously scheduled for display practices. 
The times mentioned in regulations 3(2)(b) and 3(5)(a) are Co-ordinated Universal Time (UTC), 
which is one hour behind British Summer Time (BST). 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Notice to Aviation (NOTAM).
