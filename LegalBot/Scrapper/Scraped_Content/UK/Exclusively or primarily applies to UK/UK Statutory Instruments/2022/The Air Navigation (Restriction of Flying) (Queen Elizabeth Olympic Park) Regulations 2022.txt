article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a).

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations impose restrictions on the flying of unmanned aircraft in the vicinity of Queen 
Elizabeth Olympic Park, London during the holding of an International Investment Summit that is 
scheduled to take place there on 16th May 2022. In view of the high visibility of this event and at 
the request of the Home Office, the Civil Aviation Authority and Department for Transport agree 
that the flying of unmanned aircraft should be restricted for reasons of safety and security. 
The times mentioned in regulation 3(1) are Co-ordinated Universal Time (UTC) which is one hour 
behind British Summer Time (BST). 
In relation to the permission mentioned in regulation 3(2), the Home Office may be contacted as 
notified. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Notice to Aviation (NOTAM).
