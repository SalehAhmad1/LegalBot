20 January 2022EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order amends the Government of Wales Act 2006 (Budget Motions and Designated Bodies) Order 2018 (“the 2018 Order”) by removing the body North East Property LP from the list of designated bodies contained within the Schedule to that Order, which had been inserted in error by the Government of Wales Act 2006 (Budget Motions and Designated Bodies) (Amendment) Order 2021 (“the 2021 Order”) (S.I. 2021/1345 (W. 347)).
This Order also inserts the body North East Property (GP) Limited into the Schedule to the 2018 Order. The effect of this amendment is that the information relating to resources expected to be used by this body may be included within a Budget motion.
The Welsh Ministers’ Code of Practice on the carrying out of Regulatory Impact Assessments was considered in relation to this Order. As a result, it was not considered necessary to carry out a regulatory impact assessment as to the likely costs and benefits of complying with this Order.
