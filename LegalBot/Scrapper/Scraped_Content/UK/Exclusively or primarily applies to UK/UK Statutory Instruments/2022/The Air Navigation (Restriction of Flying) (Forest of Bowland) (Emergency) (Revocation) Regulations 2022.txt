The Secretary of State had previously decided that it was necessary in the public interest to restrict 
flying in the vicinity of the Forest of Bowland by reason of an emergency having arisen in that 
area. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and Commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Forest of 
Bowland) (Emergency) (Revocation) Regulations 2022 and they come into force with immediate 
effect. 
Revocation of Regulations 
2. The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Forest of Bowland) (Emergency) Regulations 
2022(b) are revoked.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations revoke the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Forest of Bowland) 
(Emergency) Regulations 2022 (S.I. 2022/490) which imposed restrictions by reason of an 
emergency having arisen in that area. Those Regulations are no longer considered necessary.
