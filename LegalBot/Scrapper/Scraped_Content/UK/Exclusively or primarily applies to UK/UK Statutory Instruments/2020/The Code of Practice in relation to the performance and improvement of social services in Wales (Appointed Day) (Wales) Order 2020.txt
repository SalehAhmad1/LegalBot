WELSH STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS
2020 No. 328 (W. 75)
SOCIAL CARE, WALES
The Code of Practice in relation to the performance and improvement of social services in Wales (Appointed Day) (Wales) Order 2020
Made
20 March 2020
The Welsh Ministers, in exercise of the powers conferred by section 146(4)(b) of the Social Services and Well-being (Wales) Act 2014(1) (“the 2014 Act”), make the following Order.
The Welsh Minsters, as required by section 146(1) of the 2014 Act, have consulted such persons as they think fit on a draft of the Code of Practice in relation to the performance and improvement of social services in Wales.
In accordance with section 146(2) of the 2014 Act, a draft of the Code of Practice in relation to performance and improvement of social services in Wales was laid before the National Assembly for Wales on 11 February 2020.
In accordance with section 146(3) of the 2014 Act, within a period of 40 days the National Assembly for Wales did not resolve not to approve the draft Code of Practice in relation to performance and improvement of social services in Wales, and the code was issued in the form of the draft.

20 March 2020EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order appoints 1 April 2020 as the day on which the Code of Practice in relation to the performance and improvement of social services in Wales comes into force (“the Code”).
The Code is issued by the Welsh Ministers under section 145(1) of the Social Services and Well-being (Wales) Act 2014 (“the 2014 Act”).
Section 145(3) of the 2014 Act requires local authorities in Wales to act in accordance with any relevant requirements contained in a code issued under section 145(1) and to have regard to any relevant guidelines contained in it.
The Code replaces the Code of Practice in relation to measuring social services performance issued in April 2016.
The Code can be accessed on the internet at https://gov.wales/.
