PARLIAMENT
House of Commons Members’ Fund Resolution 2020
Resolution of the House of Commons, dated 7th September 2020, passed in pursuance of the House of Commons Members’ Fund Act 2016(1).
(1)
2016 c. 18.Resolved, That the sum to be deducted from the salaries of Members of the House of Commons in pursuance of section 4(1) and (2) of the House of Commons Members’ Fund Act 2016 shall continue to be £2 per month.
