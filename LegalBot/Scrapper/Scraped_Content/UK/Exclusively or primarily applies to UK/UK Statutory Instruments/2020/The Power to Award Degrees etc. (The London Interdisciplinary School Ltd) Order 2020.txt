EDUCATION, ENGLAND
The Power to Award Degrees etc. (The London Interdisciplinary School Ltd) Order 2020
Made
18th December 2020
Coming into force
27th September 2021
The Office for Students, in exercise of the powers conferred by section 42(1) of the Higher Education and Research Act 2017(1), makes the following Order:
(1)
2017 c.29.Citation and commencement
1.  This Order may be cited as the Power to Award Degrees etc. (The London Interdisciplinary School Ltd) Order 2020 and shall come into force on 27th September 2021.
Commencement Information
I1
Art. 1 in force at 27.9.2021, see art. 1Competence to grant awards

(1) MASc in Interdisciplinary Practice;
(2) PGDip in Interdisciplinary Practice;
(3) PGCert in Interdisciplinary Practice;
(4) BASc (Hons) in Interdisciplinary Problems and Methods;
(5) BASc in Interdisciplinary Problems and Methods;

(7) CertHE in Interdisciplinary Problems and Methods;
(8) MASc in Interdisciplinary Problems and Methods;

For the Office for StudentsEXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order, made under section 42(1) of the Higher Education and Research Act 2017 authorises The London Interdisciplinary School Ltd with the power to award specified taught awards for a fixed term expiring on 31st December 2024. This Order will come into force on 27th September 2021.
An impact assessment has not been produced for this instrument as it has no impact on businesses and civil society organisations. The instrument has no impact on the public sector.
