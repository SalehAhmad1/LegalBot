The Secretary of State has decided that it is in the public interest to restrict flying in the vicinity of 
Old Warden Aerodrome, Bedfordshire, by reason of the intended holding of flying displays that 
are scheduled to take place there. 
The Secretary of State makes these Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by article 239 
of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Old Warden) 
(Amendment) Regulations 2020 and come into force on 3rd May 2020. 
Amendment 
2.—(1) The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Old Warden) Regulations 2020(b) are 
amended as follows. 
(2) In regulation 3(1)(a) for “whose centre is at 520512N 0001917W, between 520225N 
0002056W, and 5202225N 0001553W” substitute “whose centre is at 520512N 0001907W 
between 520225N 0002056W, and 520258N 0001553W”. 
(3) In regulation 3(2)(b) for “between 1545 hours and 2020 hours” substitute “between 1615 
hours and 2020 hours”. 
(4) In regulation 3(2)(c) for “between 1545 hours and 2055 hours” substitute “between 1615 
hours and 2055 hours”. 
(5) In regulation 3(2)(d) for “between 1545 hours and 2050 hours” substitute “between 1615 
hours and 2040 hours”.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations amend the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Old Warden) Regulations 
2020 (S.I. 2020/63) (“the principal Regulations”). The amendment amends the coordinates of the 
restrictions at Old Warden Aerodrome, for the flying displays that are scheduled to take place 
there between 3rd May and 4th October 2020. This amendment also amends the times of the 
flying displays taking place at Old Warden on 16th May, 20th June and 18th July 2020.
