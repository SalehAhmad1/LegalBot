The Welsh Ministers, as traffic authority for the A458 
trunk road, in exercise of the powers conferred upon 
them by section 84(1)(c), (1A) and (2) of the Road 
Traffic Regulation Act 1984(1) and after consultation 
with the Chief Officer of Dyfed-Powys Police, make 
this Order. 
Title, Commencement and Interpretation  
1. The title of this Order is the A458 Trunk Road 
(Llangadfan, Powys) (40 mph & Part-time 20 mph 
Speed Limits) Order 2020 and it comes into force on 
10 August 2020.

Restrictions 
3. No person may drive any motor vehicle, other 
than an exempted vehicle, at a speed exceeding 20 
miles per hour on the length of the trunk road 
described in Schedule 1 to this Order. 
4. No person may drive any motor vehicle, other 
than an exempted vehicle, at a speed exceeding 40 
miles per hour on the length of the trunk road 
described in Schedule 2 to this Order. 
Application 
5. The restriction in article 3 applies only during 
such times and to such extent as indicated by traffic 
signs.

SCHEDULE 1 
Part-time 20 mph Speed Limit   
 
The length of the trunk road at Llangadfan, Powys that 
extends from a point 57 metres south-east of the 
centre-point of its junction with the U2090 leading to 
Maes y Ddawns to a point 215 metres north-west of 
the centre-point of that junction.  
 
SCHEDULE 2 
40 mph Speed Limit 
 
The length of the trunk road at Llangadfan, Powys that 
extends from a point 213 metres south-east of the 
centre-point of its junction with the B4395 to a point 
385 metres north-west of the centre-point of its 
junction with the U2090 leading to Maes y Ddawns.
