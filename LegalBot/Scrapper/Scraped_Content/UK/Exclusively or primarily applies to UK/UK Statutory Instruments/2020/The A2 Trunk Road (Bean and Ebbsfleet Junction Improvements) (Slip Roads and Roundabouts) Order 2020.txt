STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS
2020 No. 564
HIGHWAYS, ENGLAND
The A2 Trunk Road (Bean and Ebbsfleet Junction Improvements) (Slip Roads and Roundabouts) Order 2020
Made
27th May 2020
Coming into force
11th June 2020
The Secretary of State for Transport makes this Order in exercise of powers conferred by sections 10(2)(a)(i), (3) and (3A) of the Highways Act 1980(1) and now vested in him(2):
In accordance with section 10(2) of that Act, the Secretary of State has taken into consideration the requirements of local and national planning, including the requirements of agriculture, and is satisfied as to expediency.
This Order is made in compliance with the provisions of Parts I and III of Schedule 1 to that Act.

SCHEDULE
ROUTES OF THE SLIP ROADS AND ROUNDABOUT EXTENSION
The lengths of new highways becoming trunk roads are:
(1) Bean Junction:
1.  A A2 Trunk Road eastbound entry slip road from the B255 Bean North Roundabout carriageway as proposed to be modified by Highways England to the existing A2 Trunk Road eastbound carriageway. The entry slip road (A-B) along this route being given the number 1 on site plan 1.
2.  A A2 Trunk Road westbound entry slip road from the B255 Bean South Roundabout carriageway as proposed to be modified by Highways England to the existing A2 Trunk Road westbound carriageway. The entry slip road (C-D) along this route being given the number 2 on site plan 1.
3.  A A2 Trunk Road westbound exit slip road from the existing A2 Trunk Road westbound exit slip carriageway to the B255 Bean South Roundabout carriageway as proposed to be modified by Highways England. The exit slip road (E-F) along this route being given the number 3 on site plan 1.
(2) Ebbsfleet Junction:
1.  A A2 Trunk Road eastbound exit slip road from the existing A2 Trunk Road eastbound exit slip carriageway to the Ebbsfleet West Roundabout to Ebbsfleet East Roundabout connector road carriageway as proposed to be modified by Highways England. The exit slip road (G-H) along this route being given the number 4 on site plan 2.
2.  A A2 Trunk Road roundabout extension to the Ebbsfleet East Roundabout carriageway as proposed to be modified by Highways England. The partial roundabout extension (J-K) along this route being given the number 5 on site plan 2.
3.  A A2 Trunk Road eastbound entry slip road from the Ebbsfleet East Roundabout carriageway as proposed to be modified by Highways England (as (2)2. above) to the existing A2 Trunk Road eastbound carriageway. The entry slip road (L-M) along this route being given the number 6 on site plan 2.EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order provides that 5 new slip roads and 1 modified roundabout to connect the A2 Trunk Road to the B255 Bean North and B255 Bean South Roundabouts and Ebbsfleet East Roundabout shall become trunk roads from the date on which this Order comes into force and that Highways England, as a strategic highways company, shall become the highway authority for those trunk roads from that date.
