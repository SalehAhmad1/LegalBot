2020 No. 84 (W. 12) 
ROAD TRAFFIC, WALES 
The M4 Motorway (Junction 23, 
Rogiet to the Wales/England 
Border, Monmouthshire) 
(Temporary Prohibition of Vehicles) 
Order 2020 
Made 
7 January 2020 
Coming into force 
13 January 2020 
The Welsh Ministers, as traffic authority for the 
relevant length of the M4 Motorway, are satisfied that 
traffic on a specified length of the motorway should be 
prohibited because of works proposed on or near the 
motorway. 
 The Welsh Ministers, therefore, in exercise of the 
powers conferred upon them by section 14(1) and (4) 
of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(1), make this 
Order. 
Title, Commencement and Interpretation 
1. The title of this Order is the M4 Motorway 
(Junction 23, Rogiet to the Wales/England Border, 
Monmouthshire) (Temporary Prohibition of Vehicles) 
Order 2020. 
2. In this Order— 
“the motorway” (“y draffordd”) means the length 
of the eastbound carriageway of the M4 Motorway 
which extends from the tip of the nosing of the 
eastbound exit slip road onto the M48 at Junction 
23, Rogiet to the Wales/England border; 
                                                                               
(1) 1984 c.27; section 14 was substituted by the Road Traffic   
(Temporary Restrictions) Act 1991 (c.26), section 1(1) and 
Schedule 1.  By virtue of S.I. 1999/672, and section 162 of, and 
paragraph 30 of Schedule 11 to, the Government of Wales Act 
2006(c.32), these powers are now exercisable by the Welsh 
Ministers in relation to Wales. 
 
 
2
“works period” (“cyfnod y gwaith”) means the 
period commencing at 20:00 hours on 13 January 
2020 and ending when the temporary traffic signs 
are permanently removed. 
Prohibition 
3. No person may, during the works period, drive 
any vehicle on the length of the motorway.  
Application 
4. The prohibition in article 3 applies only during 
such times and to such extent as indicated by traffic 
signs.  
Duration of this Order 
5. The maximum duration for this Order is 18 
months. 
 
Signed under authority of the Minister for Economy 
and Transport, one of the Welsh Ministers.
