12th June 2020EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Regulations)
Regulation 52 of the Occupational and Personal Pension Schemes (Automatic Enrolment) Regulations 2010 applies employer duties relating to personal pensions contained in Part 1 of the Pensions Act 2008, and corresponding provision in Northern Ireland, to persons employed or engaged in any capacity on board a ship.
These Regulations revoke a sunset clause which would mean that regulation 52 would otherwise cease to have effect on 1st July 2020.
A full impact assessment of the effect that this instrument will have on the costs of business and the voluntary sector is available at www.legislation.gov.uk.
