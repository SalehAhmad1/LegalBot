EDUCATION, ENGLAND
The Power to Award Degrees etc. (BPP University Limited) Order of Council 2013 (Amendment) Order 2020
Made
17th August 2020
Coming into force
1st September 2020
The Office for Students, in exercise of the powers conferred by section 45(1) of the Higher Education and Research Act 2017(1), makes the following Order:
(1)
2017 c.29.Citation and commencement
1.  This Order may be cited as the Power to Award Degrees etc. (BPP University Limited) Order of Council 2013 (Amendment) Order 2020 and shall come into force on 1st September 2020.Variation of Power to Award Degrees etc. (BPP University Limited) Order of Council 2013
2.—(1) The Power to Award Degrees etc. (BPP University Limited) Order of Council 2013 is varied in accordance with paragraph (2).
(2) For paragraph 2, substitute—
“2.  BPP University Limited shall be competent to grant awards of the kind mentioned in paragraph (a) of section 76(2) of the said Act.”Susan Lapworth
Director of Competition and Registration
For the Office for Students
17th August 2020EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order, made under section 45(1) of the Higher Education and Research Act 2017, provides for a variation of the Power to Award Degrees etc. (BPP University Limited) Order of Council 2013, so that BPP University Limited’s existing Degree Awarding Powers authorisation remain valid for an indefinite period. This Order will come into force on 1st September 2020.
An impact assessment has not been produced for this instrument as it has no impact on businesses and civil society organisations. The instrument has no impact on the public sector.
