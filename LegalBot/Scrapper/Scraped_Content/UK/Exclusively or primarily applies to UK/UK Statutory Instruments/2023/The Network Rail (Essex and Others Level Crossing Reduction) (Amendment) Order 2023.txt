10th May 2023EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order amends the Network Rail (Essex and Others Level Crossing Reduction) Order 2022 which conferred powers on Network Rail to close or downgrade the level crossings specified in Schedule 2 and for the permanent acquisition of and temporary use of land in connection with the construction of public rights of way and other works in the highway required in connection with the level crossing changes. This Order clarifies the extent of stopping up of Footpath EX/213/24 (Boreham) (Schedule 2) and removes Plot 32 from Schedule 5 which is no longer needed for access.
