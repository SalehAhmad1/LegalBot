STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS
2023 No. 699
NATIONAL HEALTH SERVICE, ENGLAND
The Southport and Ormskirk Hospital National Health Service Trust (Dissolution) Order 2023
Made
23rd June 2023
Coming into force
1st July 2023
NHS England with the approval of the Secretary of State for Health and Social Care, makes the following Order in exercise of the powers conferred by section 272(8) of, and paragraph 28 of Schedule 4 to, the National Health Service Act 2006(1).
NHS England makes this Order on the application of the Southport and Ormskirk Hospital National Health Service Trust following the consultation required under paragraph 28(3) of Schedule 4 to that Act(2).
(1)
2006 c. 41. Paragraph 28 was amended by section 18(10) of the Health Act 2009 (c. 21) and section 58(5)(a) of the Health and Care Act 2022 (c. 31).
(2)
See regulation 2(5) of the National Health Service Trusts (Consultation on Establishment and Dissolution) Regulations 2010 (S.I. 2010/743). Regulation 2(5) was amended by S.I. 2013/325.Citation and commencement
1.  This Order may be cited as the Southport and Ormskirk Hospital National Health Service Trust (Dissolution) Order 2023 and comes into force on 1st July 2023.Dissolution
2.  The Southport and Ormskirk Hospital National Health Service Trust is dissolved.Revocation
3.  The Southport and Ormskirk Hospital National Health Service Trust (Establishment) Order 1999(1) is revoked.

23rd June 2023EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
Article 2 of this Order provides for the dissolution on 1 July 2023 of the Southport and Ormskirk Hospital National Health Service Trust (“the Southport and Ormskirk Trust”) which was established on 1 April 1999.
Article 3 revokes the Southport and Ormskirk Hospital National Health Service Trust (Establishment) Order 1999 (S.I. 1999/980), which established the Southport and Ormskirk Trust.
An impact assessment has not been produced for this instrument as no, or no significant, impact on the public, private or voluntary sector is foreseen.
