30th January 2023EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Regulations)
These Regulations bring into force provisions of the Northern Ireland (Executive Formation etc) Act 2022 (c. 48) (“the 2022 Act”).
Regulation 2 brings into force sections 6 to 9 of the 2022 Act on 7th February 2023. Sections 6 to 9 contain provisions allowing appointments to be made to specified public bodies during the current period without a Northern Ireland Executive or Ministers.
A full impact assessment has not been produced for this instrument as no, or no significant, impact on the private, voluntary or public sectors is foreseen.
