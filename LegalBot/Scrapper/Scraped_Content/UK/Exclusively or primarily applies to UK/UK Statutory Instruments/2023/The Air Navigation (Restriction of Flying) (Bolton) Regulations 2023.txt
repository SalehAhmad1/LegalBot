article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a).

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations impose restrictions on the flying of unmanned aircraft in the vicinity of Bolton, 
Greater Manchester due to an intended Royal visit that is scheduled to take place there on 20th 
January 2023. In view of the high visibility of this visit and at the request of the Greater 
Manchester Police Service, the Civil Aviation Authority and the Department for Transport agree 
that the flying of unmanned aircraft should be restricted in the vicinity of the event for reasons of 
public safety and security. 
The times mentioned in regulation 3(1) are Co-ordinated Universal Time (UTC) which is the same 
as Greenwich Mean Time. 
In relation to the permission mentioned in regulation 3(2), the Greater Manchester Police Service 
may be contacted as notified. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Briefing Sheet and Notice to 
Aviation (NOTAM).
