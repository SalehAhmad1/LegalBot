The Secretary of State has decided that it is necessary in the public interest to amend the specific 
timings and the area of the flying restrictions in the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) 
(London, Bletchley and Stansted) Regulations 2023(a). 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(b). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (London, 
Bletchley and Stansted) (Amendment) Regulations 2023 and they come into force on 2nd 
November 2023. 
Amendment of the Regulations 
2.—(1) The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (London, Bletchley and Stansted) 
Regulations 2023 are amended as follows. 
(2) In regulation 3— 
(a) in paragraph (3), for “1500 hours and 1600 hours” substitute “1645 hours and 1745 
hours”, 
(b) in paragraph (5), for “1600 hours” substitute “1730 hours”, and 
(c) in paragraph (7) for— 
(i) “1515 hours and 1630 hours” substitute “1700 hours and 1900 hours”, and 
(ii) “2,500 feet” substitute “3,500 feet”.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations amend the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (London, Bletchley and 
Stansted) Regulations 2023 (S.I. 2023/1136) by amending the specific times and the area of the 
restrictions. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Notice to Aviation (NOTAM).
