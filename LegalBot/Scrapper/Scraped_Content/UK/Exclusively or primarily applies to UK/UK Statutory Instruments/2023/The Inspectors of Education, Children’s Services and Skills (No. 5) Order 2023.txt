(1)
2006 c. 40.Citation and commencement
1.  This Order may be cited as the Inspectors of Education, Children’s Services and Skills (No. 5) Order 2023 and comes into force on 14th December 2023.Appointment of His Majesty’s Inspector of Education, Children’s Services and Skills
2.  The person named in the Schedule to this Order is appointed as His Majesty’s Inspector of Education, Children’s Services and Skills.Ceri King
