The Welsh Ministers make the following Order in exercise of the power conferred by section 29(1) of the Apprenticeships, Skills, Children and Learning Act 2009(1).
(1)
2009 c. 22.Title and coming into force
1.  The title of this Order is the Apprenticeships (Specification of Apprenticeship Standards for Wales) (Modification) (Revocation) Order 2023 and it comes into force on 9 December 2023.Revocation
2.  The Apprenticeships (Specification of Apprenticeship Standards for Wales) (Modification) Order 2023(1) is revoked.
