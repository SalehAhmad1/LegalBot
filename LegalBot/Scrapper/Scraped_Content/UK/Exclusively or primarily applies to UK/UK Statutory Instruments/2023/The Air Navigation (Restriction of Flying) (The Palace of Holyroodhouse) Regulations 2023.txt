The Secretary of State has decided that it is necessary in the public interest to restrict flying in the 
area specified in regulation 3(1) by reason of the intended holding there of events associated with 
Royal Week. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (The Palace of 
Holyroodhouse) Regulations 2023 and they come into force on 3rd July 2023.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations impose restrictions on flying in the vicinity of the Palace of Holyroodhouse 
during the holding of events associated with Royal Week that are scheduled to take place there 
between 3rd July 2023 and 7th July 2023 (inclusive). 
The Civil Aviation Authority and Department for Transport agree that flying should be restricted 
in the vicinity of the Palace for reasons of public safety and security. 
The times mentioned in regulation 3(1) are Co-ordinated Universal Time (UTC) which is one hour 
behind British Summer Time (BST). 
In relation to the permission mentioned in regulation 3(2), the Police Scotland Air Aviation and 
Security Unit may be contacted as notified. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Aeronautical Information Circular 
(AIC) and Notice to Aviation (NOTAM).
