The Secretary of State has decided that it is necessary in the public interest to restrict the flying of 
unmanned aircraft in the areas specified in regulation 3(1) by reason of the intended holding there 
of a multi-agency exercise. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Derbyshire) 
Regulations 2023 and they come into force on 17th October 2023. 
Interpretation 
2. In regulation 3(1) the times referred to are Co-ordinated Universal Time(b). 
Restricted airspace 
3.—(1) Subject to paragraph (2), no unmanned aircraft is to fly below— 
(a) 1,500 feet above mean sea level between 0700 hours on the 17th October 2023 and 1400 
hours on the 19th October 2023 within the area bounded by a circle having a radius of 0.7 
nautical miles centred at 530348N 0012348W, 
(b) 1,400 feet above mean sea level between 1600 hours on the 17th October 2023 and 0600 
hours on the 18th October 2023 within the area bounded by a circle having a radius of 0.5 
nautical miles centred at 531513N 0012533W, 
(c) 2,100 feet above mean sea level between 0700 hours on the 18th October 2023 and 1400 
hours on the 19th October 2023 within the area bounded by a circle having a radius of 0.5 
nautical miles centred at 530704N 0013356W, and 
(d) 1,500 feet above mean sea level between 0700 hours and 1400 hours on the 19th October 
2023 within the area bounded by a circle having a radius of 0.7 nautical miles centred at 
530329N 0012348W.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations impose restrictions on the flying of unmanned aircraft in the vicinity of four 
locations within Derbyshire due to a multi-agency exercise that is scheduled to take place there 
between 17th October 2023 and 19th October 2023 (inclusive). 
In view of the nature of the exercise and at the request of the Derbyshire Constabulary, the Civil 
Aviation Authority and the Department for Transport agree that the flying of unmanned aircraft 
should be restricted in the vicinity of the exercise areas for reasons of public safety and security. 
The times mentioned in regulation 3(1) are Co-ordinated Universal Time (UTC) which is one hour 
behind British Summer Time (BST). 
In relation to the permission mentioned in regulation 3(2), the Derbyshire Constabulary may be 
contacted as notified. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Aeronautical Information Circular 
(AIC) and Notice to Aviation (NOTAM).
