The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Cambridge) 
Regulations 2023 and they come into force on 9th May 2023.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations impose restrictions on the flying of unmanned aircraft in the vicinity of 
Cambridge City Centre during a VIP event that is scheduled to take place there on 9th May 2023. 
In view of the high visibility of this event and at the request of the Cambridgeshire Constabulary, 
the Civil Aviation Authority and the Department for Transport agree that the flying of unmanned 
aircraft should be restricted in the vicinity of the event for reasons of public safety and security. 
The times mentioned in regulation 3(1) are Co-ordinated Universal Time (UTC) which is one hour 
behind British Summer Time (BST). 
In relation to the permission mentioned in regulation 3(2), Cambridgeshire Constabulary may be 
contacted on telephone number 07850 139559. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Briefing Sheet and Notice to 
Aviation (NOTAM).
