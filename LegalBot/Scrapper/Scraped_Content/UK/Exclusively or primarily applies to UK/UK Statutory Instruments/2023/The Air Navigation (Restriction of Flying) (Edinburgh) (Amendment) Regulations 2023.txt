The Secretary of State has decided that it is necessary in the public interest to amend the specific 
timings of the flying restrictions in the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Edinburgh) 
Regulations 2023(a). 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(b). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Edinburgh) 
(Amendment) Regulations 2023 and they come into force on 29th December 2023. 
Amendment of the Regulations 
2.—(1) The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Edinburgh) Regulations 2023 are amended 
as follows. 
(2) In regulation 3(1), for “2130 hours” substitute “2300 hours”.
