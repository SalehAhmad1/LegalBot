22 May 2024EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Regulations)
These Regulations bring sections 1 and 5(a) and (c) of the Animal Welfare (Livestock Exports) Act 2024 (“the Act”) into force in relation to Wales on 22 July 2024.
All other provisions of the Act in relation to Wales came into force on 20 May 2024 (the date of Royal Assent) in accordance with section 7(3) of the Act.
