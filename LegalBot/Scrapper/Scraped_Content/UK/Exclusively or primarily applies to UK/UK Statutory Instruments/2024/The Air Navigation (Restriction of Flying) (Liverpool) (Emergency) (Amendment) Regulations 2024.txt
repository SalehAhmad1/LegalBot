Coming into force with immediate effect 
The Secretary of State has deemed it necessary in the public interest to amend the restrictions on 
flying specified in the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Liverpool) (Emergency) 
Regulations 2024(a) (“the principal Regulations”). 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(b). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Liverpool) 
(Emergency) (Amendment) Regulations 2024 and come into force with immediate effect. 
Restricted airspace 
2.—(1) The principal Regulations are amended as follows. 
(2) In the Schedule, column 2, for “1.5 nautical miles” substitute “2 nautical miles”.
