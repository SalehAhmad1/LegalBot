The Secretary of State has decided that it is necessary in the public interest to restrict flying in the 
area specified in regulation 3(1) by reason of the intended holding of flying displays there. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (The Victory 
Show, Cosby) Regulations 2024 and they come into force on 30th August 2024. 
Interpretation 
2. In regulation 3(2) the times referred to are Co-ordinated Universal Time(b). 
Restricted airspace 
3.—(1) Subject to paragraph (3), during the periods specified in paragraph (2) no aircraft is to 
fly below 3,700 feet above mean see level within the area bounded by a circle having a radius of 
3.5 nautical miles whose centre is at 523314N 0011234W. 
(2) The periods referred to in paragraph (1) are— 
(a) between 1100 hours and 2000 hours on 30th August 2024, 
(b) between 0730 hours and 2000 hours on 31st August 2024, and 
(c) between 0730 hours and 1700 hours on 1st September 2024. 
(3) Paragraph (1) does not apply— 
(a) to any aircraft flying in accordance with a permission issued by the Victory Show flying 
display director, 
(b) insofar as flying is already prohibited by the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) 
(Prisons and Young Offender Institutions) (England and Wales) Regulations 2023(c).

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations impose restrictions on flying in the vicinity of the Victory Show, Cosby, 
Leicestershire, during flying displays for the public that are scheduled to take place there from 
30th August 2024 to 1st September 2024, inclusive. 
Many aircraft will be involved in high energy manoeuvres during the displays and therefore the 
Civil Aviation Authority and the Department for Transport agree that flying should be restricted in 
the vicinity of the event for reasons of public safety. 
These Regulations apply except insofar as flying is already prohibited by the Air Navigation 
(Restriction of Flying) (Prisons and Young Offender Institutions) (England and Wales) 
Regulations 2023 (S.I. 2023/1101). 
The times mentioned in regulation 3(2) are Co-ordinated Universal Time (UTC) which is one hour 
behind British Summer Time (BST). 
In relation to the permission mentioned in regulation 3(3), the Victory Show flying display 
director may be contacted as notified. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Aeronautical Information Circular 
(AIC) and Notice to Aviation (NOTAM).
