20th May 2024EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order amends the Immigration (Leave to Enter and Remain) Order 2000 (“the 2000 Order”).
Article 2 amends article 13(4)(za) of the 2000 Order to remove references to “unlimited” leave granted under Appendix EU to the Immigration Rules. This means that leave granted under Appendix EU will not lapse unless the holder stays outside the United Kingdom and Islands for the period stipulated in article 13(4)(za) of the 2000 Order.
A full impact assessment has not been produced for this instrument as no, or no significant, impact on the private, voluntary or public sector is foreseen.
