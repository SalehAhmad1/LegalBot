The Secretary of State has decided that it is necessary in the public interest to amend the times of 
the restricted area in the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Silverstone and Turweston) 
Regulations 2024(a). 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(b). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Silverstone and 
Turweston) (Amendment) Regulations 2024 and they come into force on 4th July 2024. 
Amendment of the Regulations 
2.—(1) The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Silverstone and Turweston) Regulations 
2024 are amended as follows. 
(2) In regulation 3(2)— 
(a) in sub-paragraph (a), for “0800 hours and 1800 hours” substitute “0900 hours and 1900 
hours”, 
(b) in sub-paragraph (b), for “0700 hours and 1800 hours” substitute “0800 hours and 1900 
hours”, and 
(c) in sub-paragraph (c), for “0700 hours and 1900 hours” substitute “0800 hours and 2000 
hours”.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations amend the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Silverstone and Turweston) 
Regulations 2024 (S.I. 2024/489) by amending the periods specified in paragraph (3)(2) of the 
restricted area. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Aeronautical Information Circular 
(AIC) and Notice to Aviation (NOTAM).
