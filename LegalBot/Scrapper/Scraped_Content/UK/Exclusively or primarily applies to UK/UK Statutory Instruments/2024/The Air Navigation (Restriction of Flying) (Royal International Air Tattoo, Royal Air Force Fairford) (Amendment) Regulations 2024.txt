The Secretary of State has decided that it is necessary in the public interest to amend the 
coordinates for the flying restrictions in the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Royal 
International Air Tattoo, Royal Air Force Fairford) Regulations 2024(a). 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(b). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Royal 
International Air Tattoo, Royal Air Force Fairford) (Amendment) Regulations 2024 and they 
come into force on 17th July 2024. 
Amendment of the Regulations 
2.—(1) The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Royal International Air Tattoo, Royal Air 
Force Fairford) Regulations 2024 are amended as follows. 
(2) In regulation 3(4)— 
(a) in sub-paragraph (a), for “514343N 0015711W” substitute “514510N 0015644W”, 
(b) in sub-paragraph (b), for “514410N 0013753W” substitute “514831N 0013817W”, 
(c) in sub-paragraph (c), for “513732N 0013728W” substitute “513736N 0013723W”, 
(d) in sub-paragraph (d), for “513747N 0015652W” substitute “513745N 0015644W”, and 
(e) in sub-paragraph (e), for “514343N 0015711W” substitute “514510N 0015644W”.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations amend the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Royal International Air 
Tattoo, Royal Air Force Fairford) Regulations 2024 (S.I. 2024/561) by amending the co-ordinates 
of the restricted area on 22nd July 2024. 
Full details of the Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by a Briefing Sheet and a Notice to 
Aviation (NOTAM).
