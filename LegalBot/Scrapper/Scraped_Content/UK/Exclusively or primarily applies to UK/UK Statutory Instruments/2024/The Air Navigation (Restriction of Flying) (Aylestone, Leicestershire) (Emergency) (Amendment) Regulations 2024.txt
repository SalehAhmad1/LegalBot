Coming into force with immediate effect 
The Secretary of State for a reason affecting the public interest deems it necessary to amend the 
Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Aylestone, Leicestershire) (Emergency) Regulations 
2024(a) (“the principal Regulations”). 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(b). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Aylestone, 
Leicestershire) (Emergency) (Amendment) Regulations 2024 and they come into force with 
immediate effect. 
Restricted airspace 
2. (1) The principal Regulations are amended as follows. 
(2) In the Schedule, column 3, for “523612N 0010943W” substitute “523632N 0010901W”.
