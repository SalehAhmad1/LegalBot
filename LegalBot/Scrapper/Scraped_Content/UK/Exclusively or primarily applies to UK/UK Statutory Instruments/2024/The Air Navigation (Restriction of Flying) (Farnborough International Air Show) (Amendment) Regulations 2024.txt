The Secretary of State has decided that it is necessary in the public interest to amend the height 
applicable to the exemption where the flying restrictions do not apply within the Blackbushe 
Airport Local Flying Area in the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Farnborough 
International Air Show) Regulations 2024(a). 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(b). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Farnborough 
International Air Show) (Amendment) Regulations 2024 and they come into force on 15th July 
2024. 
Amendment of the Regulations 
2.—(1) The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Farnborough International Air Show) 
Regulations 2024 are amended as follows. 
(2) In regulation 3(3)(c) for “mean sea level” substitute “Blackbushe Aerodrome Level”.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations amend the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Farnborough International 
Air Show) Regulations 2024 (S.I. 2024/480) by amending the height applicable to the exemption 
where the flying restrictions do not apply whilst an aircraft is in communication with the Air 
Traffic Service Unit at Blackbushe Airport.
