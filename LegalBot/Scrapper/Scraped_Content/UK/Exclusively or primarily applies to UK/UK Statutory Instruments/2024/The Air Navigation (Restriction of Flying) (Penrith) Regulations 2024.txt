The Secretary of State has decided that it is necessary in the public interest to restrict the flying of 
unmanned aircraft in the area specified in regulation 3(1) by reason of the intended gathering there 
of a large number of persons. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Penrith) 
Regulations 2024 and they come into force on 31st July 2024. 
Interpretation 
2. In regulation 3(1) the times referred to are Co-ordinated Universal Time(b). 
Restricted airspace 
3.—(1) Subject to paragraph (2), between 2300 hours on 31st July 2024 and 1100 hours on 5th 
August 2024 no unmanned aircraft is to fly below 2,200 feet above mean sea level within the area 
bounded by a circle having a radius of 2 nautical miles centred at 543549N 0024329W. 
(2) Paragraph (1) does not apply to any unmanned aircraft— 
(a) operated by Cumbria Police, or 
(b) flying in accordance with a permission issued by Cumbria Police.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations impose restrictions on the flying of unmanned aircraft in the vicinity of 
Lowther Deer Park, Penrith, Cumbria by reason of the large number of persons expected to attend 
the 2024 Kendal Calling music festival which is due to take place there between 31st July 2024 
and 5th August 2024 (inclusive). 
The Civil Aviation Authority and the Department for Transport agree that the flying of unmanned 
aircraft should be restricted in the vicinity of the event location for reasons of public safety. 
The times mentioned in regulation 3(1) are Co-ordinated Universal Time (UTC) which is one hour 
behind British Summer Time (BST). 
In relation to the permission mentioned in regulation 3(2)(b), Cumbria Police may be contacted as 
notified. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by an Aeronautical Information 
Circular and Notice to Aviation (NOTAM).
