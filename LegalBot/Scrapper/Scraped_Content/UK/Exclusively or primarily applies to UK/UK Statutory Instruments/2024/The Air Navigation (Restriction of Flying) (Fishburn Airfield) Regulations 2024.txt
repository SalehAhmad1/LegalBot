EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations impose restrictions on flying in the vicinity of Fishburn Airfield, County 
Durham by reason of the intended holding there of a Royal Aero Club Air Race on 20th and 21st 
July 2024. 
A large number of aircraft will be involved in high energy manoeuvres during the Air Race and 
therefore the Civil Aviation Authority and the Department for Transport agree that flying should 
be restricted in the vicinity of the event for reasons of public safety. 
The times mentioned in regulation 3(1) are Co-ordinated Universal Time (UTC) which is one hour 
behind British Summer Time (BST). 
In relation to the permission mentioned in regulation 3(2)(a)(i), the Fishburn Airfield Air Race air-
ground service may be contacted as notified. 
In relation to the permission mentioned in regulation 3(2)(a)(ii), the Royal Aero Club Records 
Racing and Rally Association Race Officials may be contacted as notified. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Aeronautical Information Circular 
(AIC) and Notice to Aviation (NOTAM).
