The Secretary of State has decided that it is necessary in the public interest to amend the timings 
for the flying restrictions in the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Bournemouth) Regulations 
2024(a). 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(b). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Bournemouth) 
(Amendment) Regulations 2024 and they come into force on 29th August 2024. 
Interpretation 
2. In regulation 3(2) the times referred to are Co-ordinated Universal Time(c). 
Amendment of the Regulations 
3.—(1) The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Bournemouth) Regulations 2024 are 
amended as follows. 
(2) In regulation 3(1) for “1700 hours” substitute “1930 hours”.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations amend the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Bournemouth) Regulations 
2024 (S.I 2024/257) by amending the timings of the restricted area for each of the three days 
beginning on 29th August 2024 and ending on 31st August 2024. 
The times mentioned in regulation 3(2) are Co-ordinated Universal Time (UTC) which is one hour 
behind British Summer Time (BST). 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Aeronautical Information Circular 
(AIC) and Notice to Aviation (NOTAM).
