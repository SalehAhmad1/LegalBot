ROAD TRAFFIC, WALES 
The M4 Motorway (Junction 41

Made                                    5 September 2019 
Coming into force                 9 September 2019 
 
 
The Welsh Ministers, as traffic authority for the 
relevant length of the M4 Motorway, in exercise of the 
powers conferred upon them by sections 14(1) and 124 
of, and paragraph 27 of Schedule 9 to, the Road 
Traffic Regulation Act 1984(1), make this Order.

1984 c. 27; section 14 was substituted by the Road Traffic

and paragraph 30 of Schedule 11 to the Government of 
Wales Act 2006 (c. 32), these powers are now exercisable by 
the Welsh Ministers in relation to Wales.

1.  The title of this Order is the M4 Motorway 
(Junction 41 (Pentyla) to Junction 42 (Earlswood), 
Neath Port Talbot) (Temporary 50 mph Speed Limit) 
Order 2018 (Revocation) Order 2019 and comes into 
force on 9 September 2019.
