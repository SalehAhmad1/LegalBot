The Inspectors of Education, Children’s Services and Skills (No. 3) Order 2019
Made
8th October 2019
Coming into force
10th October 2019
At the Court at Buckingham Palace, the 8th day of October 2019
Present,
The Queen’s Most Excellent Majesty in Council
Her Majesty, in exercise of the powers conferred by section 114(1) of the Education and Inspections Act 2006(1), is pleased, by and with the advice of Her Privy Council, to order as follows:
(1)
2006 c.40.Citation and commencement
1.  This Order may be cited as the Inspectors of Education, Children’s Services and Skills (No. 3) Order 2019 and comes into force on 10th October 2019.Appointment of Her Majesty’s Inspectors of Education, Children’s Services and Skills
2.  The persons named in the Schedule to this Order are appointed as Her Majesty’s Inspectors of Education, Children’s Services and Skills.Ceri King
