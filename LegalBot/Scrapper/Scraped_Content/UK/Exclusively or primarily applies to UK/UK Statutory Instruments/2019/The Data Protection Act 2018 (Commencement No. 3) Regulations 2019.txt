30th October 2019EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Regulations)
These Regulations are the third commencement regulations made under the Data Protection Act 2018 (c. 12).
They bring into force paragraphs 211 and 227 of Schedule 19 to that Act on 2nd December 2019, one minute after the provisions brought into force by the Children and Social Work Act 2017 (Commencement No. 6 and Saving Provision) Regulations 2019.NOTE AS TO EARLIER COMMENCEMENT REGULATIONS
