Ministry of JusticeEXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order amends the Judicial Appointments Order 2008 to extend eligibility for Fellows of the Chartered Institute of Legal Executives to be appointed as Deputy Judges of the Upper Tribunal, Judges of the Upper Tribunal and Recorders, provided that they meet the required qualification period (7 years as at the date of this Order). It also makes minor updating amendments to reflect current legislation.
A full impact assessment has not been produced for this instrument as no, or no significant, impact on the private, voluntary or public sector is foreseen.
