The Minister of State in the Cabinet Office makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by section 31 of the European Union (Future Relationship) Act 2020(1).
In accordance with paragraph 6(1) of Schedule 5 to that Act, a draft of this instrument has been laid before Parliament and approved by a resolution of each House of Parliament.
(1)
2020 c. 29.Citation, commencement, application, extent and interpretation
1.—(1) These Regulations may be cited as the European Union (Future Relationship) Act 2020 (References to the Trade and Cooperation Agreement) Regulations 2021.
(2) These Regulations come into force on the day after the day on which they are made.
(3) The amendments made by these Regulations—
(a)
do not apply in relation to times before the coming into force of these Regulations (and, accordingly, section 37(4) and (5) of the Act continue to apply in relation to such times);
(b)
have the same extent as the enactments to which those amendments relate.
(4) In these Regulations, “the Act” means the European Union (Future Relationship) Act 2020.Amendment of references to the Trade and Cooperation Agreement in the Act
2.  In the provisions of the Act specified in column (1) of the table in the Schedule, the references to the Trade and Cooperation Agreement specified in column (2) are substituted with the references specified in column (3).Amendment of references to the Trade and Cooperation Agreement in enactments amended by the Act
3.—(1) In regulation 2(1) of the Passenger Name Record Data and Miscellaneous Amendments Regulations 2018(1) (interpretation), in the definition of “terrorist offences”, for “Annex LAW-7” substitute “Annex 45”.
(2) In the Accreditation of Forensic Service Providers Regulations 2018(2)—
(a)
in regulation 2 (interpretation), in the definitions of “dactyloscopic data”, “DNA-profile” and “laboratory activity”, for “vehicle registration data etc)” substitute “vehicle registration data)”;
(b)
in regulation 4(2)(b) (requirement of accreditation), for “Article LAW.PRUM.16” substitute “Article 538”.

Paragraph 17(3)(a) Article LAW.PNR.28(12) Article 552(12)EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Regulations)
Regulation 2 and the Schedule amend the European Union (Future Relationship) Act 2020 (c. 29) (“the Act”) to amend and update references in that Act to provisions of the Trade and Cooperation Agreement (as defined in section 37 of the Act). Regulation 3 makes similar amendments to instruments amended by the Act.
The Trade and Cooperation Agreement can be found at https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/ukeu-and-eaec-trade-and-cooperation-agreement-ts-no82021. Persons unable to access the online version of that Agreement can contact EU Secretariat, Cabinet Office, 70 Whitehall, London, SW1A 2AS.
An impact assessment has not been produced for this instrument as no, or no significant, impact on the private or voluntary sector is foreseen.
