The Secretary of State has deemed it necessary in the public interest to restrict flying in the area 
specified in the Schedule to the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Stonehaven) (Emergency) 
Regulations 2020a (“the principal Regulations”) by reason of an emergency having arisen in that 
area. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(b). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Stonehaven) 
(Emergency) (Amendment) Regulations 2020 and come into force with immediate effect. 
Restricted airspace 
2.—(1) The principal Regulations are amended as follows. 
(2) In regulation 2, for “2,000” substitute “5,000”. 
(3) In the Schedule, column 2, for “1 nautical mile” substitute “2 nautical miles”.
