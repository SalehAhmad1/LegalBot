The Office for Students, in exercise of the powers conferred by section 42(1) of the Higher Education and Research Act 2017 M1, makes the following Order:

For the Office for StudentsEXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order, made under section 42(1) of the Higher Education and Research Act 2017 authorises Arden University Limited with the power to award taught awards for a one- year fixed term expiring on 31st August 2021. This Order will come into force on 1st September 2020.
An impact assessment has not been produced for this instrument as it has no impact on businesses and civil society organisations. The instrument has no impact on the public sector.
