30th March 2020EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order amends the District Probate Registries Order 1982 (S.I.1982/379) (the “Order”) to revoke the Lord Chancellor’s direction that a probate registry be established at Birmingham and that sub-registries be established at the places listed in column 2 of the Schedule to the Order.
