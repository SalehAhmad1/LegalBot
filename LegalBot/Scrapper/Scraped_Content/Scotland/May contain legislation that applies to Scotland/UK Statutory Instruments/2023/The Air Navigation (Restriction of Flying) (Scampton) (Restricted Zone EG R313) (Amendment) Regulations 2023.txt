The Secretary of State has decided that it is in the public interest to amend the timings of when the 
Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Scampton) (Restricted Zone EG R313) Regulations 2022 
apply. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Scampton) 
(Restricted Zone EG R313) (Amendment) Regulations 2023 and they come into force on 1st 
March 2023. 
Amendment 
2. The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Scampton) (Restricted Zone EG R313) 
Regulations 2022(b) are amended as follows. 
3. In regulation 2 for “Co-ordinated Universal Time” substitute “local time”.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations amend the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Scampton) (Restricted Zone 
EG R313) Regulations 2022 (S.I. 2022/1221) as amended by the Air Navigation (Restriction of 
Flying) (Scampton) (Restricted Zone EG R313) (Amendment) Regulations 2022 (S.I. 2022/1320). 
                                                                                                                                            
(a) S.I. 2016/765, to which there are amendments not relevant to these Regulations. 
(b) S.I. 2022/1221, amended by S.I. 2022/1320. 
 
2
The amendment amends the specific timings to which the regulations apply from Co-ordinated 
Universal Time to local time. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Aeronautical Information 
Publication (AIP) and Notice to Aviation (NOTAM).
