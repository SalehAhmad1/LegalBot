The Secretary of State has decided that it is necessary in the public interest to restrict the flying of

The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Royal Air 
Force Waddington) (No. 4) Regulations 2023 and they come into force on 6th July 2023.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations impose restrictions on flying in the vicinity of Royal Air Force Waddington, 
Lincolnshire during flying display rehearsals by the Royal Air Force Aerobatic Team, the Red 
Arrows, which are due to take place there on 6th July 2023 and 7th July 2023. 
In order to protect the display aircraft, other air traffic, and spectators, the Civil Aviation 
Authority and the Department for Transport agree that that is in the public interest to restrict flying 
in the vicinity of the rehearsals for reasons of public safety. 
The times mentioned in regulation 3(1) are Co-ordinated Universal Time (UTC) which is one hour 
behind British Summer Time (BST). 
In relation to the permission mentioned in regulation 3(2), Royal Air Force Coningsby may be 
contacted as notified. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Briefing Sheet and Notice to 
Aviation (NOTAM).
