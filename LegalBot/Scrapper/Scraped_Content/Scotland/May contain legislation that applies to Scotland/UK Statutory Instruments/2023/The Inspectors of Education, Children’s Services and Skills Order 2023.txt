EDUCATION, ENGLAND
CHILDREN AND YOUNG PERSONS, ENGLAND
The Inspectors of Education, Children’s Services and Skills Order 2023
Made
15th February 2023
Coming into force
16th February 2023
At the Court at Buckingham Palace, the 15th day of February 2023
Present,
The King’s Most Excellent Majesty in Council
His Majesty, in exercise of the powers conferred by section 114(1) of the Education and Inspections Act 2006(1), is pleased, by and with the advice of His Privy Council, to order as follows:
(1)
2006 c. 40.Citation and commencement
1.  This Order may be cited as the Inspectors of Education, Children’s Services and Skills Order 2023 and comes into force on 16th February 2023.Appointment of His Majesty’s Inspectors of Education, Children’s Services and Skills
2.  The persons named in the Schedule to this Order are appointed as His Majesty’s Inspectors of Education, Children’s Services and Skills.Richard Tilbrook
Clerk of the Privy CouncilArticle 2
