Coming into force with immediate effect 
The Secretary of State for a reason affecting the public interest deems it necessary to restrict the 
flying of unmanned aircraft in the areas specified in regulation 2 by reason of an emergency 
having arisen there. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Warrington) 
(Emergency) Regulations 2023 and they come into force with immediate effect. 
Restricted airspace 
2. No unmanned aircraft is to fly below 400 feet above mean sea level over the two areas 
specified in Column 1 of the Schedule, being areas bounded by a circle of the radius specified in 
Column 2 and centred on the position specified in Column 3.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations impose restrictions on the flying of unmanned aircraft in two separate areas in 
the vicinity of Warrington, Greater Manchester. They are intended to be of temporary duration and 
will be revoked as soon as the emergency in question has passed.
