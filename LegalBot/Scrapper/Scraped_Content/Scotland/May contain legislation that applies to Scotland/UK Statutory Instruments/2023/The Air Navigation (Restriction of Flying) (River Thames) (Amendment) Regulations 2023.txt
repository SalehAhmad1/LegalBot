The Secretary of State has decided that it is necessary in the public interest to amend the 
restriction on the flying of unmanned aircraft in the vicinity of the River Thames. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (River Thames) 
(Amendment) Regulations 2023 and they come into force on 8th September 2023. 
Amendment of the Regulations 
2. The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (River Thames) Regulations 2023(b) are amended 
as follows. 
3. In regulation 1, for “9th September 2023” substitute “8th September 2023”.
