The Secretary of State has decided that it is necessary in the public interest to amend the 
restriction on the flying of unmanned aircraft in the vicinity of Lytham St Annes. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Lytham St 
Annes) (Amendment) Regulations 2023 and they come into force on 28th June 2023. 
Amendment of the Regulations 
2. The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Lytham St Annes) Regulations 2023(b) are 
amended as follows. 
3. In regulation 3(1) for “534408N 0025428W” substitute “534408N 0025728W”.
