The Keadby 3 (Carbon Capture Equipped Gas Fired Generating Station) (Correction) Order 2023
Made
18th April 2023
Coming into force
20th April 2023
The Keadby 3 (Carbon Capture Equipped Gas Fired Generating Station) Order 2022(1) (“the Order”), which granted development consent within the meaning of the Planning Act 2008(2) (“the Act”) contains correctable errors within the meaning of paragraph 1 of Schedule 4 to the Act.
In accordance with paragraph 1(5)(a) of Schedule 4 to the Act, and before the end of the relevant period (as defined in paragraph 1(6)(a) of Schedule 4 to the Act), the Secretary of State received a written request from the applicant(3) for the corrections of errors and omissions in the Order.
In accordance with paragraph 1(7) of Schedule 4 to the Act, the Secretary of State has informed the relevant local planning authority for the area in which the land to which the Order relates is situated that the request has been received.
The Secretary of State, in exercise of the powers conferred by section 119 of, and paragraph 1(4) and 1(8) of Schedule 4 to the Act, makes the following Order—

There are other amendments to the Act which are not relevant to this Order.
(3)
As provided for in paragraph 4 of Schedule 4 to the Act.Citation and commencement
