The Secretary of State has decided that it is no longer considered necessary in the public interest to 
restrict flying in the area specified in the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Arbroath) 
Regulations 2000 (S.I. 2000/2898). 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Arbroath) 
(Revocation) Regulations 2023 and they come into force on 2nd November 2023. 
Revocation of Regulations 
2. The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Arbroath) Regulations 2000(b) are revoked.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations revoke the statutory instrument which imposed restrictions on the flying of 
aircraft in the vicinity of Arbroath, Scotland (S.I. 2000/2898) as it is considered they are no longer 
required.
