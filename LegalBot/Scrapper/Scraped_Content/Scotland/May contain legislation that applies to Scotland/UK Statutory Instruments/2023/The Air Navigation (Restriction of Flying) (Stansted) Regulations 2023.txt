The Secretary of State has decided it is necessary in the public interest to restrict flying at London 
Stansted Airport by aircraft of the type specified in regulation 3(1). 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Stansted) 
Regulations 2023 and they come into force with immediate effect.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations impose restrictions of an emergency nature on the departure of a specified 
aircraft type from London Stansted Airport, Essex for reasons of public safety. 
The time referred to in regulation 3(1) is local time. 
In relation to the permission mentioned in regulation 3(2), this can be obtained by contacting the 
Department of Transport on 0300 330 3000 during the hours of 0830 to 1730 Monday to Friday, 
emailing dutyofficers@dft.gov.uk outside of these times, or by writing to contactdft@dft.gov.uk. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Notice to Aviation (NOTAM).
