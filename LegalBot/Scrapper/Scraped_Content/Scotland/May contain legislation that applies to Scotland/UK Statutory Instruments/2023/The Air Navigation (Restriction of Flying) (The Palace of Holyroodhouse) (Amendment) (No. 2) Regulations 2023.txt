The Secretary of State has decided that it is necessary in the public interest to amend the 
restriction of flying in the vicinity of Holyroodhouse. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (The Palace of 
Holyroodhouse) (Amendment) (No. 2) Regulations 2023 and they come into force on 3rd July 
2023. 
Amendment of the Regulations 
2. The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (The Palace of Holyroodhouse) Regulations 
2023(b) are amended as follows. 
3. In regulation 1, for “1 nautical mile” substitute “2 nautical miles”.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations amend the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (The Palace of 
Holyroodhouse) Regulations 2023 (S.I. 2023/529, as amended by S.I. 2023/673) by amending the 
size of the restricted area. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Notice to Aviation (NOTAM).
