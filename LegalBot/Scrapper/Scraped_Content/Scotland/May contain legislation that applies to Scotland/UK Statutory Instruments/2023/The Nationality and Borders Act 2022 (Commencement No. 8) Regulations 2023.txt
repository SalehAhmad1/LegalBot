16th November 2023EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Regulations)
These Regulations bring into force on 20th November 2023 section 46(8) of the Nationality and Borders Act 2022 (c. 36). Section 46(8) relates to the power of the First-tier Tribunal to grant immigration bail without the consent of the Secretary of State.NOTE AS TO EARLIER COMMENCEMENT REGULATIONS
