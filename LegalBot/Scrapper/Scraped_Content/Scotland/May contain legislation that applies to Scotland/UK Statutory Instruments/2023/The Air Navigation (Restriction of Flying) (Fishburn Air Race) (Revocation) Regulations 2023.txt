22nd July 2023 
The Secretary of State has decided that it is no longer necessary in the public interest to restrict 
flying in the area specified in regulation 3(1) as the air race in the vicinity of Fishburn Aerodrome 
has been cancelled. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Fishburn Air 
Race) (Revocation) Regulations 2023 and they come into force on 22nd July 2023. 
Revocation of Regulations 
2. The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Fishburn Air Race) Regulations 2023(b) are 
revoked.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations revoke the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Fishburn Air Race) 
Regulations 2023 (S.I. 2023/531) as they are no longer considered necessary as the event has been 
cancelled. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Notice to Aviation (NOTAM).
