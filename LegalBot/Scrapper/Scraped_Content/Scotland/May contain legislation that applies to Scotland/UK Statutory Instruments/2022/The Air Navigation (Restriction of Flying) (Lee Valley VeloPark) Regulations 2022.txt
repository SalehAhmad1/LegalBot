The Secretary of State has decided that it is in the public interest to restrict the flying of unmanned 
aircraft in the area specified in regulation 3(1) by reason of the intended holding there of cycling 
events associated with the XXII Commonwealth Games. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Lee Valley 
VeloPark) Regulations 2022 and they come into force on 28th July 2022. 
Interpretation 
2. In regulation 3(1) the times referred to are Co-ordinated Universal Time(b).

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations impose restrictions on the flying of unmanned aircraft in the vicinity of Lee 
Valley VeloPark. East London during cycling events associated with the XXII Commonwealth 
Games that are scheduled to take place there from 28th July 2022 to the 1st August 2022, 
inclusive. In view of the high visibility of this event and at the request of the West Midland Police 
Service, the Civil Aviation Authority and the Department for Transport agree that the flying of 
unmanned aircraft should be restricted in the vicinity of the VeloPark for reasons of public safety 
and security. 
The times mentioned in regulation 3(1) are Co-ordinated Universal Time (UTC) which is one hour 
behind British Summer Time (BST). 
In relation to the permission mentioned in regulation 3(2), the West Midlands Police Service may 
be contacted as notified. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Notice to Aviation (NOTAM).
