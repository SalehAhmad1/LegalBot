The Secretary of State has previously decided that it was in the public interest to restrict flying in 
the vicinity of Croydon by reason of an emergency having arisen in that area. These regulations 
are no longer considered necessary. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Croydon) 
(Emergency) (Revocation) Regulations 2022 and they come into force with immediate effect. 
Revocation of Regulations 
2. The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Croydon) (Emergency) Regulations 2022(b) are 
revoked.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations revoke the statutory instrument which imposed restrictions on flying in the 
vicinity of Croydon (S.I. 2022/888) as the emergency in question has now passed.
