The Secretary of State has previously decided that it is necessary in the public interest to restrict 
flying in the vicinity of Greater London, Bedfordshire, Cambridgeshire and Norfolk by reason of 
two intended helicopter flights. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Helicopter 
Flight) (Amendment) (No. 2) Regulations 2022 and they come into force on 7th February 2022. 
Interpretation 
2. In regulation 3 the times referred to are Co-ordinated Universal Time(b). 
Amendment 
3. The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Helicopter Flight) Regulations 2022(c) are 
amended as follows— 
(a) in regulation 3(4) for “1045 hours and 1205 hours” substitute “1115 hours and 1235 hours”, 
(b) in regulation 3(5) for “1045 hours and 1205 hours” substitute “1115 hours and 1235 hours”, 
and 
(c) in regulation 3(6) for “1045 hours and 1205 hours” substitute “1115 hours and 1235 hours”.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations amend the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Helicopter Flight) 
Regulations 2022 (S.I. 2022/42). The amendment changes the times of the restricted airspace on 
7th February 2022. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Notice to Aviation (NOTAM).
