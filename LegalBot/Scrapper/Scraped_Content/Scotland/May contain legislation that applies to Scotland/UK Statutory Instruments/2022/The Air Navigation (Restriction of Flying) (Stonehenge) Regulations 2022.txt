The Secretary of State has decided that it is in the public interest to restrict flying in the area 
specified in regulation 3(1) by reason of the intended gathering there of a large number of persons 
for the Summer and Winter Solstice events. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Stonehenge) 
Regulations 2022 and they come into force on 20th June 2022. 
Interpretation 
2. In regulation 3(1) the times referred to are Co-ordinated Universal Time(b). 
Restricted airspace 
3.—(1) Subject to paragraph (2), no aircraft is to fly below 3,000 feet above mean sea level 
within the area bounded by a circle having a radius of 3 nautical miles whose centre is at 511044N 
0014934W between— 
(a) 1600 hours on 20th June 2022 and 1000 hours on 21st June 2022, and 
(b) 2100 hours on 21st December 2022 and 1100 hours on 22nd December 2022.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations impose restrictions on flying in the vicinity of Stonehenge, Wiltshire during the 
summer and winter solstice events which are expected to be attended by a large gathering of 
persons on the 20th June 2022, 21st June 2022, 21st December 2022 and 22nd December 2022. 
Wiltshire Police has requested that flying should be restricted in the vicinity of Stonehenge during 
these periods, and this has been agreed by the Civil Aviation Authority and the Department for 
Transport for security and public safety reasons. 
In relation to the permissions mentioned in regulation 3(2), Wiltshire Police may be contacted as 
notified and the Senior Air Operations Officer at Salisbury Plain Air Operations may be contacted 
on 01980 674729. 
The times mentioned in regulation 3(1) are Co-ordinated Universal Time (UTC) which is one hour 
behind British Summer Time (BST) in regulation 3(1)(a) and is the same time as Greenwich Mean 
Time in regulation 3(1)(b). 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Notice to Aviation (NOTAM).
