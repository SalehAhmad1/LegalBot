The Secretary of State previously decided that it was necessary in the public interest to restrict 
flying in the area specified in the Schedule to the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (West 
Thurrock) (Emergency) Regulations 2019(a) (“the principal Regulations”) by reason of an 
emergency having arisen in that area. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(b). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (West 
Thurrock) (Emergency) (Revocation) Regulations 2019 and come into force with immediate 
effect. 
Revocation of Regulations 
2. The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (West Thurrock) (Emergency) Regulations 2019 
are revoked.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations revoke the statutory instrument which imposed emergency restrictions on 
flying in the area specified in the Schedule to that instrument, the emergency in questions having 
passed.
