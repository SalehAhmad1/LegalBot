The Secretary of State makes these Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by section 2(2) of the European Communities Act 1972(1). The Secretary of State is a Minister designated(2) for the purposes of that section in relation to the common agricultural policy.

At 12.10 p.m. on 31st October 2019EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Regulations)
These Regulations amend the Eggs and Chicks (England) Regulations 2009 (S.I. 2009/2163) and the Poultrymeat (England) Regulations 2011 (S.I. 2011/452) to adjust references in the enforcement provisions within those Regulations to Regulation (EU) No 1308/2013 of the European Parliament and of the Council (OJ No L 347, 20.12.2013, p. 671) and Council Regulation (EC) No 1234/2007 (OJ No L 299, 16.11.2007, p. 1).
An Impact Assessment has not been produced for this instrument as no, or no significant, impact on the private, voluntary or public sectors is foreseen.
