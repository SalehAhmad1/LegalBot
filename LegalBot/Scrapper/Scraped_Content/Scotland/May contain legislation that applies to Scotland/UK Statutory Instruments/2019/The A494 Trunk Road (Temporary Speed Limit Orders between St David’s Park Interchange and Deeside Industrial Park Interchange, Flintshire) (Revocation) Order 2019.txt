The Welsh Ministers, as traffic authority for the 
relevant length of the A494 Trunk Road, in exercise of 
the powers conferred upon them by sections 14(1) and 
124 of, and paragraph 27 of Schedule 9 to, the Road 
Traffic Regulation Act 1984(1), make this Order. 
Title and Commencement  
1. The title of this Order is the A494 Trunk Road 
(Temporary Speed Limit Orders between St David’s 
Park Interchange and Deeside Industrial Park 
Interchange, Flintshire) (Revocation) Order 2019 and 
it comes into force on 16 August 2019. 
Revocations 
2. The A494/A550 Trunk Road (Queensferry, 
Flintshire) (Temporary 50 mph Speed Limit) Order 
2018(2) and the A494 Trunk Road (St David’s Park 
Interchange to Deeside Industrial Park Interchange, 
Flintshire) (Temporary 50 mph Speed Limit) Order 
2018(3) are revoked.
