STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS
2019 No. 1460
ECCLESIASTICAL LAW, ENGLAND
The Church Representation and Ministers Measure 2019 (Commencement) Order 2019
Made
6th November 2019
The Archbishops of Canterbury and York, in exercise of the powers conferred by section 3 of the Church Representation and Ministers Measure 2019(1), make the following Order:
(1)
2019 No. 1.Citation and interpretation
1.—(1) This Order may be cited as the Church Representation and Ministers Measure 2019 (Commencement) Order 2019.
(2) In this Order “the Measure” means the Church Representation and Ministers Measure 2019.Commencement on 1st January 2020
2.  The Measure so far as not already in force comes into force on 1st January 2020.+Justin Cantuar:
Archbishop of Canterbury
+Sentamu Ebor:
Archbishop of York
6th November 2019EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order brings into force the substantive provisions of the Church Representation and Ministers Measure 2019, including Schedule 1 which substitutes a new Schedule 3 (Church Representation Rules) in the Synodical Government Measure 1969.
