INFRASTRUCTURE PLANNING
The Cleve Hill Solar Park (Correction) Order 2021
Made
10th August 2021
Coming into force
11th August 2021
The Cleve Hill Solar Park Order 2020 (“the Order”)(1), which granted development consent within the meaning of the Planning Act 2008 (“the Act”)(2), contains correctable errors within the meaning of paragraph 1 of Schedule 4 to the Act.
Before the end of the relevant period (as defined in paragraph 1(6)(a) of Schedule 4 to the Act), the Secretary of State received a written request from the applicant(3) for the correction of errors and omissions in the Order under paragraph 1(5)(a) of Schedule 4 to the Act.
In accordance with paragraph 1(7) of Schedule 4 to the Act, the Secretary of State has informed Kent County Council, Swale Borough Council and Canterbury City Council, as the relevant local planning authorities(4), that the request has been received.
The Secretary of State, in exercise of the powers conferred by section 119 of, and paragraphs 1(4) and 1(8) of Schedule 4 to, the Act, makes the following Order—
