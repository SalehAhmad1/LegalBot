The Secretary of State has previously decided that it is in the public interest to restrict flying by 
reason of the intended holding of flying displays by a jet formation display team. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Jet Formation 
Display Teams) (Amendment) Regulations 2021 and come into force on 4th June 2021.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations amend the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Jet Formation Display 
Teams) Regulations 2021 (S.I. 2021/536) which restrict flying in the vicinity of places where jet 
formation display teams intend to give certain flying displays. 
The Regulations remove the displays scheduled to take place at Newcastle Northern Ireland (19th 
June 2021), Teignmouth (3rd July 2021), Wales National Show (3rd and 4th July 2021), 
Honington (22nd July 2021), Dawlish (18th August 2021), and Rhyl (28th and 29th August 2021).  
These Regulations also add displays to be held at Headcorn (26th and 27th June 2021), Folkestone 
(8th August 2021), and Falmouth (11th August 2021). 
Full details of this amendment will be promulgated by Notice to Airmen (NOTAM).
