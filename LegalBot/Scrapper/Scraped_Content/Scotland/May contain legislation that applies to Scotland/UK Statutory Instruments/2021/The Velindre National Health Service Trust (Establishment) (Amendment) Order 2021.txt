3 March 2021EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order further amends the Velindre National Health Service Trust (Establishment) Order 1993 in order to amend the functions of Velindre University National Health Service Trust.
The functions are amended to account for the conferral of functions on Digital Health and Care Wales in relation to the provision of digital platforms, systems and services.
The Welsh Ministers’ Code of Practice on the carrying out of Regulatory Impact Assessments was considered in relation to this Order. As a result, a regulatory impact assessment has not been prepared as to the likely cost and benefit of complying with this Order.
