Her Majesty, in exercise of the power conferred upon Her by section 37(2) of the British Nationality Act 1981(1), by and with the advice of Her Privy Council, orders as follows.
(1)
1981 c. 61.Citation and commencement
1.  This Order may be cited as the British Nationality (Maldives) Order 2021 and comes into force on 2nd June 2021.Amendment of Schedule 3 to the British Nationality Act 1981
2.  In Schedule 3 to the British Nationality Act 1981(1) (countries whose citizens are Commonwealth citizens) after “Malaysia” insert “Maldives”.
(1)
Schedule 3 has been amended by S.I. 1983/882, 1983/1699, 1989/1331, 1990/1502, 1994/1634, 1998/3161, 2010/246, 2015/1771, 2017/568 and 2018/620.Revocation of the British Nationality (Maldives) Order 2017
3.  The British Nationality (Maldives) Order 2017(1) is revoked.
(1)
S.I. 2017/568.Richard Tilbrook
Clerk of the Privy CouncilEXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
Article 2 of this Order adds Maldives to Schedule 3 to the British Nationality Act 1981. This means that citizens of Maldives will now have the status of Commonwealth citizens.
Article 3 of this Order revokes the British Nationality (Maldives) Order 2017 which removed Maldives from Schedule 3 to the British Nationality Act 1981.
A full impact assessment has not been produced for this instrument as no, or no significant, impact on the private, voluntary or public sector is foreseen.
