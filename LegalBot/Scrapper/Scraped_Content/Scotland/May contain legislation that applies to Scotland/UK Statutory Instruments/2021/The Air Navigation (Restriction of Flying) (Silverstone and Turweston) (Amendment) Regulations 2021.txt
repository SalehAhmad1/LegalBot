The Secretary of State has previously decided that it is in the public interest to restrict flying in the 
vicinity of Silverstone motor-racing circuit and Heliport and Turweston Aerodrome by reason of 
the intended holding of the British Grand Prix 2021. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Silverstone and 
Turweston) (Amendment) Regulations 2021 and come into force on 16th July 2021. 
Interpretation 
2. In regulation 3 the times referred to are Co-ordinated Universal Time(b). 
Amendment to the Regulations 
3.—(1) The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Silverstone and Turweston) Regulations 
2021(c) are amended as follows. 
(2) In regulation 3 paragraph (2)(a) for “between 0800 and 1800 hours on 16th July 2021” 
substitute “between 0900 and 2000 hours on 16th July 2021”. 
(3) In regulation 3 paragraph (2)(b) for “between 0700 and 1800 hours on 17th July 2021” 
substitute “between 0800 and 1900 hours on 17th July 2021”. 
(4) In regulation 3 paragraph (2)(c) for “between 0600 and 1900 hours on 18th July 2021” 
substitute “between 0800 and 1900 hours on 18th July 2021”. 
(5) In regulation 4 paragraph (2)(a) for “between 0800 and 1800 hours on 16th July 2021” 
substitute “between 0600 and 1900 hours on 16th July 2021”. 
(6) In regulation 4 paragraph (2)(b) for “between 0700 and 1800 hours on 17th July 2021” 
substitute “between 0600 and 1900 hours on 17th July 2021”. 
 
                                                                                                                                            
(a) S.I. 2016/765, to which there are amendments not relevant to these Regulations. 
(b) Co-ordinated Universal Time (UTC) is one hour behind British Summer Time (BST).

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations amend the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Silverstone and Turweston) 
Regulations 2021 (S.I. 2021/604). They amend specific timings for the restrictions on 16th, 17th 
and 18th July 2021. 
The times mentioned in regulation 3 are Co-ordinated Universal Time (UTC) which is one hour 
behind British Summer Time (BST). 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Notice to Airmen (NOTAM).
