The Secretary of State has previously decided that it is in the public interest to restrict flying in the 
vicinity of Northampton Sywell Aerodrome, by reason of the intended holding of the Light 
Aircraft Association Rally 2021. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Northampton 
Sywell) (Amendment) Regulations 2021 and they come into force with immediate effect. 
Amendment 
2.—(1) The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Northampton Sywell) Regulations 2021(b) 
are amended as follows. 
(2) In regulation 3 add ‘(3) Paragraph (1) does not apply to any aircraft — (a) flying in 
accordance with a permission issued by Sywell Aeronautical Ground Communications.’’

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations amend the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Northampton Sywell) 
Regulations 2021 (S.I. 2021/837). They amend permissions for access to the restriction over the 
dates of the Rally. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Notice to Aviation (NOTAM).
