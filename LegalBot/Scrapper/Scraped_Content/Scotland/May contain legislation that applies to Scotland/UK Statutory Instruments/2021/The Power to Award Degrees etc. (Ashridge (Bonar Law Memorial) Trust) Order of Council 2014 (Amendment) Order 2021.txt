The Power to Award Degrees etc. (Ashridge (Bonar Law Memorial) Trust) Order of Council 2014 (Amendment) Order 2021
Made
13th August 2021
Coming into force
1st September 2021
The Office for Students, in exercise of the powers conferred by section 45(1) of the Higher Education and Research Act 2017(1), makes the following Order:
(1)
2017 c. 29.Citation and commencement
1.  This Order may be cited as the Power to Award Degrees etc. (Ashridge (Bonar Law Memorial) Trust) Order of Council 2014 (Amendment) Order 2021 and shall come into force on 1st September 2021.Variation of Power to Award Degrees etc. (Ashridge (Bonar Law Memorial) Trust) Order of Council 2014
2.—(1) The Power to Award Degrees etc. (Ashridge (Bonar Law Memorial) Trust) Order of Council 2014 is varied in accordance with paragraph (2).
(2) For article 2, substitute—
“2.  The Ashridge (Bonar Law Memorial) Trust (also known as ‘Ashridge’) shall be competent to grant awards of the kind mentioned in paragraph (a) of section 76(2) of the said Act.”Jean Arnold
Head of Compliance and Student Protection
For the Office for Students
13th August 2021EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order, made under section 45(1) of the Higher Education and Research Act 2017, provides for a variation of the Power to Award Degrees etc. (Ashridge (Bonar Law Memorial) Trust) Order of Council 2014, so that Ashridge (Bonar Law Memorial) Trust’s existing Degree Awarding Powers authorisation remains valid for an indefinite period. This Order will come into force on 1st September 2021.
An impact assessment has not been produced for this instrument as it has no impact on businesses and civil society organisations. The instrument has no impact on the public sector.
