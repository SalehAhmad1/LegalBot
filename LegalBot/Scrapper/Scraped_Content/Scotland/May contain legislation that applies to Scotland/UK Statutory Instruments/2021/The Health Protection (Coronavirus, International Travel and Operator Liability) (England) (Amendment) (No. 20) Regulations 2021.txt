The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by sections 45B, 45F(2) and 45P(2) of the Public Health (Control of Disease) Act 1984(1).
(1)
1984 c. 22. Part 2A was inserted by section 129 of the Health and Social Care Act 2008 (c. 14).Citation, commencement, extent, application and interpretation
1.—(1) These Regulations may be cited as the Health Protection (Coronavirus, International Travel and Operator Liability) (England) (Amendment) (No. 20) Regulations 2021.
(2) These Regulations come into force at 4.00 a.m. on 28th November 2021.
(3) These Regulations extend to England and Wales and apply in relation to England only.
(4) In these Regulations, “the principal Regulations” means the Health Protection (Coronavirus, International Travel and Operator Liability) (England) Regulations 2021(1).

At 11.45 a.m. on 27th November 2021EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Regulations)
These Regulations amend the Health Protection (Coronavirus, International Travel and Operator Liability) (England) Regulations 2021 (S.I. 2021/582). They add four countries to the list of Category 3 countries and territories in Schedule 3, and amend Schedule 13 to prevent aircraft from those countries arriving in England except in limited circumstances.
An impact assessment has not been produced for this instrument. An explanatory memorandum has been published alongside this instrument at www.legislation.gov.uk.
