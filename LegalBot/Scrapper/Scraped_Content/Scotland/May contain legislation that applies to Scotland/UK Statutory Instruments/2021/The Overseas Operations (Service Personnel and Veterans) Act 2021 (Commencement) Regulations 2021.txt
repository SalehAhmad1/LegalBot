STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS
2021 No. 678 (C. 28)
DEFENCE
The Overseas Operations (Service Personnel and Veterans) Act 2021 (Commencement) Regulations 2021
Made
7th June 2021
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by section 14(2) of the Overseas Operations (Service Personnel and Veterans) Act 2021(1):
(1)
2021 c.23.Citation
1.  These Regulations may be cited as the Overseas Operations (Service Personnel and Veterans) Act 2021 (Commencement) Regulations 2021.Commencement
2.  The Overseas Operations (Service Personnel and Veterans) Act 2021 comes into force on 30th June 2021 in so far as it is not already in force.Ben Wallace
Secretary of State
Ministry of Defence
7th June 2021EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Regulations)
These Regulations bring the provisions of the Overseas Operations (Service Personnel and Veterans) Act 2021 (c.23) into force on 30th June 2021 in so far as they are not already in force. Part 3 of that Act came into force on Royal Assent.
