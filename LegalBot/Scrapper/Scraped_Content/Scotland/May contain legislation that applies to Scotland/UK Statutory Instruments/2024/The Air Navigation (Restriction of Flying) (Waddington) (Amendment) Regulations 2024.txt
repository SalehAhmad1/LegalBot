The Secretary of State has decided that it is necessary in the public interest to amend the Air 
Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Waddington) Regulations 2024(a). 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(b). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Waddington) 
(Amendment) Regulations 2024 and they come into force on 13th July 2024. 
Amendment of the Regulations 
2.—(1) The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Waddington) Regulations 2024 are amended 
as follows. 
(2) In regulation 2 for “3(2)” substitute “3(1)”. 
(3) For regulation 3(1) substitute— 
“3.—(1) Subject to paragraph (2), between— 
(a) 1030 hours and 1330 hours on 13th July 2024, and 
(b) 1345 hours and 1615 hours on 3rd October 2024, 
no aircraft is to fly below 9,300 feet above mean sea level within the area bounded by a 
circle having a radius of 5 nautical miles whose centre is at 531001N 0003136W.”.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations amend the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Waddington) Regulations 
2024 (S.I. 2024/734) to correct a cross reference in those Regulations, and to include a second 
date, 3rd October 2024, for the flying displays. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Aeronautical Information Circular 
(AIC) and Notice to Aviation (NOTAM).
