The Secretary of State has decided that it is no longer necessary in the public interest to restrict the 
flying of unmanned aircraft in the vicinity of Lincoln, Lincolnshire as the multi-agency exercise 
due to take place there has been cancelled. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Lincoln) 
(Revocation) Regulations 2024 and they come into force on 15th July 2024. 
Revocation of Regulations 
2. The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Lincoln) Regulations 2024(b) are revoked.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations revoke the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Lincoln) Regulations 2024 
(S.I. 2024/660) as they are no longer considered necessary due to the cancellation of the multi-
agency exercise. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Notice to Aviation (NOTAM).
