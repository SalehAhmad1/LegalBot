The Scottish National Investment Bank p.l.c. (Miscellaneous Listings) Order 2020
Made
25th November 2020
Coming into force
26th November 2020
The Scottish Ministers make the following Order in exercise of the powers conferred by sections 44(3) and 96(2) of the Climate Change (Scotland) Act 2009(1), and sections 151(3) and 207(4)(a) of the Equality Act 2010(2) and all other powers enabling them to do so.
In accordance with section 44(7) of the Climate Change (Scotland) Act 2009 the Scottish Ministers have consulted such associations of local authorities and such other persons as they considered appropriate.
In accordance with section 152(3) of the Equality Act 2010(3) the Scottish Ministers have consulted the Commission for Equality and Human Rights.
In accordance with section 96(4) of the Climate Change (Scotland) Act 2009(4) and section 210(2) of the Equality Act 2010 a draft of this Order has been laid before, and approved by resolution of, the Scottish Parliament.

25th November 2020EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order makes amendments to the Equality Act 2010 (“the 2010 Act”) and the Climate Change (Duties of Public Bodies: Reporting Requirements) (Scotland) Order 2015 (“the 2015 Order”).
Article 2 adds the Scottish National Investment Bank p.l.c to Part 3 of schedule 19 of the 2010 Act, which lists Scottish public authorities required to comply with the public sector equality duty.
Article 3 adds the Scottish National Investment Bank p.l.c. to schedule 1 of the 2015 Order, which lists bodies which are subject to the reporting requirements in that Order.
