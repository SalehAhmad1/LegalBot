The Scottish Ministers make the following Order in exercise of the powers conferred by section 
84(1)(a), and 124(1)(d) of, and paragraph 27 of schedule 9 of, the Road Traffic Regulation Act 
1984(a) and all other powers enabling them to do so. 
In accordance with section 84(2)(b) of that Act, they have given public notice of their intention to 
make the following Order, and have complied with Parts II and III of the Secretary of State’s 
Traffic Orders (Procedure) (Scotland) Regulations 1987(c). 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as the A830 Trunk Road (Glenfinnan) (40mph Speed Limit) Order 
2020 and comes into force on 21 July 2020. 
Speed restriction 
2. No person may drive any motor vehicle at a speed exceeding 40 miles per hour on the length 
of road specified in the schedule. 
Revocation 
3. The A830 Trunk Road (Glenfinnan) (40mph Speed Limit) Order 1996(d) is revoked.

SCHEDULE 
Article 2 
SPECIFIED LENGTH OF ROAD 
That length of the A830 Fort William to Mallaig Trunk Road from a point 584 metres or thereby 
south-east of the extended centreline of the U1191 Glenfinnan Pier Road (Slatach) in a north-west, 
west then north-west direction to a point 296 metres or thereby west of the extended centreline of 
the U5397 Torr An Eas, Glenfinnan Road, a distance of 1.999 kilometres or thereby.
