The Scottish Ministers make the following Order in exercise of the powers conferred by sections 
82(2)(a) and (b), 83(1), 84(1)(a), and 124(1)(d) of, and paragraph 27 of schedule 9 of, the Road 
Traffic Regulation Act 1984(a) and all other powers enabling them to do so. 
In accordance with sections 83(1) and 84(2)(b) of that Act, they have given public notice of their 
intention to make the following Order, and have complied with Parts II and III of the Secretary of 
State’s Traffic Orders (Procedure) (Scotland) Regulations 1987(c). 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as the A78 Trunk Road (West Kilbride incorporating Seamill) 
(30mph Speed Limit) Order 2020 and comes into force on 14 May 2020. 
Direction in relation to a restricted road 
2. The length of road specified in Part 1 of the schedule of this Order will cease to be a restricted 
road. 
Speed restriction 
3. No person may drive any motor vehicle at a speed exceeding 30 miles per hour on the length 
of road specified in Part 2 of the schedule of this Order.

Revocation 
4. The A78 Trunk Road (West Kilbride Incorporating Seamill) (Restricted Road) Order 2005(a) 
is revoked. 
Exemption 
5. No speed restriction imposed by this Order applies to— 
(a) vehicles falling within regulation 3(4) of the Road Traffic Exemptions (Special Forces) 
(Variation and Amendment) Regulations 2011(b) when used in accordance with 
regulation 3(5) of those Regulations, or 
(b) vehicles being used in an emergency for coastguard purposes.

SCHEDULE 
Articles 2 and 3 
SPECIFIED LENGTH OF ROAD 
PART 1 
That length of the A78 Greenock – Prestwick Trunk Road at West Kilbride incorporating Seamill 
from a point 14 metres or thereby north of the extended north building line of No. 51 Snowdon 
Terrace in a southerly direction to a point 22 metres or thereby south of the extended south 
building line of No. 106 Ardrossan Road, a distance of 1.75 kilometres or thereby. 
PART 2 
That length of the A78 Greenock – Prestwick Trunk Road at West Kilbride incorporating Seamill, 
from a point 135 metres or thereby south of its junction with Portencross Road, West Kilbride, in a 
southerly direction to a point 60 metres or thereby north of the extended south building line of 
No. 10 Chapelton Mains, Seamill, a distance of 1.9 kilometres or thereby.
