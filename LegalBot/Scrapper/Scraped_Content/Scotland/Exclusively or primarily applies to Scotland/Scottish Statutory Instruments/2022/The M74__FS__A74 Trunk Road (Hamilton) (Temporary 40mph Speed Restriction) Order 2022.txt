The Scottish Ministers make the following Order in exercise of the powers conferred by sections 
2(1), 4(1) and 14(1)(a) and (4) of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(a) and all other powers 
enabling them to do so. 
In accordance with section 14(1)(a) of that Act, they are satisfied that the traffic on the lengths of 
road specified in the schedule should be restricted because works are being or are proposed to be 
executed on or near those lengths of road. 
In accordance with section 14(3) of that Act, they have had regard to the existence of alternative 
routes suitable for the traffic which will be affected by this Order. 
Citation, commencement and cessation 
1. This Order may be cited as the M74/A74 Trunk Road (Hamilton) (Temporary 40mph Speed 
Restriction) Order 2022, comes into force on 10 August 2022 and ceases to have effect on 
14 November 2022. 
Speed restriction 
2. No person may use, or permit the use of, any vehicle on a length of road, or a part or parts of 
a length of road, specified in the schedule of this Order at a speed exceeding 40 miles per hour 
when a restriction is identified as applying to that length of road, or a part or parts of that length of 
road, by means of a traffic sign of the type described in item 1 (diagram 670 – maximum speed 
limit in mph) of the sign table in Part 2 of schedule 10 of the Traffic Signs Regulations and 
General Directions 2016(b).

Exemption 
3. No speed restriction imposed by this Order applies to— 
(a) vehicles falling within regulation 3(4) of the Road Traffic Exemptions (Special Forces) 
(Variation and Amendment) Regulations 2011(a) when used in accordance with 
regulation 3(5) of those Regulations, or 
(b) vehicles being used in an emergency for coastguard purposes.

SCHEDULE 
Article 2 
SPECIFIED LENGTHS OF ROAD 
1. That length of the M74/A74(M) Glasgow – Carlisle Trunk Road being the northbound 
carriageway from the diverge of the off slip road at Junction 6 northwards to the merge with the 
on slip road from Junction 6, a distance of 1.4 kilometres or thereby. 
2. That length of the M74/A74(M) Glasgow – Carlisle Trunk Road being the southbound and 
northbound carriageways from a point directly over the centreline of the pedestrian underpass at 
Junction 6 southwards to the merge with the southbound on slip road at Junction 10 (Wellburn), a 
distance of 18.8 kilometres or thereby. 
3. That length of the M74/A74(M) Glasgow – Carlisle Trunk Road being the southbound off slip 
road to Hamilton at Junction 6 from a point directly over the centreline of the pedestrian underpass 
at Junction 6 southwards to the point where it joins the southbound carriageway of the A723 
Motherwell Road, a distance of 1 kilometre or thereby. 
4. That length of the M74/A74(M) Glasgow – Carlisle Trunk Road being the southbound off slip 
road to Hamilton and Motherwell at Junction 6 from a point directly over the centreline of the 
pedestrian underpass at Junction 6 southwards to the point where it joins the northbound 
carriageway of the A723 Motherwell Road, a distance of 925 metres or thereby. 
5. That length of the M74/A74(M) Glasgow – Carlisle Trunk Road being the northbound off slip 
road to Hamilton and Motherwell at Junction 6 from its diverge from the northbound carriageway 
to the point where it joins the northbound carriageway of the A723 Motherwell Road, a distance of 
1.14 kilometres or thereby. 
6. That length of the M74/A74(M) Glasgow – Carlisle Trunk Road being the northbound off slip 
road to Hamilton and Motherwell at Junction 6 from its diverge from the northbound carriageway 
to the point where it joins the southbound carriageway of the A723 Motherwell Road, a distance 
of 900 metres or thereby. 
7. That length of the M74/A74(M) Glasgow – Carlisle Trunk Road being the northbound 
carriageway from a point 75 metres southeast of a point directly over the centreline of the 
pedestrian underpass at Junction 6 generally north westwards to a point 606 metres west of a point 
directly under the centreline of the A721 Daldowie Drive, a distance of 8.7 kilometres or thereby. 
8. That length of the M74/A74(M) Glasgow – Carlisle Trunk Road being the southbound 
carriageway from a point 615 metres west of a point directly under the centreline of the A721 
Daldowie Drive, generally eastwards to a point 75 metres southeast of a point directly over the 
centreline of the pedestrian underpass at Junction 6, a distance of 8.6 kilometres or thereby. 
9. That length of the M74/A74(M) Glasgow – Carlisle Trunk Road being the northbound on slip 
road from the southbound carriageway of the A723 Motherwell Road at Junction 6, a distance of 
1.2 kilometres or thereby. 
10. That length of the M74/A74(M) Glasgow – Carlisle Trunk Road being the southbound on 
slip road from the southbound carriageway of the A723 Motherwell Road at Junction 6, a distance 
of 850 metres or thereby.
