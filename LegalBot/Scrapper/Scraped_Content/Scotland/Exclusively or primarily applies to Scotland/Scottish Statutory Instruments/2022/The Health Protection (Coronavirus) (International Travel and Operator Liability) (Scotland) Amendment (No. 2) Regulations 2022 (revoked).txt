(This note is not part of the Regulations)
These Regulations amend the Health Protection (Coronavirus) (International Travel and Operator Liability) (Scotland) Regulations 2021 by adding to the list of specified competitions in Part 1 of schedule 6 of those Regulations an entry for DNA (Dynamic New Athletics), an indoor athletics event which is to take place at Emirates Arena in Glasgow on 5 February 2022.
Schedule 6 contains a list of specified competitions, participants in which benefit from exemptions under the Regulations.
