13th December 2022EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order amends the Rehabilitation of Offenders Act 1974 (Exclusions and Exceptions) (Scotland) Order 2013 (“the 2013 Order”). The 2013 Order excludes and makes exceptions to section 4 (effect of rehabilitation) of the Rehabilitation of Offenders Act 1974 which would otherwise prevent a person from having to disclose a spent conviction and protects that person from being prejudiced by that conviction or any failure to disclose it.
Article 2 makes amendments to schedule A1 of the 2013 Order to update the list of offences which must always be disclosed subject to exceptions specified in the 2013 Order.
Article 3 makes amendments to schedule B1 of the 2013 Order to update the list of offences which are to be disclosed subject to rules set out in the 2013 Order.
