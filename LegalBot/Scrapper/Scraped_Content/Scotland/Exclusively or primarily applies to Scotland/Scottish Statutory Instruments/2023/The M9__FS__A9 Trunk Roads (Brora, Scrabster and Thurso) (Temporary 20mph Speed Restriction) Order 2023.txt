The Scottish Ministers make the following Order in exercise of the powers conferred by sections 
2(1), 4(1) and 14(1)(b) and (4) of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(a) and all other powers 
enabling them to do so. 
In accordance with section 14(1)(b) of that Act, they are satisfied that the traffic on the length of 
road specified in the schedule should be restricted because of the likelihood of danger to the 
public, or of serious damage to the road, which is not attributable to works. 
In accordance with section 14(3) of that Act, they have had regard to the existence of alternative 
routes suitable for the traffic which will be affected by this Order. 
Citation, commencement and cessation 
1. This Order may be cited as the M9/A9 Trunk Roads (Brora, Scrabster and Thurso) 
(Temporary 20mph Speed Restriction) Order 2023, comes into force on 25 September 2023 and 
ceases to have effect on 25 March 2025. 
Speed restriction 
2. No person may use, or permit the use of, any vehicle on the lengths of road, or a part or parts 
of the lengths of road, specified in the schedule at a speed exceeding 20 miles per hour when a 
restriction is identified as applying to those lengths of road, or a part or parts of those lengths of 
road, by means of a traffic sign of the type described in item 1 (diagram 670 – maximum speed 
limit in mph) of the sign table in Part 2 of schedule 10 of the Traffic Signs Regulations and 
General Directions 2016(b).

Exemptions 
3. No speed restriction imposed by this Order applies to— 
(a) vehicles falling within regulation 3(4) of the Road Traffic Exemptions (Special Forces) 
(Variation and Amendment) Regulations 2011(a) when used in accordance with 
regulation 3(5) of those Regulations, or 
(b) vehicles being used in an emergency for coastguard purposes.

SCHEDULE 
Article 2 
SPECIFIED LENGTHS OF ROAD 
1. That length of the M9/A9 Edinburgh – Stirling – Thurso Trunk Road at Brora from a point 18 
metres north of the centre line of its junction with the C1095 Clyne – Achrimsdale Road to a point 
115 metres south west of the centre line of its junction with the U2894 Dudgeon Park Road, a 
distance of 1.37 kilometres or thereby. 
2. That length of the M9/A9 Edinburgh – Stirling – Thurso Trunk Road at Scrabster from a point 
142 metres south west of the centre line of its junction with the access to the oil terminal to a point 
276 metres north east of the centre line of its junction to the access to the oil terminal, a distance 
of 418 metres or thereby. 
3. That length of the M9/A9 Edinburgh – Stirling – Thurso Trunk Road at Thurso from a point 10 
metres south east of the centre line of its junction withthe C1081 Janet Street to a point 10 metres 
north west of the centre line of its junction with the C1077 Durness Street, a distance of 645 
metres or thereby.
