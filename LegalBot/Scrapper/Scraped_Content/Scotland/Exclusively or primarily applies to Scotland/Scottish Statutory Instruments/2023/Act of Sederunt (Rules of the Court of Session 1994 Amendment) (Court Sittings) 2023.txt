The Court of Session makes this Act of Sederunt under the powers conferred by section 103(1) of the Courts Reform (Scotland) Act 2014(1) and all other powers enabling it to do so.
(1)
2014 asp 18.Citation and commencement, etc.
1.—(1) This Act of Sederunt may be cited as the Act of Sederunt (Rules of the Court of Session 1994 Amendment) (Court Sittings) 2023.
(2) It comes into force on 31st March 2023.
(3) A certified copy is to be inserted in the Books of Sederunt.Amendment of the Rules of the Court of Session 1994
2.—(1) Rule 10.3 (holiday dates, periods and vacation sitting days) of the Rules of the Court of Session 1994(1) is amended in accordance with this paragraph.
(2) In the definition of “public holiday”, in the column of the Table labelled “2023” below “Monday 1st May 2023” insert “Monday 8th May 2023”.

28th February 2023EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Act of Sederunt)
This Act of Sederunt amends the Rules of the Court of Session 1994 to prescribe Monday 8th May 2023 as a public holiday to commemorate the Coronation of His Majesty King Charles III. It also corrects two dates for vacation sitting days in 2024 which were accorded the incorrect day of the week in an earlier instrument.
