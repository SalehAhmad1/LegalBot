At 12.39 p.m. on 27th June 2023EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Regulations)
These Regulations make amendments to the Council Tax Reduction (Scotland) Regulations 2021 (“the 2021 Regulations”).
Regulation 2 amends regulation 42 of the 2021 Regulations to increase the maximum deduction that may be made, in respect of relevant childcare charges, when calculating the income of a council tax applicant who has an award of Universal Credit.
