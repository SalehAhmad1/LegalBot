14th November 2023EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Regulations)
These Regulations amend the Transport (Scotland) Act 2019 (Commencement No. 7) Regulations 2023 (“the 2023 Regulations”) in order to correct a clerical error.
Regulation 2(2) amends the date appointed by the 2023 Regulations for the coming into force of section 69 of the Transport (Scotland) Act 2019.
