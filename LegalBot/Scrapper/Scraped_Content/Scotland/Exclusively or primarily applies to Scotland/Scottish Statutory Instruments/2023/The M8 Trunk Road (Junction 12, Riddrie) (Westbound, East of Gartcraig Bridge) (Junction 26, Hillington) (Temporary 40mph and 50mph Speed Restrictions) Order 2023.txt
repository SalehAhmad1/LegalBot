The Scottish Ministers make the following Order in exercise of the powers conferred by sections 
2(1), 4(1) and 14(1)(a) and (4) of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(a) and all other powers 
enabling them to do so. 
In accordance with section 14(1)(a) of that Act, they are satisfied that the traffic on the length of 
road specified in the schedule should be restricted because works are being or are proposed to be 
executed on or near that length of road. 
In accordance with section 14(3) of that Act, they have had regard to the existence of alternative 
routes suitable for the traffic which will be affected by this Order. 
Citation, commencement and cessation 
1. This Order may be cited as the M8 Trunk Road (Junction 12, Riddrie) (Westbound, East of 
Gartcraig Bridge) (Junction 26, Hillington) (Temporary 40mph and 50mph Speed Restrictions) 
Order 2023, comes into force on 28 December 2023 and ceases to have effect on 28 April 2024. 
Speed restrictions 
2. No person may use, or permit the use of, any vehicle on the lengths of road, or a part or parts 
of the lengths of road, specified in Part 1 of the schedule at a speed exceeding 40 miles per hour 
when a restriction is identified as applying to those lengths of road, or a part or parts of those 
lengths of road, by means of a traffic sign of the type described in item 1 (diagram 670 – 
maximum speed limit in mph) of the sign table in Part 2 of schedule 10 of the Traffic Signs 
Regulations and General Directions 2016(b). 
3. No person may use, or permit the use of, any vehicle on the lengths of road, or a part or parts 
of the lengths of road, specified in Part 2 of the schedule at a speed exceeding 50 miles per hour

SCHEDULE 
Articles 2 and 3 
SPECIFIED LENGTHS OF ROAD 
PART 1 
1. That length of the M8/A8/A8(M) Edinburgh – Greenock Trunk Road from Junction 12 
eastbound off slip road from a point 114 metres or thereby east from where it diverges from the 
M8 mainline to a point 41 metres or thereby west from the centreline of the A80 (Cumbernauld 
Road) a distance of 122 metres or thereby. 
2. That length of the M8/A8/A8(M) Edinburgh – Greenock Trunk Road being the M8 
westbound from a point 381 metres or thereby east from the centre of Gartcraig road bridge, to a 
point 42 metres or thereby west from the centre of Gartcraig road bridge, a distance of 423 metres 
or thereby. 
PART 2 
3. That length of the M8/A8/A8(M) Edinburgh – Greenock Trunk Road being the westbound 
carriageway from the point where it diverges with the M8 Junction 26 westbound off slip, to a 
point 210 metres or thereby west of the centre of the Hillington Interchange underpass (west side), 
a distance of 540 metres or thereby. 
4. That length of the M8/A8/A8(M) Edinburgh – Greenock Trunk Road being the westbound off 
slip road at M8 Junction 26 from the point where it diverges from the M8 westbound carriageway 
for a distance of 95 metres or thereby.
