The Scottish Ministers make the following Order in exercise of the powers conferred by sections 
2(1), 4(1) and 14(1)(a) and (4) of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(a) and all other powers 
enabling them to do so. 
In accordance with section 14(1)(a) of that Act, they are satisfied that the traffic on the length of 
road specified in the schedule should be restricted because works are being or are proposed to be 
executed on or near that length of road. 
In accordance with section 14(3) of that Act, they have had regard to the existence of alternative 
routes suitable for the traffic which will be affected by this Order. 
Citation, commencement and cessation 
1. This Order may be cited as the A725 Trunk Road (Craighead) (Temporary 40mph Speed 
Restriction) Order 2023, comes into force on 28 December 2023 and ceases to have effect on 28 
April 2024. 
Speed restriction 
2. No person may use, or permit the use of, any vehicle on the lengths of road, or a part or parts 
of the lengths of road, specified in the schedule at a speed exceeding 40 miles per hour when a 
restriction is identified as applying to those lengths of road, or a part or parts of those lengths of 
road, by means of a traffic sign of the type described in item 1 (diagram 670 – maximum speed 
limit in mph) of the sign table in Part 2 of schedule 10 of the Traffic Signs Regulations and 
General Directions 2016(b).

Exemptions 
3. No speed restriction imposed by this Order applies to— 
(a) vehicles falling within regulation 3(4) of the Road Traffic Exemptions (Special Forces) 
(Variation and Amendment) Regulations 2011(a) when used in accordance with 
regulation 3(5) of those Regulations, or 
(b) vehicles being used in an emergency for coastguard purposes.

SCHEDULE 
Article 2 
SPECIFIED LENGTHS OF ROAD 
1. That length of the A725/A726 Shawhead – East Kilbride – Phillipshill Roundabout Trunk 
Road on the eastbound mainline from a point 62 metres or thereby east from the centre of the 
bridge over the A724, to a point where the extended centreline of the slip road from the B7012 
Whistleberry Road to the northbound carriageway of the A725/A726 intersects with the centreline 
of that carriageway, a distance of 682 metres or thereby. 
2. That length of the A725/A726 Shawhead – East Kilbride – Phillipshill Roundabout Trunk 
Road being the full length of eastbound on slip road, from the point where it diverges from the 
A724 (Glasgow Road) to a point where it merges with the A725 eastbound mainline, a distance of 
180 metres or thereby.
