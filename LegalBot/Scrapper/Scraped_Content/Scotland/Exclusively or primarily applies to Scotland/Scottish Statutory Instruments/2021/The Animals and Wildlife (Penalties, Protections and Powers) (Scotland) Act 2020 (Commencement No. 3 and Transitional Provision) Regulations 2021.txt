(1)
2020 asp 14.Citation, commencement and interpretation
1.—(1) These Regulations may be cited as the Animals and Wildlife (Penalties, Protections and Powers) (Scotland) Act 2020 (Commencement No. 3 and Transitional Provision) Regulations 2021 and come into force on 30 September 2021.
(2) In these Regulations—
“the 2006 Act” means the Animal Health and Welfare (Scotland) Act 2006(1),
“the 2020 Act” means the Animals and Wildlife (Penalties, Protections and Powers) (Scotland) Act 2020.

7th September 2021EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Regulations)
These Regulations bring into force the provisions of the of the Animals and Wildlife (Penalties, Protections and Powers) (Scotland) Act 2020 (“the 2020 Act”) which have not previously been commenced.
Regulation 2 brings into force sections 4, 5 and 19 to 21 of the 2020 Act on 30 September 2021. Regulation 3 makes transitional provision in relation to the commencement of section 21 (disposal orders where animals taken: restriction of application) of the 2020 Act.
The Bill for the 2020 Act received Royal Assent on 21 July 2020. Sections 17, 22 and 23 came into force the following day.NOTE AS TO EARLIER COMMENCEMENT REGULATIONS
