9th December 2021EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Regulations)
These Regulations amend the Early Years Assistance (Best Start Grants) (Scotland) Regulations 2018 (S.S.I. 2018/370), the Funeral Expense Assistance (Scotland) Regulations 2019 (S.S.I. 2019/292), the Carer’s Assistance (Young Carer Grants) (Scotland) Regulations 2019 (S.S.I. 2019/324), and the Winter Heating Assistance for Children and Young People (Scotland) Regulations 2020 (S.S.I. 2020/352).
The amendments clarify when the period for re-determination begins in a case where the First-tier Tribunal has decided that a request for re-determination has been validly made.
