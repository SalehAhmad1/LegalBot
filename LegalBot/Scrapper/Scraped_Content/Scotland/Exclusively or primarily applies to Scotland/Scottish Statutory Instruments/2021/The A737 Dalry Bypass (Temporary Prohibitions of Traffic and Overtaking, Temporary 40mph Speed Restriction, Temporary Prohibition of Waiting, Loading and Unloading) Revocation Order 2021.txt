The Scottish Ministers make the following Order in exercise of the powers conferred by sections 
2(1), 4(1), 14(1)(a) and (4), 15(2) and 124(1)(d) of, and paragraph 27(1) of schedule 9 of, the 
Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(a) and all other powers enabling them to do so. 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as the A737 Dalry Bypass (Temporary Prohibitions of Traffic and 
Overtaking, Temporary 40mph Speed Restriction, Temporary Prohibition of Waiting, Loading and 
Unloading) Revocation Order 2021 and comes into force on 16 September 2021. 
Revocation 
2. The A737 Dalry Bypass (Temporary Prohibitions of Traffic and Overtaking, Temporary 
40mph Speed Restriction, Temporary Prohibition of Waiting, Loading and Unloading) Order 
2017(b) is revoked.
