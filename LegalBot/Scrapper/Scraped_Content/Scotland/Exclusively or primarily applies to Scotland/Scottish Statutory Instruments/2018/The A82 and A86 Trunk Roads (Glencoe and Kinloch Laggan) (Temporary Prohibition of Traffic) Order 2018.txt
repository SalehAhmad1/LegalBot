The Scottish Ministers make the following Order in exercise of the powers conferred by section 
62(1) of the Roads (Scotland) Act 1984(a) and all other powers enabling them to do so. 
In accordance with section 62(1) of that Act, they are of the opinion that considerations of public 
safety or convenience require that traffic should be temporarily prohibited on the length of road 
specified in the schedule while filming on the road takes place. 
Citation, commencement and cessation 
1. This Order may be cited as the A82 and A86 Trunk Roads (Glencoe and Kinloch Laggan) 
(Temporary Prohibition of Traffic) Order 2018, comes into force on 7 November 2018 and ceases 
to have effect on 9 November 2018. 
Prohibition on use of vehicles 
2. No person may use, or permit the use of, any vehicle on the lengths of road, or a part or parts 
of the lengths of road, specified in the schedule of this Order when a prohibition is identified as 
applying to those lengths of road, or a part or parts of those lengths of road, by means of a traffic 
sign of the type described in item 8 (diagram 7105 – position of barrier to mark length of road 
closed or to guide traffic past an obstruction) of the sign table in Part 6 of schedule 13 of the 
Traffic Signs Regulations and General Directions 2016(b). 
Exemptions 
3. No prohibition imposed by this Order applies to a vehicle being used— 
(a) in an emergency for fire brigade, ambulance, police or coastguard purposes; 
(b) in connection with the filming on the road; or

SCHEDULE 
Article 2 
SPECIFIED LENGTHS OF ROAD 
1. That length of the A82 Dalnottar – Inverness Trunk Road from a point 5.3 kilometres or 
thereby west of its junction with the C1094 Glen Etive Road junction in a generally westwards 
direction to a point 8.5 kilometres or thereby west of the C1094 Glen Etive Road junction, a 
distance of 3.2 kilometres or thereby. 
2. That length of the A86 Spean Bridge – Kingussie Trunk Road from its junction with the 
unclassified road leading to Ardverikie Estate, Kinloch Laggan in a generally south westwards 
direction to the junction with the unclassified access road to Laggan Dam, a distance of 20.4 
kilometres or thereby.
