The Scottish Ministers make the following Order in exercise of the powers conferred by sections  
84(1)(a) and (c) and 124(1)(d) of, and paragraph 27 of schedule 9 of, the Road Traffic Regulation 
Act 1984(a), and all other powers enabling them to do so. 
In accordance with section 84(2)(b) of that Act, they have given public notice of their intention to 
make the following order, and have complied with Parts I and III of the Secretary of State’s 
Traffic Orders (Procedure) (Scotland) Regulations 1987(c). 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as the A90/A9000 Trunk Roads (Forth Road Bridge, and 
Queensferry Crossing and Forth Road Bridge Approach Roads) (Speed Limits) Order 2018, and 
comes into force on 24th April 2018. 
Interpretation 
2. In this Order— 
“A90” means that part of the M90/A90 Edinburgh – Fraserburgh Trunk Road at Ferrytoll 
Junction and from South Queensferry to Dalmeny; 
“A9000” means the A9000 Dalmeny – Inverkeithing Trunk Road; 
“M90” means that part of the M90/A90 Edinburgh – Fraserburgh Trunk Road from Ferrytoll 
Junction to Queensferry Junction.

Speed restrictions 
3.—(1) No person may drive any motor vehicle at a speed exceeding 30 miles per hour on the 
lengths of road specified in Part 1 of the schedule of this Order. 
(2) No person may drive any motor vehicle at a speed exceeding 40 miles per hour on the 
lengths of road specified in Part 2 of the schedule. 
(3) No person may drive any motor vehicle at a speed exceeding 50 miles per hour on the 
lengths of road specified in Part 3 of the schedule. 
(4) No person may drive any motor vehicle on the lengths of road specified in Part 4 of the 
schedule, at a speed exceeding 30 miles per hour or 40 miles per hour, as the case may be, when 
that particular speed limit is identified as applying to those lengths of road or part or parts of those 
lengths of road by means of an appropriate traffic sign of the type described in item 1 (diagram 
670 – maximum speed limit in mph) of the sign table in Part 2 of schedule 10 of the Traffic Signs 
Regulations and General Directions 2016(a). 
Exemption  
4. No speed limit imposed by this Order applies to— 
(a) vehicles falling within regulation 3(4) of the Road Traffic Exemptions (Special Forces) 
(Variation and Amendment) Regulations 2011(b) when used in accordance with 
regulation 3(5) of those Regulations; or 
(b) vehicles being used in an emergency for coastguard purposes. 
Revocation 
5. The following Orders are revoked— 
(a) The Lothian Regional Council and Fife Regional Council (Forth Road Bridge) (50 mph 
Speed Limit) Order 1992(c); 
(b) The City of Edinburgh Council (M9 Spur / A90, Queensferry Road, Edinburgh) (50 mph 
Speed Limit) Order 2006(d); 
(c) The City of Edinburgh Council and the Forth Estuary Transport Authority (A90 
Queensferry Road at Forth Road Bridge) (Variable Speed Limit) Order 2008(e); and 
(d) The Fife Council (A90 Northbound – 50 m.p.h. Speed Limit) Order 2009(f).
