COURT OF SESSION
Act of Sederunt (Rules of the Court of Session 1994 Amendment) (Sanctions and Anti-Money Laundering) (No.2) 2019
Made
19th March 2019
Laid before the Scottish Parliament
20th March 2019
Coming into force
29th March 2019
The Court of Session makes this Act of Sederunt under the powers conferred by sections 66 and 67 of the Counter-Terrorism Act 2008(1), section 103(1) of the Courts Reform (Scotland) Act 2014(2) and all other powers enabling it to do so.
(1)
2008 c.28. Sections 66 and 67 of the Counter-Terrorism Act 2008 are applied by section 28(4) of the Terrorist Asset-Freezing etc. Act 2010 (c.38).
(2)
2014 asp 18.Citation and commencement, etc.
1.—(1) This Act of Sederunt may be cited as the Act of Sederunt (Rules of the Court of Session 1994 Amendment) (Sanctions and Anti-Money Laundering) (No.2) 2019.
(2) It comes into force on 29th March 2019.
(3) A certified copy is to be inserted in the Books of Sederunt.Amendment of the Act of Sederunt (Rules of the Court of Session 1994 Amendment) (Sanctions and Anti-Money Laundering) 2019
2.—(1) The Act of Sederunt (Rules of the Court of Session 1994 Amendment) (Sanctions and Anti-Money Laundering) 2019(1) is amended in accordance with this paragraph.
(2) Before paragraph 3(1) (revocation) insert—
“(A1) Chapter 101 of the Rules of the Court of Session 1994 (terrorist asset-freezing)(2) is revoked.”.
(1)
S.S.I. 2019/72.
(2)
The Rules of the Court of Session 1994 are in schedule 2 of the Act of Sederunt (Rules of the Court of Session 1994) 1994 (S.I. 1994/1443, last amended by S.S.I. 2019/85). Chapter 101 was inserted by S.S.I. 2010/459 and amended by S.S.I. 2011/385.CJM SUTHERLAND

19th March 2019EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Act of Sederunt)
This Act of Sederunt amends Act of Sederunt (Rules of the Court of Session 1994 Amendment) (Sanctions and Anti-Money Laundering) 2019 to insert provision to revoke Chapter 101 of the Rules of the Court of Session 1994 which should have been included in that Act of Sederunt.
