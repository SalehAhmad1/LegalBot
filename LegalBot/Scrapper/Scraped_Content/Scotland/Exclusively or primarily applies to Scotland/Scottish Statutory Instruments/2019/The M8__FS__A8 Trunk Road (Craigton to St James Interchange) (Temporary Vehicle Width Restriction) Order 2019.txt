The Scottish Ministers make the following Order in exercise of the powers conferred by sections 
2(1) and (2), 4(1), and 14(1)(a) and (4) of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(a) and all other 
powers enabling them to do so. 
In accordance with section 14(1)(a) of that Act, they are satisfied that the traffic on the length of 
road specified in the schedule should be restricted or prohibited because works are being or are 
proposed to be executed on or near that length of road. 
In accordance with section 14(3) of that Act, they have had regard to the existence of alternative 
routes suitable for the traffic which will be affected by this Order. 
Citation, commencement and cessation 
1. This Order may be cited as the M8/A8 Trunk Road (Craigton to St James Interchange) 
(Temporary Vehicle Width Restriction) Order 2019, comes into force on 22 May 2019 and ceases 
to have effect on 31 October 2019. 
Width Restriction 
2. No person may use, or permit the use of, any vehicle on the length of road specified in the 
schedule if the width of the vehicle exceeds 2 metres when such restriction is identified as 
applying to any part or parts of that length of road by means of a traffic sign as prescribed in item 
14 (diagram 7243 – temporary road layout with lane restrictions) of Part 6 of schedule 13 of the 
Traffic Signs Regulations and General Directions 2016(b).

Exemptions 
3. No prohibition imposed by this Order applies to— 
(a) a vehicle while being used in an emergency for fire brigade, coastguard, ambulance or 
police purposes; or 
(b) a vehicle being used for carrying out the works.

SCHEDULE 
Article 2 
SPECIFIED LENGTH OF ROAD 
That length of the M8/A8 Edinburgh – Greenock Trunk Road being the westbound carriageway 
from a point 2.2 kilometres or thereby west of junction 29 (St James Interchange) on ramp to the 
off ramp at junction 30 (Craigton), a distance of 2.5 kilometres or thereby. 
That length of the M8/A8 Edinburgh – Greenock Trunk Road being the eastbound from the off 
slip of junction 30 (Craigton) to a point 2.2 kilometres or thereby west of junction 29 (St James 
Interchange) off ramp, a distance of 2.5 kilometres or thereby.
