The Scottish Ministers make the following Order in exercise of the powers conferred by sections 
2(1), 4(1), 14(1)(b) and (4) and 124(1)(d) of, and paragraph 27 of schedule 9 of, the Road Traffic 
Regulation Act 1984(a) and all other powers enabling them to do so. 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as the A725 Trunk Road (Raith) (Temporary Prohibition of Specified 
Classes of Traffic and Pedestrians) Revocation Order 2019 and comes into force on 3 October 
2019. 
Revocation 
2. The A725 Trunk Road (Raith) (Temporary Prohibition of Specified Classes of Traffic and 
Pedestrians) Order 2018(b) is revoked.
