The Secretary of State has decided that it is necessary in the public interest to amend the 
restriction of flying for the Scampton Restricted Zone EG R313. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Scampton) 
(Restricted Zone EG R313) (Amendment) (No. 2) Regulations 2023 and they come into force on 
22nd November 2023. 
Amendment of the Regulations 
2.—(1) The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Scampton) (Restricted Zone EG R313) 
Regulations 2022(b) are amended as follows. 
(2) In regulation 3(1), for “between the hours of 0830 to 1700 on each of the five days of 
Monday to Friday every week, or as notified by Notice to Aviation (NOTAM) (c) outside these 
hours” substitute “as notified by Notice to Aviation (NOTAM) (c)”.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations amend the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Scampton) (Restricted Zone 
EG R313) Regulations 2022 (S.I. 2022/1221, as amended by S.I. 2022/1320 and S.I. 2023/234) by 
amending the specific times of the restricted airspace. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Aeronautical Information Circular 
(AIC) and Notice to Aviation (NOTAM).
