(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order, made by the Welsh Ministers, brings into force certain provisions of the Agriculture (Wales) Act 2023(1) (“the Act”).
Article 2 of this Order brings the following provisions of the Act into force on 17 October 2023—
(a)
sections 21 to 23 (Intervention in agricultural markets);
(b)
sections 25-33 (Collection and sharing of data);
(c)
section 34 (Marketing Standards);
(d)
section 35 (Carcass classification);
(e)
section 55 (Consequential amendments and repeals etc.);
(f)
Schedule 1 (Agricultural products relevant to marketing standards provisions);
(g)
Schedule 2 (Minor and Consequential Amendments etc. relating to Parts 1 to 3) to the extent that it is not already in force;
(h)
Schedule 3 (Consequential amendments etc. to the CMO Regulations).
Article 3 of this Order brings the following provisions into force on 1 April 2024—
(a)
sections 36-44 (Forestry) to the extent they are not already in force.
Article 4 provides saving provisions in relation to regulations or declarations made under existing provisions in Schedule 5 to the Agriculture Act 2020 which is repealed by the Act.
