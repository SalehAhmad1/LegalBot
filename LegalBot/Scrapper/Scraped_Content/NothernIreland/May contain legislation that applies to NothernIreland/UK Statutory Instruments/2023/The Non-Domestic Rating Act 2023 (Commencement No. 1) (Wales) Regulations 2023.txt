8 November 2023EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Regulations)
These Regulations are the first commencement regulations made by the Welsh Ministers under the Non-Domestic Rating Act 2023 (“the 2023 Act”). Regulation 2 brings into force certain provisions in section 15 of the 2023 Act in relation to the non-domestic rating multiplier in Wales on 21 November 2023.
