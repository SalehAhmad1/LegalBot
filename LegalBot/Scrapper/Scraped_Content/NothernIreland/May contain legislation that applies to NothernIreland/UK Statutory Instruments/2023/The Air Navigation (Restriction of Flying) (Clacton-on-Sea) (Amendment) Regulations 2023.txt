The Secretary of State has decided that it is necessary in the public interest to amend the 
restriction of flying of aircraft in the area specified in regulation 3(1) by reason of the intended 
holding there of flying displays associated with the Clacton Air Show. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Clacton-on-
Sea) (Amendment) Regulations 2023 and they come into force on 24th August 2023. 
Amendment of the Regulations 
2. The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Clacton-on-Sea) Regulations 2023(b) are 
amended as follows. 
3. In regulation 3(1), for “5,500 feet above mean sea level” substitute “3,500 feet above mean 
sea level”.
