The Secretary of State has decided that it is in the public interest to amend the restriction of the 
flying of unmanned aircraft in the vicinity of Greenock, Scotland. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Greenock) 
(Amendment) Regulations 2023 and they come into force on 10th March 2023. 
Amendment 
2.—(1) The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Greenock) Regulations 2023(b) are 
amended as follows. 
3. In regulation 3 paragraph 1 for “between 12.00 hours on 10th March 2023 and 12.00 hours on 
12th March 2023” substitute “between 0600 hours on 12th March 2023 and 23.59 hours on 14th 
March 2023”.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations amend the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Greenock) Regulations 2023 
(S.I. 2023/282). They amend the timings and dates of the flying restriction. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Briefing Sheet and Notice to 
Aviation (NOTAM).
