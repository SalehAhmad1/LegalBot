The Secretary of State has decided that it is necessary in the public interest to amend the 
restriction of flying for the Jet Formation Display Teams. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Jet Formation 
Display Teams) (Amendment) Regulations 2023 and they come into force on 20th July 2023. 
Amendment of the Regulations 
2.—(1) The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Jet Formation Display Teams) Regulations 
2023(b) are amended as follows. 
(2) In Table 1, insert the row “Shrivenham 513557N 0013736W 6,500 feet 20th July 2023”.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations amend the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Jet Formation Display 
Teams) Regulations 2023 (S.I. 2023/486) by adding an additional flying display at Shrivenham on 
20th July 2023. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Notice to Aviation (NOTAM).
