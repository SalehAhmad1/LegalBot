The Secretary of State has decided that it is necessary in the public interest to amend the specific 
timings of the flying restrictions in Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Stansted and Central 
London) (No. 2) Regulations 2023(a). 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(b). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Stansted and 
Central London) (No. 2) (Amendment) Regulations 2023 and they come into force on 31st 
October 2023. 
Amendment of the Regulations 
2.—(1) The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Stansted and Central London) (No. 2) 
Regulations 2023 are amended as follows. 
(2) In regulation 3— 
(a) in paragraph (1), for “2200 hours and 2300 hours” substitute “1945 hours and 2115 
hours”, 
(b) in paragraph (2), for “2200 hours and 2300 hours” substitute “2015 hours and 2115 
hours”, and 
(c) in paragraph (4), for “2200 hours” substitute “2015 hours”.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations amend the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Stansted and Central 
London) (No. 2) Regulations 2023 (S.I. 2023/1122) by amending the specific times of the 
restrictions. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Notice to Aviation (NOTAM).
