EDUCATION, WALES
The Education (Inspectors of Education and Training in Wales) Order 2023
Made
19th July 2023
Coming into force
26th July 2023
At the Court at Buckingham Palace, the 19th day of July 2023
Present,
The King’s Most Excellent Majesty in Council
His Majesty, in exercise of the powers conferred by section 19(2) of the Education Act 2005(1) is pleased, by and with the advice of His Privy Council, to order as follows:
(1)
2005 c. 18. The functions of the National Assembly for Wales under that section were transferred to the Welsh Ministers by paragraph 30 of Schedule 11 to the Government of Wales Act 2006 (c. 32).Citation and commencement
1.  This Order may be cited as the Education (Inspectors of Education and Training in Wales) Order 2023 and comes into force on 26th July 2023.Appointment of His Majesty’s Inspectors of Education and Training in Wales or Arolygwyr Ei Fawrhydi dros Addysg a Hyfforddiant yng Nghymru
2.  The persons named in the Schedule to this Order are appointed as His Majesty’s Inspectors of Education and Training in Wales or Arolygwyr Ei Fawrhydi dros Addysg a Hyfforddiant yng Nghymru.Ceri King
