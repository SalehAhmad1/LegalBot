STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS
2020 No. 1537 (C. 44)
CRIMINAL LAW, ENGLAND AND WALES
The Prisoners (Disclosure of Information About Victims) Act 2020 (Commencement) Regulations 2020
Made
9th December 2020
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by section 3(2) of the Prisoners (Disclosure of Information About Victims) Act 2020(1).
(1)
2020 c. 19.Citation
1.  These Regulations may be cited as the Prisoners (Disclosure of Information About Victims) Act 2020 (Commencement) Regulations 2020.Commencement
2.  Sections 1 and 2 of the Prisoners (Disclosure of Information About Victims) Act 2020 come into force on 4th January 2021.Robert Buckland
Secretary of State for Justice
Ministry of Justice
9th December 2020EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Regulations)
These Regulations bring all the remaining uncommenced provisions of the Prisoners (Disclosure of Information About Victims) Act 2020 into force on 4 January 2021 (section 3 having come into force the day the Act was passed).
