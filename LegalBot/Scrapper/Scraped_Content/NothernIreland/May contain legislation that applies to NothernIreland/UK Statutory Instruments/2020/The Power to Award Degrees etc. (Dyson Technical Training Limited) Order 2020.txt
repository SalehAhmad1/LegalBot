The Power to Award Degrees etc. (Dyson Technical Training Limited) Order 2020
Made
27th November 2020
Coming into force

The Office for Students, in exercise of the powers conferred by section 42(1) of the Higher Education and Research Act 2017(1), makes the following Order:
(1)
2017 c.29.Citation and commencement
1.  This Order may be cited as the Power to Award Degrees etc. (Dyson Technical Training Limited) Order 2020 and shall come into force on 1st September 2021.
Commencement Information
I1
Art. 1 in force at 1.9.2021, see art. 1Competence to grant awards
[F12.  Dyson Technical Training Limited shall be competent to grant the following specified taught awards of the kind mentioned in section 42(2)(b) of the said Act:
(a)
BEng in Engineering;
(b)
BEng (Hons) in Engineering;
(c)
BEng (Hons) in Engineering Degree Apprenticeship;
(d)
CertHE in Engineering; and
(e)
DipHE in Engineering.]

For the Office for StudentsEXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order, made under section 42(1) of the Higher Education and Research Act 2017 authorises Dyson Technical Training Limited with the power to award specified taught awards for a three- year and three-month fixed term expiring on 30th November 2024. This Order will come into force on 1st September 2021.
An impact assessment has not been produced for this instrument as it has no impact on businesses and civil society organisations. The instrument has no impact on the public sector.
