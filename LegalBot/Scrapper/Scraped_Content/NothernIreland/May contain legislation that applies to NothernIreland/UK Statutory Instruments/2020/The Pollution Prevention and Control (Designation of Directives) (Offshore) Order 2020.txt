The Secretary of State, in exercise of the powers conferred by section 7(9)(a) and (b) of, and paragraph 20(2)(c) of Schedule 1 to, the Pollution Prevention and Control Act 1999(1), makes the following Order:
(1)
1999 c. 24. Paragraph 20(2)(c) was amended by S.I. 2011/1043 and by S.I. 2018/942.Citation, commencement, application and interpretation
1.—(1) This Order may be cited as the Pollution Prevention and Control (Designation of Directives) (Offshore) Order 2020 and comes into force on the day after the day on which it is made.
(2) In article 2, paragraphs (a) to (d), (f) and (g) apply—
(a)
to the United Kingdom territorial sea adjacent to—
(i)
Northern Ireland; and
(ii)
Scotland, except that part which extends seaward for 3 nautical miles from the landward baseline; and
(b)
to the sea in any designated area within the meaning of the 1964 Act,
including the places above the sea and the bed and subsoil of the sea.
(3) In article 2, paragraph (e) of article 2 applies—
(a)
to the United Kingdom territorial sea adjacent to Northern Ireland; and
(b)
to the sea in any designated area within the meaning of the 1964 Act,
including the places above the sea and the bed and subsoil of the sea.
(4) In this Order—
“the 1964 Act” means the Continental Shelf Act 1964(1);
“the 1999 Act” means the Pollution Prevention and Control Act 1999; and
“baseline” means a baseline established by any Order in Council under section 1(1)(b) of the Territorial Sea 1987(2).

21st October 2020EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order designates a number of directives of the Council of the European Union that include measures relating to the limitation of emissions of certain pollutants as relevant directives for the purposes of paragraph 20(1)(b) of Schedule 1 to the Pollution Prevention and Control Act 1999 (c. 24) (“the 1999 Act”) to apply in respect of the areas set out in article 1(2) and 1(3).
The effect of designation is that regulations may be made under section 2 of the 1999 Act (to regulate, prevent and control activities which are capable of causing environmental pollution) which make provision which corresponds or is similar to any provision made, or capable of being made, under section 2(2) of the European Communities Act 1972 (c. 68) in connection with the directives designated by this order.
An impact assessment has not been produced for this instrument as no, or no significant, impact on the private, voluntary or public sectors is foreseen.
