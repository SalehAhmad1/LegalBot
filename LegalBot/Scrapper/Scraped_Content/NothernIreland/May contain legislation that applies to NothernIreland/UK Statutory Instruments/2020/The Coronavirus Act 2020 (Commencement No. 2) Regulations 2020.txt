At 3.30 p.m. on 31st March 2020EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Regulations)
These Regulations bring section 15 of, and Part 1 of Schedule 12 to the Coronavirus Act 2020 (“the Act”) into force so that local authorities in England do not have to comply with certain duties in relation to meeting needs, and carrying out assessments, under the Care Act 2014, and to modify duties to meet needs under the Care Act 2014, until such time as regulations are in force under section 88 of the Act (power to suspend and revive provisions of the Act), or the Act is no longer in force.NOTE AS TO EARLIER COMMENCEMENT REGULATIONS
