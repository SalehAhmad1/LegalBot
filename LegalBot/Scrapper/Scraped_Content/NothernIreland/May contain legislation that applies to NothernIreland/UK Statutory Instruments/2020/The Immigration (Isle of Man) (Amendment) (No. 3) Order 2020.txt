(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order amends the Immigration (Isle of Man) Order 2008 (S.I. 2008/680) so as to extend certain provisions of the Immigration and Social Security Co-ordination (EU Withdrawal) Act 2020 to the Isle of Man to reflect the withdrawal of the United Kingdom from the European Union, principally ending rights to free movement of persons under retained EU law and for connected purposes.
