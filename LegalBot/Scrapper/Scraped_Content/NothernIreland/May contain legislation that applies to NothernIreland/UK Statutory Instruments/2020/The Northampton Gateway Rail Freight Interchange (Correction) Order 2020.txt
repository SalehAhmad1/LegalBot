INFRASTRUCTURE PLANNING
The Northampton Gateway Rail Freight Interchange (Correction) Order 2020
Made
6th October 2020
Coming into force
7th October 2020
The Northampton Gateway Rail Freight Interchange Order 2019(1) (“the Northampton Gateway RFI Order”) contains correctable errors as defined in paragraph 1(3) of Schedule 4 to the Planning Act 2008(2) (“the Act”).
Before the end of the relevant period referred to in paragraph 1(6)(a) of Schedule 4 to the Act, the Secretary of State received a request in writing to correct errors in the Northampton Gateway RFI Order and, in accordance with paragraph 1(7) of Schedule 4 to the Act, the Secretary of State has informed each local planning authority for the area in which the land to which the Northampton Gateway RFI Order relates is situated that the request has been received.
The Secretary of State, in exercise of the powers conferred by section 119 of, and paragraph 1(4) and (8) of Schedule 4 to, the Act, makes the following Order—
(1)
S.I. 2019/1358.
(2)
2008 c. 29.Citation and Commencement
1.  This Order may be cited as the Northampton Gateway Rail Freight Interchange (Correction) Order 2020 and comes into force on 7th October 2020.Citation and Commencement
2.—(1) The Northampton Gateway RFI Order is amended as follows—
(2) In article 23(1) (guarantees in respect of payment of compensation), for “33” substitute “32”.
(3) In article 34(3)(b) (temporary use of land for carrying out the authorised development), after “in relation to that”, for “and” substitute “land”.
(4) In Schedule 16 (certification of plans and documents), in relation to the entry in the table for “the speed limit plans”, in the third column of that table (document date/plan number with revision number)—
(a)
for Sheet 2, replace the plan number with “NGW-BWB-LSI-02-DR-C-00192-P6”; and
(b)
for Sheet 4, replace the plan number with “NGW-BWB-LSI-04-DR-C-00194-P5”.Signed by authority of the Secretary of State for Transport

6th October 2020EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order corrects errors in the Northampton Gateway Rail Freight Interchange Order 2019 (S.I. 2020/1358), a development consent order made under the Planning Act 2008, following a request made under paragraph 1(5)(a) of Schedule 4 to that Act.
