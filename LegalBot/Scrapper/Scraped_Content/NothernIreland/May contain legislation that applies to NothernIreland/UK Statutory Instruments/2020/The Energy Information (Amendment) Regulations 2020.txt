At 1.25 p.m. on 2nd November 2020EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Regulations)
This instrument amends the Energy Information Regulations 2011 (S.I. 2011/1524) in relation to household combined washer-dryers, household washing machines, household dishwashers, household refrigerating appliances and electronic displays. It substitutes new Commission Delegated Regulations into the list of EU measures in Schedule 1 to the Energy Information Regulations 2011 which allows them to be enforced by the market surveillance authorities.
A full impact assessment has not been produced for this instrument as no, or no significant, impact on the private, voluntary or public sector is foreseen.
