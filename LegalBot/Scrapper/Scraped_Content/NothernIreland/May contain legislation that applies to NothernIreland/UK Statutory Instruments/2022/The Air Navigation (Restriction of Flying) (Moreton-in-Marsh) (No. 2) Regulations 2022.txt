The Secretary of State has decided that it is in the public interest to restrict flying in the area 
specified in regulation 3(1) by reason of the intended holding there of unmanned aircraft trials. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Moreton-in-
Marsh) (No. 2) Regulations 2022 and they come into force on 18th October 2022. 
Interpretation 
2. In regulation 3(1) the times referred to are Co-ordinated Universal Time(b). 
Restricted airspace 
3.—(1) Subject to paragraph (2), between 0800 hours and 2000 hours on each of the ten days 
beginning 18th October 2022 and ending on 27th October 2022 no aircraft is to fly below 2,000 
feet above mean sea level within the area bounded by a circle having a radius of 1 nautical mile 
whose centre is at 515931N 0014037W. 
(2) Paragraph (1) does not apply to any aircraft— 
(a) operated by or on behalf of— 
(i) the National Police Air Service, 
(ii) the Helicopter Emergency Medical Service, or 
(iii) the Maritime and Coastguard Agency for the purpose of search and rescue only, or 
(b) flying in accordance with a permission granted by AirCordon Ltd.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations impose restrictions on flying in the vicinity of Moreton-in-Marsh during 
unmanned aircraft trials that are scheduled to take place there on the ten days beginning on 18th 
October and ending on 27th October 2022 
Multiple unmanned aircraft will be involved in manoeuvres during the trials and therefore the 
Civil Aviation Authority and the Department for Transport agree that flying should be restricted in 
the vicinity of the event for reasons of public safety. 
The times mentioned in regulation 3(1) are Co-ordinated Universal Time (UTC) which is one hour 
behind British Summer Time (BST). 
In relation to the permission mentioned in regulation 3(2)(b), AirCordon Ltd may be contacted via 
enquiries@aircordon.com or on +44 (0)7960 009845.
