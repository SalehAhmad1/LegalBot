The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a).

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations impose restrictions on flying in the vicinity of Scarborough, North Yorkshire 
during an air display associated with the Armed Forces Day (National Event) 2022 that is 
scheduled to take place on 25th June 2022. Many aircraft will be involved in high energy 
manoeuvres during the display and therefore the Civil Aviation Authority and Department for 
Transport agree that flying should be restricted in the vicinity of the event for reasons of public 
safety. 
The times mentioned in regulation 3(1) are Co-ordinated Universal Time (UTC) which is one hour 
behind British Summer Time (BST). 
In relation to the permission mentioned in regulation 3(2), the Flying Display Director of the 
Armed Forces Day (National Event) may be contacted as notified. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Aeronautical Information Circular 
(AIC) and Notice to Aviation (NOTAM).
