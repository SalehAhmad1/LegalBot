ROAD TRAFFIC, WALES 
The A483 Trunk Road (Llanelwedd, 
Powys) (Restricted Road) Order 
2022 
Made 
13 April 2022 
Coming into force 
15 April 2022 
The Welsh Ministers, as traffic authority for the 
relevant length of the A483 trunk road, in exercise of 
the powers conferred upon them by sections 82(2), 
83(1), and 124 of, and paragraph 27 of Schedule 9 to, 
the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(1) make this 
Order. 
The Welsh Ministers have consulted with the Chief 
Officer of Dyfed-Powys Police and in accordance with 
section 83(1) have given public notice of their 
intention to make this Order. 
Title and commencement  
1. The title of this Order is the A483 Trunk Road 
(Llanelwedd, Powys) (Restricted Road) Order 2022 
and this Order comes into force on 15 April 2022. 
Direction  
2. The length of the trunk road specified in the 
Schedule to this Order shall be a restricted road for the 
purposes of section 81 of the Road Traffic Regulation 
Act 1984. 
                                                                               
(1) 
1984 c. 27; sections 82(2) and 83(1) were amended by the 
New Roads and Street Works Act 1991 (c. 22), section 168 
and Schedule 8, paragraphs 59(3) and 60(2). By virtue of S.I. 
1999/672 and section 162 of, and paragraph 30 of Schedule 
11 to, the Government of Wales Act 2006 (c. 32), these 
powers are now exercisable by the Welsh Ministers in 
relation to Wales. 
 
2
Revocation  
3. The A483 Trunk Road (Llanelwedd, Powys) (De-
Restriction & 40 MPH Speed Limit) Order 2005(1) is 
hereby revoked.

A470/A483 Llanelwedd Roundabout to a point 305 
metres north-east of that said roundabout.
