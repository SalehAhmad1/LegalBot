Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Helicopter 
Flight) (No. 6) Regulations 2022 and they come into force on 3rd October 2022. 
Interpretation 
2. In regulation 3(1) and 3(2) the times referred to are Co-ordinated Universal Time(b).

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations impose restrictions on flying in the vicinity of Birkhall, Royal Deeside, 
Aberdeenshire and Edinburgh by reason of two intended helicopter flights. These Regulations

The Civil Aviation Authority and the Department for Transport agree that it is in the public 
interest to restrict flying in the vicinity of the relevant areas to avoid the risk that transient aircraft 
may disrupt the flights. 
The times mentioned in regulation 3 are Co-ordinated Universal Time (UTC) which is one hour 
behind British Summer Time (BST). 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Briefing Sheet and Notice to
