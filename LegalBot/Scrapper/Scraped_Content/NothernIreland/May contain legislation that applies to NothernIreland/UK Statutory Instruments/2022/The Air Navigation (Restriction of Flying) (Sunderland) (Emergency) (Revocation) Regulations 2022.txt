The Secretary of State has decided that it is necessary in the public interest to revoke the 
restriction on the flying of unmanned aircraft in the vicinity of Sunderland as this is no longer 
needed. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and Commencement 
1. These regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Sunderland) 
(Emergency) (Revocation) Regulations 2022 and they come into force with immediate effect. 
Revocation of Regulations 
2. The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Sunderland) (Emergency) Regulations 2022(b) 
are revoked.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations revoke the statutory instrument which imposed restrictions on the flying of 
unmanned aircraft in the vicinity of Sunderland (S.I. 2022/604). Those Regulations are no longer 
considered necessary and are revoked.
