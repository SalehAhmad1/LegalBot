The Secretary of State has decided that it is necessary in the public interest to amend the restrict 
on flying in the vicinity of Sandringham House, Norfolk, having regard to the security 
considerations associated with this location by reason of it being the residence of members of the 
Royal Family. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Sandringham 
House) (Restricted Zone EG R219) (Amendment) Regulations 2022 and they come into force at 
6.00 p.m. on 13th September 2022. 
Amendment 
2.—(1) The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Sandringham House) (Restricted Zone EG 
R219) Regulations 2015(b) are amended as follows. 
(2) For regulation 3(1) substitute— 
“3.—(1) Subject to paragraph (3)— 
(a) in the period beginning at 1700 hours on 13th September 2022 and ending at 2259 
hours on 24th September 2022, and 
(b) in the period beginning at 0001 hours on 1st December 2022 and ending at 0001 
hours on 1st March 2023, and in the equivalent period in successive years, 
no aircraft is to fly below 2,000 feet above mean sea level within the restricted airspace.” 
(3) In regulation 3(3)(d) for “Queen’s” substitute “King’s”.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations amend the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Sandringham House) 
(Restricted Zone EG R219) Regulations 2015 (S.I. 2015/1734) which restrict flying in the vicinity 
of Sandringham House. The Regulations amend the times of the restriction. They also amend the 
reference to The King’s Helicopter Flight following the death of Her late Majesty Queen Elizabeth 
II. 
Full details of this amendment will be promulgated by Notice to Aviation (NOTAM).
