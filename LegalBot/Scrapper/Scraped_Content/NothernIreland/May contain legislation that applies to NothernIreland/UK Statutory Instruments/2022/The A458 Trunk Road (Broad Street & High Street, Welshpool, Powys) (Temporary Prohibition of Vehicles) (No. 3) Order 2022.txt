ROAD TRAFFIC, WALES 
The A458 Trunk Road (Broad 
Street & High Street, Welshpool, 
Powys) (Temporary Prohibition of 
Vehicles) (No. 3) Order 2022 
Made 
12 October 2022 
Coming into force 
17 October 2022 
The Welsh Ministers, as traffic authority for the 
relevant length of the A458 trunk road, are satisfied 
that traffic on a specified length of the trunk road 
should be prohibited because of works proposed on or 
near the road. 
The Welsh Ministers, therefore, in exercise of the 
powers conferred upon them by section 14(1) and (4) 
of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(1), make this 
Order. 
Title and commencement  
1. The title of this Order is the A458 Trunk Road 
(Broad Street & High Street, Welshpool, Powys) 
(Temporary Prohibition of Vehicles) (No. 3) Order 
2022 and this Order comes into force on 17 October 
2022.

“exempted vehicle” (“cerbyd esempt”) means any

Prohibition 
3. No person may, during the works period, drive 
any vehicle, other than an exempted vehicle, on the 
length of the trunk road at Welshpool, Powys that 
extends from its junction with The Cross to its junction 
with Jehu Road. 
Application 
4. The prohibition in article 3 applies only during 
such times and to such extent as indicated by traffic 
signs. 
Duration of this Order 
5. The maximum duration of this Order is 18 
months.
