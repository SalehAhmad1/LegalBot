The Secretary of State has previously decided that it is in the public interest to amend the flying in 
the area specified in regulation 3(1) and now considers it necessary to amend the authority which 
may grant access to the restricted area. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Scampton) 
(Restricted Zone EG R313) (Amendment) Regulations 2022 and they come into force on 14th 
December 2022. 
Amendment 
2. The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Scampton) (Restricted Zone EG R313) 
Regulations 2022(b) are amended as follows. 
3. In regulation 3 paragraph 2 for “Paragraph (1) does not apply to any aircraft flying under the 
direction of the Air Traffic Control unit at Royal Air Force Waddington” substitute “Paragraph (1) 
does not apply to any aircraft flying under the direction of Waddington Radar within the 
Lincolnshire Terminal Air Traffic Control Centre (TATCC) at Royal Air Force Coningsby”.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations amend the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Scampton) (Restricted Zone 
EG R313) Regulations 2022 (S.I. 2022/1221). The amendment amends the details relating to the 
authority which may grant access to the restricted area. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Notice to Aviation (NOTAM).
