12th July 2022EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order amends the School Teachers’ Incentive Payments (England) Order 2019 (S.I. 2019/1133). It adds the Levelling Up Premium to the list of schemes payment under which is not to be treated as remuneration for the purposes of section 122(1) of the Education Act 2002 (c. 32).
An impact assessment has not been produced for this instrument as it has no impact on business or civil society organisations. The instrument has minimal impact on the public sector.
