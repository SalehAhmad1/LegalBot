ROAD TRAFFIC, WALES 
The A4232 Trunk Road (Capel 
Llanilltern to Culverhouse Cross, 
Cardiff) (Temporary Prohibition of 
Vehicles) Order 2022 
Made 
17 February 2022 
Coming into force 
22 February 2022 
The Welsh Ministers, as traffic authority for the A4232 
trunk road, are satisfied that traffic on specified lengths 
of the trunk road should be prohibited because of 
works proposed on or near the road. 
The Welsh Ministers, therefore, in exercise of the 
powers conferred upon them by section 14(1) and (4) 
of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(1), make this 
Order. 
Title and commencement  
1. The title of this Order is the A4232 Trunk Road 
(Capel Llanilltern to Culverhouse Cross, Cardiff) 
(Temporary Prohibition of Vehicles) Order 2022 and 
this Order comes into force on 22 February 2022. 
Interpretation 
2. In this Order— 
“exempted vehicle” (“cerbyd esempt”) means any 
vehicle being used by the emergency services or in 
connection with the works giving rise to this Order; 
“the trunk road” (“y gefnffordd”) means the A4232 
London to Fishguard trunk road; 
                                                                               
(1) 
1984 c. 27; section 14 was substituted by the Road Traffic   
(Temporary Restrictions) Act 1991 (c. 26), section 1(1) and 
Schedule 1.  By virtue of S.I. 1999/672, and section 162 of, 
and paragraph 30 of Schedule 11 to, the Government of 
Wales Act 2006 (c. 32), these functions are now exercisable 
by the Welsh Ministers in relation to Wales. 
 
2
“works period” (“cyfnod y gwaith”) means 
intermittent periods commencing at 20:00 hours on 
22 February 2022 and ending when the temporary 
traffic signs for each period of works are 
permanently removed. 
Prohibition 
3.  No person may, during the works period, drive 
any vehicle, other than an exempted vehicle, on the 
lengths of the trunk road specified in the Schedule to 
this Order. 
Application 
4.  The prohibition in article 3 applies only during 
such times and to such extent as indicated by traffic 
signs. 
Duration of this Order 
5.  The maximum duration of this Order is 18 
months. 
 
Signed on behalf of the Deputy Minister for Climate

SCHEDULE  
 
The lengths of the A4232 northbound and southbound 
carriageways (including the southbound dedicated lane 
at Junction 33) that extend from a point 352 metres 
north-west of the centre-point of Culverhouse Cross 
Interchange to its junction with the circulatory 
carriageway at Capel Llanilltern Interchange (M4 
Junction 33).  
 
The lengths of the A4232 northbound entry and 
southbound exit slip roads at Culverhouse Cross 
Interchange.
