At 10.55 a.m. on 9th June 2021EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Regulations)
These Regulations appoint 10th June 2021 as the day on which the Customs Tariff (Establishment) (EU Exit) (Amendment) (No. 2) Regulations 2021 (S.I. 2021/661) come into force. Those Regulations amend the definition of “Tariff of the United Kingdom” in the Customs Tariff (Establishment) (EU Exit) Regulations 2020 (S.I. 2020/1430) to refer to the version dated 28th May 2021.
A Tax Information and Impact Note has not been prepared for this instrument as it contains no substantive changes to tax policy and it is an Appointed Day instrument.
