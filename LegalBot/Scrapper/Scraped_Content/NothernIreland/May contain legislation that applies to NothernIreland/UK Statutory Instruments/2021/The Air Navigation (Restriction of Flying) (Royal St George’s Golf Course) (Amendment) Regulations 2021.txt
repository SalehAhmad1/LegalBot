The Secretary of State has previously decided that it is in the public interest to restrict flying in the 
vicinity of Royal St George’s Golf Course, Sandwich, Kent, by reason of the intended holding of 
the 2021 Open Golf Championship which is scheduled to take place there. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Royal St 
George’s Golf Course) (Amendment) Regulations 2021 and they come into force on 11th July 
2021. 
Amendment 
2.—(1) The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Royal St George’s Golf Course) Regulations 
2021(b) are amended as follows. 
(2) In regulation 3 paragraph 1 for “521628N” substitute “511628N”.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations amend the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Royal St George’s Golf 
Course) Regulations 2021 (S.I. 2021/577). They amend a co-ordinate for the 2021 Open Golf 
Championships at Royal St Georges Golf Course, Sandwich, Kent. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Notice to Airmen (NOTAM).
