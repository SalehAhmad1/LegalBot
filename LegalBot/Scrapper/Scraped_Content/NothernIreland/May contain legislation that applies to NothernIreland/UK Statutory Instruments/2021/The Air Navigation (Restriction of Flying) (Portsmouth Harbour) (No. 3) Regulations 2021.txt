The Secretary of State for Transport has decided it is necessary in the public interest to restrict 
unmanned flying in the vicinity of Portsmouth Harbour on 9th December owing to a large public 
gathering taking place there to celebrate the arrival of Carrier Strike Group 21. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Portsmouth 
Harbour) (No. 3) Regulations 2021 and they come into force on 9th December 2021. 
Interpretation 
2. In regulation 3(1) the times referred to are Co-ordinated Universal Time(b). 
Restricted airspace 
3.—(1) Subject to paragraph (2), between 1500 hours and 1700 hours on 9th December 2021 no 
unmanned aircraft is to fly below 1,000 feet above mean sea level within the area bounded by 
straight lines joining successively the following points— 
(a) 504833N 0010746W, 
(b) 504845N 0010556W, 
(c) 504720N 0010556W, 
(d) 504718N 0010724N, and. 
(e) 504833N 0010746W. 
(2) Paragraph (1) does not apply to any unmanned aircraft operating with the permission of 
Hampshire Constabulary who may be contacted as notified.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations impose restrictions on unmanned flying in the vicinity of Portsmouth Harbour 
by reason of the intended arrival into Her Majesty’s Naval Base Portsmouth of HMS Queen 
Elizabeth and Carrier Strike Group 21 which is scheduled to take place on 9th December 2021. In 
view of the large gathering expected, Hampshire Constabulary considers that the flying of 
unmanned aircraft should be restricted in the vicinity of the event for reasons of public safety and 
security. The restrictions have been agreed by the Civil Aviation Authority and the Department for 
Transport. 
The times mentioned in regulation 3(1) are Co-ordinated Universal Time (UTC) which is the same 
as Greenwich Mean Time (GMT) 
In relation to the permission mentioned in regulation 3(2), Hampshire Constabulary may be 
contacted as notified. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Notice to Aviation (NOTAM).
