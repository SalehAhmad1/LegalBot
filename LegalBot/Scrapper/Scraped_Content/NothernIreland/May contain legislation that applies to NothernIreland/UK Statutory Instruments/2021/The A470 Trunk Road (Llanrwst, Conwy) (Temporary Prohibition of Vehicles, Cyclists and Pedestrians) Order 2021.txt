ROAD TRAFFIC, WALES 
The A470 Trunk Road (Llanrwst, 
Conwy) (Temporary Prohibition of 
Vehicles, Cyclists and Pedestrians) 
Order 2021 
Made 
24 September 2021 
Coming into force 
1 October 2021 
The Welsh Ministers, as traffic authority for the 
relevant length of the A470 trunk road, are satisfied 
that traffic on a specified length of the trunk road 
should be prohibited because of works proposed on or 
near the road and likelihood of danger to the public 
during potential flooding incidents. 
The Welsh Ministers, therefore, in exercise of the 
powers conferred upon them by section 14(1) and (4) 
of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(1), make this 
Order. 
Title and commencement  
1. The title of this Order is the A470 Trunk Road 
(Llanrwst, 
Conwy) 
(Temporary 
Prohibition 
of 
Vehicles, Cyclists and Pedestrians) Order 2021 and 
this Order comes into force on 1 October 2021.

Prohibitions 
3. No person may, during the works period, drive 
any vehicle, other than an exempted vehicle, on the 
length of the trunk road. 
4. No person may, during the works period, ride any 
pedal cycle on the length of the trunk road. 
5. No pedestrian may, during the works period, 
proceed on the length of the trunk road. 
Application 
6. The prohibitions in articles 3, 4 and 5 apply only 
during such times and to such extent as indicated by 
traffic signs. 
Duration of this Order 
7. The maximum duration of this Order is 18 
months.
