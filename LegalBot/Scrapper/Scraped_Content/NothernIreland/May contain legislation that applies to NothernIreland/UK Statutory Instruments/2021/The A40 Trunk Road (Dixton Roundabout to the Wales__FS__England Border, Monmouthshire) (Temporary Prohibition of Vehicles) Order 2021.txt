ROAD TRAFFIC, WALES 
The A40 Trunk Road (Dixton 
Roundabout to the Wales/England 
Border, Monmouthshire) 
(Temporary Prohibition of Vehicles) 
Order 2021 
Made 
4 March 2021 
Coming into force 
6 March 2021 
The Welsh Ministers, as traffic authority for the A40 
trunk road, are satisfied that traffic on specified lengths 
of the trunk road should be prohibited because of 
works proposed on or near the road. 
The Welsh Ministers, therefore, in exercise of the 
powers conferred upon them by section 14(1) and (4) 
of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984(1), make this 
Order. 
Title, Commencement and Interpretation 
1. The title of this Order is the A40 Trunk Road 
(Dixton Roundabout to the Wales/England Border, 
Monmouthshire) (Temporary Prohibition of Vehicles) 
Order 2021 and it comes into force on 6 March 2021. 
2. In this Order:—  
“exempted vehicle” (“cerbyd esempt”) means any 
vehicle being used by the emergency services or in 
connection with the works giving rise to this 
Order; 
“the trunk road” (“y gefnffordd”) means the A40 
Newport to Worcester trunk road; 
                                                                               
(1) 
1984 c. 27; section 14 was substituted by the Road Traffic   
(Temporary Restrictions) Act 1991 (c. 26), section 1(1) and 
Schedule 1.  By virtue of S.I. 1999/672, and section 162 of, 
and paragraph 30 of Schedule 11 to, the Government of 
Wales Act 2006(c. 32), these powers are now exercisable by 
the Welsh Ministers in relation to Wales. 
 
2
“works period” (“cyfnod y gwaith”) means the  
period commencing at 04:00 on the 6 March 2021  
and ending when the temporary traffic signs are 
permanently removed.       
Prohibition  
3. No person may, during the works period, drive 
any vehicle, other than an exempted vehicle, on the 
lengths of the trunk road specified in Schedule 1 and 2 
to this Order.  
Application 
4. The prohibition in article 3 applies only during 
such times and to such extent as indicated by traffic 
signs. 
Duration of this Order 
5. The maximum duration of this Order is 6 months.
