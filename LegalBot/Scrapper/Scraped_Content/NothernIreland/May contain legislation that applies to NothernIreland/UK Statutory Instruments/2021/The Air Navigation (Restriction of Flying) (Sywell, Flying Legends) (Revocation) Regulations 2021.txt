The Secretary of State has previously decided that it is in the public interest to restrict flying in the 
vicinity of Sywell Aerodrome, Northamptonshire, by reason of the holding of flying displays. 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Sywell, Flying 
Legends) (Revocation) Regulations 2021 and come into force with immediate effect. 
Revocation of Regulations 
2. The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Sywell, Flying Legends) Regulations (b) are 
revoked.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations revoke the statutory instrument which imposed restrictions on flying in the area 
specified in the Schedule to that instrument, due to the cancellation of the airshow.
