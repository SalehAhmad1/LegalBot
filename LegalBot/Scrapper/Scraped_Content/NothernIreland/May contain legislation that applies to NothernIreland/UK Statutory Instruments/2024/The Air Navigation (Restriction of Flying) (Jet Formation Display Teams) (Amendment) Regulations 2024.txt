The Secretary of State has decided that it is necessary in the public interest to amend the flying 
restrictions in the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Jet Formation Display Teams) 
Regulations 2024(a). 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(b). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Jet Formation 
Display Teams) (Amendment) Regulations 2024 and they come into force on 28th June 2024. 
Amendment of the Regulations 
2.—(1) The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Jet Formation Display Teams) Regulations 
2024 are amended as follows. 
(2) In the Schedule— 
(a) in the entry for Goodwood, for “3,500 feet” substitute “5,500 feet”, and 
(b) in the second entry for Duxford, for “2,500 feet” substitute “5,500 feet”.
