The Secretary of State has decided that it is necessary in the public interest to amend the location 
of the restricted area in the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Notting Hill) Regulations 
2024(a). 
The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(b). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Notting Hill) 
(Amendment) Regulations 2024 and they come into force on 25th August 2024. 
Amendment of the Regulations 
2.—(1) The Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Notting Hill) Regulations 2024 are amended 
as follows. 
(2) In regulation 3(1) for “0000123W” substitute “0001235W”.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations amend the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Notting Hill) Regulations 
2024 (S.I. 2024/751) by amending the location of the restricted area. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Aeronautical Information Circular 
(AIC) and Notice to Aviation (NOTAM).
