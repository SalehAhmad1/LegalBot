PARLIAMENT
House of Commons Members’ Fund Resolution 2024
Resolution of the House of Commons, dated 29 April 2024, passed in pursuance of the House of Commons Members’ Fund Act 2016(1).
(1)
2016 c. 18.Resolved, That the sum to be deducted from the salaries of Members of the House of Commons in pursuance of section 4(1) and (2) of the House of Commons Members’ Fund Act 2016 shall for the financial year 2024/25 be £2.20 per month.
