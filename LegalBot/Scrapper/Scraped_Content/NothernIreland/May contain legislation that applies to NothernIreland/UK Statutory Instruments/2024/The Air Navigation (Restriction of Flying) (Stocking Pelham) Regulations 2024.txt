The Secretary of State makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by 
article 239 of the Air Navigation Order 2016(a). 
Citation and commencement 
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Air Navigation (Restriction of Flying) (Stocking 
Pelham) Regulations 2024 and they come into force on 14th July 2024.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Regulations) 
These Regulations impose restrictions on flying in the vicinity Stocking Pelham, Hertfordshire 
during a police investigation that is taking place there from 14th to 24th July 2024, inclusive. 
Due to the nature of the investigation, the Civil Aviation Authority and the Department for 
Transport agree that flying should be restricted in the vicinity of the investigation for reasons 
affecting the public interest. 
The times mentioned in regulation 3(1) are Co-ordinated Universal Time (UTC) which is one hour 
behind British Summer Time (BST). 
In relation to the permission mentioned in regulation 3(2), the Hertfordshire Constabulary may be 
contacted as notified. 
Full details of this Statutory Instrument will be promulgated by Briefing Sheet and Notice to 
Aviation (NOTAM).
