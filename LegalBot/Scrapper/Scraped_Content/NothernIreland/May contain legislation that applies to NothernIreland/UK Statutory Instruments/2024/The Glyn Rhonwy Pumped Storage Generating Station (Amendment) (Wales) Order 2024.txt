WELSH STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS
2024 No. 112 (W. 26)
INFRASTRUCTURE PLANNING, WALES
The Glyn Rhonwy Pumped Storage Generating Station (Amendment) (Wales) Order 2024
Made
31 January 2024
Coming into force
2 February 2024
An application has been made, under paragraph 2 of Schedule 6 to the Planning Act 2008(1), to the Welsh Ministers in accordance with the Infrastructure Planning (Changes to, and Revocation of, Development Consent Orders) Regulations 2011(2) for a non-material change to the Glyn Rhonwy Pumped Storage Generating Station Order 2017(3).
The Welsh Ministers have had regard to the effect of the change on the Glyn Rhonwy Pumped Storage Generating Station Order 2017 as amended and are satisfied that the change is not material.
The Welsh Ministers, having considered the application, the responses to the publicity and consultation required by regulations 6 and 7 of the Infrastructure Planning (Changes to, and Revocation of, Development Consent Orders) Regulations 2011, have decided to make the change proposed in the application.
Accordingly, the Welsh Ministers, in exercise of the powers conferred by paragraph 2(1) and (9) of Schedule 6 to the Planning Act 2008, make the following Order.

31 January 2024EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order amends the Glyn Rhonwy Pumped Storage Generating Station Order 2017, a development consent order under the Planning Act 2008, following an application made in accordance with the Infrastructure Planning (Changes to, and Revocation of, Development Consent Orders) Regulations 2011 for a non-material amendment under paragraph 2 of Schedule 6 to the Planning Act 2008. This Order amends the time limit within which the development authorised by the Order must be commenced.
