The Welsh Ministers, as traffic authority for the A483 
Trunk Road, in exercise of the powers conferred upon 
them by sections 14(1) and 124 of, and paragraph 27 
of Schedule 9 to, the Road Traffic Regulation Act 
1984(1), make this Order. 
Title and Commencement  
1. The title of this Order is the A483 Trunk Road 
(Junction 5 (Mold Road Interchange) to Junction 6 
(Gresford Interchange), Wrexham County Borough) 
(Temporary 50 mph Speed Limit) Order 2018 
(Revocation) Order 2019 and it comes into force on 16 
August 2019. 
Revocation 
2. The A483 Trunk Road (Junction 5 (Mold Road 
Interchange) to Junction 6 (Gresford Interchange), 
Wrexham County Borough) (Temporary 50 mph 
Speed Limit) Order 2018(2) is revoked.
