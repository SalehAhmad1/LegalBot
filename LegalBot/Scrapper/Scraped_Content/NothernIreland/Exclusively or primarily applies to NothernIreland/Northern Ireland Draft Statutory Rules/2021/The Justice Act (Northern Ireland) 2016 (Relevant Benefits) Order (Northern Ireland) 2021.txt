Minister’s signature
Minister of JusticeEXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order amends section 14 of the Justice Act (Northern Ireland) 2016 (2016 c.21 (N.I.)) to add universal credit to the list of benefits from which deductions may be made under a collection order for the payment of a fine or other penalty.
