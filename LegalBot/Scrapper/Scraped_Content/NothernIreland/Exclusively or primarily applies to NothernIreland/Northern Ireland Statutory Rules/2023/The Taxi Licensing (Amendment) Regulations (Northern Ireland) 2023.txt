Department for InfrastructureEXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
These Regulations amend Schedule 1 to the Taxi Licensing Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2015 to increase fees payable as follows—
(1) Application for a taxi licence fee increased from £138.50 to £147.00; and
(2) Further application for a taxi licence within 21 days increased from £19.50 to £29.50.
