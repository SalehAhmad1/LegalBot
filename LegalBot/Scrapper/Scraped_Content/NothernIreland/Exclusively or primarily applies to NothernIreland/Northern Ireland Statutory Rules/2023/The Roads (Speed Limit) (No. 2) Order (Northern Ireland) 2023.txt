The Department for Infrastructure(a) makes the following Order in exercise of the powers 
conferred by Articles 37(3), 37(4) and 38(1) of the Road Traffic Regulation (Northern Ireland) 
Order 1997(b) and now vested in it(c). 
The Department has consulted such persons as it considered appropriate in compliance with 
paragraphs 1 and 2 of Schedule 5 to that Order. 
The Department has published a notice in compliance with paragraphs 1 and 3 of that Schedule. 
No objection or other representation has been received. 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as the Roads (Speed Limit) (No. 2) Order (Northern Ireland) 2023 and 
shall come into operation on 30th May 2023. 
Speed restriction on certain roads 
2. The Department directs that the lengths of road specified in Schedule 1 shall be restricted roads. 
Application of speed limits 
3. The driving of motor vehicles at a speed exceeding 40 miles per hour on the lengths of road 
specified in Schedule 2 are prohibited. 
Revocations 
4. The provision described in Schedule 3 is revoked.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Order) 
This Order— 
(a) makes the lengths of road specified in Schedule 1 restricted roads; 
(b) applies a speed limit of 40 miles per hour to the lengths of road specified in Schedule 2; 
(c) revokes the provision in Schedule 3. 
In relation to the revoked provision in Schedule 5 the speed limit on the length of Belmont Road, 
Route A6, Antrim (paragraph 1 of Schedule 5) is now included in a longer length of road at 40 
miles per hour.
