The Department for Infrastructure(a) makes the following Order in exercise of the powers 
conferred by Articles 37(3), 37(4) and 38(1) of the Road Traffic Regulation (Northern Ireland) 
Order 1997(b) and now vested in it(c). 
The Department has consulted such persons as it considered appropriate in compliance with 
paragraphs 1 and 2 of Schedule 5 to that Order. 
The Department has published a notice in compliance with paragraphs 1 and 3 of that Schedule. 
No objection or other representation has been received. 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as the Roads (Speed Limit) (No. 3) Order (Northern Ireland) 2023 
and shall come into operation on 17th July 2023. 
Speed restriction on certain roads 
2. The Department directs that the lengths of road specified in Schedule 1 shall be restricted 
roads. 
Application of speed limits 
3. The driving of motor vehicles at a speed exceeding 40 miles per hour on the length of road 
specified in Schedule 2 is prohibited. 
Revocations 
4. The provisions described in Schedule 3 are revoked.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Order) 
This Order— 
(a) makes the lengths of road specified in Schedule 1 restricted roads; 
(b) applies a speed limit of 40 miles per hour to the lengths of road specified in Schedule 2; 
(c) revokes the provisions in Schedule 3. 
In relation to the revoked provisions in Schedule 3— 
1. The speed limit on the lengths of Dublin Road, Kilcoo (paragraph 1, item 51 of paragraph 4 
and paragraph 5 of Schedule 3) are now partly 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting) 
and partly set at 40 miles per hour. 
2. The speed limit on the lengths of Concession Road, Cullaville (item 163 of paragraph 2 and 
item 5 of paragraph 3 of Schedule 3) are now partly 30 miles per hour (restricted by street 
lighting) and partly set at 30 miles per hour (restricted road). 
3. The speed limit on the length of Corrasmoo Road, Cullaville (item 164 of paragraph 2 of 
Schedule 3) is now set at 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting). 
4. The speed limit on the length of Cullaville Road, Cullaville (item 165 of paragraph 2 and 
item 6 of paragraph 3 of Schedule 3) is now set at 30 miles per hour (restricted by street 
lighting). 
5. The speed limit on the length of Ballymoney Road, Kilcoo (item 164 of paragraph 2 of 
Schedule 3) is now set at 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting). 
6. The speed limit on the length of Tullyree Road, Kilcoo (item 166 of paragraph 2 of Schedule 
3) is now set at 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting). 
7. The speed limit on the entire length of Craigmore Way, Newry (paragraph 6 of Schedule 3) 
is now set at 40 miles per hour. 
8. The speed limit on the length of Francis Street, Lurgan (paragraph 7 of Schedule 3) is now 
set at 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting).
