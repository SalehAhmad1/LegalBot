(This note is not part of the Regulations)
These Regulations amend the Taxis (Taximeters, Devices and Maximum Fares) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2015 in order to increase the fee payable for the inspection, testing and sealing of an approved taximeter and device from £35.00 to £42.00.
