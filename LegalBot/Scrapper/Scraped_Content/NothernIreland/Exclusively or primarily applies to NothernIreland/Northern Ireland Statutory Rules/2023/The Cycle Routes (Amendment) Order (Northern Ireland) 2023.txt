EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Order) 
This Order amends The Cycle Routes Order (Northern Ireland) 2008 (“the Order”) by the addition 
to Schedule 1 of cycle lanes on the lengths of carriageway specified in Article 2(2) and by the 
addition to Schedule 2 of two-way shared cycle tracks on the lengths of footway specified in 
Article 2(3)(d). 
This Order also revokes item no. 225 in Schedule 2 of the Order (Article 2(3)(a)) and amends the 
descriptions of—  
(a) item no. 226 (Article 2(3)(b)); 
(b) item no. 494 (Article 2(3)(c)); and 
(c) item no. 495 (Article 2(3)(c)) thereby extending the existing length of cycle track. 
Any person who acts in contravention of Article 2 of the Order by causing or permitting a vehicle 
other than a cycle to wait in a cycle lane shall be liable to a penalty charge (£90) payable in 
accordance with the Traffic Management (Northern Ireland) Order 2005 and The Penalty Charges 
(Prescribed Amounts) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2006. 
Any person who contravenes any other provision of the Order shall be guilty of an offence under 
the Road Traffic Regulation (Northern Ireland) Order 1997 (“the Regulation Order”) and shall be 
liable on summary conviction to a fine not exceeding level 3 on the standard scale (£1,000) in 
accordance with The Road Traffic Offenders (Northern Ireland) Order 1996 as amended by the 
Regulation Order. 
Traffic signs and road markings indicating the effect of this Order will in due course be placed on 
the roads.
