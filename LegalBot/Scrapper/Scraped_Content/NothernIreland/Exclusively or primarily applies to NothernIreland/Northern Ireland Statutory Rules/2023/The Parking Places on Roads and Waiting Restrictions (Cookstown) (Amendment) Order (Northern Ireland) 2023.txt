EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Order) 
This Order amends The Parking Places on Roads and Waiting Restrictions (Cookstown) Order 
(Northern Ireland) 2018 (“the Order”) by the addition of a no waiting at any time restriction 
(loading and unloading permitted) on a length of Molesworth Road as specified in Article 2(3) and 
updates the definitions of “postal packet” and “universal service provider”. 
Vehicles are excepted from the restriction in certain circumstances.  
A penalty charge (£90) is payable with respect to a vehicle involved in a contravention of the 
Order in accordance with The Traffic Management (Northern Ireland) Order 2005 and The 
Penalty Charges (Prescribed Amounts) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2006. 
Traffic signs indicating the effect of this Order will in due course be placed on the road.
