Department for InfrastructureEXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Regulations)
These Regulations amend the Taxi Drivers’ Licences Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2014 in order to increase the fee for a practical test of competence to drive a taxi from £60.00 to £70.00 where a test commences at any time between 8.30am and 4.30pm during the days Monday to Friday and from £90.00 to £109.00 where a test commences at any other time.
