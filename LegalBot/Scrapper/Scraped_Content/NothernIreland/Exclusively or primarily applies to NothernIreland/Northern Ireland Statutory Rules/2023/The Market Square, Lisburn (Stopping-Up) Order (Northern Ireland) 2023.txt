The Department for Infrastructure(a) makes the following Order in exercise of the powers 
conferred by Article 68(1), (3) and (5) of the Roads (Northern Ireland) Order 1993(b) and now 
vested in it(c). 
The Department in accordance with Article 68(4) of that Order is of the opinion that the lengths of 
the road described in the Schedule are not necessary for road traffic. 
Notice has been published, served and displayed in compliance with paragraphs 1, 2 and 3 of 
Schedule 8 to that Order. 
No objection has been received. 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as the Market Square, Lisburn (Stopping-Up) Order (Northern 
Ireland) 2023 and shall come into operation on 1st February 2024. 
Application 
2.—(1) The lengths of road described in the Schedule shall be stopped-up to motor vehicles at 
the lines specified therein. 
(2) The Department shall carry out such works as it considers necessary to prevent or restrict to 
such extent as the Department thinks fit, access to the lengths of road described in the Schedule by 
motor vehicles. 
3.—(1) All existing cables, wires, mains, pipes or other apparatus placed along, across, over or 
under the road shall be retained. 
(2) All existing rights as to the use or maintenance of such cables, wires, mains, pipes or other 
apparatus shall be preserved.

SCHEDULE

LENGTHS OF ROAD TO BE STOPPED UP TO MOTOR VEHICLES 
1. Market Square, Lisburn (north-west side) at a line at its junction with Market Square North for a 
distance of 16.7 metres in an easterly direction, more particularly delineated and shown by the line 
marked a-b on Map No. IN1/22/574528. 
2. Market Square, Lisburn (south-west side) at a line at its junction with Market Square South for
