STATUTORY RULES OF NORTHERN IRELAND
2019 No. 191
MENTAL HEALTH AND MENTAL CAPACITY
The Review Tribunal (Revocation) Rules (Northern Ireland) 2019
Made
27th September 2019
Coming into operation
1st October 2019
The Department of Justice makes the following Rules in exercise of the powers conferred by Article 83(1) and (4) of the Mental Health (Northern Ireland) Order 1986(1) and now vested in the Department of Justice(2) after consultation with the Lord Chief Justice.
(1)
S.I. 1986/595 (N.I. 4), as amended by section 15(2) of and paragraphs 74, 75 and 76 of Schedule 5 to the Constitutional Reform Act 2005 (c.5); S.I. 2010/976 and S.R. 2011/44.
(2)
S.I. 2010/976, Article 15 and paragraph 39 of Schedule 17.Citation and commencement
1.  These Rules may be cited as the Review Tribunal (Revocation) Rules (Northern Ireland) 2019 and shall come into operation on 1st October 2019.Revocation
2.  The Review Tribunal (Amendment) Rules (Northern Ireland) 2019 are revoked.Sealed with the Official Seal of the Department of Justice on 27th September 2019.
Peter May
A senior officer of the Department of JusticeEXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
These Rules revoke the Review Tribunal (Amendment) Rules (Northern Ireland) 2019.
