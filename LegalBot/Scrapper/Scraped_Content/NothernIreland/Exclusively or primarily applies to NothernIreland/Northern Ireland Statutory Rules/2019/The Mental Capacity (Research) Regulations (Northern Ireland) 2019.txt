STATUTORY RULES OF NORTHERN IRELAND
2019 No. 193
MENTAL CAPACITY
The Mental Capacity (Research) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2019
Made
27th September 2019
Coming into operation
1st October 2019
The Department of Health M1, in exercise of the powers conferred by section 132 of the Mental Capacity Act (Northern Ireland) 2016 M2, makes the following Regulations:
Marginal Citations
M1
2016 c. 5 (NI), s. 1(5)
M2
2016 c. 18 (NI)Citation, commencement and interpretation
1.—(1) These Regulations may be cited as the Mental Capacity (Research) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2019 and shall come into operation on 1st October 2019.
(2) In these Regulations “HSC Trust” has the same meaning as in the Mental Capacity Act (Northern Ireland) 2016.Appropriate body for the purpose of research
