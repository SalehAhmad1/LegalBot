The Department for Infrastructure(a) makes the following Order in exercise of the powers 
conferred by Article 68(1), (3) and (5) of the Roads (Northern Ireland) Order 1993(b) and now 
vested in it(c). 
The Department in accordance with Article 68(4) of that Order proposes on the completion of 
works providing alternative facilities for road traffic to stop-up Millmount Road, Dundonald to 
vehicular traffic at the lines described in the Schedule. 
Notice has been published, served and displayed in compliance with paragraphs 1, 2 and 3 of 
Schedule 8 to that Order. 
No objection has been received. 
Citation, commencement and interpretation 
1.—(1) This Order may be cited as The Millmount Road, Dundonald (Stopping-Up) Order 
(Northern Ireland) 2019 and shall come into operation on 7th October 2019. 
(2) In this Order “the Developer” means Lagan Homes Limited whose registered office is 
situated at Lagan House, 19 Clarendon Road, Belfast, BT1 3BG. 
Application 
2.—(1) The Developer shall carry out such works as the Department considers necessary for the 
provision of alternative facilities for road traffic and on the completion of works Millmount Road 
shall be stopped-up to vehicular traffic at the lines specified in the Schedule. 
(2) The contractor shall carry out such works as the Department considers necessary to prevent 
or restrict to such extent as the Department think fit, access to the road by vehicular traffic. 
3.—(1) All existing cables, wires, mains, pipes or other apparatus placed along, across, over or 
under the road to be stopped-up shall be retained.

SCHEDULE 
Article 2(1) 
ROAD TO BE STOPPED-UP TO VEHICULAR TRAFFIC 
1. Millmount Road, Dundonald at its north-eastern junction with Millmount Roundabout at a 
line commencing at a point on the western edge of the carriageway of Millmount Road and 
extending for a distance of 23.5 metres in an easterly direction more particularly delineated and 
marked A-B and coloured red on map number IN1/19/90885. 
2. Millmount Road, Dundonald at a line commencing at a point on the north-western side of the 
road 86 metres north-east of its north-eastern junction with Millmount Roundabout and extending 
for a distance of 6 metres in a south-easterly direction and marked C-D and coloured red on map 
number IN1/19/90885. 
A copy of the map has been deposited at the Department’s Headquarters, Room 301, Clarence 
Court, 10-18 Adelaide Street, Belfast and at DfI Roads and Rivers Eastern Division, Lands 
Section, Annexe 7, Block 2, Castle Buildings, Stormont Estate, Upper Newtownards Road, 
Belfast.
