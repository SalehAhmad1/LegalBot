(This note is not part of the Regulations)
These Regulations make minor amendments to miscellaneous legislation relating to animal disease and trade in animals and animal related products. The Regulations update provisions in that legislation which refer to EU legislation that has been amended.
An impact assessment has not been produced for these Regulations as no impact on the private or voluntary sector is foreseen.
