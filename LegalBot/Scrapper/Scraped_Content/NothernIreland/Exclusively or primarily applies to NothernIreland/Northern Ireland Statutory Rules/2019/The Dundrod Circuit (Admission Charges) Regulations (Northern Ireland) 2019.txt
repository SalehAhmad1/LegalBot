STATUTORY RULES OF NORTHERN IRELAND
2019 No. 120
ROAD TRAFFIC AND VEHICLES
The Dundrod Circuit (Admission Charges) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2019
Made
18th June 2019
Coming into operation
16th July 2019
The Department for Infrastructure(1), in exercise of the powers conferred by Article 3(7A)(b) of the Road Races (Northern Ireland) Order 1986(2) and now exercisable by it(3), and of every other power enabling it in that behalf makes the following Regulations.
(1)
2016 c. 5 (N.I.)
(2)
S.I. 1986/1887 (N.I. 17); see Article 8 and the Schedule in relation to the Dundrod Circuit; Article 3 was modified by paragraph 3(3) of the Schedule
(3)
S.R. 1999 No. 481 Article 6(d) and Schedule 4 Part IVCitation and commencement
1.  These Regulations may be cited as The Dundrod Circuit (Admission Charges) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2019 and shall come into operation on 16th July 2019.Charges
2.  The maximum charge for a spectator badge in respect of each person shall be £30.00.Revocation
3.  The Dundrod Circuit (Admission Charges) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2011(1) are revoked.

A senior officer of the Department for InfrastructureEXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
These Regulations revoke and replace the Dundrod Circuit (Admission Charges) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2011.
The Regulations increase the maximum admission charge from £25.00 to £30.00.
