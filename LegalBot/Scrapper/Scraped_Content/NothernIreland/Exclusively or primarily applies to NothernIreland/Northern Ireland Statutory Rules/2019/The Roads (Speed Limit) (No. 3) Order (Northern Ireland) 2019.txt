The Department for Infrastructure(a) makes the following Order in exercise of the powers 
conferred by Articles 37(3), 37(4) and 38(1)(a) of the Road Traffic Regulation (Northern Ireland) 
Order 1997(b) and now vested in it(c). 
The Department has consulted such persons as it considered appropriate in compliance with 
paragraphs 1 and 2 of Schedule 5 to that Order. 
The Department has published a notice in compliance with paragraphs 1 and 3 of that Schedule. 
No written objections or other representations have been received. 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as The Roads (Speed Limit) (No. 3) Order (Northern Ireland) 2019 
and shall come into operation on 23rd December 2019. 
Application of speed limits 
2. The driving of motor vehicles at a speed exceeding 40 miles per hour on the road and lengths 
of road specified in Schedule 1 is prohibited. 
3. The driving of motor vehicles at a speed exceeding 50 miles per hour on the road and lengths 
of road specified in Schedule 2 is prohibited. 
Revocations 
4. The provisions described in Schedule 3 are revoked.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Order) 
This Order— 
(a) applies a speed limit of 40 miles per hour to the road and lengths of road specified in 
Schedule 1; 
(b) applies a speed limit of 50 miles per hour to the road and lengths of road specified in 
Schedule 2; and 
(c) revokes the provisions in Schedule 3. 
In relation to the revoked provisions in Schedule 3— 
1. The speed limit on the lengths of Old Warrenpoint Road, Newry (paragraphs 1 and 2 of 
Schedule 3 is now 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting). 
2. The speed limit on the entire length of Hannahstown Hill, Belfast (items 18 and 19 of 
paragraph 3 of Schedule 3) is now set at 40 miles per hour. 
3. The speed limits on the lengths of Upper Springfield Road, Belfast (items 23 and 24 of 
paragraph 3 of Schedule 3) are now included in a longer length of road set at 50 miles per hour. 
4. The speed limit on the entire length of Ballycastle Road Roundabout, Coleraine (item 87 of 
paragraph 4 of Schedule 3) is now set at 50 miles per hour. 
5. The speed limit on the length of Ballyrashane Road, Coleraine (item 89 of paragraph 4 of 
Schedule 3) is now included in a longer length of road set at 50 miles per hour. 
6. The speed limits on the lengths of Camus Road, Douglas Bridge, Strabane (items 133 and 135 
of paragraph 5 of Schedule 3) are now 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting). 
7. The speed limit on the length of Lisky Road, Victoria Bridge, Strabane (item 393 of 
paragraph 5 of Schedule 3) is now included in a longer length of road set at 40 miles per hour. 
8. The speed limit on the length of Cornakinnegar Road, Lurgan (paragraph 6 of Schedule 3) is 
now included in a longer length of road set at 40 miles per hour. 
9. The speed limit on the length of the Ring Road, Route A29, Coleraine (paragraph 7 of 
Schedule 3) is now set at 50 miles per hour.
