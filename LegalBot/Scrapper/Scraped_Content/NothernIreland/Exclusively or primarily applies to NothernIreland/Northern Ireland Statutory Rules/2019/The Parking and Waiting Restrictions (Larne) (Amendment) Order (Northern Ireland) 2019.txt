The Department for Infrastructure(a) makes the following Order in exercise of the powers 
conferred by Article 4(1), (2) and (3) of the Road Traffic Regulation (Northern Ireland) Order 
1997(b) and now vested in it(c). 
The Department has consulted such persons as it considered appropriate in compliance with 
paragraphs 1 and 2 of Schedule 1 to that Order. 
The Department has published a notice in compliance with paragraphs 1 and 3 of that Schedule. 
No written objection or other representation has been received. 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as The Parking and Waiting Restrictions (Larne) (Amendment) 
Order (Northern Ireland) 2019 and shall come into operation on 12th August 2019. 
Amendment 
2. The Parking and Waiting Restrictions (Larne) Order (Northern Ireland) 2017(d) is amended 
by the addition to Part 2 of Schedule 2 of the following item— 
 
“6. 
Old Belfast Road (south-east side) – from a point 15 metres south-west of its junction 
with Willowbank Road, for a distance of 443 metres in a north-easterly direction.”

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Order) 
This Order amends The Parking and Waiting Restrictions (Larne) Order (Northern Ireland) 2017 
(“the Order”) by the addition to Part 2 of Schedule 2 of the item specified in Article 2. 
A penalty charge (£90) is payable with respect to a vehicle involved in a contravention of the 
Order in accordance with the Traffic Management (Northern Ireland) Order 2005 and The Penalty 
Charges (Prescribed Amounts) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2006. 
Traffic signs indicating the effect of the Order will in due course be placed on the road.
