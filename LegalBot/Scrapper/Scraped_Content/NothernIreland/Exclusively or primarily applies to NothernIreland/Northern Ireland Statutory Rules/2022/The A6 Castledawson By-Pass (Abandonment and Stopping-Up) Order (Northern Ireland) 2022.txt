The Department for Infrastructure(a) makes the following Order in exercise of the powers 
conferred by Article 68(1), (3) and (5) of the Roads (Northern Ireland) Order 1993(b) and now 
vested in it(c). 
The Department in accordance with Article 68(4) of that Order, being of the opinion that another 
road is available which provides alternative facilities for road traffic, proposes to abandon the 
lengths of Bellshill Road as described in Part 1 of the Schedule and to stop-up the lengths of 
Bellshill Road and Annaghmore Road to road traffic at the lines described in Part 2 of the 
Schedule. 
Notice has been published, served and displayed in compliance with paragraphs 1, 2 and 3 of 
Schedule 8 to that Order. 
No objection has been received. 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as the A6 Castledawson By-Pass (Abandonment and Stopping-Up) 
Order (Northern Ireland) 2022 and shall come into operation on 30th June 2022. 
Application 
2. The lengths of road described in Part 1 of the Schedule are abandoned. 
3.—(1) The lengths of road described in Part 2 of the Schedule shall be stopped-up to road 
traffic at the lines specified therein. 
(2) The Department shall carry out such works as it considers necessary to prevent or restrict to 
such extent as the Department thinks fit, access to the lengths of road described in the Schedule by 
road traffic.

4.—(1) All existing cables, wires, mains, pipes or other apparatus placed along, across, over or 
under the abandoned lengths of road and the stopped-up lengths of road shall be relocated. 
(2) All existing rights as to the use or maintenance of such cables, wires, mains, pipes or other

PART 1 
LENGTHS OF ROAD TO BE ABANDONED

marked A1 on map number IN1/20/33554. 
2. The U1129 Bellshill Road in the townland of Annaghmore, extending from a point 112 
metres south of its junction with the former A6 Castledawson By-Pass for a distance of 58 metres 
in a southerly direction, more particularly delineated and shown cross-hatched, coloured red and 
marked A2 on map number IN1/20/33554. 
Copies of the map have been deposited at the Department’s Headquarters, Room 301, Clarence 
Court, 10-18 Adelaide Street, Belfast; at the Department’s Road and Rivers Northern Division, 
County Hall, Castlerock Road, Coleraine.

4. The existing C558 Annaghmore Road in the townland of Annaghmore at a line commencing

extending for a distance of 48 metres in a southerly direction, more particularly delineated and

Copies of the maps have been deposited at the Department’s Headquarters, Room 301, Clarence 
Court, 10-18 Adelaide Street, Belfast and at DfI Roads and Rivers Northern Division, Lands 
Section, County Hall, Castlerock Road, Coleraine.
