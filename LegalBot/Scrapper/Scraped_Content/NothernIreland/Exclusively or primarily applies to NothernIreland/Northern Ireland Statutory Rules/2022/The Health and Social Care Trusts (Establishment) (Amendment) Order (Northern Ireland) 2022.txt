Made

The Department of Health, makes the following Order in exercise of the powers conferred by Article 10(1) of, and paragraphs 3(1)(b) and 3A of Schedule 3 to, the Health and Personal Social Services Order (Northern Ireland) 1991(1).
In accordance with Article 10(2) of that Order the Department has consulted with the Patient and Client Council and such other persons and bodies as it considered appropriate.

(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order amends orders made under Article 10(1) of, and paragraphs 3 (1) (b) and 3A of Schedule 3 to the Health and Personal Social Services (Northern Ireland) Order 1991 establishing Health and Social Care Trusts.
Article 2 of the order removes the definition of “relevant functions” from the relevant establishment orders and updates the functions of the Trusts and the Department of Health’s power, by direction, to provide for specified functions to be undertaken by trust on behalf of the Department.
