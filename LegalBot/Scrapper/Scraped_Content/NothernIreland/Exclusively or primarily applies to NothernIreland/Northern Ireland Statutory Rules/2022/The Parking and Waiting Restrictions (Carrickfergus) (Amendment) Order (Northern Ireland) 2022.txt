EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Order) 
This Order amends The Parking and Waiting Restrictions (Carrickfergus) Order (Northern 
Ireland) 2016 (“the Order”) by substituting Article 11 to include the new waiting restrictions in 
Part 5 of Schedule 2 (Article 2(2)), the addition of a no waiting at any time (loading and unloading 
permitted) restriction on the lengths of Rhanbuoy Close specified in Article 2(3) and the addition 
to Schedule 2 of the waiting restrictions referred to in Part 5 of that Schedule (Article 2(4)). 
Vehicles are excepted from the restrictions in certain circumstances. 
A penalty charge (£90) is payable with respect to a vehicle involved in a contravention of the 
Order in accordance with the Traffic Management (Northern Ireland) Order 2005 and the Penalty 
Charges (Prescribed Amounts) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2006. 
Traffic signs indicating the effect of this Order will in due course be placed on the roads.
