The Department for Infrastructure(a) makes the following Order in exercise of the powers 
conferred by Articles 37(3), 37(4) and 38(1) of the Road Traffic Regulation (Northern Ireland) 
Order 1997(b) and now vested in it(c). 
The Department has consulted such persons as it considered appropriate in compliance with 
paragraphs 1 and 2 of Schedule 5 to that Order. 
The Department has published a notice in compliance with paragraphs 1 and 3 of that Schedule. 
No objection or other representation has been received. 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as the Roads (Speed Limit) Order (Northern Ireland) 2022 and shall 
come into operation on 4th March 2022. 
Speed restriction on certain roads 
2. The Department directs that the length of road specified in Schedule 1 shall be a restricted 
road. 
Application of speed limits 
3. The driving of motor vehicles at a speed exceeding 40 miles per hour on the lengths of road 
specified in Schedule 2 are prohibited. 
Revocations 
4. The provisions described in Schedule 3 are revoked.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Order) 
This Order— 
(a) makes the length of road specified in Schedule 1 a restricted road; 
(b) applies a speed limit of 40 miles per hour to the lengths of road specified in Schedule 2; 
and 
(c) revokes the provisions in Schedule 3. 
In relation to the revoked provisions in Schedule 3— 
1. The speed limit on the length of Chapel Road, Glenavy (paragraph 1 of Schedule 3) is now 
partly 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting) and partly set at 40 miles per hour. 
2. The speed limit on the length of Point Road, Lawrencetown (paragraphs 2 and 3 of Schedule 
3) is now 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting). 
3. The speed limit on the length of Cloughfin Road, Cookstown (paragraph 4 of Schedule 3) is 
now set at 40 miles per hour. 
4. The speed limit on the lengths of Belfast Road, Antrim (paragraphs 5 and 6 of Schedule 3) is 
now partly 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting) and partly set at 40 miles per hour. 
(This is a correction to S.R. 2021 No. 195 where item 8 was wrongly transcribed as item 12).
