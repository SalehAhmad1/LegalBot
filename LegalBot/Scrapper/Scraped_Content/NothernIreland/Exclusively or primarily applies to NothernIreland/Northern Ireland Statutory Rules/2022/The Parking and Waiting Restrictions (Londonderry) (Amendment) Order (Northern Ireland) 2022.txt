The Department for Infrastructure(a) makes the following Order in exercise of the powers 
conferred by Articles 4(1), (2) and (3), 10(4) and 13(1) of the Road Traffic Regulation (Northern 
Ireland) Order 1997(b) and now vested in it(c). 
It appears to the Department to be necessary to provide a suitable parking place for vehicles on the 
lengths of the roads specified in Article 2(2). 
The Department has consulted such persons as it considered appropriate in compliance with 
paragraphs 1 and 2 of Schedules 1 and 4 to the Road Traffic Regulation (Northern Ireland) Order 
1997. 
The Department has published a notice in compliance with paragraphs 1 and 3 of those Schedules. 
The Department in compliance with paragraph 3(4) of Schedule 4 to that Order, has taken such 
other steps as appear to the Department reasonably practicable for the purpose of bringing 
specifically to the knowledge of the occupiers of land adjacent to the parking place the particulars 
specified in paragraph 3(2) of that Schedule. 
No written objection or other representation has been received. 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as the Parking and Waiting Restrictions (Londonderry) 
(Amendment) Order (Northern Ireland) 2022 and shall come into operation on 21st February 
2022.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Order) 
This Order amends The Parking and Waiting Restrictions (Londonderry) Order (Northern Ireland) 
2015 (“the Order”) by the reduction of the length of a parking place on Little James Street by 12.6 
metres and changing the 12.6 metres to a no waiting at any time prohibition (loading and 
unloading not permitted) as specified in Article 2(2)(a) and (3); the addition of a no waiting at any 
time prohibition (loading and unloading not permitted) on the lengths of road specified in Article 2 
(3) and the addition of a no waiting at any time prohibition (loading and loading permitted) on the 
lengths of road specified in Article 2(4). 
Vehicles are excepted from the prohibitions in certain circumstances. 
A penalty charge (£90) is payable with respect to a vehicle involved in a contravention of the 
Order in accordance with the Traffic Management (Northern Ireland) Order 2005 and the Penalty 
Charges (Prescribed Amounts) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2006. 
Traffic signs indicating the effect of this Order will in due course be placed on the roads.
