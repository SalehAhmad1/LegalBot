”EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Regulations)
These Regulations amend the Marriage Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2003, the Civil Partnership Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2005 and the Civil Registration Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2012 to enable certified copies of life events to be produced in English, Irish or Bi-lingual English/Irish.
The Regulations prescribe alternative forms for the registration of births, still-births and deaths and alternative forms for the production of certified copies.
The Regulations also prescribe updated marriage and civil partnership notice forms and provide alternative forms for marriage and civil partnership schedules and alternative forms for the production of certified copies of marriage or civil partnership registration.
