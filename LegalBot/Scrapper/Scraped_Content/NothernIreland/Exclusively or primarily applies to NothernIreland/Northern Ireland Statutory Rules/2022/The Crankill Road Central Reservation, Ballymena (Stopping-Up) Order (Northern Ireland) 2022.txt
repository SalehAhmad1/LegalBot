The Department for Infrastructure(a) makes the following Order in exercise of its powers 
conferred by Article 68(1), (3) and (5) of the Roads (Northern Ireland) Order 1993(b) and now 
vested in it(c). 
The Department in accordance with Article 68(4) of that Order is of the opinion that the length of 
the road described in the Schedule is not necessary for road traffic. 
Notice has been published, served and displayed in compliance with paragraphs 1, 2 and 3 of 
Schedule 8 to that Order. 
One objection has been received and duly considered. 
The Department has modified the draft Order. 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as the Crankill Road Central Reservation, Ballymena (Stopping-Up) 
Order (Northern Ireland) 2022 and shall come into operation on 28th February 2022. 
Application 
2.—(1) The length of road described in the Schedule shall be stopped-up to road traffic at the 
line specified therein. 
(2) The Department shall carry out such works as it considers necessary to prevent or restrict to 
such extent as the Department thinks fit, access to the length of road described in the Schedule by 
road traffic. 
3.—(1) All existing cables, wires, mains, pipes or other apparatus placed along, across, over or 
under the road shall be retained.
