conferred by Article 4(1), (2) and (3) of, and paragraph 5 of Schedule 1 to, the Road Traffic 
Regulation (Northern Ireland) Order 1997(b) and now vested in it (c). 
The Department has consulted such persons as it considered appropriate in compliance with 
paragraphs 1 and 2 of Schedule 1 to that Order. The Department has published a notice in 
compliance with paragraphs 1 and 3 of those Schedules. 
One objection has been received and duly considered and no other representations have been 
received. 
The Department has modified the draft Order. 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as the Parking Places on Roads and Waiting Restrictions (Newry) 
(Amendment) Order (Northern Ireland) 2022 and shall come into operation on 17th November 
2022.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Order) 
This Order amends The Parking Places on Roads and Waiting Restrictions (Newry) Order 
(Northern Ireland) 2015 (“the Order”) by the addition to Part 1 of Schedule 2 of the items relating 
to Albert Basin and Hospital Road, Newry specified in Article 2. 
A penalty charge (£90) is payable with respect to a vehicle involved in a contravention of the 
Order in accordance with the Traffic Management (Northern Ireland) Order 2005 and The Penalty 
Charges (Prescribed Amounts) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2006. 
Traffic signs indicating the effect of this Order will in due course be placed on the roads.
