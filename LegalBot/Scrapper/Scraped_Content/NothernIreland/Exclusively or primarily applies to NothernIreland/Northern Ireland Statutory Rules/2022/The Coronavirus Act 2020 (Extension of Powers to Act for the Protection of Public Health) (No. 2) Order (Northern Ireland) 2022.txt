Order laid before the Assembly under section 96(2) of the Coronavirus Act 2020, for approval by resolution of the Assembly before the expiration of 40 days beginning with the day on which the Order is made
STATUTORY RULES OF NORTHERN IRELAND
2022 No. 224
PUBLIC HEALTH
The Coronavirus Act 2020 (Extension of Powers to Act for the Protection of Public Health) (No. 2) Order (Northern Ireland) 2022
Made
22nd September 2022
Laid before the Assembly
22nd September 2022
Coming into operation
24th September 2022
The Department of Health(1), in exercise of the powers conferred by section 90(2) of the Coronavirus Act 2020(2), makes the following order.
(1)
Formerly the Department of Health, Social Services and Public Safety, see 2016 c. 5 (N.I.), s. 1(5)
(2)
2020 c. 7Citation and commencement
1.  This order may be cited as The Coronavirus Act 2020 (Extension of Powers to Act for the Protection of Public Health) (No.2) Order (Northern Ireland) 2022 and shall come into operation on 24th September 2022.
Commencement Information
I1
Art. 1 in operation at 24.9.2022, see art. 1Alteration of expiry date
2.  Section 48 of, and Schedule 18 to, the Coronavirus Act 2020 do not expire at the time when they would otherwise expire and expire instead on 24th March 2023.
Commencement Information
I1
Art. 2 in operation at 24.9.2022, see art. 1Sealed with the Official Seal of the Department of Health on 22nd September 2022
(L.S.)
Robin Swann MLA
Minister of HealthEXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the order)
This order extends the expiry date of section 48 of, and Schedule 18 to, the Coronavirus Act 2020 to 24th March 2023.
