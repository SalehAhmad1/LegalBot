EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Order) 
This Order amends the Parking and Waiting Restrictions (Ballymena) Order (Northern Ireland) 
2021 (“the Order”) by the addition of a no waiting at any time (loading and unloading permitted) 
restriction on a length of Springwell Street, Ballymena and a no waiting at any time (loading and 
unloading not permitted) restriction on a length of Windsor Walk, Ballymena as specified in 
Article 2. This Order will also revoke and re-enact existing waiting restrictions on Springwell 
Street. 
Vehicles are excepted from the restrictions in certain circumstances. 
A penalty charge (£90) is payable with respect to a vehicle involved in a contravention of the 
Order in accordance with the Traffic Management (Northern Ireland) Order 2005 and the Penalty 
Charges (Prescribed Amounts) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2006. 
Traffic signs indicating the effect of this Order will in due course be placed on the roads.
