The Department for Infrastructure(a) makes the following Order in exercise of the powers 
conferred by Article 68(1) of the Roads (Northern Ireland) Order 1993(b) and now vested in it(c). 
The Department in accordance with Article 68(4) of that Order proposes on the completion of 
works providing alternative facilities for road traffic to abandon the area of road described in the 
Schedule. 
Notice has been published, served and displayed in compliance with paragraphs 1, 2 and 3 of 
Schedule 8 to that Order. 
Three objections were received and subsequently withdrawn. 
Citation, commencement and interpretation 
1.—(1) This Order may be cited as the Footpath linking Hollyhill Link car park and Tempo 
Road, Enniskillen, (Abandonment) Order (Northern Ireland) 2022 and shall come into operation 
on 6th December 2022. 
(2) In this Order “the Developer” means Station Green Enniskillen Limited whose registered 
office is situated at 135 Nutfield Road, Carntrone, Lisnaskea, BT92 0AD. 
Application 
2. The Developer shall carry out such works as the Department considers necessary for the 
provision of alternative facilities for road traffic and on completion of the works the area of road 
described in the Schedule shall be abandoned.

SCHEDULE 
Article 2 
AREA OF ROAD TO BE ABANDONED 
An area of 147 square metres of footpath linking Hollyhill Link car park and Tempo Road, 
Enniskillen commencing at its junction with the eastern boundary of the car park and extending 
for 60 metres in an easterly direction more particularly delineated and shown cross-hatched on 
map number IN1/18/625435. 
A copy of the map has been deposited at the Department’s Headquarters, Room 301, Clarence 
Court, 10-18 Adelaide Street, Belfast and at DfI Roads and Rivers Western Division, County Hall, 
Drumragh Avenue, Omagh, BT79 7AF. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Order) 
This Order abandons the area of road described in the Schedule on completion of works to provide 
alternative facilities for road traffic.
