Offensive Weapons Act 2019Citation and interpretation
1.—(1) This Order may be cited as the Offensive Weapons Act 2019 (Commencement No. 1) Order (Northern Ireland) 2022.
(2) In this Order “the 2019 Act” means the Offensive Weapons Act 2019”

Minister of JusticeEXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Regulations)
This Order brings into operation on 6th October 2022 section 6 of the Act, which is the offence of having a corrosive substance in a public place.
For the purposes of this Order corrosive substance means a substance which is capable of burning human skin by corrosion and public place includes any place to which, at the time in question, the public have or are permitted access, whether on payment or otherwise.
