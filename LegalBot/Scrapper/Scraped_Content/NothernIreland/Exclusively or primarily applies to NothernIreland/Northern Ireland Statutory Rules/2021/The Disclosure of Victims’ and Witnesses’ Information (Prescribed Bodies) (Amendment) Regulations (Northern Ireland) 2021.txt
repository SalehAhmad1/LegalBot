Minister of JusticeEXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Regulations)
Paragraph 1(1) of Schedule 3 to the Justice Act (Northern Ireland) 2015 (2015 c. 9 (N.I.)) makes provision for a police officer or member of the police support staff to disclose relevant information, as set out in paragraph 1(2), relating to a victim to a prescribed body for the purposes of enabling that body to advise the victim about support services provided by the body, or to offer or provide support services to the victim.
Relevant information relating to a victim means their name and address, any telephone number or e-mail address at which they may be contacted at and other appropriate information in order that they can be advised about, offered or provided services.
These Regulations prescribe the following bodies for the purposes of paragraph 1 of Schedule 3 to the Act:
(a)
Victim Support Northern Ireland; and
(b)
Advocacy Support Safety Information Services Together Northern Ireland (ASSIST NI) (in so far as the disclosure of relevant information relates to the provision of advocacy support services by that body).
