The Department for Infrastructure(a) makes the following Order in exercise of the powers 
conferred by Articles 37(3), 37(4) and 38(1) of the Road Traffic Regulation (Northern Ireland) 
Order 1997(b) and now vested in it(c). 
The Department has consulted such persons as it considered appropriate in compliance with 
paragraphs 1 and 2 of Schedule 5 to that Order. 
The Department has published a notice in compliance with paragraphs 1 and 3 of that Schedule. 
No objection or other representation has been received. 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as The Roads (Speed Limit) (No. 2) Order (Northern Ireland) 2021 
and shall come into operation on 15th December 2021. 
Application of speed limits 
2. The driving of motor vehicles at a speed exceeding 40 miles per hour on the lengths of road 
specified in Schedule 1 are prohibited. 
Revocations 
3. The provisions described in Schedule 2 are revoked.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Order) 
This Order— 
(a) applies a speed limit of 40 miles per hour to the lengths of road specified in Schedule 1; 
and 
(b) revokes the provisions in Schedule 2. 
In relation to the revoked provisions in Schedule 2— 
1. The speed limit on the length of Minterburn Road, Caledon (paragraph 1 of Schedule 2) is 
now included in a longer length of road set at 40 miles per hour. 
2. The speed limit on the lengths of Lurgan Road, Moira (paragraphs 2 and 3 of Schedule 2) is 
now partly set at 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting) and partly set at 40 miles per 
hour.  
3. The speed limit on Ardboe Road, Moortown, Cookstown (paragraphs 4 and 5 of Schedule 2) 
is now 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting). 
4. The speed limit on the length of Tullywiggan Road, Cookstown (paragraph 6 of Schedule 2) 
is now included in a longer length of road set at 40 miles per hour. 
5. The speed limit on the length of Churchtown Road, Cookstown (item 2 of paragraph 7 of 
Schedule 2) is now 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting). 
6. The speed limit on the length of Claggan Road, Cookstown (item 3 of paragraph 7 of 
Schedule 2) is now partly 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting) and partly set at 40 miles 
per hour. 
7. The speed limit on the length of Muff Road, Cookstown (item 5 of paragraph 7 of Schedule 2) 
is now 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting). 
8. The speed limit on the length of Turnaface Road, Cookstown (item 7 of paragraph 7 of 
Schedule 2) is now 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting). 
9. The speed limit on the length of Cookstown Road, Moneymore (item 23 of paragraph 7 of 
Schedule 2) is now included in a longer length of road set at 40 miles per hour. 
10. The speed limit on the length of Cloghog Road, Coalisland (paragraph 8 of Schedule 2) is 
now included in a longer length of road set at 40 miles per hour.
