The Department for Infrastructure(a) makes the following Order in exercise of the powers 
conferred by Articles 10(4) and 13(1) of the Road Traffic Regulation (Northern Ireland) Order 
1997(b) and now vested in it(c). 
It appears to the Department to be necessary to provide a suitable parking place for vehicles on the 
length of road specified in Article 2. 
The Department has consulted such persons as it considered appropriate in compliance with 
paragraph 1 and 2 of Schedule to the Road Traffic Regulation (Northern Ireland) Order 1997. 
The Department has published a notice in compliance with paragraphs 1 and 3 of that Schedule. 
The Department, in compliance with paragraph 3(4) of Schedule 4 to that Order, has taken such 
other steps as appear to the Department reasonably practicable for the purpose of bringing 
specifically to the knowledge of the occupiers of land adjacent to the parking place the particulars 
specified in paragraph 3(2) of that Schedule. 
No objection or other representation has been received. 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as The Parking Place (Greencastle Street, Kilkeel) Order (Northern 
Ireland) 2021 and shall come into operation on 28th June 2021. 
Amendments 
2. The Parking and Waiting Restrictions (Kilkeel) Order (Northern Ireland) 2020(d) is amended

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Order) 
This Order amends Part 1 of Schedule 1 to the Parking and Waiting Restrictions (Kilkeel) Order 
(Northern Ireland) 2020 by substituting the specified length of parking place with a longer length 
of parking place at Greencastle Street, Kilkeel. (Article 2). 
The Order revokes The Parking Places and Loading Bay on Roads (Kilkeel) Order (Northern 
Ireland) 2008 (Article 4) and amends The Loading Bays on Roads Order (Northern Ireland 2007 
(Article 3) to remove a loading bay on this length of Greencastle Street, Kilkeel. 
Traffic signs indicating the effect of the Order will in due course be placed on the road.
