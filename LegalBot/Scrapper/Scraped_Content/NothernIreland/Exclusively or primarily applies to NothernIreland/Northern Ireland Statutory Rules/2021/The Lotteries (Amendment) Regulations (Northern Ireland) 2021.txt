(This note is not part of the Regulations)
These Regulations amend the Lotteries Regulations (Northern Ireland) 1994 to permit the sale of tickets or chances in societies’ lotteries by means of the internet. Except for these purposes, the sale of tickets or chances in societies’ lotteries by means of a machine remains prohibited.
An Equality Impact Assessment has been prepared and can be viewed at www.communities-ni.gov.uk.
