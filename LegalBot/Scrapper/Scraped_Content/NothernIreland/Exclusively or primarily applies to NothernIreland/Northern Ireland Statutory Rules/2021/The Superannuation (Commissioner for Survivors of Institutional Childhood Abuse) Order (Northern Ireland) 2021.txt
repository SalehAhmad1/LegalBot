Department of FinanceEXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Order)
This Order adds the Office of Commissioner for Survivors of Institutional Childhood Abuse to the offices listed in Schedule 1 to the Superannuation (Northern Ireland) Order 1972, for which the Department of Finance may make pension schemes. It has retrospective effect from 2nd November 2020 by virtue of Article 3(7) of the 1972 Order.
