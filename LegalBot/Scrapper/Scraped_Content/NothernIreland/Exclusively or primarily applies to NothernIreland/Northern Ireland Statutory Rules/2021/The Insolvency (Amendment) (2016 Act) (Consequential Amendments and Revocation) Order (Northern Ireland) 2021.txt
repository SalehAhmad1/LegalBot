(This note is not part of the -Order)
This Order revokes the Deeds of Arrangement Regulations (Northern Ireland) 1996 and makes amendments to subordinate legislation consequential to the repeal by section 11 of the Insolvency (Amendment) Act (Northern Ireland) 2016 (2016 c. 2 (N.I.)) of Chapter 1 of Part 8 of the Insolvency (Northern Ireland) Order 1989 (S.I. 1989/2405 (N.I. 19)) which made provision for deeds of arrangement.
A Regulatory Impact Assessment has not been produced for this Order as it will have no effect on the costs of business or the voluntary sector.
