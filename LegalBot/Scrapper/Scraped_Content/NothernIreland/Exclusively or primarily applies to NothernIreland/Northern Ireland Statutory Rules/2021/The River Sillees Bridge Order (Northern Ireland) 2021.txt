The Department for Infrastructure (a) in accordance with Article 4 of the Roads (Northern Ireland) 
Order 1993(b) proposes by order to provide for the construction of a bridge over the navigable 
waters of the River Sillees, Enniskillen. 
The Department has taken into consideration the reasonable requirements of navigation over the 
waters affected by the order in accordance with Article 4(3) of that Order. 
The Department has published and served a notice in compliance with paragraphs 1 and 2 of 
Schedule 8 to that Order. 
In the absence of any objections being set before the Department during the statutory consultation 
period, the Department decided to proceed without subjecting the scheme to an examination 
through a public inquiry. 
The Department makes the following order in exercise of the powers conferred by Article 4 of the 
Roads (Northern Ireland) Order 1993 and now exercisable by it(c). 
Citation, commencement and interpretation 
1. This Order may be cited as the River Sillees Bridge Order (Northern Ireland) 2021 and comes 
into operation on 12 August 2021. 
2. In this Order— 
“Drawing 1” means the drawing numbered ESB-ATK-SBR-A0000-DR-S-0001, a copy of 
which has been deposited at the Department’s offices, Clarence Court, 10-18 Adelaide Street, 
Belfast BT2 8GB and DfI Western Division, County Hall, Drumragh Avenue, Omagh, BT79 
0JF. 
Position and dimensions of the River Sillees Bridge 
3. The position and minimum dimensions of the bridge (including its minimum headways and 
waterways) are specified in the Schedule.

SCHEDULE 
Articles 2 
POSTION AND DIMENSIONS OF THE RIVER ERNE BRIDGE 
The bridge is located over a section of the River Sillees in association with the construction of the 
A4 Enniskillen Southern Bypass scheme. 
Points of Commencement and Termination 
The Bridge is to cross from the townland of Mullylogan at the newly formed Derrylin Road 
roundabout on the West bank (OS Ref 222630, 342255) (marked A on Drawing 1) over to the 
townland of Gortadrehid Little on the East bank (OS Ref 222660, 342250) (marked B on Drawing 
1). 
Navigation Waterway 
Single waterway, minimum 21.530 metres wide between abutments marked C and D on the main 
span as shown on Drawing 1. Navigation is also provided under the adjacent areas of the main 
span as shown on Drawing 1 as approved by Waterways Ireland and marked E and F on Drawing 
1. 
Headway 
Headway clearances are indicated on Drawing 1 measured as above ordnance datum (AOD), 1 in 
100 year flood level.  A headway minimum of 2.30 metres is provided over a width of 21.530m 
and marked C and D on Drawing 1.

Sillees in association with the A4 Enniskillen Southern Bypass scheme.

waterways) are specified in the Schedule.
