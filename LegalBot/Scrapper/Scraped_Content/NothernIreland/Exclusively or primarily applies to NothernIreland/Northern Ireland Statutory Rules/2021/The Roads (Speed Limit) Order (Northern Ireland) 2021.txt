The Department for Infrastructure(a) makes the following Order in exercise of the powers 
conferred by Articles 37(3), 37(4) and 38(1) of the Road Traffic Regulation (Northern Ireland) 
Order 1997(b) and now vested in it(c). 
The Department has consulted such persons as it considered appropriate in compliance with 
paragraphs 1 and 2 of Schedule 5 to that Order. 
The Department has published a notice in compliance with paragraphs 1 and 3 of that Schedule. 
No objection or other representations has been received. 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as The Roads (Speed Limit) Order (Northern Ireland) 2021 and shall 
come into operation on 26th July 2021. 
Application of speed limits 
2. The driving of motor vehicles at a speed exceeding 40 miles per hour on the road and lengths 
of road specified in Schedule 1 are prohibited. 
3. The driving of motor vehicles at a speed exceeding 50 miles per hour on the lengths of road 
specified in Schedule 2 are prohibited. 
De-restriction of speed on certain road 
4. The Department directs that Drumrane Road, Route B192, Burnfoot, Dungiven, from a point 
137 metres north of its junction with Bovevagh Road, Unclassified No. 246, to a point 370 metres 
north of its junction with Straw Road, Unclassified No. 243, shall not be a restricted road 
notwithstanding that a system of street lighting furnished by means of lamps placed not more than 
185 metres apart is provided on that length of road.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Order) 
This Order— 
(a) applies a speed limit of 40 miles per hour to the road and lengths of road specified in 
Schedule 1; 
(b) applies a speed limit of 50 miles per hour to the lengths of road specified in Schedule 2; 
and 
(c) revokes the provisions in Schedule 3. 
In relation to the revoked provisions in Schedule 3— 
1. The speed limit on Stewartstown Road, Dunmurry (paragraphs 1, 3, 4 and 5 of Schedule 3) is 
now 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting). 
2. The speed limit on the lengths of Station Road, Castlewellan (paragraphs 2 and 7 of Schedule 
3) is now partly set at 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting) and partly set at 40 miles per 
hour. 
3. The speed limit on the length of Birch Hill Road, Antrim (paragraph 6 and item 9 of 
paragraph 16 of Schedule 3) is now 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting. The national 
speed limit applies to the remainder of Birch Hill Road. 
4. The speed limit on the length of Drumrane Road, Burnfoot, Dungiven (item 8 of paragraph 8 
and paragraph 10 of Schedule 3) is now partly set at 40 miles per hour and partly 60 miles per 
hour (national speed limit). 
5. The speed limit on the length of Crossgar Road, Saintfield (item 33 of paragraph 8 and 
paragraph 9 of Schedule 3) is not partly 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting) and partly 
set at 40 miles per hour. 
6. The speed limit on the lengths of Creighton Road, Dunmurry (item 5 of paragraph 11 and 
item 5 of paragraph 12 of Schedule 3) is now partly 30 miles per hour (restricted by street 
lighting) and partly set at 50 miles per hour. 
7. The speed limit on the length of McKinstry Road, Derriaghy (item 1 of Schedule 1 and item 1 
of Schedule 2 of paragraph 12 of Schedule 3) is now partly 30 miles per hour (restricted by street 
lighting) and partly set at 50 miles per hour. 
8. The speed limit on the lengths of Mill Hill, Castlewellan (paragraph 13 of Schedule 3) is now 
partly set at 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting) and partly set at 40 miles per hour. 
9. The speed limit on the length of Moneynick Road, Toome (paragraphs 14 and 15 of Schedule 
3) is now 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting). 
10. The speed limit on the length of Low Road, Islandmagee (item 35 of paragraph 16 of 
Schedule 3) is now set at 50 miles per hour. 
11. The speed limit on the length of Hydepark Road, Mallusk (paragraphs 17 and 19 of Schedule 
3) is now included in a longer length of road set at 40 miles per hour. 
12. The speed limit on the Moyola Road, Castledawson (paragraph 18 of Schedule 3) is now 
partly 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting) and partly set at 40 miles per hour. 
13. The speed limit on the length of Clarehill Road, Moira (paragraph 20 of Schedule 3) is now 
included in a longer length of road set at 40 miles per hour. 
14. The speed limit on the lengths of Belfast Road, Antrim (paragraph 21 of Schedule 3) is now 
partly 30 miles per hour (restricted by street lighting) and partly set at 40 miles per hour.
