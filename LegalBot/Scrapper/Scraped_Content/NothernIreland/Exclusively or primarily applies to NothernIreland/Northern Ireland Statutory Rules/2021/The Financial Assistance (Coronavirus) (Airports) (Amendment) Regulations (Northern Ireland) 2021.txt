(This note is not part of the Regulations)
These Regulations amend The Financial Assistance (Coronavirus) (Airports) (Northern Ireland) Scheme 2020 which provided for financial assistance (Coronavirus Financial Assistance) to be awarded to Belfast International Airport and Belfast City Airport by means of provision of financial assistance to the respective operators.
Regulation 1 provides for citation and commencement.
Regulations 2 and 3 provide for the amendment of paragraph 4(2) of the Schedule to The Financial Assistance (Coronavirus) (Airports) (Northern Ireland) Scheme 2020.
