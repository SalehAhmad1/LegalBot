The Department for Infrastructure (a) makes the following Order in exercise of the powers 
conferred by Article 68(1) and (5) of the Roads (Northern Ireland) Order 1993(b) and now vested 
in it(c). 
The Department in accordance with Article 68(4) of that Order proposes to abandon the areas of 
road described in the Schedule as they are not necessary. 
Notice has been published, served and displayed in compliance with paragraphs 1, 2 and 3 of 
Schedule 8 to that Order. 
Two objections were received and duly considered. One of the objections was subsequently 
withdrawn. 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as the Annesborough Industrial Estate, Lurgan (Abandonment) Order 
(Northern Ireland) 2024 and shall come into operation on 5th March 2024. 
Application 
2. The areas of road described in the Schedule are abandoned. 
3.—(1) Eight street lighting columns shall be removed. 
(2) All other existing cables, wires, mains, pipes or other apparatus placed along, across, over or 
under the abandoned area of road shall be retained. 
(3) All existing rights as to the use or maintenance of such cables, wires, mains, pipes or other 
apparatus shall be preserved.
