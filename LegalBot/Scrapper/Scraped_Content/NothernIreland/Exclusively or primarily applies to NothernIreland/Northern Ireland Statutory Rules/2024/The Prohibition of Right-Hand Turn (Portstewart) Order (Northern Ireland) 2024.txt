The Department for Infrastructure(a) makes the following Order in exercise of the powers 
conferred by Article 4(1), (2) and (3) of the Road Traffic Regulation (Northern Ireland) Order 
1997(b) and now vested in it(c). 
The Department has consulted such persons as it considered appropriate in accordance with 
paragraphs 1 and 2 of Schedule 1 to that Order. 
The Department has published a notice in compliance with paragraphs 1 and 3 of the Schedule. 
No written objection or other representation has been received. 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as the Prohibition of Right-Hand Turn (Portstewart) Order (Northern 
Ireland) 2024 and shall come into operation on 3rd April 2024. 
Prohibition of right-hand turn 
2. Subject to Article 3, a person shall not cause or permit any vehicles proceeding in a south-
easterly direction along Harbour Place, Portstewart, to make a right-hand turn into The Promenade 
during the hours of 12.00 noon to 12.00 midnight on the days Saturday and Sunday. 
Exceptions 
3. Article 2 shall not apply to— 
(a) a vehicle being used for fire and rescue, ambulance, police or military purposes; or 
(b) a vehicle, the driver or rider of which is acting upon the direction or with the permission 
of a constable in uniform.

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Order) 
This Order prohibits vehicles travelling in a south-easterly direction along Harbour Place, 
Portstewart from making a right-hand turn into The Promenade during the hours 12.00 noon to 
12.00 midnight on the days Saturday and Sunday. 
Any person who acts in contravention of this Order shall be guilty of an offence under the Road 
Traffic Regulation (Northern Ireland) Order 1997 (“the Regulation Order”) and shall be liable on 
summary conviction to a fine not exceeding level 3 on the standard scale (£1,000), in accordance 
with the Road Traffic Offenders (Northern Ireland) Order 1996 as amended  by the Regulation 
Order. 
Traffic signs indication the effect of this Order will in due course be placed on the road.
