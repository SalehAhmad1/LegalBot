The Department for Infrastructure(a) makes the following Order in exercise of its powers 
conferred by Article 68(1), (3) and (5) of the Roads (Northern Ireland) Order 1993(b) and now 
vested in it(c). 
The Department in accordance with Article 68(4) of that Order proposes on the completion of 
works providing alternative facilities for road traffic to stop-up Lurgan Road, Portadown to motor 
vehicles at the lines described in the Schedule. 
Notice has been published, served and displayed in compliance with paragraphs 1, 2 and 3 of 
Schedule 8 to that Order. 
No objection has been received. 
Citation, commencement and interpretation 
1.—(1) This Order may be cited as the Lurgan Road, Portadown (Stopping-Up) Order (Northern 
Ireland) 2024 and shall come into operation on 30th September 2024. 
(2) In this Order “the developer” means Sustar Limited whose registered office is situated at 23 
Annaghmore Road, Portadown, BT62 1NA. 
Application 
2.—(1) The developer shall carry out such works as the Department considers necessary for the 
provision of alternative facilities for road traffic and on the completion of works Lurgan Road, 
Portadown shall be stopped-up to motor vehicles at the lines described in the Schedule. 
(2) The Department shall carry out such works as it considers necessary to prevent or restrict to 
such extent as the Department thinks fit, access to the road described in the Schedule by motor 
vehicles.

3.—(1) All existing cables, wires, mains, pipes or other apparatus placed along, across, over or
