The Department for Infrastructure(a) makes the following Order in exercise of the powers 
conferred by Article 4(1), (2) and (3) of the Road Traffic Regulation (Northern Ireland) Order 
1997(b) and now vested in it(c). 
The Department has consulted such persons as it considered appropriate in compliance with 
paragraphs 1 and 2 of Schedule 1 to that Order. 
The Department has published a notice in compliance with paragraphs 1 and 3 of that Schedule. 
Four objections have been received. Two have been withdrawn and two have been duly 
considered and no other representations have been received. 
Citation and commencement 
1. This Order may be cited as The Waiting Restrictions (Lisburn) (Amendment) Order (Northern 
Ireland) 2020 and shall come into operation on 11th January 2021. 
Amendment 
2. The Waiting Restrictions (Lisburn) Order (Northern Ireland) 2015(d) shall be amended by the 
addition to Schedule 1 of the following items. 
 
“28. 
Forthill (west and north-west side) – from a point 235 metres north-east of its 
junction with Magheralave Road for a distance of 77 metres in a northerly and then 
north-easterly direction. 
29. 
Forthill (east and south-east side) – from a point 273 metres north-east of its 
junction with Magheralave Road for a distance of 98 metres in a northerly and then 
north-easterly direction.”

EXPLANATORY NOTE 
(This note is not part of the Order) 
This Order amends The Waiting Restrictions (Lisburn) Order (Northern Ireland) 2015 (“the 
Order”) by the addition of lengths of no waiting at any time restrictions at Forthill, Lisburn. 
A penalty charge (£90) is payable with respect to a vehicle involved in a contravention of the 
Order in accordance with the Traffic Management (Northern Ireland) Order 2005 and The Penalty 
Charges (Prescribed Amounts) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2006. 
Traffic signs indicating the effect of the Order will in due course be placed on the road.
