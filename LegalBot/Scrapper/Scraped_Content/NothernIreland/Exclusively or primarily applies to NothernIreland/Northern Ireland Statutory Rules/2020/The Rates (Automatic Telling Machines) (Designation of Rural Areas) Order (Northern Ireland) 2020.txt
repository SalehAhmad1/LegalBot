STATUTORY RULES OF NORTHERN IRELAND
2020 No. 160
RATES
The Rates (Automatic Telling Machines) (Designation of Rural Areas) Order (Northern Ireland) 2020
Made
29th July 2020
Coming into operation
24th August 2020
The Department of Finance(1) makes the following Order in exercise of the powers conferred by Article 42(1G) of the Rates (Northern Ireland) Order 1977(2).
(1)
The Department of Finance and Personnel was renamed the Department of Finance by section 1(4) of, and Schedule 1 to, the Departments Act (Northern Ireland) 2016 (c.5 (N.I.))
(2)
S.I. 1977/2157 (N.I.28); Article 42 was inserted by Article 25 of the Rates (Amendment) (Northern Ireland) Order 2006 (S.I. 2006/2954 (N.I.18))Citation and commencement
1.  This Order may be cited as the Rates (Automatic Telling Machines) (Designation of Rural Areas) Order (Northern Ireland) 2020 and shall come into operation on 24th August 2020.Designation of rural areas
2.  Wards as set out in the Schedule are designated as rural areas for the purpose of Article 42 (1F) of the Rates (Northern Ireland) Order 1977.Revocation
3.  The Rates (Automatic Telling Machines) (Designation of Rural Areas) Order (Northern Ireland) 2016(1) is revoked.
(1)
