STATUTORY RULES OF NORTHERN IRELAND
2020 No. 231
LICENSING
The Licensing (Designation of Outdoor Stadia) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2020
Made
26th October 2020
Coming into force
30th November 2020
The Department for Communities(1), makes the following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred on it by Article 2AA(c) of the Licensing (Northern Ireland) Order 1996(2), and now vested in it(3).
(1)
Formerly the Department for Social Development; see 2016 c. 5 (N.I.), S.I(7)
(2)
S.I. 1996/3158; Article 2AA was inserted by Section 2 of the Licensing (Northern Ireland) Act 1996 (c.24 (N.I.)
(3)
See Article 8(b) of and Part II of Schedule 6 to the Department (Transfer and Assignment of Functions) Order (Northern Ireland) (S.R.1999 No. 481)Citation and commencement
1.  These Regulations may be cited as the Licensing (Designation of Outdoor Stadia) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2020 and shall come into operation on 30th November 2020.Designation of outdoor stadium
2.  Down Royal Racecourse at Maze, Lisburn, BT27 5RW is designated as a stadium which is of importance to the whole of Northern Ireland.Sealed with the Official Seal of the Department for Communities on the 26th October 2020.
(L.S.)
Anthony Carleton
A senior officer of the Department for CommunitiesEXPLANATORY NOTE
(This note is not part of the Regulations)
These Regulations designate Down Royal Racecourse, Maze, Lisburn, BT27 5RW as a stadium which is of importance to the whole of Northern Ireland. This designation allows Down Royal Racecourse to apply to the Courts for a liquor licence at the stadium.
